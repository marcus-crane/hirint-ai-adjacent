### ABOUT THE JOB

Anduril's Manufacturing Engineering is seeking a Manufacturing Tooling Engineer to join our team in Ohio. The Manufacturing Engineering department develops and implements robust manufacturing processes and tooling solutions to ensure Anduril's defense products are produced efficiently, reliably, and at scale at Anduril.  
  
As Manufacturing Tooling Engineer, you will design, develop, and maintain innovative tooling systems that enhance our manufacturing capabilities and production efficiency. The right person for this role has strong mechanical design skills, expertise in CAD software, experience with tool fabrication processes, and knowledge of manufacturing systems integration. If you are someone who thrives in a fast-paced environment, enjoys solving complex tooling challenges, and is passionate about supporting cutting-edge defense technology manufacturing with creative tooling solutions then this role is for you.  
  
**This role will be based temporarily at Santa Ana, CA for a 3 month training period before transitioning to Asheville, OH.**   
  
**WHAT YOU’LL DO**

* Build things that build things
* Design and develop custom tooling, fixtures, and jigs to support manufacturing operations across multiple product lines
* Design integrated end of arm tooling for manufacturing operations that demand high quality and repeatability
* Create detailed technical drawings, 3D models, and specifications for tool fabrication using advanced CAD software
* Troubleshoot and resolve tooling-related issues in production environments to minimize downtime
* Collaborate with cross-functional teams including design engineers, manufacturing engineers, and production staff to integrate tooling solutions with manufacturing systems
* Conduct failure analysis on tooling and implement design improvements to enhance performance and durability. Develop and release PM procedures.
* Manage tooling budgets, vendor relationships, and outside fabrication of specialized tooling
* Support continuous improvement initiatives aimed at reducing costs and improving manufacturing throughput
* Ensure all tooling designs comply with safety standards and regulatory requirements

**REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS**

* Bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering, Manufacturing Engineering, or related technical field
* 5+ years of experience in manufacturing tooling design and implementation
* Expert capability with (at least one) 3D CAD software (NX, SolidWorks, Inventor, or CATIA)
* Demonstrated experience with tooling design for precision manufacturing processes
* Knowledge of machining processes, materials properties, and manufacturing methods
* Expert in GD&T (Geometric Dimensioning and Tolerancing)
* Eligible to obtain and maintain an active U.S. Secret security clearance

**PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS**

* Experience in defense, aerospace, or other high-reliability manufacturing environments
* Knowledge of automation systems and integration with tooling solutions
* Experience with tool lifecycle management and maintenance planning
* Familiarity with lean manufacturing principles and Six Sigma methodologies
* Background in rapid prototyping and iterative design
* Currently possesses and is able to maintain an active U.S. Secret security clearance

### **Protecting Yourself from Recruitment Scams**

Anduril is committed to maintaining the integrity of our Talent acquisition process and the security of our candidates. We've observed a rise in sophisticated phishing and fraudulent schemes where individuals impersonate Anduril representatives, luring job seekers with false interviews or job offers. These scammers often attempt to extract payment or sensitive personal information.

To ensure your safety and help you navigate your job search with confidence, please keep the following critical points in mind:

* **No Financial Requests:**Anduril will never solicit payment or demand personal financial details (such as banking information, credit card numbers, or social security numbers) at any stage of our hiring process. Our legitimate recruitment is entirely free for candidates.
* **Please always verify communications:**
  + Direct from Anduril: If you receive an email from one of our recruiters, it will *only* come from an `@anduril.com` address.
  + Via Agency Partner: If contacted by a recruiting agency for an Anduril role, their email will clearly identify their agency. If you suspect any suspicious activity, please verify the agency's authenticity by reaching out to [contact@anduril.com](mailto:contact@anduril.com).
* **Exercise Caution with Unsolicited Outreach:** If you receive any communication that appears suspicious, contains grammatical errors, or makes unusual requests, do not engage. Always confirm the sender's email domain is @anduril.com before providing any personal information or clicking on links.
* **What to Do If You Suspect Fraud:** Should you encounter any questionable or fraudulent outreach claiming to be from Anduril, please report it immediately to [contact@anduril.com](mailto:contact@anduril.com). Your proactive caution is invaluable in protecting your personal information and upholding the security and trustworthiness of our recruitment efforts.
