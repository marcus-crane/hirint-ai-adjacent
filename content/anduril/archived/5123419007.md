Anduril Industries is a defense technology company with a mission to transform U.S. and allied military capabilities with advanced technology. By bringing the expertise, technology, and business model of the 21st century’s most innovative companies to the defense industry, Anduril is changing how military systems are designed, built and sold. Anduril’s family of systems is powered by Lattice OS, an AI-powered operating system that turns thousands of data streams into a realtime, 3D command and control center. As the world enters an era of strategic competition, Anduril is committed to bringing cutting-edge autonomy, AI, computer vision, sensor fusion, and networking technology to the military in months, not years.

## 

### ABOUT THE JOB

This is a high-intensity, high-access position sitting directly next to the CEO and working closely with the Chief of Staff and Deputy Chief of Staff. You will help run the executive office by owning the calendar, supporting leadership briefs, serving as a key point of contact for internal and external meeting-related communications, helping track execution across teams, and coordinating key external engagements and VIP visits.  
  
You will help ensure the CEO’s time, attention, and follow-through are directed toward the highest-leverage issues in the business. The position sits at the intersection of scheduling, logistics, execution, and leadership support. It requires strong judgment, exceptional organization, and the ability to operate calmly in a fast-moving environment.  
  
Done well, you will improve the efficiency and effectiveness of the CEO Office and serve as a force multiplier for the team.  
  
High performers in this position will gain an unusually strong understanding of how Anduril operates at the top of the company and may later move into key roles across the business, including program management, business operations, strategy, or growth.

#### WHAT YOU’LL DO

* Own and help prioritize the CEO’s calendar, ensuring time is allocated to the highest-leverage activities
* Prepare and review leadership prep packets, executive materials, and meeting-related communications
* Serve as a key point of contact for internal and external communications on behalf of the CEO, especially related to meetings and logistics
* Manage scheduling, meeting sequencing, and day-of execution across the CEO’s internal and external commitments
* Coordinate and support key external engagements and VIP visits, both internally and externally
* Plan and execute seamless domestic and international travel for the CEO, including trip mechanics, logistics, and contingency planning
* Help the CEO Office operate at a high level through strong judgment, organization, and follow-through
* Support meeting notes, follow-up tracking, and other projects across the CEO Office as needed, including escalating blockers early and helping ensure key next steps are closed out

#### REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS

* 1–4 years of experience in a high-responsibility environment, such as operations, banking, program management, consulting, government campaigns, military service, startups, executive support, or similarly demanding roles
* Exceptional written and verbal communication skills, including the ability to prepare clear, high-stakes written materials and communicate effectively with senior leaders and external stakeholders
* Strong organizational skills, attention to detail, and follow-through, with the ability to manage competing priorities under pressure
* Experience coordinating across multiple stakeholders and functions in a fast-moving environment
* High ownership, sound judgment, and maturity, especially when handling sensitive information, shifting priorities, and ambiguous situations
* Demonstrated track record of leadership, competition, or high performance, whether through athletics, military service, student leadership, campus organizations, or prior roles
* Willingness to work flexible hours and operate on the CEO’s schedule when needed
* Completed undergraduate degree
* Must be a U.S. Person due to required access to U.S. export controlled information or facilities

### **Protecting Yourself from Recruitment Scams**

Anduril is committed to maintaining the integrity of our Talent acquisition process and the security of our candidates. We've observed a rise in sophisticated phishing and fraudulent schemes where individuals impersonate Anduril representatives, luring job seekers with false interviews or job offers. These scammers often attempt to extract payment or sensitive personal information.

To ensure your safety and help you navigate your job search with confidence, please keep the following critical points in mind:

* **No Financial Requests:**Anduril will never solicit payment or demand personal financial details (such as banking information, credit card numbers, or social security numbers) at any stage of our hiring process. Our legitimate recruitment is entirely free for candidates.
* **Please always verify communications:**
  + Direct from Anduril: If you receive an email from one of our recruiters, it will *only* come from an `@anduril.com` address.
  + Via Agency Partner: If contacted by a recruiting agency for an Anduril role, their email will clearly identify their agency. If you suspect any suspicious activity, please verify the agency's authenticity by reaching out to [contact@anduril.com](mailto:contact@anduril.com).
* **Exercise Caution with Unsolicited Outreach:** If you receive any communication that appears suspicious, contains grammatical errors, or makes unusual requests, do not engage. Always confirm the sender's email domain is @anduril.com before providing any personal information or clicking on links.
* **What to Do If You Suspect Fraud:** Should you encounter any questionable or fraudulent outreach claiming to be from Anduril, please report it immediately to [contact@anduril.com](mailto:contact@anduril.com). Your proactive caution is invaluable in protecting your personal information and upholding the security and trustworthiness of our recruitment efforts.
