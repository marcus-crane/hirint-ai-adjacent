By bringing the expertise, technology, and business model of the 21st century’s most innovative companies to the defense industry, Anduril is changing how military systems are designed, built and sold. As the world enters an era of strategic competition, Anduril is committed to bringing cutting-edge autonomy, AI, computer vision, sensor fusion, and networking technology to the military in months, not years.

### ABOUT THE JOB

As the Analytics Engineerfor Sentry, you will be at the forefront of transforming raw data into strategic advantages for our business line. This is a hands-on, builder-operator role for a highly technical and strategic individual who thrives on untangling complex, ambiguous problems. You will design, implement, and maintain robust data systems, enabling our teams to make more informed decisions. This role requires a deep understanding of data engineering principles, a strong analytical mindset, and the ability to effectively bridge the gap between technical capabilities and business needs. You will be instrumental in developing data products and tooling that directly impact our operational efficiency, product development, and overall business strategy.

## WHAT YOU'LL DO

* **Build and maintain data pipelines and analytical systems:** Design data workflows that ingest, transform, and model data from diverse sources (deployment systems, inventory databases, monitoring tools, business systems), ensuring data quality and accessibility
* **Develop dashboards and analytics that shape decision-making:** Create data products ranging from ad hoc analyses for rapid iteration to scalable dashboards and trackers that provide visibility into deployment tracking, inventory status, component lifecycle, fleet performance, and system reliability
* **Own the full product lifecycle:** Act as PM, designer, and builder for analytics tools—defining requirements, designing user experiences, building technical solutions, and iterating based on user feedback
* **Collaborate to bridge technical and operational needs:** Partner closely with cross-functional teams to understand their data challenges and translate them into effective data solutions, working within systems like ERP, MRP, and other operational platforms
* **Drive operational efficiency through data analysis:** Perform quantitative analyses including deployment data validation, inventory reconciliation, trend analysis, variance investigation, and root-cause analysis to identify bottlenecks and optimize processes
* **Enhance Sentry fleet management and sustainment:** Utilize data to understand fleet performance in the field, optimize deployment strategies, and improve the efficiency of sustainment operations for the growing Sentry fleet
* **Iterate based on feedback and evolving requirements:** Continuously improve data products by incorporating stakeholder feedback and adapting to changing operational needs as Sentry scales
* **Document and champion data quality:** Ensure data definitions, business logic, and methodologies are well-documented and understood across teams, promoting data literacy and self-service capabilities

## REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS

* 5+ years of professional experience in an analytics-focused role (Analytics Engineer, Data Engineer, Data Analyst, or similar)
* Degree in a quantitative or analytical field (Statistics, Engineering, Data Science, Math, Operations Research, Computer Science) or equivalent practical experience
* **You're technical**—you are data-driven, understand systems, and can write SQL and Python (or similar analytical languages) to manipulate and analyze data.
* **You're a builder**—you take ownership end-to-end, from defining the problem to designing the solution to building and deploying it
* **You're clear**—you communicate effectively in both technical and non-technical contexts, translating business problems into data solutions
* You have a **strong bias for action** and a preference for designing better solutions rather than patching around existing problems
* You **thrive on untangling complex, messy problems** and are energized by creating order from chaos in a rapidly evolving operational environment
* Ability to work cross-functionally with engineering, product, supply chain, and operations teams to drive outcomes
* Must be authorized to work in the United States

### **Protecting Yourself from Recruitment Scams**

Anduril is committed to maintaining the integrity of our Talent acquisition process and the security of our candidates. We've observed a rise in sophisticated phishing and fraudulent schemes where individuals impersonate Anduril representatives, luring job seekers with false interviews or job offers. These scammers often attempt to extract payment or sensitive personal information.

To ensure your safety and help you navigate your job search with confidence, please keep the following critical points in mind:

* **No Financial Requests:**Anduril will never solicit payment or demand personal financial details (such as banking information, credit card numbers, or social security numbers) at any stage of our hiring process. Our legitimate recruitment is entirely free for candidates.
* **Please always verify communications:**
  + Direct from Anduril: If you receive an email from one of our recruiters, it will *only* come from an `@anduril.com` address.
  + Via Agency Partner: If contacted by a recruiting agency for an Anduril role, their email will clearly identify their agency. If you suspect any suspicious activity, please verify the agency's authenticity by reaching out to [contact@anduril.com](mailto:contact@anduril.com).
* **Exercise Caution with Unsolicited Outreach:** If you receive any communication that appears suspicious, contains grammatical errors, or makes unusual requests, do not engage. Always confirm the sender's email domain is @anduril.com before providing any personal information or clicking on links.
* **What to Do If You Suspect Fraud:** Should you encounter any questionable or fraudulent outreach claiming to be from Anduril, please report it immediately to [contact@anduril.com](mailto:contact@anduril.com). Your proactive caution is invaluable in protecting your personal information and upholding the security and trustworthiness of our recruitment efforts.
