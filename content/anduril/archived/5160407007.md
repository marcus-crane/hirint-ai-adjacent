Anduril Industries is a defense technology company with a mission to transform U.S. and allied military capabilities with advanced technology. By bringing the expertise, technology, and business model of the 21st century’s most innovative companies to the defense industry, Anduril is changing how military systems are designed, built and sold. Anduril’s family of systems is powered by Lattice OS, an AI-powered operating system that turns thousands of data streams into a realtime, 3D command and control center. As the world enters an era of strategic competition, Anduril is committed to bringing cutting-edge autonomy, AI, computer vision, sensor fusion, and networking technology to the military in months, not years.

### **ABOUT THE TEAM**

As Software Engineer, you will join the team responsible for pulling our massive simulation workloads out of the cloud and onto first-of-its-kind, on-premise hardware racks. You will build the software automations, tooling, and infrastructure to deploy these physical server racks directly to customer sites and internal testing labs. This will allow operators to train and test drone swarm capabilities 24/7 with zero variable cloud costs.  

This is a pure software and systems engineering role. You will write actual code to solve low-level infrastructure, networking, PKI, and orchestration challenges this is not a standard consumer-web SRE role, a manual sysadmin position, or an ML/data pipeline role. We need an experienced engineer who can operate with high autonomy, design MVP systems from scratch, and mentor junior engineers as the team grows. 

Responsibilities 

* Build, automate, and maintain the next-generation simulation platform that migrates massive virtual testing environments off AWS and onto physical, on-premise hardware racks.
* Write clean, production-grade software and configuration management tooling (primarily in Python, Go, or Rust) to turn complex hardware configurations, security boundaries, and networking requirements into a seamless "push-button" deployment.
* Debug complex, distributed systems issues across multiple layers of the stack, from physical network protocols and bare-metal hardware up to the software application layer.
* Improve core system reliability through root-cause analysis, system monitoring, and the creation of custom tooling capable of managing large-scale, physical edge deployments.

**Required Qualifications**

* 3+ years of software engineering experience, with at least 1+ year focused specifically on low-level infrastructure, bare-metal deployment, or systems engineering.
* Production-grade software development skills in Python (required), with a strong willingness to write or learn Go or Rust for infrastructure tooling.
* Deep hands-on experience and familiarity with either Kubernetes OR NixOS.
* Demonstrated technical expertise in systems engineering, including networking protocols, application development, and operating system internals.
* Bachelors, Masters, PhD in Computer Science, Computer Engineering, Software Engineering, Physics, Robotics, Math, or relevant field
* Must be eligible to obtain and maintain an active U.S. Secret Security Clearance.

**Preferred Qualifications**

* Active U.S. Secret or TS/SCI security clearance (highly preferred).
* Experience building, scaling, and managing physical, on-prem server racks, private networks, PKIs, and certificate infrastructure (rather than purely cloud-native AWS/GCP setups).
* Experience with Nix/NixOS for reproducible configurations and deployment.
* Experience managing GPU-accelerated workflows and physical hardware nodes for graphics, parallel computing, or intensive simulations.
* Prior experience delivering and maintaining systems that run on air-gapped, security-hardened networks within the defense, aerospace, or space sectors.

### **Protecting Yourself from Recruitment Scams**

Anduril is committed to maintaining the integrity of our Talent acquisition process and the security of our candidates. We've observed a rise in sophisticated phishing and fraudulent schemes where individuals impersonate Anduril representatives, luring job seekers with false interviews or job offers. These scammers often attempt to extract payment or sensitive personal information.

To ensure your safety and help you navigate your job search with confidence, please keep the following critical points in mind:

* **No Financial Requests:**Anduril will never solicit payment or demand personal financial details (such as banking information, credit card numbers, or social security numbers) at any stage of our hiring process. Our legitimate recruitment is entirely free for candidates.
* **Please always verify communications:**
  + Direct from Anduril: If you receive an email from one of our recruiters, it will *only* come from an `@anduril.com` address.
  + Via Agency Partner: If contacted by a recruiting agency for an Anduril role, their email will clearly identify their agency. If you suspect any suspicious activity, please verify the agency's authenticity by reaching out to [contact@anduril.com](mailto:contact@anduril.com).
* **Exercise Caution with Unsolicited Outreach:** If you receive any communication that appears suspicious, contains grammatical errors, or makes unusual requests, do not engage. Always confirm the sender's email domain is @anduril.com before providing any personal information or clicking on links.
* **What to Do If You Suspect Fraud:** Should you encounter any questionable or fraudulent outreach claiming to be from Anduril, please report it immediately to [contact@anduril.com](mailto:contact@anduril.com). Your proactive caution is invaluable in protecting your personal information and upholding the security and trustworthiness of our recruitment efforts.
