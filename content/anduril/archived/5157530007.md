### ABOUT THE JOB

We're seeking a **Senior Manager, People Operations Technical Programs & Strategy** to serve as the technical program management leader for our People Operations & Total Rewards organization. You'll be the critical bridge between our People Systems team, functional Centers of Excellence (Compensation, Benefits, Payroll, Global Mobility), People Data analytics team, and cross-functional partners in Corporate Technology, Finance, and Legal.  
  
This role thrives at the intersection of strategy and execution—translating People priorities into technical programs, orchestrating complex HRIS implementations, and driving process automation across our global People operations. If you excel at turning complexity into clarity and can speak both technical and business languages fluently, this is your opportunity to shape how a high-growth defense tech company scales.

#### 

#### WHAT YOU’LL DO

**Lead Cross-Functional Technical Programs**

* Own the technical program roadmap across People Systems, COEs, and People Data teams
* Manage end-to-end HRIS implementations and system enhancements from planning through adoption
* Drive complex initiatives spanning multiple teams including system upgrades, integrations, and process automation

**Bridge Technical and Business Stakeholders**

* Translate technical requirements into business context and vice versa between People Systems administrators, COE subject matter experts, and business partners
* Partner with People Data team to ensure technical programs support analytics and reporting needs
* Collaborate with Corporate Technology, Finance, Legal, and Corporate Operations on enterprise-wide initiatives

**Drive Process Excellence and Scale**

* Identify and implement automation opportunities across Benefits, Payroll, Compensation, and Mobility functions
* Establish data governance frameworks ensuring compliance with GDPR, CCPA, and SOX requirements
* Maintain business continuity during system transitions and organizational changes

**Support Strategic Initiatives**

* Contribute to IPO readiness projects including SOX-compliant process development and control documentation
* Support M&A integrations from a technical program perspective including system consolidation and integration planning

**Future Team Leadership**

* Mentor and supervise 1-2 technical program managers as the team scales

#### REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS

* 8+ years in Technical Program Management, Product Management, and/or HR Operations in high-growth technology environments
* Proven track record leading complex, cross-functional technical programs spanning multiple business functions

* Exceptional collaboration skills with ability to influence across technical and non-technical stakeholders
* Excellent written and verbal communication skills

### **Protecting Yourself from Recruitment Scams**

Anduril is committed to maintaining the integrity of our Talent acquisition process and the security of our candidates. We've observed a rise in sophisticated phishing and fraudulent schemes where individuals impersonate Anduril representatives, luring job seekers with false interviews or job offers. These scammers often attempt to extract payment or sensitive personal information.

To ensure your safety and help you navigate your job search with confidence, please keep the following critical points in mind:

* **No Financial Requests:**Anduril will never solicit payment or demand personal financial details (such as banking information, credit card numbers, or social security numbers) at any stage of our hiring process. Our legitimate recruitment is entirely free for candidates.
* **Please always verify communications:**
  + Direct from Anduril: If you receive an email from one of our recruiters, it will *only* come from an `@anduril.com` address.
  + Via Agency Partner: If contacted by a recruiting agency for an Anduril role, their email will clearly identify their agency. If you suspect any suspicious activity, please verify the agency's authenticity by reaching out to [contact@anduril.com](mailto:contact@anduril.com).
* **Exercise Caution with Unsolicited Outreach:** If you receive any communication that appears suspicious, contains grammatical errors, or makes unusual requests, do not engage. Always confirm the sender's email domain is @anduril.com before providing any personal information or clicking on links.
* **What to Do If You Suspect Fraud:** Should you encounter any questionable or fraudulent outreach claiming to be from Anduril, please report it immediately to [contact@anduril.com](mailto:contact@anduril.com). Your proactive caution is invaluable in protecting your personal information and upholding the security and trustworthiness of our recruitment efforts.
