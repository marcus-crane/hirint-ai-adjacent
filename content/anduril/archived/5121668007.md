### ABOUT THE JOB

As a Manufacturing Engineering Technician, you’ll play a critical part in ensuring production operations run smoothly and efficiently. Your primary responsibility will be responding to and resolving production system issues in real-time, leveraging your strong Linux knowledge and troubleshooting skills to unblock a plethora of problems ranging from networking to hardware connectivity on our Group 3 UAS platform: Altius. Working closely with our Production Operations, Manufacturing Engineering, and Software Engineering teams, you will focus on diagnosing problems, curating and documenting troubleshooting workflows, and escalating deeper technical challenges to engineering support.   
  
This role combines on-the-ground technical troubleshooting, operational rigor, and proactive process improvement. You’ll act as the first line of defense for keeping production systems up and running, ensuring minimal downtime for critical manufacturing processes. Key to success in this role will be robust Linux systems expertise, strong communication, and strong collaboration skills. You’ll also have opportunities to impact long-term improvements by building tools, scripts, and documentation to streamline future troubleshooting efforts.

#### WHAT YOU'LL DO

* Provide hands-on, real-time technical support to resolve Linux-based production system issues.
* Debug hardware, software, and networking faults using diagnostic tools like logs, network traffic analyzers (e.g., Wireshark)
* Investigate and resolve recurring issues such as system bricking, connectivity problems, and compatibility errors
* Develop, maintain, and curate documentation, troubleshooting guides, and standard operating procedures (SOPs) for common problems
* Collaborate with cross-functional teams to escalate complex issues and drive systemic problem resolution
* Use Bash/Python scripting to build lightweight tools and automate repetitive debugging tasks
* Advocate for systems/process improvements, and communicate tooling needs to engineering teams responsible for long-term solutions
* Support production technicians when systems become blocked and ensure seamless handoffs with second-line support (T2 escalation)

#### REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS

* 2+ years of hands-on experience with Linux systems in a troubleshooting or support role.
* Proficiency with Bash scripting/familiarity with Python
* Strong understanding of diagnostic tools
* Demonstrated ability to troubleshoot and triage hardware and software issues
* Experience operating in high-pressure environments where uptime is critical (manufacturing, robotics, IoT, cloud systems, etc.)
* Effective communication skills with the ability to distill technical findings into actionable plans for technical and non-technical stakeholders
* Experience collaborating with cross-functional teams in a production or technical support setting
* Willingness to work production hours and overtime as needed

#### PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS

* Familiarity with manufacturing or robotics systems with Linux-based deployments
* Interest in automation devices, home electronics, or personal projects involving Arduino, Raspberry Pi, or similar technologies
* Knowledge of hardware troubleshooting, including connectors, harnesses, and pin configurations
* Experience documenting and curating detailed knowledge bases, workflow tools, or SOPs
* Exposure to enterprise-scale systems, IoT devices, or environments with similar high-availability requirements
* A passion for figuring out complex systems, diving deep into logs, and building tools to improve future workflows

### **Protecting Yourself from Recruitment Scams**

Anduril is committed to maintaining the integrity of our Talent acquisition process and the security of our candidates. We've observed a rise in sophisticated phishing and fraudulent schemes where individuals impersonate Anduril representatives, luring job seekers with false interviews or job offers. These scammers often attempt to extract payment or sensitive personal information.

To ensure your safety and help you navigate your job search with confidence, please keep the following critical points in mind:

* **No Financial Requests:**Anduril will never solicit payment or demand personal financial details (such as banking information, credit card numbers, or social security numbers) at any stage of our hiring process. Our legitimate recruitment is entirely free for candidates.
* **Please always verify communications:**
  + Direct from Anduril: If you receive an email from one of our recruiters, it will *only* come from an `@anduril.com` address.
  + Via Agency Partner: If contacted by a recruiting agency for an Anduril role, their email will clearly identify their agency. If you suspect any suspicious activity, please verify the agency's authenticity by reaching out to [contact@anduril.com](mailto:contact@anduril.com).
* **Exercise Caution with Unsolicited Outreach:** If you receive any communication that appears suspicious, contains grammatical errors, or makes unusual requests, do not engage. Always confirm the sender's email domain is @anduril.com before providing any personal information or clicking on links.
* **What to Do If You Suspect Fraud:** Should you encounter any questionable or fraudulent outreach claiming to be from Anduril, please report it immediately to [contact@anduril.com](mailto:contact@anduril.com). Your proactive caution is invaluable in protecting your personal information and upholding the security and trustworthiness of our recruitment efforts.
