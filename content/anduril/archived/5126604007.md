### ABOUT THE JOB

We are seeking an analytical and strategic **Talent Acquisition (TA) Spend Manager** to lead efforts to keep Talent Acquisition cost effective as we work to hire the world’s best talent to work on the world’s hardest problems.  
  
This critical role is embedded in our Talent Acquisition team and will be responsible for driving financial discipline, transparency, and decision-making around all TA expenditures. This person will be instrumental in evolving our budgeting methodologies, driving spend accuracy and transparency, articulating investment trade-offs, identifying cost efficiencies, and articulating the return on investment (ROI) of our talent acquisition efforts. If you are passionate about connecting financial strategy with talent success and thrive in a fast-paced, data-driven environment, this is the role for you.

#### WHAT YOU’LL DO

**Budget & Planning Excellence:**

* **Drive financial forecasting efforts** for TA, anticipating future spend based on hiring demand, market trends, and critical initiatives.
* **Develop and refine robust budget methodologies** (e.g., "backpack of expenses," HC ratios) to accurately forecast and manage TA spend as real world demand changes, aligning directly with capacity planning and hiring forecasts.
* **Lead the creation and maintenance of detailed budget breakouts** across all TA sub-teams, ensuring clarity and accountability for spend allocation.
* **Collaborate closely with TA leadership and Finance** to ensure budget plans reflect company-wide priorities and operational realities.

**Spend Management & Transparency:**

* **Conduct comprehensive audits of invoices and expenditures**, ensuring accuracy, compliance, and adherence to established contracts.
* **Perform deep-dive analyses into actual spend versus budget**, identifying variances, their root causes, and elevating key insights and spend / investment trade-offs to the TA Senior Leadership Team.
* **Implement and optimize processes** for tracking, reporting, and managing TA financial data, leveraging existing tools and recommending improvements.
* **Ensure all TA financial activities adhere to company policies, procurement guidelines, and relevant financial regulations.**

**Cost Optimization & Vendor Management:**

* **Proactively identify and drive significant cost-saving opportunities** by rationalizing vendors, negotiating favorable terms and pricing with new and existing suppliers, and re-allocating unused licenses or subscriptions.
* **Manage end-to-end relationships with key TA vendors**, monitoring performance, ensuring contractual obligations are met, and identifying opportunities for greater value.
* **Partner with Procurement and Legal** on contract reviews, negotiations, and vendor selection processes.

**Executive Reporting & ROI:**

* **Develop and present compelling financial reports** on key TA metrics, expenditure trends, and forecasts to TA leadership and cross-functional stakeholders.
* **Shape the narrative of TA's value proposition to the business**, going beyond traditional cost-per-hire metrics to illustrate the long-term ROI of new hires over a 12-month period.
* **Co-develop and implement (with Finance)** a fair and transparent mechanism for allocating TA's operational costs to relevant division P&Ls.
* **Translate complex financial data into actionable recommendations** that inform TA resource allocation and overall business direction.

* **Experience:** 5-8+ years of progressive experience in financial analysis, spend management, procurement, or a dedicated HR/TA finance role.
* **Analytical Prowess:** Proven track record of strong analytical, quantitative, and data modeling skills, with the ability to translate complex data into clear, concise, and actionable insights for executive leadership.
* **Cost Optimization:** Demonstrated success in identifying and driving cost savings and efficiency initiatives.
* **Technical Skills:** Advanced proficiency in Microsoft Excel (pivot tables, VLOOKUPs, financial modeling) and experience with large-scale ERP or financial management systems.

### **Protecting Yourself from Recruitment Scams**

Anduril is committed to maintaining the integrity of our Talent acquisition process and the security of our candidates. We've observed a rise in sophisticated phishing and fraudulent schemes where individuals impersonate Anduril representatives, luring job seekers with false interviews or job offers. These scammers often attempt to extract payment or sensitive personal information.

To ensure your safety and help you navigate your job search with confidence, please keep the following critical points in mind:

* **No Financial Requests:**Anduril will never solicit payment or demand personal financial details (such as banking information, credit card numbers, or social security numbers) at any stage of our hiring process. Our legitimate recruitment is entirely free for candidates.
* **Please always verify communications:**
  + Direct from Anduril: If you receive an email from one of our recruiters, it will *only* come from an `@anduril.com` address.
  + Via Agency Partner: If contacted by a recruiting agency for an Anduril role, their email will clearly identify their agency. If you suspect any suspicious activity, please verify the agency's authenticity by reaching out to [contact@anduril.com](mailto:contact@anduril.com).
* **Exercise Caution with Unsolicited Outreach:** If you receive any communication that appears suspicious, contains grammatical errors, or makes unusual requests, do not engage. Always confirm the sender's email domain is @anduril.com before providing any personal information or clicking on links.
* **What to Do If You Suspect Fraud:** Should you encounter any questionable or fraudulent outreach claiming to be from Anduril, please report it immediately to [contact@anduril.com](mailto:contact@anduril.com). Your proactive caution is invaluable in protecting your personal information and upholding the security and trustworthiness of our recruitment efforts.
