Anduril Industries is a defense technology company with a mission to transform U.S. and allied military capabilities with advanced technology. By bringing the expertise, technology, and business model of the 21st century’s most innovative companies to the defense industry, Anduril is changing how military systems are designed, built and sold. Anduril’s family of systems is powered by Lattice OS, an AI-powered operating system that turns thousands of data streams into a realtime, 3D command and control center. As the world enters an era of strategic competition, Anduril is committed to bringing cutting-edge autonomy, AI, computer vision, sensor fusion, and networking technology to the military in months, not years.

We are looking for a Propulsion Test Infrastructure Engineer to enhance our Propulsion Development team. This role is critical in developing and testing advanced propulsion systems to meet our aggressive and innovative goals. You will be responsible for developing test cells, test stands, and rigs for the testing and maturation of air breathing propulsion systems.

### WHAT YOU'LL DO

* Own the design and implementation of engine test cells
* Derive actionable requirements for the design of development and production engine test cells.
* Develop test rigs to mature propulsion subsystems
* Collaborate with EHS and Facilities teams to identify locations for test cells and rigs.

### REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS

* BS or higher degree in Mechanical or Aerospace Engineering
* 3+ years relevant engineering experience
* Experience in developing test cells for gas turbine engines, rockets, or internal combustion engines
* Experience distilling testing needs into appropriate facility/infrastructure needs
* Experience designing mechanical parts (CAD) in NX or Solidworks. Understanding of manufacturing methods (machining, sheet metal, composites, 3D printing, injection molding, castings, etc.)
* U.S. Person status is required as this position needs to access export-controlled data.

### PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS

* Experience with exhaust treatment in sensitive areas
* Experience in the development of wind tunnels or altitude test facilities
* Experience with combustion rigs or rotating hardware rigs
* Experience with test cell instrumentation and calibration
* Knowledge of proper storage and treatment procedures of jet fuel
* Knowledge of power budgeting and basic power electronics
* Familiarity with ARP741D test cell correlation procedures

Anduril is committed to maintaining the integrity of our Talent acquisition process and the security of our candidates.   + Direct from Anduril: If you receive an email from one of our recruiters, it will *only* come from an `@anduril.com` address.
