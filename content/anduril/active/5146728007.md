### ABOUT THE JOB

We are seeking a detail-oriented and analytically-minded EPM Analyst to join our rapidly growing team. Reporting to the EPM Architect & Administrator, you will play a critical role in supporting the design, implementation, and ongoing operation of our Enterprise Performance Management (EPM) system. You will apply foundational expertise to execute EPM configurations, maintain data integrity, and contribute to the development of efficient and scalable financial processes. This role is ideal for someone who thrives on solving technical challenges within a financial context, enjoys hands-on system work, and is eager to grow their EPM expertise by collaborating with experienced team members. You will operate with limited oversight, receiving guidance on more complex tasks and strategies while effectively managing assigned responsibilities.

#### WHAT YOU’LL DO

* **EPM System Administration & Support:** Assist the EPM Architect/Admin in the technical administration, maintenance, and support of the core EPM platform, ensuring its stability, performance, and data integrity.
* **Data Pipeline Contribution:** Contribute to the development, testing, and maintenance of data pipelines that feed into and from the EPM system, ensuring accuracy and consistency across integrated platforms.
* **Configuration & Enhancement Execution:** Perform configuration, maintenance, and enhancements within the EPM system as directed, adhering to established technical standards and governance principles.
* **Logic Development & Testing:** Develop, test, and maintain business rules, calculations, and workflows within the EPM system under the guidance of the EPM Architect/Admin.
* **Roadmap Feature Delivery Participation:** Participate in the execution of the EPM roadmap feature delivery, contributing to technical requirements, data conversions, testing, and other execution tasks.
* **Integration Support:** Support the integration of the EPM system with upstream and downstream sources, including performing data mapping, reconciliation, and testing activities.
* **Documentation & Best Practices:** Assist in maintaining user and system documentation and contribute to the continuous improvement of EPM processes and best practices.

#### REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS

* 3-5 years of experience in EPM system administration, development, or analysis, within a fast-paced environment.
* Foundational expertise with Planning within the Oracle Cloud EPM suite (PBCS)
* Ability to operate with limited oversight on assigned tasks and seek guidance on more complex issues or strategies.
* Strong analytical and problem-solving skills with meticulous attention to detail.
* A solid understanding of core financial concepts (e.g., budgeting, forecasting, financial reporting, general ledger).
* Excellent communication and collaboration skills to work effectively with finance and technical stakeholders.
* Appreciation for and adherence to engineering best practices, such as version control, audit-ability, and scalability.
* Must be authorized to work in the United States.

#### PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS

* Experience with ERP systems (e.g., Oracle Fusion) or other finance-related applications.
* Familiarity with data visualization tools or self-service analytics platforms (e.g., Palantir Foundry).
* Exposure to scripting languages (e.g., SQL) for data extraction, transformation, and loading.

### **Protecting Yourself from Recruitment Scams**

Anduril is committed to maintaining the integrity of our Talent acquisition process and the security of our candidates. We've observed a rise in sophisticated phishing and fraudulent schemes where individuals impersonate Anduril representatives, luring job seekers with false interviews or job offers. These scammers often attempt to extract payment or sensitive personal information.

To ensure your safety and help you navigate your job search with confidence, please keep the following critical points in mind:

* **No Financial Requests:**Anduril will never solicit payment or demand personal financial details (such as banking information, credit card numbers, or social security numbers) at any stage of our hiring process. Our legitimate recruitment is entirely free for candidates.
* **Please always verify communications:**
  + Direct from Anduril: If you receive an email from one of our recruiters, it will *only* come from an `@anduril.com` address.
  + Via Agency Partner: If contacted by a recruiting agency for an Anduril role, their email will clearly identify their agency. If you suspect any suspicious activity, please verify the agency's authenticity by reaching out to [contact@anduril.com](mailto:contact@anduril.com).
* **Exercise Caution with Unsolicited Outreach:** If you receive any communication that appears suspicious, contains grammatical errors, or makes unusual requests, do not engage. Always confirm the sender's email domain is @anduril.com before providing any personal information or clicking on links.
* **What to Do If You Suspect Fraud:** Should you encounter any questionable or fraudulent outreach claiming to be from Anduril, please report it immediately to [contact@anduril.com](mailto:contact@anduril.com). Your proactive caution is invaluable in protecting your personal information and upholding the security and trustworthiness of our recruitment efforts.
