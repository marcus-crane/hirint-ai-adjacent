### ABOUT THE JOB

You own whether the test program delivers on schedule, on cost, and at the throughput the production ramp demands. Your accountability is the program-level outcome: are we testing fast enough, at low enough cost, with high enough confidence to support delivery commitments?  
  
Success in this position hinges on three key facets:

* **Program-level test performance** - you drive test plan development and acceptance criteria alongside engineering, but you own the schedule and cost outcome. When test is the bottleneck, you diagnose why, challenge priorities, and make the call on what moves.
* **Automation maturation** - what takes days today needs to take hours as rate scales. You drive the prioritization and investment decisions that make test automation real, not aspirational.
* **Technical credibility** - you understand the systems well enough to ask the right questions when a test fails. You do not wait for escalation. You drive resolution across engineering and production because you understand the technical context.

### WHAT YOU'LL DO

* Own schedule and cost performance of the production acceptance test program, driving throughput to match production ramp targets
* Drive test plan development and acceptance criteria alongside engineering, ensuring test scope is sufficient without being excessive
* Identify and drive automation maturation opportunities, prioritizing investment against schedule and cost targets
* Drive resolution of test failures across engineering and production, diagnosing root cause and ensuring corrective action holds
* Interface with government test organizations, ensuring test cadence supports customer delivery commitments
* Build the systems that connect test results to engineering and production decisions, making test data actionable not just archival
* Own test schedule risk identification and mitigation, intervening before bottlenecks compound into delivery slips
* Leverage quantitative models (test optimization, predictive pass/fail analysis, throughput modeling) to inform test program decisions
* Prepare technical documentation, analysis reports, and briefings for internal teams, customers, and government stakeholders
* Maintain reproducible analysis pipelines using version control, scripting, and scalable workflows

### REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS

* 5+ years in technical program management, test engineering, or test operations in complex hardware or aerospace environments
* Demonstrated ownership of test program schedule and cost performance in production or acceptance testing contexts
* Experience driving test automation strategy and implementation in hardware production environments
* Technical depth sufficient to understand test failures, challenge root cause analysis, and drive resolution across teams
* Experience coordinating with government test organizations or customer acceptance processes
* Comfortable building or interpreting quantitative models to inform test and schedule decisions
* Familiar with version control, scripting, scalable workflows, and reproducible analysis practices
* Eligible to obtain and maintain an active U.S. Secret security clearance

### PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS

* Experience in aerospace, defense, or autonomous systems production test environments
* Proficiency with data tools such as Palantir Foundry, test data analytics platforms, or custom analysis stacks
* Background in test optimization modeling, discrete event simulation applied to test throughput, or predictive analytics
* Experience scaling test programs alongside production ramp from low rate to full rate
* Familiarity with acceptance test procedure development for complex electromechanical or avionics systems

### **Protecting Yourself from Recruitment Scams**

Anduril is committed to maintaining the integrity of our Talent acquisition process and the security of our candidates. We've observed a rise in sophisticated phishing and fraudulent schemes where individuals impersonate Anduril representatives, luring job seekers with false interviews or job offers. These scammers often attempt to extract payment or sensitive personal information.

To ensure your safety and help you navigate your job search with confidence, please keep the following critical points in mind:

* **No Financial Requests:**Anduril will never solicit payment or demand personal financial details (such as banking information, credit card numbers, or social security numbers) at any stage of our hiring process. Our legitimate recruitment is entirely free for candidates.
* **Please always verify communications:**
  + Direct from Anduril: If you receive an email from one of our recruiters, it will *only* come from an `@anduril.com` address.
  + Via Agency Partner: If contacted by a recruiting agency for an Anduril role, their email will clearly identify their agency. If you suspect any suspicious activity, please verify the agency's authenticity by reaching out to [contact@anduril.com](mailto:contact@anduril.com).
* **Exercise Caution with Unsolicited Outreach:** If you receive any communication that appears suspicious, contains grammatical errors, or makes unusual requests, do not engage. Always confirm the sender's email domain is @anduril.com before providing any personal information or clicking on links.
* **What to Do If You Suspect Fraud:** Should you encounter any questionable or fraudulent outreach claiming to be from Anduril, please report it immediately to [contact@anduril.com](mailto:contact@anduril.com). Your proactive caution is invaluable in protecting your personal information and upholding the security and trustworthiness of our recruitment efforts.
