Anduril Industries is a defense technology company with a mission to transform U.S. and allied military capabilities with advanced technology. By bringing the expertise, technology, and business model of the 21st century’s most innovative companies to the defense industry, Anduril is changing how military systems are designed, built and sold. Anduril’s family of systems is powered by Lattice OS, an AI-powered operating system that turns thousands of data streams into a realtime, 3D command and control center. As the world enters an era of strategic competition, Anduril is committed to bringing cutting-edge autonomy, AI, computer vision, sensor fusion, and networking technology to the military in months, not years.

### ABOUT THE TEAM

Anduril is hiring an Associate Director, Capital Markets to **design and execute debt and structured financing solutions that enable the company’s most ambitious initiatives**. This role sits within Anduril’s **Investor Relations & Capital Markets team** and is explicitly **outward-facing and execution-focused**, with a primary emphasis on **debt capital markets**.

### ABOUT THE JOB

You will translate Anduril’s operational and financial needs—spanning manufacturing, hardware deployment, and infrastructure—into **well-structured, executable financing transactions across both corporate and asset-backed contexts**.  
We are looking for candidates with strong experience in **leveraged finance, private credit, project finance, or structured financing**, who are comfortable operating in complex, non-standard environments and driving transactions from structuring through close. You will own relationships with banks, private credit funds, and other debt capital providers, and partner closely with **Finance and Treasury** to align financing strategy with liquidity, capital allocation, and long-term planning. Success in this role means ensuring that **access to debt capital is a strategic advantage, not a constraint**.

#### WHAT YOU’LL DO

* **Lead end-to-end execution of debt transactions**
  + Drive financings from initial structuring through diligence, negotiation, and closing across credit facilities, term loans, and structured debt
* **Structure solutions across corporate and asset-backed contexts**
  + Develop financing approaches tailored to Anduril’s operating model, including situations that do not fit traditional corporate lending frameworks
* **Partner with Finance and Treasury**
  + Align financing strategies with liquidity planning, capital allocation priorities, and broader financial strategy
* **Own lender engagement and process management**
  + Build and manage relationships with banks, private credit funds, and institutional lenders; run financing processes and coordinate diligence
* **Operate across a range of debt structures**
  + Execute primarily corporate debt transactions, with flexibility to incorporate asset-backed or hybrid structures where appropriate
* **Drive speed, certainty, and flexibility in capital access**
  + Ensure the company can move quickly by proactively developing financing capacity and options
* **Negotiate and close transactions**
  + Lead term sheet negotiation, documentation, and closing processes in partnership with Legal and Finance leadership

#### REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS

* 6–10+ years of experience in **leveraged finance, debt capital markets, private credit, or structured finance**
* Significant experience **executing debt transactions end-to-end**, including structuring, diligence, and closing
* Strong understanding of **credit analysis, capital structures, and lender dynamics**
* Experience working directly with **banks, direct lenders, and private credit funds**
* Ability to independently drive complex, external-facing processes under tight timelines
* Advanced financial modeling skills with a focus on **credit and downside analysis**
* Must be a U.S. Person due to required access to U.S. export controlled information or facilities

#### PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS

* Experience in **structured or asset-backed financing** (e.g., equipment finance, receivables facilities, project-based debt)
* Experience in capital-intensive or operationally complex industries (e.g., aerospace, defense, manufacturing, infrastructure)
* Familiarity with government contracting environments

#### WHAT WE VALUE

* High ownership and accountability for external execution
* Ability to operate effectively in ambiguous, fast-moving situations
* Strong judgment in structuring and negotiating complex transactions
* Bias toward action and results
* Alignment with Anduril’s mission

### **Protecting Yourself from Recruitment Scams**

Anduril is committed to maintaining the integrity of our Talent acquisition process and the security of our candidates. We've observed a rise in sophisticated phishing and fraudulent schemes where individuals impersonate Anduril representatives, luring job seekers with false interviews or job offers. These scammers often attempt to extract payment or sensitive personal information.

To ensure your safety and help you navigate your job search with confidence, please keep the following critical points in mind:

* **No Financial Requests:**Anduril will never solicit payment or demand personal financial details (such as banking information, credit card numbers, or social security numbers) at any stage of our hiring process. Our legitimate recruitment is entirely free for candidates.
* **Please always verify communications:**
  + Direct from Anduril: If you receive an email from one of our recruiters, it will *only* come from an `@anduril.com` address.
  + Via Agency Partner: If contacted by a recruiting agency for an Anduril role, their email will clearly identify their agency. If you suspect any suspicious activity, please verify the agency's authenticity by reaching out to [contact@anduril.com](mailto:contact@anduril.com).
* **Exercise Caution with Unsolicited Outreach:** If you receive any communication that appears suspicious, contains grammatical errors, or makes unusual requests, do not engage. Always confirm the sender's email domain is @anduril.com before providing any personal information or clicking on links.
* **What to Do If You Suspect Fraud:** Should you encounter any questionable or fraudulent outreach claiming to be from Anduril, please report it immediately to [contact@anduril.com](mailto:contact@anduril.com). Your proactive caution is invaluable in protecting your personal information and upholding the security and trustworthiness of our recruitment efforts.
