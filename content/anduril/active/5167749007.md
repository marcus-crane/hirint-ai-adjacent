Anduril Industries is a defense technology company with a mission to transform U.S. and allied military capabilities with advanced technology. By bringing the expertise, technology, and business model of the 21st century’s most innovative companies to the defense industry, Anduril is changing how military systems are designed, built and sold. Anduril’s family of systems is powered by Lattice OS, an AI-powered operating system that turns thousands of data streams into a realtime, 3D command and control center. As the world enters an era of strategic competition, Anduril is committed to bringing cutting-edge autonomy, AI, computer vision, sensor fusion, and networking technology to the military in months, not years.

### **About the Team & Role**

The **Quality Intelligence** team sits at the vital intersection of data, AI, and manufacturing quality at Anduril. We empower our factory floors through three distinct pillars: **Analytics, Manufacturing AI, and Vision Inspection**.

As a Quality Specialist within Quality Intelligence, you will own the end-to-end data and analytics products that directly impact our production lines, hardware quality engineers, and program leaders. Rather than sitting in a siloed back office, you will operate in a hub-and-spoke model. While HQ builds platform-grade analytics centrally, you will be the boots on the ground in Atlanta—partnering directly with the site's applications lead to localize, run, and scale the analytics our production floor depends on.

We build scorecards that catch quality drift before customers do, construct data pipelines that turn raw ERP/MES/QMS data into operational decisions, and deploy AI tools that compress hours of manual triage into minutes.

*This position is based at our Atlanta, GA facility and requires up to 25% travel to other Anduril sites and key vendors.*

### **What You’ll Do**

* **Operate at the Intersection of Hardware & Data:** Spend time on the shop floor to deeply understand quality workflows. You will translate physical manufacturing processes into well-designed, actionable digital dashboards and analytics tools.
* **Own Atlanta's Analytics Architecture:** Design, build, and operate the production scorecards, Piece Part Plan pipelines, and inspection-data analytics that run the Atlanta facility. Your local decisions will set the design patterns for other Anduril sites.
* **Wrangle Production Data:** Pull from diverse hardware production systems (ERP, MES, QMS, inventory), build robust pipelines and ontologies in Palantir Foundry and Databricks, and partner with ML practitioners to ship analytics products operators rely on.
* **Investigate Data Quality at the Root:** When a dashboard is inaccurate or a metric looks off, you are the lead investigator. You will run deep-dive analyses in SQL and Python, trace anomalies back through the technical stack, identify root causes, and fix them at the source.
* **Drive Technical Rigor:** Implement automated data-quality checks, validation rules, and monitoring directly into our pipelines to ensure our quality data is provably trustworthy.
* **Lead Data Projects End-to-End:** Partner cross-functionally with program quality leads and manufacturing engineers from initial requirements gathering to final deployment.
* **Build AI-Assisted Workflows:** Develop small applications and automated workflows in Foundry Workshop / AIP that reduce repetitive analyst work by 10x.
* **Leverage AI Aggressively:** We expect you to use AI tools (Cursor, Claude Code, Copilot, AIP) to draft pipelines, write tests, generate dashboards, and accelerate the repetitive parts of your day-to-day workflow.

### **Required Qualifications**

* **Education:** Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Mechanical Engineering, Industrial Engineering, or a related technical field from an accredited engineering program.
* **Experience:** 4+ years in a Data Engineer, Analytics Engineer, or similar role, with at least 2 years of applied data engineering experience directly within a manufacturing or hardware product engineering environment.
* **Domain Knowledge:** A strong understanding of manufacturing workflows, quality gates, and how shop-floor data impacts product quality. Comfort working directly on active production floors.
* **Data Tooling:** Production experience building and maintaining data pipelines in Palantir Foundry, Databricks, or an equivalent cloud lakehouse.
* **Advanced SQL & Python:** Strong SQL skills for handling large, multi-source datasets (heterogeneous system joins, window functions, query tuning) and Python for data transformation (Pandas, PySpark, etc.).
* **AI Tooling Competency:** Demonstrated experience utilizing modern AI coding tools with the critical-thinking ability to review and debug AI-generated outputs.
* **Problem Solving:** Proven capability to perform root-cause analysis on complex data issues.
* **Communication:** Ability to communicate plainly and effectively—translating complex data insights to a director without using jargon, and to an engineer without losing technical precision.
* **Security Clearance:** Must be eligible to obtain and maintain a U.S. Government security clearance (this role is subject to ITAR).

### **Preferred Qualifications**

* Experience supporting analytics for hardware manufacturing (New Product Introduction (NPI), production ramp, or high-volume manufacturing) across systems like ERP (Oracle, NetSuite, SAP), MES, QMS, PLM (Teamcenter), or inventory management systems.
* Familiarity with hardware quality methodologies: RCCA / 8D, FMEA, GD&T, IQC / OQC, and control-plan design.
* Experience working in defense or highly regulated manufacturing environments (ITAR, AS9100, IPC-610, MIL-STD-1916, or similar).
* Adherence to software engineering best practices: Git, code reviews, continuous integration (CI), and testing data code with the same rigor as application code.
* Experience integrating Large Language Models (LLMs) or Machine Learning models into operational workflows (e.g., RAG over operational data, AI-assisted triage, or agentic data exploration).

### **Protecting Yourself from Recruitment Scams**

Anduril is committed to maintaining the integrity of our Talent acquisition process and the security of our candidates. We've observed a rise in sophisticated phishing and fraudulent schemes where individuals impersonate Anduril representatives, luring job seekers with false interviews or job offers. These scammers often attempt to extract payment or sensitive personal information.

To ensure your safety and help you navigate your job search with confidence, please keep the following critical points in mind:

* **No Financial Requests:**Anduril will never solicit payment or demand personal financial details (such as banking information, credit card numbers, or social security numbers) at any stage of our hiring process. Our legitimate recruitment is entirely free for candidates.
* **Please always verify communications:**
  + Direct from Anduril: If you receive an email from one of our recruiters, it will *only* come from an `@anduril.com` address.
  + Via Agency Partner: If contacted by a recruiting agency for an Anduril role, their email will clearly identify their agency. If you suspect any suspicious activity, please verify the agency's authenticity by reaching out to [contact@anduril.com](mailto:contact@anduril.com).
* **Exercise Caution with Unsolicited Outreach:** If you receive any communication that appears suspicious, contains grammatical errors, or makes unusual requests, do not engage. Always confirm the sender's email domain is @anduril.com before providing any personal information or clicking on links.
* **What to Do If You Suspect Fraud:** Should you encounter any questionable or fraudulent outreach claiming to be from Anduril, please report it immediately to [contact@anduril.com](mailto:contact@anduril.com). Your proactive caution is invaluable in protecting your personal information and upholding the security and trustworthiness of our recruitment efforts.
