Anduril Industries is a defense technology company with a mission to transform U.S. and allied military capabilities with advanced technology. By bringing the expertise, technology, and business model of the 21st century’s most innovative companies to the defense industry, Anduril is changing how military systems are designed, built and sold. Anduril’s family of systems is powered by Lattice OS, an AI-powered operating system that turns thousands of data streams into a realtime, 3D command and control center. As the world enters an era of strategic competition, Anduril is committed to bringing cutting-edge autonomy, AI, computer vision, sensor fusion, and networking technology to the military in months, not years.

### ABOUT THE TEAM

The Anduril Imaging team develops state-of-the-art imaging systems across both hardware and software, deployed to tackle the most significant security challenges of America and its allies. The team is heavily involved in utilizing computer vision, perception, electro-optical, infrared, and sensor data to give our war fighters and allies an advantage.

### ABOUT THE JOB

We are seeking a hands-on **Systems Engineer** to join our team as we transition a Ladar product from the Engineering Development Unit (EDU) phase through Engineering and Manufacturing Development (EMD), and ultimately into high-rate production.   
  
You will play a pivotal role in scaling our build capacity to 10s of units per month. You will bridge the gap between design and manufacturing, lead rigorous testing campaigns, and help mature our hardware and software systems to achieve high Technology Readiness Levels (TRL) ahead of our upcoming Critical Design Review (CDR) and Manufacturing Readiness Review (MRR).  
  
A successful candidate will have a strong background in Electro-Optical (EO) systems, precision pointing, and a passion for transitioning complex hardware from R&D to the production floor.

**WHAT YOU’LL DO**

* **Drive the EDU-to-EMD Transition:** Oversee the deployment and test of our EDU builds, ensuring they are fully prepared to support field testing to demonstrate system capability and performance.
* **Lead Qualification & Testing:** Design and execute a comprehensive Design Verification Testing (DVT) campaign to environmentally test and qualify the system.
* **Prepare for Major Milestones:** Author technical documentation, define verification matrices, and lead systems-level preparation for upcoming Critical Design Reviews (CDR) and Manufacturing Readiness Reviews (MRR).
* **Scale for Production:** Collaborate with manufacturing and hardware teams to implement Design for Manufacturing (DFM) principles. Scale our production capabilities from a few units per year to a target volume of ~10s of systems per month.
* **Optimize Test Infrastructure:** Architect, design, and ruggedize manufacturing test equipment for efficiency, throughput, and reliability on the production floor.
* **Automate & Integrate:** Collaborate with software and firmware teams to implement robust test automation, while pushing the user interface (UI) and background software towards product-level maturity.
* Adapt or derive physics-based models of sensor system performance and derive requirements based on those models, and validate models against laboratory and real world data
* Support both laboratory-based and field-based experiments and demonstrations

**REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS**

* **EO/Ladar Domain Expertise:** 5+ years of hardware-centric systems engineering experience working with Electro-Optical (EO) systems, Ladar/Lidar, or high-precision pointing systems.
* **Development-to-Production Experience:** Direct experience transitioning complex, multidisciplinary systems from low Technology Readiness Levels (TRL) to high TRL / production-ready status.
* **Testing & Environmental Qualification:** Strong familiarity with DVT campaigns, environmental testing standards, and field testing operations.
* **Production Scaling Mindset:** Practical knowledge of Design for Manufacturing (DFM), test equipment ruggedization, and test process optimization/automation.
* **Milestone Execution:** Experience leading or heavily contributing to systems engineering reviews such as CDR, MRR, or PRR.
* **Collaboration & Communication:** Outstanding ability to communicate across multidisciplinary teams (optical, mechanical, electrical, firmware, software, and manufacturing).
* Advanced proficiency in an analytical toolchains/languages (e.g., MATLAB, Python)
* Strong laboratory skills (e.g. design of experiments)
* Willingness to travel to test sites.
* Must be able to obtain and hold a U.S. security clearance.

**PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS**

* Experience with software/hardware integration, specifically defining software interfaces, UI productization, and background software requirements.
* Experience with software/firmware automated test frameworks.
* Knowledge of current and next-generation advanced sensing systems and platforms

### **Protecting Yourself from Recruitment Scams**

Anduril is committed to maintaining the integrity of our Talent acquisition process and the security of our candidates. We've observed a rise in sophisticated phishing and fraudulent schemes where individuals impersonate Anduril representatives, luring job seekers with false interviews or job offers. These scammers often attempt to extract payment or sensitive personal information.

To ensure your safety and help you navigate your job search with confidence, please keep the following critical points in mind:

* **No Financial Requests:**Anduril will never solicit payment or demand personal financial details (such as banking information, credit card numbers, or social security numbers) at any stage of our hiring process. Our legitimate recruitment is entirely free for candidates.
* **Please always verify communications:**
  + Direct from Anduril: If you receive an email from one of our recruiters, it will *only* come from an `@anduril.com` address.
  + Via Agency Partner: If contacted by a recruiting agency for an Anduril role, their email will clearly identify their agency. If you suspect any suspicious activity, please verify the agency's authenticity by reaching out to [contact@anduril.com](mailto:contact@anduril.com).
* **Exercise Caution with Unsolicited Outreach:** If you receive any communication that appears suspicious, contains grammatical errors, or makes unusual requests, do not engage. Always confirm the sender's email domain is @anduril.com before providing any personal information or clicking on links.
* **What to Do If You Suspect Fraud:** Should you encounter any questionable or fraudulent outreach claiming to be from Anduril, please report it immediately to [contact@anduril.com](mailto:contact@anduril.com). Your proactive caution is invaluable in protecting your personal information and upholding the security and trustworthiness of our recruitment efforts.
