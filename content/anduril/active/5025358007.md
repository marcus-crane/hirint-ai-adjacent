Anduril Industries is a defense technology company with a mission to transform U.S. and allied military capabilities with advanced technology. By bringing the expertise, technology, and business model of the 21st century’s most innovative companies to the defense industry, Anduril is changing how military systems are designed, built and sold. Anduril’s family of systems is powered by Lattice OS, an AI-powered operating system that turns thousands of data streams into a realtime, 3D command and control center. As the world enters an era of strategic competition, Anduril is committed to bringing cutting-edge autonomy, AI, computer vision, sensor fusion, and networking technology to the military in months, not years.

For our critical Rocket Motor Systems (RMS) division, we are seeking a visionary **Head of Production** to architect, build, and scale our manufacturing operations. Based at our pivotal McHenry, Mississippi facility, you will be the ultimate owner of all RMS production activities – from strategic planning and supply chain management to manufacturing engineering, day-to-day operations, and quality assurance. This pivotal role is responsible for leading the transition from New Product Introduction (NPI) to sustained high-rate manufacturing, ensuring unparalleled operational excellence, scalability, and strict adherence to rigorous quality and safety standards. If you are a seasoned leader ready to define the future of propulsion manufacturing and shape one of Anduril's most mission-critical business lines in a fast-paced, mission-driven environment, this unique opportunity is for you.

### **WHAT YOU'LL DO:**

* Lead End-to-End Production & Scale RMS:

  + Own all production outcomes for Rocket Motor Systems, guiding the full lifecycle from New Product Introduction (NPI) to high-volume manufacturing.
  + Architect, build, and optimize critical functions including Planning, Supply Chain, Manufacturing Engineering, Operations, and Quality to support rapid growth and complex RMS operations.
  + Drive high-rate production, implementing the systems, processes, and infrastructure necessary to achieve ambitious delivery targets for current and future contracts.
  + Lead, mentor, and expand a high-performing team across multiple sites, fostering a culture of ownership and excellence.
* Serve as Site Leader for McHenry, MS:

  + Be the primary leader for our McHenry facility, overseeing all site operations, resources, and strategic development.
  + Ensure strict compliance with all safety, regulatory, and environmental standards, especially those related to energetics and explosive materials.
  + Build and maintain strong relationships with local stakeholders, community partners, and county officials.
* Strategize & Execute Facilities Growth:

  + Lead the expansion of existing production facilities and the development of new sites, from initial planning and permitting to construction and operational readiness.
  + Collaborate cross-functionally to develop and execute long-term growth strategies for RMS, ensuring infrastructure keeps pace with demand.
* Champion Operational Excellence & Innovation:

  + Develop and refine manufacturing processes and operational playbooks to ensure efficient, scalable, and repeatable production.
  + Drive the adoption of advanced manufacturing technologies (PLM, ERP, MES, QMS) for optimized efficiency and data-driven decision-making.
  + Establish and manage to critical KPIs for quality, delivery, financial performance, and safety, continuously driving improvement.
* Be a Hands-On, Visible Leader:

  + Maintain a strong physical presence on the production floor at McHenry, engaging directly with teams to ensure operational excellence and foster a culture of safety and collaboration.
  + Manage significant production budgets and drive financial discipline across all activities to support long-term business growth.

### **REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS:**

* 10+ years of progressive leadership experience in a high-volume manufacturing/production environment within a highly technical, hardware-focused industry (e.g., aerospace, defense, automotive, energy).
* Proven track record of architecting, building, and scaling production operations from NPI to sustained high-rate manufacturing (e.g., 1000s of units/year). Experience establishing new facilities or significantly expanding existing ones is highly valued.
* Direct, hands-on leadership and success across end-to-end supply chain, procurement, and warehousing functions critical for scaled manufacturing.
* Experience managing and inspiring large, diverse teams across various functions (e.g., quality, supply chain, manufacturing engineering, operations) and successfully leading a significant production site.
* A leader who thrives on the production floor, with the ability to be on-site at the McHenry, MS facility five days a week to provide direct, hands-on leadership and support.
* Ability to develop a long-term strategic vision for production while also diving into tactical execution to overcome immediate challenges.
* Exceptional collaborator, proven to partner effectively with engineering, program management, and other technical teams to optimize manufacturing processes for complex hardware systems.
* Skilled in building and maintaining positive relationships with local stakeholders, and deep understanding of safety, environmental, and regulatory requirements (especially for energetics/propulsion).
* Strong analytical skills with a data-driven approach to continuous improvement and operational excellence.
* Energetics Experience: Familiarity with the manufacturing of energetics or propulsion systems is a significant advantage.
* Technical Background: Bachelor of Science or higher degree in a technical discipline (Engineering, Manufacturing, Supply Chain) preferred; equivalent practical experience with a proven track record will be considered.
* Security Clearance: Eligible to obtain and maintain an active U.S. Secret security clearance.

### **Protecting Yourself from Recruitment Scams**

Anduril is committed to maintaining the integrity of our Talent acquisition process and the security of our candidates. We've observed a rise in sophisticated phishing and fraudulent schemes where individuals impersonate Anduril representatives, luring job seekers with false interviews or job offers. These scammers often attempt to extract payment or sensitive personal information.

To ensure your safety and help you navigate your job search with confidence, please keep the following critical points in mind:

* **No Financial Requests:**Anduril will never solicit payment or demand personal financial details (such as banking information, credit card numbers, or social security numbers) at any stage of our hiring process. Our legitimate recruitment is entirely free for candidates.
* **Please always verify communications:**
  + Direct from Anduril: If you receive an email from one of our recruiters, it will *only* come from an `@anduril.com` address.
  + Via Agency Partner: If contacted by a recruiting agency for an Anduril role, their email will clearly identify their agency. If you suspect any suspicious activity, please verify the agency's authenticity by reaching out to [contact@anduril.com](mailto:contact@anduril.com).
* **Exercise Caution with Unsolicited Outreach:** If you receive any communication that appears suspicious, contains grammatical errors, or makes unusual requests, do not engage. Always confirm the sender's email domain is @anduril.com before providing any personal information or clicking on links.
* **What to Do If You Suspect Fraud:** Should you encounter any questionable or fraudulent outreach claiming to be from Anduril, please report it immediately to [contact@anduril.com](mailto:contact@anduril.com). Your proactive caution is invaluable in protecting your personal information and upholding the security and trustworthiness of our recruitment efforts.
