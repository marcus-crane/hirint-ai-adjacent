### ABOUT THE JOB

As our Sr. Program Manager, People Operations, you will lead program management for a critical HCM implementation while establishing enterprise-grade internal controls and ITGC frameworks for our People systems. This is a **strategic program management and compliance role** requiring deep expertise in HCM implementations (Workday/ADP), internal controls design, and audit readiness. You will serve as the bridge between technical and business stakeholders, translating compliance requirements into actionable system requirements and ensuring seamless collaboration between IT, Finance, Legal, Security, and People teams during an HCM transformation. Your work will establish the control environment that protects our most critical people data and ensures regulatory compliance as Anduril scales.

#### WHAT YOU’LL DO

**HCM Implementation & Systems Leadership**

* Lead program management for end-to-end HCM implementation for payroll and compensation modules (Workday, ADP, or similar platforms), serving as the compliance owner and primary liaison between technical teams and business stakeholders throughout the implementation lifecycle
* Partner with technical leads on system configurations, security settings, integrations, and data migration to ensure compliance requirements are translated into technical specifications and audit readiness is achieved from day one
* Serve as the conduit between technical IT teams and non-technical business users, translating complex technical concepts for business stakeholders and articulating business/compliance requirements to technical teams
* Define business requirements, compliance specifications, and control objectives that guide technical implementation decisions
* Drive change management initiatives related to system upgrades, process changes, and new control implementations

**Internal Controls & ITGC**

* Design, implement, and maintain internal controls and ITGC frameworks for HR and payroll systems, including control documentation, testing protocols, and audit remediation strategies
* Own and maintain internal compliance controls related to People processes, including documentation, testing, remediation, and continuous monitoring of key controls
* Partner with cross-functional stakeholders across Finance, IT, and compliance functions to prepare for control testing and evidence gathering
* Act as subject matter expert and liaison to Finance, Legal, Security, and external auditors on compliance matters

**Security & Access Controls**

* Design and implement security controls for sensitive people data, including access management, segregation of duties, data privacy protocols, and system security configurations
* Develop and maintain comprehensive process documentation, standard operating procedures, and control matrices for all compliance activities

**Payroll & Compensation Operations**

* Oversee end-to-end payroll processing and compensation administration as part of the HCM implementation, including base salary changes, equity grants, bonus calculations, and commission processing, ensuring data integrity, audit readiness, and compliance across multi-state and multi-entity payroll operations

#### REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS

* 7+ years of proven experience with internal controls, ITGC, and compliance frameworks in HR/payroll systems environments, including control design, testing, documentation, and audit support
* Demonstrated track record in program management roles during enterprise HCM implementations (Workday, ADP, or similar platforms), specifically in payroll and compensation modules, with proven ability to bridge technical and non-technical stakeholders
* Experience serving as the liaison between IT/technical teams and business stakeholders, translating compliance and business requirements into technical specifications
* Experience serving as primary point of contact during audit cycles, including control evidence gathering, testing coordination, and remediation management
* Strong hands-on experience with internal compliance, including ITGCs, control design, testing, documentation, and audit support
* Demonstrated experience implementing and maintaining security controls in HR/payroll systems, including access management, segregation of duties, and data privacy protocols
* Strong program management capabilities with ability to coordinate across technical and non-technical teams and manage multiple complex initiatives simultaneously
* Deep understanding of IT security frameworks (SOC 2, ISO 27001) as applied to HRIS/payroll systems, with ability to communicate technical concepts to business audiences
* Exceptional communication and interpersonal skills, with proven ability to translate technical compliance requirements for diverse audiences and facilitate collaboration between technical and business teams
* Deep knowledge of multi-state payroll processing, tax compliance, wage and hour laws, and compensation regulations
* Exceptional analytical skills with keen attention to detail and ability to identify control weaknesses and remediation opportunities
* Bachelor's degree in Human Resources, Finance, Accounting, Information Systems, Business Administration, or a related field

### **Protecting Yourself from Recruitment Scams**

Anduril is committed to maintaining the integrity of our Talent acquisition process and the security of our candidates. We've observed a rise in sophisticated phishing and fraudulent schemes where individuals impersonate Anduril representatives, luring job seekers with false interviews or job offers. These scammers often attempt to extract payment or sensitive personal information.

To ensure your safety and help you navigate your job search with confidence, please keep the following critical points in mind:

* **No Financial Requests:**Anduril will never solicit payment or demand personal financial details (such as banking information, credit card numbers, or social security numbers) at any stage of our hiring process. Our legitimate recruitment is entirely free for candidates.
* **Please always verify communications:**
  + Direct from Anduril: If you receive an email from one of our recruiters, it will *only* come from an `@anduril.com` address.
  + Via Agency Partner: If contacted by a recruiting agency for an Anduril role, their email will clearly identify their agency. If you suspect any suspicious activity, please verify the agency's authenticity by reaching out to [contact@anduril.com](mailto:contact@anduril.com).
* **Exercise Caution with Unsolicited Outreach:** If you receive any communication that appears suspicious, contains grammatical errors, or makes unusual requests, do not engage. Always confirm the sender's email domain is @anduril.com before providing any personal information or clicking on links.
* **What to Do If You Suspect Fraud:** Should you encounter any questionable or fraudulent outreach claiming to be from Anduril, please report it immediately to [contact@anduril.com](mailto:contact@anduril.com). Your proactive caution is invaluable in protecting your personal information and upholding the security and trustworthiness of our recruitment efforts.
