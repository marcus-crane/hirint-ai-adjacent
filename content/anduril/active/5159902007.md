Anduril Industries is a defense technology company with a mission to transform U.S. and allied military capabilities with advanced technology. By bringing the expertise, technology, and business model of the 21st century’s most innovative companies to the defense industry, Anduril is changing how military systems are designed, built and sold. Anduril’s family of systems is powered by Lattice OS, an AI-powered operating system that turns thousands of data streams into a realtime, 3D command and control center. As the world enters an era of strategic competition, Anduril is committed to bringing cutting-edge autonomy, AI, computer vision, sensor fusion, and networking technology to the military in months, not years.

### ABOUT THE JOB

* Anduril's Strategic Security Services team is looking for an emerging leader to join its inaugural Emerging Talent Rotational Program in Costa Mesa, CA. In this role, you'll develop deep expertise across the four pillars of Strategic Security Services, gaining hands-on experience in the disciplines that protect our people, enable our mission, and keep Anduril operating at the edge of what's possible. We're looking for someone who brings sharp instincts, a genuine hunger to learn, and the ability to grow fast in a high-tempo environment. If you're a recent grad who thrives on ambiguity, earns trust quickly, and wants to make a real impact from day one, this program is built for you.

### WHAT YOU’LL DO

* Rotate through four teams within Strategic Security Services over 12 months (3 months each):

+ **Travel Security and Risk Advisory:** Assess travel risks, develop mitigation strategies, and directly support employees operating in complex or high-risk environments. You'll learn to translate threat data into clear, actionable guidance for travelers and stakeholders.
+ **Global Security Operations Center (GSOC):** Contribute to 24/7 monitoring operations and incident response protocols. You'll develop real-time situational awareness skills and learn how to triage, escalate, and communicate during critical incidents.
+ **Threat Intelligence:** Support the collection, analysis, and dissemination of threat intelligence to internal stakeholders. You'll develop your analytical tradecraft and learn how to connect disparate signals into coherent assessments that drive decision-making.
+ **Protective Intelligence:** Support proactive efforts to identify, assess, and mitigate threats to Anduril personnel and assets. You'll learn how to apply structured analytical methodologies to threat cases and contribute to protective operations.

* Beyond your rotations, you'll:

+ Translate complex security data, risk assessments, and incident reporting into crisp executive briefings, dashboards, and written summaries, developing a strong "so what" voice for both security and non-security audiences.
+ Collaborate cross-functionally with Growth, Legal, People Operations, IT, and other teams, building an understanding of how Strategic Security Services operates as a force multiplier across the business.
+ Access structured mentorship from Strategic Security Services leaders, including regular 1:1s and dedicated skill-building focused on intelligence analysis, risk frameworks, and operational security fundamentals.
+ Graduate from the 12-month program ready to step into a full-time role on one of the four teams, with the context, relationships, and credibility to contribute from day one and eventually mentor the next cohort.

### REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS

* Recent graduate with a Bachelor's degree in Security Management, Criminal Justice, Homeland Security, International Relations, Political Science, Business Administration, or a related field, or demonstrated strong coursework and interest in corporate security or intelligence.
* You learn fast and you prove it, able to absorb new domains quickly, connect dots across disciplines, and apply lessons without needing the same guidance twice.
* Strong analytical and critical thinking skills, with a demonstrated ability to synthesize complex or ambiguous information into clear, actionable outputs.
* Excellent written and verbal communication skills; you can write a tight executive summary and hold your own in a room with senior stakeholders.
* High proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite and project management tools, including data organization and professional presentation development.
* Proven discretion and judgment when handling sensitive, confidential, or operationally critical information.
* Must be authorized to work in the United States.

### PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS

* Prior internship, research, or work experience in corporate security, intelligence analysis, law enforcement, military, risk consulting, or a related field.
* Demonstrated leadership, initiative, or ownership in academic projects, extracurricular activities, or work experience; we care more about *how* you showed up than *where*.
* Familiarity with threat intelligence frameworks, risk assessment methodologies, or security operations tools (e.g., GSOC platforms, open-source intelligence, incident management systems).
* Comfort operating in fast-moving, high-stakes environments where priorities shift and judgment matters.

### **Protecting Yourself from Recruitment Scams**

Anduril is committed to maintaining the integrity of our Talent acquisition process and the security of our candidates. We've observed a rise in sophisticated phishing and fraudulent schemes where individuals impersonate Anduril representatives, luring job seekers with false interviews or job offers. These scammers often attempt to extract payment or sensitive personal information.

To ensure your safety and help you navigate your job search with confidence, please keep the following critical points in mind:

* **No Financial Requests:**Anduril will never solicit payment or demand personal financial details (such as banking information, credit card numbers, or social security numbers) at any stage of our hiring process. Our legitimate recruitment is entirely free for candidates.
* **Please always verify communications:**
  + Direct from Anduril: If you receive an email from one of our recruiters, it will *only* come from an `@anduril.com` address.
  + Via Agency Partner: If contacted by a recruiting agency for an Anduril role, their email will clearly identify their agency. If you suspect any suspicious activity, please verify the agency's authenticity by reaching out to [contact@anduril.com](mailto:contact@anduril.com).
* **Exercise Caution with Unsolicited Outreach:** If you receive any communication that appears suspicious, contains grammatical errors, or makes unusual requests, do not engage. Always confirm the sender's email domain is @anduril.com before providing any personal information or clicking on links.
* **What to Do If You Suspect Fraud:** Should you encounter any questionable or fraudulent outreach claiming to be from Anduril, please report it immediately to [contact@anduril.com](mailto:contact@anduril.com). Your proactive caution is invaluable in protecting your personal information and upholding the security and trustworthiness of our recruitment efforts.
