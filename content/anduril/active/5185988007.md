### **ABOUT THE JOB**

This role is at the Senior Financial Analyst level. Supporting Air Defense business line, you will own product and program financial management, income statement forecasting, and financial reporting across multiple products and programs. Partnering closely with program management, proposals and capture, production, and business line leadership, you will explain financial performance and variance analysis at the contract, product, and per-unit level to cross-functional teams and senior leadership.

#### **WHAT YOU’LL DO**

* Own the financial management of key programs within the Air Defense business area, from bookings through delivery and closeout
* Partner closely with the proposals and capture team to win new opportunities—shaping cost structure, pricing strategy, and margin targets that make bids competitive and profitable
* Drive the financial execution and profitability of new and existing programs, owning the P&L outcome across the program lifecycle
* Apply a working knowledge of government contracting (contract types, cost accounting, and compliance) while thinking strategically and outside the box to support the needs of a non-traditional defense company
* Own rolling monthly forecasts, working closely with key engineering stakeholders (Product, Program, and Engineering Leads)
* Work with program management to develop and maintain ETC forecasts and EAC integrity
* Analyze and monitor contract and product profit margins
* Deliver monthly internal management reporting through presentations and written communications
* Prepare monthly, quarterly, and annual variance analysis at multiple accounting levels
* Build reports and automated dashboards in FP&A and ERP tools (Oracle, Foundry)
* Partner with manufacturing, supply chain, and program teams across the full work order lifecycle—from work definitions and bills of material through build associations, WIP, and finished goods—to ensure production activity is captured and costed accurately
* Ad hoc support and special projects
* Drive continuous improvement in financial reporting tools, systems, and procedures

#### **REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS**

* 5+ years (Senior Financial Analyst) of experience as a financial analyst or in a related finance/accounting role
* Bachelor’s degree or higher in Finance, Accounting, Economics, or related field
* DoD and/or tech industry experience (preferred)
* Familiarity with ERP and EPM systems (e.g., Oracle, Oracle EPM) and the data flowing between the production floor and the general ledger
* Ability to operate independently in a fast-paced environment
* Strong understanding of financial systems, data management, and complex modeling
* Proven ability to work effectively in a team environment
* Strong analytical, problem-solving, communication, and presentation skills
* Must be a U.S. citizen and eligible to obtain and maintain a U.S. security clearance

### **Protecting Yourself from Recruitment Scams**

Anduril is committed to maintaining the integrity of our Talent acquisition process and the security of our candidates. We've observed a rise in sophisticated phishing and fraudulent schemes where individuals impersonate Anduril representatives, luring job seekers with false interviews or job offers. These scammers often attempt to extract payment or sensitive personal information.

To ensure your safety and help you navigate your job search with confidence, please keep the following critical points in mind:

* **No Financial Requests:**Anduril will never solicit payment or demand personal financial details (such as banking information, credit card numbers, or social security numbers) at any stage of our hiring process. Our legitimate recruitment is entirely free for candidates.
* **Please always verify communications:**
  + Direct from Anduril: If you receive an email from one of our recruiters, it will *only* come from an `@anduril.com` address.
  + Via Agency Partner: If contacted by a recruiting agency for an Anduril role, their email will clearly identify their agency. If you suspect any suspicious activity, please verify the agency's authenticity by reaching out to [contact@anduril.com](mailto:contact@anduril.com).
* **Exercise Caution with Unsolicited Outreach:** If you receive any communication that appears suspicious, contains grammatical errors, or makes unusual requests, do not engage. Always confirm the sender's email domain is @anduril.com before providing any personal information or clicking on links.
* **What to Do If You Suspect Fraud:** Should you encounter any questionable or fraudulent outreach claiming to be from Anduril, please report it immediately to [contact@anduril.com](mailto:contact@anduril.com). Your proactive caution is invaluable in protecting your personal information and upholding the security and trustworthiness of our recruitment efforts.
