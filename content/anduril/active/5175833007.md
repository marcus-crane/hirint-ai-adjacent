Anduril Industries is a defense technology company with a mission to transform U.S. and allied military capabilities with advanced technology. By bringing the expertise, technology, and business model of the 21st century’s most innovative companies to the defense industry, Anduril is changing how military systems are designed, built and sold. Anduril’s family of systems is powered by Lattice OS, an AI-powered operating system that turns thousands of data streams into a realtime, 3D command and control center. As the world enters an era of strategic competition, Anduril is committed to bringing cutting-edge autonomy, AI, computer vision, sensor fusion, and networking technology to the military in months, not years.

### ABOUT THE TEAM

The Corporate Operations team serves as the strategic orchestrator for how our company plans, forecasts, and makes critical operational decisions across teams, particularly through the lens of mergers and acquisitions, turning strategy into day-to-day execution.  
  
We sit at the center of the business, taking on high-impact, often ambiguous problems inherent in integrating new entities. These challenges directly affect growth, efficiency, and how the business scales post-acquisition. This means performing deep-dive analysis, leading process re-design for seamless integration, and building pragmatic solutions that ensure newly acquired businesses run better.  
  
We work closely with senior leaders across corporate functions and acquiring business units, move quickly, and maintain an unwavering focus on outcomes. This is a team for people who like solving hard integration problems, building and iterating on M&A playbooks, and leaving things better than they found them.

### ABOUT THE JOB

As the **Senior Manager, M&A People Integration**, you will be the strategic owner and lead orchestrator of all people-related integration workstreams across our M&A portfolio. You will play a pivotal role in ensuring that every acquisition successfully integrates talent, culture, and HR operations, directly supporting our mission continuity and value realization goals. This is a high-impact leadership role demanding a blend of strategic vision, deep HR expertise, and hands-on execution in a fast-paced environment.  
  
This role requires strong judgment, executive presence, and the ability to operate at both a strategic and detailed level within the M&A context. You will regularly present to senior leadership, influence decision-making for integration strategies, and ensure that ideas turn into actionable, value-generating outcomes. This role is well suited for someone who has excelled at analytical problem-solving and is ready to lead broader integration efforts with real accountability.

#### WHAT YOU’LL DO

* **Own and drive the People Integration Playbook:** Develop, refine, and champion the People Integration Playbook, standardizing processes for compensation harmonization, leveling, organizational design, benefits integration, and HRIS/payroll transition across all acquisitions.
* **Lead G&A People Integration:** Direct the execution of our Day 1 G&A functional reporting policy, ensuring seamless integration of G&A personnel into core corporate functions from close. This includes pre-close talent assessments and proactive issue resolution.
* **Mitigate Talent Risk & Drive Retention:** Design and implement robust strategies for identifying key talent, executing retention packages, and fostering cultural alignment to minimize attrition and maximize the value of acquired talent.
* **Orchestrate HR Operational Readiness:** Coordinate critical HR deliverables for Day 1 and beyond, including offer letter execution, background checks, benefits enrollment, manager talking points, and initial HRIS/payroll setup.
* **Manage Cross-functional HR Dependencies:** Act as the primary People Integration liaison, collaborating extensively with Legal (for employment agreements, compliance), People Ops (for HRIS integration), Finance (for compensation budgeting), and Business Line leadership.
* **Provide Expert Guidance:** Serve as the subject matter expert for all HR-related integration challenges, including international considerations (e.g., Works Councils), clearance transfers, and policy harmonization (e.g., Code of Conduct).

#### REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS

* **7-10+ years** of progressive HR experience, with 1+ years specifically in M&A integration roles focusing on People/HR workstreams.
* Proven track record of successfully leading complex HR integration projects from diligence through post-close stabilization, including compensation, benefits, and HRIS integration.
* Deep expertise in HR regulations and best practices, particularly within an M&A context and ideally with exposure to government contracting or classified environments.
* Strong analytical skills with the ability to translate complex HR data into actionable integration strategies and clear executive communication.
* Exceptional leadership, communication, and change management skills, capable of influencing senior stakeholders and driving cross-functional alignment.
* Proficiency in HRIS platforms and project management methodologies; experience with Confluence, Airtable, and SharePoint for playbook development and tracking is a plus.
* Must be a U.S. Person due to required access to U.S. export controlled information or facilities

#### PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS

* Experience with international HR integration experience, including Works Councils, and GDPR considerations.
* Demonstrated success in managing complex HRIS migrations and data integrations within an M&A context
* Prior experience in working directly in Corporate Development and Employment Legal on employment-related issues

### **Protecting Yourself from Recruitment Scams**

Anduril is committed to maintaining the integrity of our Talent acquisition process and the security of our candidates. We've observed a rise in sophisticated phishing and fraudulent schemes where individuals impersonate Anduril representatives, luring job seekers with false interviews or job offers. These scammers often attempt to extract payment or sensitive personal information.

To ensure your safety and help you navigate your job search with confidence, please keep the following critical points in mind:

* **No Financial Requests:**Anduril will never solicit payment or demand personal financial details (such as banking information, credit card numbers, or social security numbers) at any stage of our hiring process. Our legitimate recruitment is entirely free for candidates.
* **Please always verify communications:**
  + Direct from Anduril: If you receive an email from one of our recruiters, it will *only* come from an `@anduril.com` address.
  + Via Agency Partner: If contacted by a recruiting agency for an Anduril role, their email will clearly identify their agency. If you suspect any suspicious activity, please verify the agency's authenticity by reaching out to [contact@anduril.com](mailto:contact@anduril.com).
* **Exercise Caution with Unsolicited Outreach:** If you receive any communication that appears suspicious, contains grammatical errors, or makes unusual requests, do not engage. Always confirm the sender's email domain is @anduril.com before providing any personal information or clicking on links.
* **What to Do If You Suspect Fraud:** Should you encounter any questionable or fraudulent outreach claiming to be from Anduril, please report it immediately to [contact@anduril.com](mailto:contact@anduril.com). Your proactive caution is invaluable in protecting your personal information and upholding the security and trustworthiness of our recruitment efforts.
