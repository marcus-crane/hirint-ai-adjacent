Our agentic AI is purpose-built to compete with China—from cross-Strait conflict to gray zone coercion. Our AI systems are some of the most trusted in the industry and actively used on the front lines of the Indo-Pacific to keep the peace and save lives.

### About the role

We’re looking for a senior engineer to lead the development of new Cyber products in support of national security missions. This is a 0→1 builder role - taking a product from concept to operational deployment while working directly with end users and shaping both the product and the problem space.

You’ll own our flagship Cyber product, *Cyber Recon -* an external attack surface management platform built for cyber operators. This is not a traditional forward-deployed role, but it requires the same level of mission ownership, user empathy, and execution velocity - applied to building a durable product.

### What you’ll do

* Own end-to-end development of Cyber Recon and adjacent products (backend systems, data pipelines, and user-facing applications)
* Build intuitive, high-performance interfaces for exploring and acting on complex cyber data
* Work directly with users in mission environments to understand needs, iterate quickly, and deploy into real environments
* Design systems to ingest, normalize, and analyze large-scale external attack surface data
* Translate ambiguous problems into concrete product and engineering decisions
* Drive product and technical direction for Cyber
* Deploy and support software in secure, constrained environments
* Travel 2-3 times per month to customer sites (primarily East Coast)

### What we value

* 10+ years building and shipping production software systems
* Strong full-stack skills (backend systems, APIs, data modeling, modern frontend)
* Comfortable moving between frontend, backend, and product decisions on a daily basis
* Experience building user-facing products with strong usability and performance
* Experience operating in ambiguous, fast-moving environments with high ownership
* Experience building cybersecurity products or conducting cyber operations in government environments
* Familiarity with external attack surface management, internet-scale data collection, or adversary infrastructure analysis
* Prior experience working with classified systems or secure deployments
* Currently holds or previously held a CI Polygraph (preferred); ability and willingness to obtain a CI Polygraph required
