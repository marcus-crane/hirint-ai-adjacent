### About the role

As the Strategic Finance Lead, you’ll help shape the company’s growth trajectory, resource allocation, and long-term financial strategy. This role sits at the intersection of strategy, operations, and finance - partnering closely with executive leadership to turn company priorities into clear financial plans and tradeoffs.  
  
You’ll own high-leverage analyses that guide where we invest, how we scale, and how we measure success. This is a high-ownership role for someone who enjoys operating in ambiguity, building frameworks from first principles, and influencing senior decision-making in a fast-moving environment.

### What you’ll do

* Strategic Planning & Capital Allocation

+ Own financial planning and modeling that informs company strategy, headcount growth, and investment priorities.
+ Evaluate tradeoffs across growth initiatives, resourcing decisions, and new bets to maximize long-term value.
+ Build and maintain multi-year financial models that connect strategy to outcomes.
+ Support board- and investor-level planning, including scenario analysis and downside / upside planning.

* Revenue, Pricing & Growth Strategy

+ Partner with sales, capture, and product leadership to shape pricing, packaging, and contract structures aligned with strategic goals.
+ Develop financial frameworks to assess new products, customer segments, and go-to-market motions.
+ Translate operating metrics into clear insights that guide growth decisions.
+ Design scalable reporting that ties revenue performance to strategic intent.

* Executive Partnership & Decision Support

+ Serve as a strategic thought partner to executive leadership, providing clear, opinionated financial perspectives.
+ Build analyses and narratives that support executive decisions, board discussions, and internal alignment.
+ Help define and track the metrics that matter most for long-term enterprise value.

* Financial Infrastructure in support of strategy

+ Partner with the finance and accounting team to ensure systems, controls, and reporting scale with the business.
+ Drive automation and process improvements that reduce friction and enable faster decision-making.
+ Ensure financial data is accurate, timely, and useful—not just compliant.

### What we’re looking for

* 7+ years of experience in strategic finance, FP&A, corporate strategy, or similar roles in high-growth environments.
* Strong financial modeling and planning skills; comfortable building models from scratch.
* Deep understanding of SaaS and/or government contracting economics.
* Track record of influencing senior stakeholders with data-driven insights.
* Ability to move fluidly between strategic thinking and hands-on execution.
* Low-ego, high-ownership mindset; thrives in ambiguity and rapid iteration.
