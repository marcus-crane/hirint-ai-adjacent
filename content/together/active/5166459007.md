### **About the Role**

As a Workplace Coordinator in our San Francisco office, you will partner with Workplace Operations to manage day-to-day office operations and create an environment where employees can thrive. The ideal candidate is organized, personable, and brings strong problem-solving skills to everything they do. This position is based  in our San Francisco office five days per week and is a great opportunity to make a significant impact in a fast paced environment.

### **Responsibilities**

*Daily Operations & Employee Support*

* Assist with welcoming and onboarding new hires
* Provide on-site support and serve as a first point of contact for employee questions
* Assist the Workplace team with day-to-day activities as directed
* Deliver exceptional customer service through Gmail, Slack, and our Support Portal — for both in-office employees and remote employees
* Gather and relay employee feedback on the workplace to help drive continuous improvement
* Manage the  swag program
* Cover the reception desk as needed

*Facilities & Office Maintenance*

* Handle incoming mail and package processing
* Initiate maintenance and repair calls to keep the office running efficiently
* Liaise with janitorial team to maintain office organization and cleanliness
* Serve as a liaison to building management regarding facilities issues
* Coordinate badge and key fob distribution and access
* Oversee guest access procedures
* Serve as an emergency warden
* Maintain first aid supplies and ensure health and safety standards are met

*Food, Beverage & Events*

* Assist with the Food & Beverage program
* Help maintain kitchen cleanliness and ensure the snack and beverage program runs smoothly with our third-party vendor
* Assist with planning and executing internal events, both in-office and offsite, including team meetings, offsites, and employee celebrations
* Partner on logistics, vendor coordination, and on-site support for all internal events

### **Requirements**

* Strong work ethic with a commitment to reliability, dependability, and confidentiality
* Exceptional attention to detail and time management skills
* Empathetic, approachable demeanor with a genuine passion for helping others
* Ability to identify root causes rather than settle for quick fixes
* Familiarity with tools and resources for resolving office-related issues, including desk booking systems, AV equipment, and facilities management platforms
* Excellent communication skills across teams and platforms
* Flexibility and adaptability in a dynamic, changing environment
* This position is in-office five days per week
* Previous experience in office management, facilities, or hospitality preferred
