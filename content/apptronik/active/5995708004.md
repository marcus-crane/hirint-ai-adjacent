**JOB SUMMARY**

We are seeking a skilled **CAM Programmer** to develop, optimize, and maintain CNC programs for precision machining. The ideal candidate will translate engineering drawings and CAD models into efficient machining instructions, ensuring high-quality production while minimizing cycle time and material waste.

**ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES or KEY ACCOUNTABILITIES**

* Create and modify CNC programs using CAM software for milling, turning, and multi-axis machines
* Interpret technical drawings, blueprints, and CAD models to determine machining requirements
* Select appropriate cutting tools, speeds, feeds, and machining strategies
* Simulate and verify toolpaths to prevent collisions and ensure accuracy
* Collaborate with engineers, machinists, and production teams to improve manufacturability
* Optimize programs for efficiency, quality, and cost-effectiveness
* Troubleshoot machining issues and revise programs as needed
* Maintain documentation for programs, tooling setups, and processes
* Ensure compliance with safety and quality standards

**SKILLS AND REQUIREMENTS**

* Proven experience as a CAM Programmer or CNC Programmer
* Proficiency in CAM software (e.g., Fusion 360, Mastercam, PowerMill, or similar)
* Strong understanding of CNC machining processes (milling, turning, multi-axis)
* Ability to read and interpret engineering drawings and GD&T
* Knowledge of cutting tools, materials, and machining techniques

**EDUCATION and/or EXPERIENCE**

* Diploma or degree in Mechanical/Manufacturing Engineering or related field
* 5+ Experience with 4-axis or 5-axis machining
* Familiarity with CAD software (e.g., SolidWorks, AutoCAD)
* Experience in high-precision or high-volume manufacturing environments
* Strong problem-solving and analytical skills

**PHYSICAL REQUIREMENTS**

* Shop floor and office environment
* Prolonged periods of sitting at a desk and working on a computer
* Must be able to lift 15 pounds at times
* Vision to read printed materials and a computer screen
* Hearing and speech to communicate
