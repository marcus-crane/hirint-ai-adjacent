# **About Suno**

We're building the world's first creative entertainment platform, where the entire world can feel the joy and fulfilment of making music. Music is for everyone: Our users include everyone from grandmothers creating songs for their loved ones, to Grammy winners using Suno Studio, our power tool, to make the most popular hits in the world.

Building the future of entertainment requires ambition. The pace is fast, the problems are hard, and the work demands ownership and intensity. For the right people, it’s incredibly rewarding: a chance to shape a new medium, work with a small team that cares deeply about quality, make music, drink too much coffee, and build something that millions of people use to express themselves in ways that were never before possible.

Suno is the fastest growing consumer entertainment company and the leader in AI music. We are backed by leading investors including Bond Capital, Menlo Ventures, Lightspeed Venture Partners, IVP, Forerunner, Union Square Ventures, Alkeon, Quiet, Matrix Partners, Schroders Capital and, NVentures (venture arm of NVIDIA).

## **About the Role**

We're looking for a **Senior Manager, Business Operations & Strategy** to be the operational backbone of our engineering, product, and design organization. You'll be the person who turns product ambiguity into executable plans — building the rhythms, frameworks, and cross-functional alignment that allow our product teams to move fast and make great decisions.

You'll sit at the intersection of product, data, and strategy, working on everything from product planning and roadmap operations to go-to-market coordination and performance insights. You'll often be running multiple workstreams simultaneously. You will report to the Head of BizOps & Strategy and partner closely with Product, Engineering, Data, and Finance leadership.

## **What You'll Own**

**Product Rhythm & Operations**

* Own the operational cadences of the product organization — sprint reviews, roadmap planning, and cross-functional syncs — ensuring the right decisions get made with the right context
* Build and maintain the systems and processes that keep product teams aligned and unblocked
* Facilitate strategic working sessions, product offsites, and critical prioritization moments across the organization

**Product Insights & Performance**

* Oversee the development of models, forecasts, and scenarios to support product goal-setting and resource allocation
* Partner with the Data team to develop a deep understanding of retention, feature adoption, growth drivers, and product performance
* Translate product analytics into clear, compelling insights for leadership and the board

**Product Strategy & Prioritization**

* Own the frameworks that guide product prioritization — audience focus, TAM sizing, marketplace dynamics, and monetization strategy
* Answer hard strategic questions: how should we sequence the roadmap? Where is the growth opportunity? What should we build vs. buy vs. partner?
* Lead pricing and packaging analysis, partnering with Finance and Product to evaluate new monetization approaches

**Partnerships & Integrations**

* Manage the product partnerships pipeline from initial evaluation through execution, assessing strategic fit, commercial terms, and integration complexity
* Build financial models for partnership ROI, scenario planning, and business case evaluation
* Coordinate cross-functional workstreams — product, engineering, legal — to drive partnership execution

**Special Projects**

* Own planning, coordination, and execution for high-priority product initiatives, partnering with cross-functional leaders to ensure delivery against milestones
* Step into undefined, ambiguous problem spaces and bring structure and momentum where none exists

## **What You'll Need**

**You have:**

* 8+ years at a top-tier consulting firm or high-growth tech company in strategy, product ops, or business operations
* 3+ years as an operator embedded within a product or technology organization
* Fluency in product metrics and a strong intuition for what drives user growth, retention, and engagement
* Experience building financial models and doing quantitative analysis (comfortable in Excel/Google Sheets)
* A track record of managing complex, ambiguous projects with multiple stakeholders
* Experience working directly with C-suite and product leadership, with strong instincts for communicating up

**You're energized by:**

* 0→1 work where you define both the problem and the solution
* Being the connective tissue between product, data, and strategy
* Making things happen in fast-moving, high-stakes environments
