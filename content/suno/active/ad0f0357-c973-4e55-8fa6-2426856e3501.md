# **About Suno**

We're building the world's first creative entertainment platform, where the entire world can feel the joy and fulfilment of making music. Music is for everyone: Our users include everyone from grandmothers creating songs for their loved ones, to Grammy winners using Suno Studio, our power tool, to make the most popular hits in the world.

Building the future of entertainment requires ambition. The pace is fast, the problems are hard, and the work demands ownership and intensity. For the right people, it’s incredibly rewarding: a chance to shape a new medium, work with a small team that cares deeply about quality, make music, drink too much coffee, and build something that millions of people use to express themselves in ways that were never before possible.

Suno is the fastest growing consumer entertainment company and the leader in AI music. We are backed by leading investors including Menlo Ventures, Lightspeed Venture Partners, Matrix Partners, NVentures (venture arm of NVIDIA).

# **About the Role**

If you’re the kind of engineering leader who’d rather ship than spectate, this one’s for you. You’ll lead a team building core product and platform capabilities for over 100 million creators — shaping system design, driving execution, and partnering with product, design, and data science to deliver experiences people actually love. This is a hands-on leadership role: you’ll set technical direction, grow your team, and keep things moving in a fast-paced, music-obsessed environment.

[Check out the Suno version of this role here!](https://suno.com/s/H3GzRpa6Ze9A6DKr)

# **What You’ll Do**

* Own the technical direction for your team’s domain, driving architecture decisions and ensuring scalable, high-quality systems that serve millions of creators
* Build, coach, and grow a team of engineers — hiring strong talent, running effective performance cycles, and creating a culture where people do their best work
* Partner with product management, design, and data science to define roadmaps, scope projects, and ship features that move the needle
* Navigate the full development lifecycle from ideation through launch, managing tradeoffs and removing blockers so your team stays focused and shipping
* Champion engineering craft across your team — code quality, robust testing, and the right balance of rapid iteration with long-term maintainability
* Contribute to Suno’s mission of making creative fulfillment a daily reality for everyone by delivering the tools and platform that turn imagination into music

# **What You’ll Need**

**Must-Haves**

* 5–7 years managing a software engineering team, with a track record of shipping impactful consumer products
* Strong technical foundation — you can review code, guide architectural decisions, and jump into the codebase when needed
* Experience translating ambiguous problems into clear plans and shipped features alongside product, design, and data science partners
* A demonstrated ability to hire, develop, and retain strong engineers — you care deeply about growing people’s careers
* Hands-on experience building and operating systems at scale in a production environment
* Comfort thriving in fast-paced, ambiguous environments while keeping your team focused across multiple priorities
* Forward-looking perspective on integrating AI and agentic coding into production engineering workflows
* A love of music (listening, exploring, making — we’re not picky about genre)

**Nice-to-Haves**

* Experience at a consumer tech or creator-platform company
* Time spent at both larger organizations and smaller, high-growth startups
* Familiarity with AI/ML-powered product development
* Working knowledge of Python, TypeScript, consumer media systems, or real-time systems
