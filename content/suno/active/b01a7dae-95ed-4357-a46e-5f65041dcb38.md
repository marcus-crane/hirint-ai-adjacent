# **About the Role**

We’re seeking a highly creative, multidisciplinary **Senior Graphic Designer** to join Suno’s Brand & Creative Team. You’ll work at the intersection of brand, culture, and product—helping translate Suno’s creative vision into expressive, high-impact design across platforms and formats.

This role plays a key part in evolving Suno’s brand across marketing campaigns, artist partnerships, in-product brand moments, and culture-defining activations. You’ll help shape how Suno shows up visually at moments that matter—from product launches and campaigns to artist collaborations and creative brand expressions.

In this role, you’ll partner closely with creative leadership, designers, writers, motion artists, project managers, product designers, and cross-functional teams to concept, design, and execute work that evolves Suno’s brand.

This role is ideal for a designer who delivers innovative, standout work with precision—evolving the brand through bold ideas that are executed confidently and consistently within Suno’s creative direction.  
  
Check out the Suno version of this role here! <https://suno.com/s/p71hcfuWMLRPqgt8>

# **What You’ll Do**

* Design and execute multidisciplinary creative work that evolves Suno’s brand across campaigns, artist partnerships, in-product moments, and brand activations.
* Create innovative, high-impact design while maintaining precision and consistency within Suno’s creative direction and brand guidelines.
* Support artist collaborations and partnerships, developing visuals and assets that authentically reflect both the artist’s voice and Suno’s identity.
* Contribute to product launches and in-product brand moments, partnering with the Design Director and cross-functional teams (product design, GTM, Growth) to ensure a cohesive visual experience.
* Translate ideas into polished, production-ready assets across digital, social, print, and motion formats.
* Iterate quickly and thoughtfully in a fast-paced, collaborative environment, embracing feedback to evolve ideas and elevate the work.
* Design with a global audience and end-to-end user experience in mind.
* Maintain a high bar for craft and attention to detail across every execution.
* Collaborate closely with designers, writers, motion artists, and cross-functional partners to bring great work to life.

# **What You’ll Need**

* **8+ years of experience** as a graphic designer working across multiple disciplines (brand, marketing, digital, social, campaigns, content, etc.).
* **A strong portfolio** showcasing innovative thinking and executional excellence across a range of formats and platforms.
* **A multidisciplinary designer.** You have experience working across brand, marketing, campaigns, content, and digital surfaces, and you adapt your craft fluidly across formats. Motion experience is a strong bonus.
* **Craft-obsessed.** You can zoom out to see the big picture and zoom all the way in to the smallest details—from typography and layout to motion, spacing, and final polish. You know great work is built through care, precision, and attention to detail.
* **Collaborative to your core.** You love working closely with designers, motion artists, writers, product teams, and partners—embracing feedback and shaping ideas together to create work that’s both inspiring and practical.
* **A proactive builder.** You thrive in ambiguity, spot opportunities early, and bring structure to creative chaos—proposing ideas, improving systems, and pushing work forward.
* **Comfortable in a fast-paced environment.** You can juggle multiple projects, iterate quickly and thoughtfully, and maintain a high bar for quality under changing timelines.
* **Brand-forward and user-aware.** You think globally, care about the end-to-end user experience, and design with both consistency and cultural relevance in mind.
* **Proficiency in Adobe Creative Suite and Figma**; motion design skills (After Effects, animated prototypes, etc.) are a strong bonus.
* **Passionate about music and culture.** You’re excited by artists, creative expression, and emerging technology—and motivated by shaping how people experience music.
