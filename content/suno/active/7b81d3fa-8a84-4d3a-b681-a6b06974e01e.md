# About the Role

As a data Scientist on Growth Product, you'll own the analytics for the early-life user journey: everything from the moment someone lands on Suno through their first sessions, first songs, and first reasons to come back. A user's first few experiences are some of the most important inflection points in their lifecycle, and getting them right is one of the highest-leverage problems at Suno. You'll lead data science support on the Growth Activation team, working very closely with product, engineering, and design.

# What You'll Do

* Own the analytics for how new users activate and form habits on Suno, from signup and onboarding through everything that happens in their first week on Suno
* Design experiments that improve the early-life journey, from push notifications and lifecycle messaging to product surfaces like Hooks, Create, and net-new experiences
* Sit on a centralized data science team but serve as the lead DS on Growth Activate, collaborating closely with product, engineering, and design
* Advance our mission of making creative fulfillment a daily reality for everyone by turning first visits into daily creative habits
* Contribute to building strong data foundations as part of our awesome data science team

# What You'll Need

### Must-Haves

* 5-7+ years in data science or product analytics roles, ideally on consumer products
* Advanced proficiency in SQL, Python, and statistical modeling
* Deep experimentation experience grounded in statistical rigor: designing and running experiments end to end, deriving clear insights, and making strategic recommendations
* A broad data science toolkit and the judgment to pick the right tool for the right question, all ultimately in service of making thoughtful product decisions
* A self-starter mentality with an ownership mindset: you thrive in ambiguity, wear multiple hats, and never stop learning
* Strong cross-functional collaboration: you work closely with product, eng, and design, and influence stakeholders and teams through data insights

### Nice-to-Haves

* Experience at a fast-growing consumer app, or a startup that scaled through strong product-led growth
* Background in activation, lifecycle, or growth analytics
* Hands-on AI usage in day-to-day data science work (agents, prompting, building small tools)
* A love of music or creative tools
