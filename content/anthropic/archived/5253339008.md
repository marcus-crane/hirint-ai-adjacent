## **About Anthropic**

Anthropic’s mission is to create reliable, interpretable, and steerable AI systems. We want AI to be safe and beneficial for our users and for society as a whole. Our team is a quickly growing group of committed researchers, engineers, policy experts, and business leaders working together to build beneficial AI systems.

## **About the role**

As a Product Manager for Enterprise at [Claude.ai](http://Claude.ai), you will own the core enterprise platform capabilities that make Claude trustworthy and deployable for large organizations. You'll focus on security, compliance, administration, and governance features that enterprise customers require to adopt AI at scale. This role requires strong technical judgment, deep understanding of enterprise security and compliance frameworks, and the ability to translate complex requirements into elegant product experiences.

You'll work closely with engineering, design, sales, and security teams to build the foundational enterprise capabilities that unlock Claude's adoption across regulated industries and security-conscious organizations.

## **Key responsibilities**

**Security & Compliance Product Development**

* Own the roadmap for enterprise security features including audit logging, data retention policies, and access controls
* Drive compliance certifications and feature development (SOC 2, HIPAA, FedRAMP, GDPR) in partnership with security and legal teams
* Develop and maintain enterprise admin experiences including user management, workspace controls, and policy enforcement
* Build features that give IT and security teams visibility and control over Claude usage within their organizations

**Enterprise Platform Capabilities**

* Define and execute on core enterprise infrastructure including deployment options, data residency, and integration capabilities
* Partner with engineering to build scalable, secure architecture that meets enterprise requirements
* Develop APIs and integration points that enable enterprise workflows and system connectivity

**Cross-functional Partnership**

* Work closely with sales and customer success to understand enterprise customer requirements and translate them into product priorities
* Partner with security team to ensure features meet enterprise security standards
* Collaborate with legal and policy teams on compliance requirements and certifications
* Coordinate with research teams on enterprise-specific model behavior and safety requirements

**Customer & Market Understanding**

* Build deep expertise in enterprise security, compliance, and procurement requirements
* Develop relationships with enterprise customers to understand their needs and validate solutions
* Stay current on competitive landscape and enterprise AI market trends

## Minimum qualifications

* Strong understanding of enterprise security concepts (RBAC, audit logging, encryption, data residency)
* Experience with compliance frameworks (SOC 2, HIPAA, GDPR) and certification processes
* Track record of shipping enterprise features that drive adoption in security-conscious organizations
* Excellent communication skills with ability to work across technical and business stakeholders
* Experience partnering closely with sales teams and engaging directly with enterprise customers

## **Preferred qualifications**

* 6+ years of product management experience, with at least 3 years focused on enterprise software
* Experience in AI/ML products or platforms
* Background in identity & access management, security tools, or compliance software
* Understanding of regulated industries (healthcare, financial services, government)
* Technical background or experience working closely with infrastructure/platform teams
* Experience with enterprise admin console design and IT buyer personas
* Strong technical judgment with ability to go deep on security and infrastructure topics
* Structured thinking and ability to break down complex compliance requirements into shippable features
* Customer empathy with ability to understand enterprise buyer and admin personas
* Execution focus with track record of shipping in fast-paced environments

## **How we're different**

We believe that the highest-impact AI research will be big science. At Anthropic we work as a single cohesive team on just a few large-scale research efforts. And we value impact — advancing our long-term goals of steerable, trustworthy AI — rather than work on smaller and more specific puzzles. We view AI research as an empirical science, which has as much in common with physics and biology as with traditional efforts in computer science. We're an extremely collaborative group, and we host frequent research discussions to ensure that we are pursuing the highest-impact work at any given time. As such, we greatly value communication skills.

The easiest way to understand our research directions is to read our recent research. This research continues many of the directions our team worked on prior to Anthropic, including: GPT-3, Circuit-Based Interpretability, Multimodal Neurons, Scaling Laws, AI & Compute, Concrete Problems in AI Safety, and Learning from Human Preferences.
