## **About Anthropic**

Anthropic’s mission is to create reliable, interpretable, and steerable AI systems. We want AI to be safe and beneficial for our users and for society as a whole. Our team is a quickly growing group of committed researchers, engineers, policy experts, and business leaders working together to build beneficial AI systems.

## **About the role**

We are seeking a Strategic Finance Lead to own the consolidated financial view across Anthropic's compute infrastructure organization. Compute finance spans across accelerators, cloud spend, direct infrastructure, datacenters and data partnerships. This role sits at the center: owning the consolidation, the operating cadence, the cost allocation methodology, and the executive-facing narrative that ties them together.

You will be a key partner to Corporate FP&A, Accounting, and Finance Systems, ensuring our compute financials integrate cleanly into the company plan, our models reconcile across pillars, and our leadership and board materials reflect a single, defensible source of truth.

## **Key responsibilities:**

***Consolidation & Reporting***

* Own consolidation and workback of the financial review cadences across all compute finance pillars into a single narrative for finance and compute leadership
* Manage the compute finance contribution to board materials and executive reviews
* Own and maintain team dashboards, recurring content, and reporting infrastructure — keeping them live, accurate, and reconciled
* Drive integration of financial models across the compute finance team into a coherent, full three-statement view

***Operating Rhythm & Systems***

* Own the calendar and cadence between Compute Finance and Corporate FP&A — close, forecast, budget, and long-range plan handoffs
* Run team operating rhythm: meeting agendas, sprint cadence, recurring deliverable tracking, and cross-pillar coordination
* Own compute finance's planning-system footprint — datasets, sources of truth, and pipeline into corporate systems
* Drive model hygiene, version control, and central organization of team financial models

## **Minimum qualifications**

* Experience in FP&A, strategic finance, finance operations, consulting, or investment banking, preferably in a multi-business-unit or infrastructure-heavy environment
* Built or run a consolidation, allocation, or planning process across multiple finance owners
* Exceptional analytical skills and the ability to reconcile complex models into a single defensible view
* Strong process design and operating-cadence instincts
* Excellent written communication; comfortable producing board- and executive-level materials
* Comfort working cross-functionally and communicating complex financial information to non-finance audiences
* Bias towards action, strong work ethic, and experience driving outcomes under tight timelines
* Passion for Anthropic's mission to build safe, transformative AI systems

## **Preferred qualifications**

* 7+ years of experience in FP&A, strategic finance, finance operations, consulting, or investment banking
* Experience with cost allocation / chargeback methodologies in a cloud or infrastructure context
* Background in AI, ML, cloud service provider economics, or large-scale computing infrastructure
* Proficiency with SQL, Python, and Excel
* Prior chief-of-staff, BizOps, or finance-systems experience in a high-growth environment

## **How we're different**

We believe that the highest-impact AI research will be big science. At Anthropic we work as a single cohesive team on just a few large-scale research efforts. And we value impact — advancing our long-term goals of steerable, trustworthy AI — rather than work on smaller and more specific puzzles. We view AI research as an empirical science, which has as much in common with physics and biology as with traditional efforts in computer science. We're an extremely collaborative group, and we host frequent research discussions to ensure that we are pursuing the highest-impact work at any given time. As such, we greatly value communication skills.

The easiest way to understand our research directions is to read our recent research. This research continues many of the directions our team worked on prior to Anthropic, including: GPT-3, Circuit-Based Interpretability, Multimodal Neurons, Scaling Laws, AI & Compute, Concrete Problems in AI Safety, and Learning from Human Preferences.
