## **About Anthropic**

Anthropic’s mission is to create reliable, interpretable, and steerable AI systems. We want AI to be safe and beneficial for our users and for society as a whole. Our team is a quickly growing group of committed researchers, engineers, policy experts, and business leaders working together to build beneficial AI systems.

### **About the role**

As an early member of our South Korea Revenue Strategy & Operations team, you'll play a hands-on role supporting revenue growth across key market segments by partnering with sales leadership to execute region-specific strategies. You'll be close to the day-to-day of the business — running the operational cadence, building the analysis, and keeping our systems and processes running smoothly as we scale in South Korea.

You'll work closely with S&O counterparts across Japan, India , Singapore, Australia, and the wider APJ region, learning from and contributing to how we operate across the region.

### **Responsibilities:**

**Go-to-Market Operations:**

* Support the Go-to-Market planning process for South Korea, including quarterly and annual cycles, and help prepare inputs for target setting with Finance.
* Assist in territory and account segmentation, keeping assignments accurate and up to date as the business evolves.
* Build and maintain reporting on region-specific sales metrics, pipeline health, and forecast accuracy.
* Pull together market and competitive data to support regional expansion priorities.
* Support partner and alliance execution, including co-sell tracking, partner pipeline hygiene, and joint planning logistics.

**Analysis & Insights:**

* Dig into sales data to surface trends, risks, and opportunities, and translate findings into clear recommendations for sales leadership.
* Build dashboards and recurring reports that give the team visibility into what's working and what isn't.
* Prepare materials for business reviews, QBRs, and leadership updates — pulling the data, building the slides, and helping shape the narrative.

**Operations & Systems:**

* Execute day-to-day operational processes including account transitions, CRM updates, opportunity hygiene, and data management.
* Help maintain and improve our Salesforce setup, reports, and dashboards for the South Korea team.
* Troubleshoot issues for sales reps and leaders, and partner with global RevOps to implement improvements.
* Support adoption of sales tools and enablement resources across the South Korea team.

### Required qualifications:

* 8-10 years of experience in revenue operations, sales strategy, business operations, or a related analytical role in B2B SaaS or enterprise software
* Strong analytical and Excel/Google Sheets skills, with the ability to turn raw data into clear insights
* Hands-on experience with Salesforce (reports, dashboards, data management) and comfort working in BI tools (e.g., Tableau, Looker)
* Strong attention to detail and a bias toward action — you like getting into the weeds and shipping work
* Good cross-functional communication skills, with the ability to partner with sales reps, managers, and adjacent teams
* Based in South Korea with working proficiency in English

**Strong candidates may also have:**

* Exposure to territory design, quota setting, or sales compensation work
* Experience supporting GTM planning cycles or building executive-level reporting
* Familiarity with the broader APJ region and curiosity about how different markets operate
* Experience in a high-growth B2B software environment
* Background in consulting, finance, or business operations

## **How we're different**

We believe that the highest-impact AI research will be big science. At Anthropic we work as a single cohesive team on just a few large-scale research efforts. And we value impact — advancing our long-term goals of steerable, trustworthy AI — rather than work on smaller and more specific puzzles. We view AI research as an empirical science, which has as much in common with physics and biology as with traditional efforts in computer science. We're an extremely collaborative group, and we host frequent research discussions to ensure that we are pursuing the highest-impact work at any given time. As such, we greatly value communication skills.

The easiest way to understand our research directions is to read our recent research. This research continues many of the directions our team worked on prior to Anthropic, including: GPT-3, Circuit-Based Interpretability, Multimodal Neurons, Scaling Laws, AI & Compute, Concrete Problems in AI Safety, and Learning from Human Preferences.
