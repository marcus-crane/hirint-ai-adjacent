## **About Anthropic**

Anthropic’s mission is to create reliable, interpretable, and steerable AI systems. We want AI to be safe and beneficial for our users and for society as a whole. Our team is a quickly growing group of committed researchers, engineers, policy experts, and business leaders working together to build beneficial AI systems.

## **About the role**

This is a critical leadership role responsible for building and scaling Anthropic's consulting partner ecosystem and AI-native boutique partners across Australia and New Zealand (ANZ) region. You will own the strategic roadmap, investment prioritization, development plans, and revenue accountability for a portfolio of partnerships that will collectively become critical drivers of Anthropic's enterprise growth. **This is a builder role, not a manager role.** Given Anthropic's stage, we need someone who has proven they can take partnerships from zero to scaled revenue engines — someone who has built these relationships from the ground up, not just inherited and managed existing ones.

## **Key responsibilities**

* Partnership development and execution:

+ You will treat each partner as a structured sales pursuit with detailed stakeholder mapping, account plans, and targeted engagement strategies.
+ You will guide partners in creating repeatable customer solutions that address specific use cases across industries including financial services, healthcare, retail, manufacturing, public sector, and professional services.

* Performance and KPI ownership:

+ Own and drive key performance indicators: partner-sourced pipeline, partner-influenced revenue, consultants trained and certified, joint proposals submitted
+ Establish clear success metrics with each partner and drive regular business reviews against targets
+ Report on partnership health, progress, and commercial outcomes with data-driven insights
+ Every deal influenced through your partner portfolio must be architected for customer success and long-term value expansion. You will establish success criteria for joint accounts, ensure proper implementation and adoption support, and create feedback loops that drive continuous improvement.

* Market Development & Brand Building

+ Amplify the Anthropic brand across ANZ through strategic partner relationships
+ Develop and execute market-specific GTM strategies adapted to local business practices and cultural nuances
+ Navigate regional regulatory, compliance, and data sovereignty considerations
+ Create competitive differentiation through unique partner capabilities and joint value propositions

## **Minimum qualifications**

* 15+ years of experience in enterprise technology partnerships, ideally within cloud, AI/ML, or platform businesses
* Demonstrated track record of building and scaling enterprise technology partnerships from zero to meaningful, repeatable revenue
* Experience driving partner-sourced and partner-influenced pipeline through consulting, systems integrator, or platform partners
* Hands-on proficiency with CRM and pipeline management tools (e.g., Salesforce) and with building account plans, stakeholder maps, and partner business reviews
* Ability to work cross-functionally across sales, product, marketing, and regional teams
* Based in, and authorized to work in, the ANZ region

## **Preferred qualifications**

* Deep existing relationships at VP+ level within target partners
* Track record of driving incremental revenue through partnerships specifically in ANZ
* History of delivering exceptional customer success and brand protection through partner channels
* Strong data-driven orientation toward measurable outcomes and KPI attainment
* A builder mentality, comfortable starting from zero; a competitive drive obsessed with winning and differentiation; and a passion for AI aligned with Anthropic's mission of beneficial AI development

## **How we're different**

We believe that the highest-impact AI research will be big science. At Anthropic we work as a single cohesive team on just a few large-scale research efforts. And we value impact — advancing our long-term goals of steerable, trustworthy AI — rather than work on smaller and more specific puzzles. We view AI research as an empirical science, which has as much in common with physics and biology as with traditional efforts in computer science. We're an extremely collaborative group, and we host frequent research discussions to ensure that we are pursuing the highest-impact work at any given time. As such, we greatly value communication skills.

The easiest way to understand our research directions is to read our recent research. This research continues many of the directions our team worked on prior to Anthropic, including: GPT-3, Circuit-Based Interpretability, Multimodal Neurons, Scaling Laws, AI & Compute, Concrete Problems in AI Safety, and Learning from Human Preferences.
