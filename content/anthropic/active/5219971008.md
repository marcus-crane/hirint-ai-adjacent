## **About Anthropic**

Anthropic’s mission is to create reliable, interpretable, and steerable AI systems. We want AI to be safe and beneficial for our users and for society as a whole. Our team is a quickly growing group of committed researchers, engineers, policy experts, and business leaders working together to build beneficial AI systems.

## About the role

As an Enterprise Account Executive based in Seoul, you'll lead Anthropic's engagement with Korea's largest conglomerate customers. You'll own the full sales cycle from prospecting to closing, working across a group's holding company and affiliates to drive adoption of safe, frontier AI technology.

## Responsibilities

* Own and exceed revenue targets across a portfolio of major conglomerate groups
* Map each group's structure - holding company, key affiliates, and in-group SI or services arms - to find where a win can expand across the group
* Build relationships with senior stakeholders at both the group-strategy level and within individual affiliates
* Navigate group governance: know which decisions need holding-company alignment and which affiliates can move independently
* Work with internal teams (Product, Engineering, Applied AI, Partnerships) to tailor solutions to each group's standards and requirements
* Turn single-affiliate wins into multi-affiliate or group-wide expansion

## You may be a good fit if you have

* 8+ years of enterprise sales experience in Korea, with a track record selling into large conglomerate group accounts
* Real understanding of conglomerate group structure and governance — holding companies, affiliate hierarchies, group-strategy offices, and in-group supplier/SI relationships
* Experience managing multi-affiliate, multi-stakeholder sales cycles
* A track record of expanding a single account into a multi-affiliate or group-wide deal
* Excellent communication skills in both Korean and English
* A passion for AI built and deployed safely

## **How we're different**

We believe that the highest-impact AI research will be big science. At Anthropic we work as a single cohesive team on just a few large-scale research efforts. And we value impact — advancing our long-term goals of steerable, trustworthy AI — rather than work on smaller and more specific puzzles. We view AI research as an empirical science, which has as much in common with physics and biology as with traditional efforts in computer science. We're an extremely collaborative group, and we host frequent research discussions to ensure that we are pursuing the highest-impact work at any given time. As such, we greatly value communication skills.

The easiest way to understand our research directions is to read our recent research. This research continues many of the directions our team worked on prior to Anthropic, including: GPT-3, Circuit-Based Interpretability, Multimodal Neurons, Scaling Laws, AI & Compute, Concrete Problems in AI Safety, and Learning from Human Preferences.
