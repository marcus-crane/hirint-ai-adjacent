## **About Anthropic**

Anthropic’s mission is to create reliable, interpretable, and steerable AI systems. We want AI to be safe and beneficial for our users and for society as a whole. Our team is a quickly growing group of committed researchers, engineers, policy experts, and business leaders working together to build beneficial AI systems.

## **About the role**

As an Employee Relations Partner at Anthropic, you will play a critical role in fostering a workplace culture that enables our teams to do their best work while ensuring fairness, compliance, and alignment with our mission to build safe, transformative AI systems. You will be responsible for working with current ER staff to continue building and refining Anthropic's ER function, serving as a subject matter expert on complex investigations, performance management issues, and other employee relations matters globally. You will be the SME for ER projects as needed, working with internal stakeholders and ER team members to build and operationalize scalable and compliant processes and documentation.

This role requires balancing strategic thinking with hands-on execution as you will be the primary ER Partner for specific business groups, and serve as a partner for iRisk, Security and Compliance investigations. You'll help this newly formed team design scalable ER processes and policies that span multiple regions. You'll partner closely with People Partners, Employment Legal, and cross-functional teams to navigate sensitive employee matters with care, integrity, and sound judgment. As we continue to scale rapidly, you'll help build the foundations of an ER function that can grow with the organization.

## **Key responsibilities**

* **Workplace Investigations**: You and the ER team will own Anthropic's overall approach to workplace investigations. Conduct complex, high-stakes investigations personally while providing guidance and coaching to People Partners on lower-level investigations. Partner with Internal Risk, Compliance  and other teams to ensure coordinated, thorough responses and investigations into related concerns.
* **Performance Management Advisory**: Serve as senior advisor to People Partners on complex performance management matters, particularly those intersecting with investigations or involving senior employees. Provide strategic guidance on performance improvement plans, disciplinary actions, and separations.
* **Policy Development & Education**: Collaborate with ER leadership, Integrity/Compliance, Enablement, and other teams on employee relations policies. Own education strategy for investigations and ER best practices, delivering training to People Partners, managers, and employees as needed.
* **Program Development**: Along with the ER team, build scalable, repeatable processes for investigations, case management, and employee relations issues. Develop frameworks for consistent decision-making while maintaining flexibility for nuanced situations.
* **Data & Insights**: Track ER metrics and trends to identify patterns, inform policy decisions, and proactively address systemic issues, particularly as they relate to the client groups you support. Use data to improve programs and measure impact.
* **Cross-functional Partnership**: Work closely with Employment Legal on risk assessment, compliance matters, and sensitive cases. Partner with Security, Trust & Safety, and other teams as needed on matters with broader organizational implications.

* Excel at de-escalating charged situations and building trust with employees at all levels

## **How we're different**

We believe that the highest-impact AI research will be big science. At Anthropic we work as a single cohesive team on just a few large-scale research efforts. And we value impact — advancing our long-term goals of steerable, trustworthy AI — rather than work on smaller and more specific puzzles. We view AI research as an empirical science, which has as much in common with physics and biology as with traditional efforts in computer science. We're an extremely collaborative group, and we host frequent research discussions to ensure that we are pursuing the highest-impact work at any given time. As such, we greatly value communication skills.

The easiest way to understand our research directions is to read our recent research. This research continues many of the directions our team worked on prior to Anthropic, including: GPT-3, Circuit-Based Interpretability, Multimodal Neurons, Scaling Laws, AI & Compute, Concrete Problems in AI Safety, and Learning from Human Preferences.
