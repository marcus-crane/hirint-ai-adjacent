## **About Anthropic**

Anthropic’s mission is to create reliable, interpretable, and steerable AI systems. We want AI to be safe and beneficial for our users and for society as a whole. Our team is a quickly growing group of committed researchers, engineers, policy experts, and business leaders working together to build beneficial AI systems.

## About the role

Partnerships is where Anthropic meets the world. Our Global Partnerships organization builds the relationships with cloud service providers, systems integrators, technology partners, resellers, and strategic customers that bring Claude to hundreds of millions of people at global scale.

As Chief of Staff to Steve Corfield, Global Head of Partnerships, you'll be his thought partner — extending his reach, amplifying his impact, and making sure his time goes to the decisions only he can make. It's a rare seat: you'll see the business end-to-end, operate in the rooms where the biggest calls get made, and work alongside some of the most experienced GTM leaders in the industry. The role is high-trust, high-leverage, and deeply embedded with the principal.

## Responsibilities

* Act as a thought partner to Steve and amplify his impact — joining the rooms he can't and carrying his point of view into them
* Lead a small portfolio of strategic, cross-functional initiatives on Steve's behalf that don't yet have a clear owner
* Partner with Chiefs of Staff across the company to knit the Partnerships agenda into the global leadership rhythm, keeping priorities aligned and decisions moving across regions and functions
* Own Steve's communications surface area — board updates, all-hands content, executive briefings, and key partner correspondence, working in coordination with his Anthropic Business Partner
* Prepare Steve for his most important moments: partner executive meetings, CEO syncs, and external speaking engagements
* Serve as connective tissue between Steve and his leadership team — surfacing signal, closing loops, and making sure decisions stick

## You may be a good fit if you have

* 7+ years of experience, including time as a Chief of Staff, in management consulting, or in a senior individual-contributor role supporting a C-suite leader
* Working knowledge of the cloud and AI partner ecosystem (AWS, GCP, Azure, SIs, ISVs) — enough to represent a senior GTM leader credibly from day one
* Strong written communication skills, with the ability to structure ambiguous problems quickly and draft for a CEO or board audience
* High EQ and the ability to hold both confidence and candor with senior leaders across functions
* A bias toward action and genuine energy for making other people more effective

## Strong candidates may also have

* Experience at a hyperscaler or enterprise SaaS company during a period of rapid scale
* Directly supported a senior GTM leader as their Chief of Staff or right hand

## **How we're different**

We believe that the highest-impact AI research will be big science. At Anthropic we work as a single cohesive team on just a few large-scale research efforts. And we value impact — advancing our long-term goals of steerable, trustworthy AI — rather than work on smaller and more specific puzzles. We view AI research as an empirical science, which has as much in common with physics and biology as with traditional efforts in computer science. We're an extremely collaborative group, and we host frequent research discussions to ensure that we are pursuing the highest-impact work at any given time. As such, we greatly value communication skills.

The easiest way to understand our research directions is to read our recent research. This research continues many of the directions our team worked on prior to Anthropic, including: GPT-3, Circuit-Based Interpretability, Multimodal Neurons, Scaling Laws, AI & Compute, Concrete Problems in AI Safety, and Learning from Human Preferences.
