## **About Anthropic**

Anthropic’s mission is to create reliable, interpretable, and steerable AI systems. We want AI to be safe and beneficial for our users and for society as a whole. Our team is a quickly growing group of committed researchers, engineers, policy experts, and business leaders working together to build beneficial AI systems.

## About the role

We are seeking a Strategic Deals Lead to originate, structure, and close complex infrastructure deals critical to Anthropic's compute, networking, and memory strategy. In this role, you will lead end-to-end deal execution — from initial term sheet through final agreement — working across internal and external stakeholders to secure the infrastructure capacity Anthropic needs to stay at the frontier of AI development.

Anthropic's AI models depend on large-scale, tightly coordinated infrastructure spanning accelerator clusters, high-speed interconnects, and memory subsystems. Ensuring we can access and deploy that infrastructure at scale requires sophisticated deal-making with some of the world's largest technology companies. You will work closely with engineering, finance, and legal teams to structure transactions, negotiate terms, and close agreements with hyperscalers, semiconductor manufacturers, networking vendors, and memory suppliers.

This role is fundamentally about deal leadership — bringing together technical understanding, financial acumen, and negotiating skill to secure strategic infrastructure at the terms and timelines Anthropic requires. You will be an integral part of a team focused on ensuring Anthropic has the resources it needs to pursue its mission of developing safe, beneficial AI systems.

## Key responsibilities

* Lead the full deal lifecycle for complex infrastructure transactions — from opportunity identification and term sheet development through negotiation, execution, and close
* Structure and negotiate large-scale agreements with hyperscalers, AI infrastructure companies, telecom providers, semiconductor manufacturers, and memory suppliers across compute, networking, and memory domains
* Develop and drive financial models, term analyses, and commercial frameworks to support deal structuring and inform executive decision-making
* Translate complex technical requirements from engineering into commercially viable deal structures and contractual terms
* Build and maintain senior-level relationships with strategic counterparties to identify and advance deal opportunities
* Partner with Legal, Finance, and Engineering to align deal terms with Anthropic's technical, financial, and risk requirements
* Evaluate vendor and partner proposals, identify risks and trade-offs, and drive toward optimal deal outcomes
* Provide clear reporting on deal status, risks, and strategic implications to senior leadership

## Minimum qualifications

* Demonstrated track record of leading and closing complex, high-value technology transactions or strategic agreements
* Experience structuring and negotiating deals within the technology space (hyperscalers, AI infrastructure, semiconductors, networking, telecom, or data center infrastructure)
* Strong financial modeling and analytical skills, including experience with deal economics, vendor financing structures, and investment analysis
* Technical fluency in high-speed networking (InfiniBand, Ethernet, optical interconnects) or memory technologies (DRAM, HBM, or related); familiarity with compute or silicon development a plus
* Demonstrated ability to drive complex negotiations with large, sophisticated counterparties to favorable outcomes
* Experience working at or negotiating with hyperscalers, telecom providers, or AI infrastructure companies
* Strong communication and executive presence, with the ability to influence senior stakeholders internally and externally

## Preferred qualifications

* 8–10 years of experience in strategic deals, corporate development, investment banking, or business development roles at technology companies, hyperscalers, telecom companies, or AI infrastructure firms
* Background in corporate development, including experience with M&A or strategic investments in networking, storage, memory, or adjacent infrastructure domains
* Familiarity with AI/ML infrastructure requirements, including training cluster design, network topology, and memory bandwidth and capacity constraints
* Experience with vendor financing, equipment leasing, or capital markets structures applied to infrastructure procurement
* Understanding of supply chain dynamics for high-demand infrastructure components (CPUs, network ASICs, HBM)
* Background in technical due diligence or technology mergers and acquisitions
* A passion for Anthropic's mission and ensuring safe AI development

## **How we're different**

We believe that the highest-impact AI research will be big science. At Anthropic we work as a single cohesive team on just a few large-scale research efforts. And we value impact — advancing our long-term goals of steerable, trustworthy AI — rather than work on smaller and more specific puzzles. We view AI research as an empirical science, which has as much in common with physics and biology as with traditional efforts in computer science. We're an extremely collaborative group, and we host frequent research discussions to ensure that we are pursuing the highest-impact work at any given time. As such, we greatly value communication skills.

The easiest way to understand our research directions is to read our recent research. This research continues many of the directions our team worked on prior to Anthropic, including: GPT-3, Circuit-Based Interpretability, Multimodal Neurons, Scaling Laws, AI & Compute, Concrete Problems in AI Safety, and Learning from Human Preferences.
