## **About Anthropic**

Anthropic’s mission is to create reliable, interpretable, and steerable AI systems. We want AI to be safe and beneficial for our users and for society as a whole. Our team is a quickly growing group of committed researchers, engineers, policy experts, and business leaders working together to build beneficial AI systems.

## About the Role

As an Australia Public Sector Account Executive at Anthropic, you'll drive the adoption of safe, frontier AI within the Australian government space, establishing and growing our presence in this strategic market. You'll leverage your consultative sales expertise and deep understanding of the Australian public sector landscape to propel revenue growth while becoming a trusted partner to customers, helping them embed and deploy AI while uncovering its full range of capabilities. In collaboration with GTM, product, and marketing teams, you'll help define our market entry strategy and value proposition for the Australian public sector.

## **Responsibilities**

* Lead Anthropic's market entry and expansion in the Australian public sector, establishing our presence and building relationships with key stakeholders across government departments and agencies
* Own the full sales cycle for Australian public sector opportunities, from initial outreach through to successful deployment, with a focus on building long-term strategic partnerships
* Navigate the unique procurement requirements, security standards, and compliance frameworks specific to Australian government technology adoption
* Collaborate with policy teams to ensure alignment with Australian government AI strategies and initiatives
* Design and execute innovative sales strategies tailored to the Australian public sector environment, meeting and exceeding revenue targets while maintaining strong relationships with procurement teams and decision-makers
* Provide market intelligence and feedback to product teams to ensure our offerings meet Australian public sector requirements and use cases
* Build and maintain relationships with relevant systems integrators and technology partners in the Australian market
* Develop and document best practices for Australian public sector engagement, creating playbooks that can scale our presence in the market
* Serve as a leader within the broader public sector team, sharing insights and collaborating on cross-market opportunities

## **You may be a good fit if you have:**

* 10+ years of enterprise sales experience in the Australian public sector, with a proven track record of driving adoption of emerging technologies
* Deep understanding of Australian government procurement processes, security requirements, and technology adoption frameworks
* Security clearances required
* Demonstrated ability to build and grow new market segments, with potential to develop into a leadership role
* Strong track record of managing complex sales cycles and securing strategic deals in the public sector
* Experience navigating multiple stakeholders across government departments, building consensus and providing innovative solutions
* Excellent communication skills and ability to present confidently to all levels, from technical teams to senior government officials
* Strategic thinking combined with hands-on execution capabilities
* Ability to work independently while maintaining strong collaboration with global teams
* A passion for safe and ethical AI development, with the ability to articulate its importance in the public sector con

## **How we're different**

We believe that the highest-impact AI research will be big science. At Anthropic we work as a single cohesive team on just a few large-scale research efforts. And we value impact — advancing our long-term goals of steerable, trustworthy AI — rather than work on smaller and more specific puzzles. We view AI research as an empirical science, which has as much in common with physics and biology as with traditional efforts in computer science. We're an extremely collaborative group, and we host frequent research discussions to ensure that we are pursuing the highest-impact work at any given time. As such, we greatly value communication skills.

The easiest way to understand our research directions is to read our recent research. This research continues many of the directions our team worked on prior to Anthropic, including: GPT-3, Circuit-Based Interpretability, Multimodal Neurons, Scaling Laws, AI & Compute, Concrete Problems in AI Safety, and Learning from Human Preferences.
