## **About Anthropic**

Anthropic’s mission is to create reliable, interpretable, and steerable AI systems. We want AI to be safe and beneficial for our users and for society as a whole. Our team is a quickly growing group of committed researchers, engineers, policy experts, and business leaders working together to build beneficial AI systems.

## **About the role:**

Anthropic's GTM Systems team builds and operates the systems and processes behind the company's go-to-market motion — quoting, provisioning, customer success tooling, and the data infrastructure that ties them together. As a Program Manager on the team, you'll own cross-functional coordination across our concurrent systems initiatives: aligning stakeholders, running quarterly business reviews, and making sure every program stays tied to the business outcome it exists to drive.

You'll work across Sales, Customer Success, Revenue Operations, Deal Desk, and Business Technology leadership to keep multiple in-flight programs moving in lockstep — CPQ, deployment tracking, account data quality, and compliance-driven process change. You'll be the person in the room translating between technical implementation detail and executive priorities, and the person following up afterward to make sure alignment turns into action.

In a typical week, you'll prepare for and run quarterly business reviews, meet with operational leads and stakeholders across the GTM organization, and pull together the documentation that keeps initiatives aligned. Throughout, you'll dig into the "why" behind each program — not just tracking status, but making sure the team can always articulate the business outcome a program is meant to drive and how it's being measured.

## **Key responsibilities:**

* Own cross-functional coordination across concurrent GTM systems programs, including CPQ, deployment tracking, account data quality, and compliance-driven process change
* Prepare and run quarterly business reviews for GTM systems initiatives, and drive the decisions and follow-ups that come out of them
* Partner with operational leads and stakeholders across Sales, Customer Success, Revenue Operations, Deal Desk, and Business Technology to keep programs aligned and moving
* Translate between technical implementation detail and executive priorities in both directions, so engineers understand the business context and leadership understands the trade-offs
* Define and track the business outcome each program exists to drive, along with how it's measured, and hold programs to it
* Aggregate and maintain the documentation, plans, and status views that keep cross-functional initiatives coherent
* Surface risks, dependencies, and competing priorities early, and drive them to resolution without direct authority over the teams involved

## **Minimum qualifications:**

* Hands-on experience with GTM processes at a software or technology company — lead-to-cash, quote-to-cash, renewal and expansion motions, or customer health
* Working understanding of how systems such as Salesforce, CPQ platforms, or customer success tools support those processes
* A track record of owning cross-functional alignment with senior stakeholders — running recurring business reviews, facilitating rooms with competing priorities, and driving decisions without direct authority
* Strong program management fundamentals: planning, tracking, dependency and risk management
* Clear written and verbal communication, with the ability to move between technical detail and executive summary depending on the audience
* Work effectively in fast-moving environments with high ambiguity, including where process doesn't exist yet
* Excited about Anthropic's mission to develop AI that is safe and beneficial

## **Preferred qualifications:**

* Experience with lead and campaign automation, CPQ implementations, or customer success and health-scoring programs (Gainsight or equivalent) at a software company
* Experience operating in a compliance-sensitive environment, such as SOX or IPO readiness
* Experience partnering closely with engineering or business technology teams on systems implementations, not only with business stakeholders
* Familiarity with the data models behind GTM systems, and comfort using data to diagnose whether a program is working
* Experience at a company that scaled significantly during your tenure

## **How we're different**

We believe that the highest-impact AI research will be big science. At Anthropic we work as a single cohesive team on just a few large-scale research efforts. And we value impact — advancing our long-term goals of steerable, trustworthy AI — rather than work on smaller and more specific puzzles. We view AI research as an empirical science, which has as much in common with physics and biology as with traditional efforts in computer science. We're an extremely collaborative group, and we host frequent research discussions to ensure that we are pursuing the highest-impact work at any given time. As such, we greatly value communication skills.

The easiest way to understand our research directions is to read our recent research. This research continues many of the directions our team worked on prior to Anthropic, including: GPT-3, Circuit-Based Interpretability, Multimodal Neurons, Scaling Laws, AI & Compute, Concrete Problems in AI Safety, and Learning from Human Preferences.
