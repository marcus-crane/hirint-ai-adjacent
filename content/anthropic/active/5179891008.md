## **About Anthropic**

Anthropic’s mission is to create reliable, interpretable, and steerable AI systems. We want AI to be safe and beneficial for our users and for society as a whole. Our team is a quickly growing group of committed researchers, engineers, policy experts, and business leaders working together to build beneficial AI systems.

## About the Role:

Claude.ai is Anthropic's most visible product surface, and how well our models perform there shapes how tens of millions of people experience Claude. We're hiring a Product Operations Manager to embed within the [Claude.ai](http://claude.ai) product team. The right candidate will help program manage model performance, launch readiness, and the general operating model on the [Claude.ai](http://claude.ai) and Growth product team.

You'll balance hands-on execution with the systems that let the team ship new models and new features with confidence. You won't write evals yourself, but you'll make sure eval coverage exists, burn down lists get completed, and issues get resolved before they become surprises. You'll also run the team's core operating rhythms and invest in our feedback-loop systems connecting consumer users to what the product team does next. You'll shape how Product Operations works with within [Claude.ai](http://claude.ai) from the ground up.

## Responsibilities:

* Operationalize model performance: prompting-update tracking, issue prioritization, and go/no-go decision making in tight collaboration with Research, Product, and Engineering
* Own Launch Readiness for Claude.ai: launch checklists, coordination with XFN Teams, and observability into status and roadmap
* Run team operations for Claude.ai & Growth
* Invest in feedback-loop systems: intake, triage, synthesis for consumer users
* Coordinate across functions without formal authority

## You may be a good fit if you have:

* Have 5+ years of experience in product operations, program management, or launch readiness roles at fast-paced tech companies.
* Have operationalized model performance before, ideally at an application-layer AI product company, without necessarily writing evals yourself
* Have built or fixed an operational process end-to-end, not just executed someone else's plan
* Thrive in ambiguity, this role is still being defined
* Bring real cross-functional influence
* Have built a feedback or issue-triage system from scratch

### Bonus:

## **How we're different**

We believe that the highest-impact AI research will be big science. At Anthropic we work as a single cohesive team on just a few large-scale research efforts. And we value impact — advancing our long-term goals of steerable, trustworthy AI — rather than work on smaller and more specific puzzles. We view AI research as an empirical science, which has as much in common with physics and biology as with traditional efforts in computer science. We're an extremely collaborative group, and we host frequent research discussions to ensure that we are pursuing the highest-impact work at any given time. As such, we greatly value communication skills.

The easiest way to understand our research directions is to read our recent research. This research continues many of the directions our team worked on prior to Anthropic, including: GPT-3, Circuit-Based Interpretability, Multimodal Neurons, Scaling Laws, AI & Compute, Concrete Problems in AI Safety, and Learning from Human Preferences.
