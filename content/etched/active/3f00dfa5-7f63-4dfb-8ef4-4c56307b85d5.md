**About Etched**

Etched is building hardware for frontier intelligence. We co-design chips, racks, software, and manufacturing to deliver best-in-class throughput and latency across both prefill and decode workloads. Our first products are heavily focused on **inference**. Backed by hundreds of millions from top-tier investors and staffed by leading engineers, Etched is redefining the infrastructure layer for the fastest growing industry in history.

**Job Summary**

We're looking for a Stock Administrator who wants to own one of the most important functions at a rapidly scaling company. You'll be the driving force behind our equity programs, partnering with leaders across the business to support major company milestones while ensuring every employee has a world-class equity experience. If you're excited by ownership, complexity, and the opportunity to build systems that scale alongside an ambitious company, we'd love to talk.

**Key Responsibilities**

* Own the day-to-day administration of the company's global equity programs, including grants, exercises, vesting, releases, ESPP purchases, tender offers, and other equity transactions.
* Maintain accurate equity records across Carta, transfer agents, and internal systems while ensuring data integrity and operational excellence.
* Partner closely with Legal, Finance, Payroll, and People to ensure equity transactions are accurately reflected in payroll, accounting, tax reporting, and SEC filings.
* Support monthly, quarterly, and annual financial reporting, including ASC 718 stock-based compensation accounting and audit requests.
* Ensure compliance with applicable securities laws, tax regulations, SOX controls, and company equity policies.
* Serve as the primary point of contact for employee questions regarding equity awards, vesting, taxation, and liquidity events.
* Manage relationships with external vendors, including transfer agents and equity administration providers.
* Continuously improve equity operations by building scalable processes, automating manual workflows, and strengthening internal controls as the company grows.

**You may be a good fit if you have**

* Bachelor's degree or equivalent practical experience.
* 3+ years of equity administration experience at a high-growth technology company, public company, or leading law firm.
* Experience with stock options, RSUs, ESPPs, tender offers, and other equity programs.
* Strong knowledge of ASC 718, SOX, SEC reporting, and equity taxation.
* Experience with platforms such as Carta, Rippling, AST, Shareworks, or similar.
* Strong attention to detail, sound judgment, and excellent communication skills.

**Strong candidates may also have experience with**

* Certified Equity Professional (CEP) designation.
* Experience supporting an IPO or operating at a newly public company.
* Experience with international equity programs and cross-border compliance.
* A track record of building scalable equity operations and automating manual processes.
