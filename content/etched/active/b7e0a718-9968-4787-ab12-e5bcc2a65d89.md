**About Etched**

Etched is building hardware for frontier intelligence. We co-design chips, racks, software, and manufacturing to deliver best-in-class throughput and latency across both prefill and decode workloads. Our first products are heavily focused on **inference**. Backed by hundreds of millions from top-tier investors and staffed by leading engineers, Etched is redefining the infrastructure layer for the fastest growing industry in history.

**Job Summary**

The **People Operations** team architects and maintains the people systems, operating rhythms, and org design at Etched. You'll work directly with leadership to figure out how we organize, develop, and retain the best people as we rapidly scale in one of the world’s most important industries.

You'll own everything from org design and performance systems to people analytics and company culture. People are our most important asset at Etched: you'll learn how great startups are built, own important strategic people initiatives, and help drive some of the most critical decisions and workflows that determine how we organize and scale the business.

**Key responsibilities**

* **Operations** - Own payroll, benefits, and compliance. Work with legal and vendors to stay ahead of regulatory requirements. Keep people data accurate across all systems.
* **Employee Inquiries:** Field and resolve employee questions regarding reimbursements, payroll, benefits, and other HR-related matters, ensuring timely and accurate responses.
* **People Analytics** - Build dynamic dashboards to visualize and understand the employee lifecycle. Use data to understand performance patterns and org health.

**You may be a good fit if you have (Must-have qualifications)**

* A process-oriented mindset, with a track record of building systems that scale without requiring constant maintenance.
* A commitment to operational excellence, shaped by experience in high-ownership, high-pressure environments such as finance, consulting, or fast-growing startups.
* Strong strategic thinking skills, with an understanding of how talent decisions directly connect to business outcomes.
* A highly organized and detail-oriented approach — nothing slips through the cracks under your watch.
* Comfort working with data and building live dashboards and reports to drive clarity and decision-making.
* Exceptional communication skills, with the ability to engage and collaborate effectively at all levels of the organization.
* A high sense of ownership — you instinctively take initiative and tackle problems head-on.

**Strong candidates may also have experience with (Nice-to-have qualifications)**

* HR tools such as HRIS systems (e.g., Rippling), payroll platforms, or compliance solutions.
* Employment law, immigration, or cross-border employment issues.
* The AI ecosystem, semiconductors, or other complex technical products.
