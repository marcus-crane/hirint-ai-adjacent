### **The Role**

The **Fleet Service and Maintenance Technician** plays a critical role in maintaining the safety, performance, and operational readiness of Wayve’s autonomous vehicle fleet. From routine servicing and diagnostics to urgent repairs and vehicle readiness for high-visibility events, technicians ensure our fleet remains in peak condition to support testing, demonstrations, and real-world deployment.

Reporting to the Fleet Service Manager, this hands-on role requires strong mechanical knowledge, the ability to work with modern diagnostic systems, and a proactive, safety-conscious mindset. Working closely with engineering, drivers, and operations teams, you’ll help keep autonomy moving forward, every single day.

### *Key responsibilities:*

* **Routine & Preventative Maintenance** – Execute scheduled servicing and inspections per manufacturer and internal guidelines.
* **Mechanical & Electrical Fault Resolution** – Diagnose and repair a wide range of issues using OEM documentation and advanced diagnostic equipment.
* **Vehicle Readiness for Demonstrations** – Ensure vehicles meet high standards of cleanliness, reliability, and functionality for public-facing use.
* **Workshop Safety & Compliance** – Adhere to safety best practices, risk assessments, and regulations.
* **Repair Documentation & Tracking** – Accurately record faults found, actions taken, and parts used using digital tools.
* **Feedback into Fleet Performance** – Communicate recurring issues or improvement ideas to the Fleet Service Manager and Field Engineering teams.
* **Customer-Facing Communication** – Engage with fleet drivers to resolve issues they report and ensure a positive operational experience.

### About you

In order to set you up for success as a Fleet Servicing and Maintenance Technician at Wayve, we’re looking for the following skills and experience.

### ***Essential***

* Relevant qualifications in automotive servicing, diagnostics and repairs
* Proven experience as a Vehicle Technician in an automotive servicing or fleet environment.
* Strong diagnostics capability using OEM or third-party diagnostic equipment.
* Familiarity with mechanical and bodywork repairs using OEM repair manuals.
* Competent with computer systems for tracking and recording maintenance tasks (e.g., Word, Excel).
* Comfortable interacting with engineers, operations staff, and drivers.
* Strong understanding of workshop safety and compliance standards.
* US driving license.

### Desirable:

* Experience working with vehicles equipped with ADAS or autonomous technology.
* Understanding of AV fleet operational needs in a high-uptime environment.
* Passion for emerging mobility technologies and willingness to learn new systems.
