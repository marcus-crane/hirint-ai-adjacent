## **The role**

If you’re passionate about driving, technology, and the future of mobility, this role offers a rare opportunity to work at the forefront of autonomous driving development.

As a Vehicle Safety Operator, you will support the development of next-generation AI driving systems by operating Wayve vehicles on public roads, evaluating vehicle behaviour, and providing operational feedback to engineering teams. You will act as a critical bridge between real-world driving conditions and the engineers developing Wayve’s AI systems.

**Key responsibilities:**

* Support real-world autonomous vehicle testing operations across Japan.
* Safely monitor autonomous driving systems during on-road testing and intervene whenever required.
* Provide structured operational feedback to engineers and research teams to help improve AI driving performance.
* Support data collection and validation activities that contribute to the training and improvement of Wayve’s AI systems.
* Maintain high operational and safety standards while accurately reporting vehicle, testing, or safety-related issues.
