## **The Role**

We are seeking an experienced Hardware Engineer to join our Hardware team and play a key role in developing next-generation autonomous driving compute platforms. This role focuses on low-level electronics design, embedded systems, and high-performance compute integration translating autonomy requirements into robust, scalable hardware systems.

You will work hands-on with cutting-edge computing platforms, collaborating across hardware, software, and systems teams to design, bring up, and validate mission-critical electronics that enable embodied AI.

## **Key Responsibilities**

* Design and develop embedded electronics systems for autonomous driving applications
* Architect and implement compute platforms for ML workloads
* Collaborate with software and autonomy teams to optimize hardware for performance, reliability, and scalability
* Own board-level design, bring-up, validation, and debugging across hardware and software boundaries
* Develop high-speed interfaces such as PCIe, Ethernet, USB, and related technologies in an automotive environment
* Work with mechanical engineering teams to design robust electronics hardware
* Lead schematic design, PCB layout reviews, and signal integrity considerations
* Create design and product specification documentation for electronics hardware
* Work with suppliers and manufacturing partners to ensure design robustness and production readiness

## **About You**

### **Essential:**

* 7+ years of experience in electronics or embedded systems design
* Strong experience with board-level design, bring-up, and debugging
* Deep understanding of embedded systems and compute architectures
* Experience with high-speed digital interfaces (e.g., PCIe, Ethernet, USB)
* Strong debugging skills across hardware and software systems
* Familiarity with Linux-based systems and embedded environments
* Proficiency in at least one programming language (e.g., C, C++, Python)
* Ability to architecture and analyze complex systems
* Strong communication skills and ability to collaborate across multidisciplinary teams
* Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Electrical Engineering, Computer Engineering, or related field

## **Desirable Experience**

* Experience designing hardware for AI/ML workloads or high-performance computing systems
* Experience with signal integrity, power integrity, and high-speed PCB design
* Knowledge of networking systems and protocols (Ethernet, TCP/IP, etc.)
* Experience with firmware or low-level software development (bootloaders, drivers, BSPs)
* Familiarity with NVIDIA and Qualcomm SoC applications
* Experience working with contract manufacturers and scaling hardware to production
* Autonomous vehicle, robotics, or other safety-critical system experience
