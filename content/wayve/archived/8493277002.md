## **The role**

As Wayve rapidly scales, our IT team plays a crucial role in keeping our employees productive and our operations efficient. We’re looking for an IT Support Engineer (TC2) to support the day-to-day IT needs across our Japan offices. This is a full-time, on-site role, with flexibility required to support each of our 3 offices as needed. You’ll be a hands-on, first-line problem solver—supporting everything from laptop configuration to meeting room A/V to network troubleshooting. You’ll also support joiner/mover/leaver processes, including onboarding and off-boarding tasks.

This role is ideal for someone early in their IT career who is eager to learn, thrives in a fast-paced environment, and wants to grow their skills while contributing to cutting-edge work in autonomous vehicle development.

## **Key responsibilities**

**End-User Support**

* Provide timely support for laptops (Windows, macOS, Linux), mobile devices, printers, and peripherals.
* Troubleshoot hardware and software issues and escalate as needed.
* Manage onboarding and off-boarding tasks, including account provisioning and hardware setup when needed.

**IT Systems & Tools**

* Maintain and monitor endpoint management systems (e.g., MDM, patching, asset inventory).
* Assist in configuring and maintaining core business tools (e.g., GSuite, Slack, Jira, …).
* Help with access control and identity systems (SSO, MFA, user permissions).

**Network & Infrastructure Support**

* Support basic network troubleshooting (Wi-Fi, VPN, wired connectivity) under the guidance of senior engineers.
* Help manage and maintain meeting room A/V equipment, including Google Meet and Teams rooms.
* Provide on-site support across Japan offices as needed (3 offices).

**Documentation & Process**

* Contribute to internal IT documentation and knowledge base articles.
* Log and track tickets using the internal IT ticketing system (e.g., Jira, ServiceNow).

**Team Collaboration**

* Work closely with IT, Security, and Facilities teams to ensure smooth daily operations.
* Help support company-wide IT initiatives and system rollouts as needed.

## **About you**

In order to set you up for success as an IT Support Engineer at Wayve, we’re looking for the following skills and experience.

Essential

* 5 years + of professional experience in an IT support or systems administration role
* Familiarity with macOS
* Empathetic and service-oriented with strong communication skills.
* Willingness to learn and grow in a rapidly evolving tech environment.
* Ability to prioritize tasks and follow through with minimal supervision.
* Exposure to identity platforms (e.g., Okta, Google Workspace, Azure AD).
* Familiarity with ticketing systems and ITSM workflows.
* JLPT N1 required (or equivalent business-level fluency) in Japanese, written and spoken.
* Fluency in English (written and spoken) required

Desirable

* Basic understanding of networking concepts (IP, DNS, DHCP, VPN).
* Familiarity with Linux operating systems.
* Experience with endpoint management tools (e.g., Kandji, Intune, …).

This is a full-time role based in our office in Tokyo, Japan.  At Wayve we want the best of all worlds so we operate a hybrid working policy that combines time together in our offices and workshops to fuel innovation, culture, relationships and learning, and time spent working from home.
