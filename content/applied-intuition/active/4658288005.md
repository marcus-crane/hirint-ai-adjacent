## About the role

We are looking for a Systems Safety Engineer with hands-on experience in vehicle-level safety analysis and safety case development. The role focuses on enabling safe integration of vehicle functions through practical safety engineering. Looking for someone who can build and drive vehicle-level safety cases end-to-end, with strong system understanding and hands-on safety engineering experience.

## At Applied Intuition, you will:

* Develop and own vehicle-level safety cases for driving functions and platform integration
* Translate safety analysis into clear, implementable safety requirements and constraints
* Apply ISO 26262 Part 3 (Concept Phase), Part 4 (System Development), and Part 9 (analysis & decomposition)
* Work across vehicle features (braking, steering, ADAS, body functions) to identify risks and define mitigations
* Hands-on development of safety artifacts aligned to road-driving features (e.g., hazard analysis, FMEA, safety case structures)
* Collaborate with HW, SW, and integration teams to ensure safety is correctly implemented end-to-end
* System level working knowledge and good exposure to HW FMEA’s for vehicle safety case measures.
* Support vehicle bring-up and validation with safety checks aligned to system behavior
* Create and maintain safety artifacts (hazard analysis, FMEA, safety case, traceability)
* Experience with or willingness to support mining / off-road vehicle safety programs
* Familiarity with ISO 19014 or ISO 13849
* Exposure to system-level hazard analysis and safety requirements for heavy machinery

## We're looking for someone who has:

* Bachelor’s or master’s degree in electrical engineering, Mechanical Engineering , Systems Engineering, Mechatronics, or a related technical discipline.
* 2+ years of experience in functional safety or systems engineering, preferably within an OEM or Tier-1 ADAS program.
* Hands-on experience in vehicle-level safety case development
* Strong knowledge of ISO 26262 and road vehicle features enablement

## Nice to have:

* Experience working in OEM / Tier-1 / vehicle programs preferred
* Experience with HARA/HAZOP, System FMEA, and safety case derivation
* Good understanding of vehicle systems (ADAS, braking, steering, control systems)
* Ability to bridge system, hardware, and software interactions
