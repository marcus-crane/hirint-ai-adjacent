## About the role

We are looking for a Pipeline Technical Artist/Technical Director to drive the development of our content pipelines and backends. At Applied, you will be responsible for a lot of the technical development inside Unreal and/or the pipelines associated with it. You will assist the pipelining and unit-testing initiatives for automatically triggering procedural content generation and importing this content into Unreal engine. . This position will join an existing team of talented engineers and technical artists with backgrounds in games, VFX, autonomous systems, and robotics.

## At Applied Intuition, you will:

* Establish content pipelines and standards for others to follow
* Create an automated pipeline that can with one button click ingest customer data,  run various systems, and create a final package to be used by the client
* Work with C++ and Python to create flexible pipelines that plug into Houdini and Unreal
* Establish flexible modular pipelines that are maintainable long term
* Enforce good Python standards
* Work with both Object Sim and Unreal to connect features where needed
* Create tools for artists to work efficiently
* Assist with the setup and maintenance of a render farm

## We're looking for someone who has:

* Has 3+ years of experience with Pipeline
* Is an expert user of at least one 3D DCC tool (e.g. Maya/Houdini)
* Has a solid understanding of Python and game/film pipelines
* Can work closely with Engineers to create custom logic as required

## Nice to have:

* Experience with Unreal or other real-time engines (e.g. Frostbite, Unity)
* Houdini experience
* HLSL/GLSL experience
* Experience with Machine Learning
* Computer Science/Software Engineering background
