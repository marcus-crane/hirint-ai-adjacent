## About the role

As a Senior Accountant specializing in project accounting, cost accounting, government contracts, and time-and-material billing, you will be a critical driver of our financial integrity and operational scale across our growing defense and commercial programs. You will own the end-to-end financial management of government and commercial contracts, ensuring accurate cost allocation, compliant billing, and timely reporting. This is a hands-on, detail-oriented role requiring deep expertise in FAR/CAS compliance, T&M invoicing, and project-level financial controls — combined with strong NetSuite and Excel skills and a passion for building scalable processes in a fast-paced environment.

## At Applied Intuition, you will:

* Set up and maintain projects in NetSuite, including budgets, funding ceilings, contract line items (CLINs), and work breakdown structures (WBS)
* Track and reconcile project-level costs; prepare monthly variance analyses against approved budgets and contract funding
* Support project closeout activities including final billing, revenue true-up, and balance sheet reconciliation
* Prepare monthly project financial reports for program managers and finance leadership
* Allocate direct and indirect costs to contracts in compliance with FAR Part 31 cost principles and Cost Accounting Standards (CAS)
* Maintain and update indirect rate structures including fringe, overhead, G&A, and fee pools; support forward pricing rate proposals (FPRPs)
* Validate labor distributions against timesheets and payroll records to ensure accurate cost capture across programs
* Support government contract compliance and audits, including incurred cost submissions and DCAA audit activities
* Ensure compliance with FAR, CAS, DFARS, and agency requirements, including preparation and submission of required government financial reports

## We’re looking for someone who has:

* Bachelor’s degree in Accounting, Finance, or related field
* 5+ years of progressive cost accounting experience
* Demonstrated experience with T&M contract billing, cost-reimbursable contracts, and incurred cost submissions
* Proficiency with NetSuite and Excel required
* Strong analytical and data aggregation skills, with a high level of attention to detail
* Excellent communication and interpersonal skills, with the ability to collaborate effectively across Finance, Contracts, and Program Management teams
* Ability to manage multiple priorities across a portfolio of contracts in a fast-paced, high-growth environment

## Nice to have:

* At least 3 years in government contract accounting (FAR/CAS/DCAA compliance)
* Active CPA license or CPA eligibility
