## About Applied Intuition

Applied Intuition, Inc. is powering the future of physical AI. Founded in 2017 and now valued at $15 billion, the Silicon Valley company is creating the digital infrastructure needed to bring intelligence to every moving machine on the planet. Applied Intuition services the automotive, defense, trucking, construction, mining and agriculture industries in three core areas: tools and infrastructure, operating systems, and autonomy. Eighteen of the top 20 global automakers, as well as the United States military and its allies, trust the company’s solutions to deliver physical intelligence. Applied Intuition is headquartered in Sunnyvale, California, with offices in Washington, D.C.; San Diego; Ft. ## **About the role**

As a Business Systems Engineer at Applied Intuition, you will be the lead architect of our corporate data ecosystem. We are looking for a highly technical, senior-level engineer to bridge the gap between our core business functions and the software that powers them. In this role, you will be responsible for architecting a seamless data ecosystem that connects back-end finance and HR systems to provide a unified, real-time view of our global operations, enabling data-driven strategic decisions at the highest levels**.** You will ensure that our systems—including Oracle NetSuite, Workday, and Pigment—are unified into a robust, scalable architecture that supports rapid global growth.

## **At Applied Intuition, you will:**

* **Architect & Integrate:** Design, build, and maintain mission-critical integrations between core enterprise systems, including Oracle NetSuite (ERP), Workday (HRIS), and Pigment (FP&A)
* **Build Scalable Pipelines:** Develop and manage data pipelines and API-led architectures to ensure data integrity and real-time synchronization across the business stack
* **Strategic Automation:** Partner closely with Finance and People Operations to automate complex workflows and eliminate manual data entry, reducing operational risk
* **Tooling Leadership:** Lead the selection, implementation, and management of iPaaS tools (e.g., Workato, Tray.io, MuleSoft) to standardize how we connect global SaaS applications
* **Standardize Data:** Define and document enterprise data standards and integration protocols to ensure a consistent source of truth for all business-critical KPIs
* **Optimize Performance:** Proactively identify and resolve complex system bottlenecks, ensuring that our finance teams can generate accurate reports and forecasts with zero downtime
* **Mentor & Lead:** Serve as the subject matter expert for enterprise architecture, mentoring junior IT and systems staff on scalability and best practices

## **We're looking for someone who has:**

* 7+ years of experience in business systems engineering, enterprise architecture, or a related integration-heavy role in a fast-paced environment
* Deep technical expertise withOracle NetSuite and Workday APIs, including experience managing complex data migrations or system overhauls
* Hands-on experience with multi-dimensional planning tools like Pigment, Anaplan, or Adaptive Insights
* Proficiency in at least one major iPaaS platform (e.g., Workato, Tray.io, Boomi) and advanced scripting skills (e.g., Python, JavaScript, or SQL) for custom integration logic
* Strong understanding of financial concepts (e.g., GL, procure-to-pay, order-to-cash) and HR lifecycles
* Excellent problem-solving skills and the ability to design architectures that balance long-term scalability with immediate business needs

## **Nice to have:**

* Experience in a high-growth startup environment preparing for public company readiness (SOX compliance)
* Familiarity with Salesforce (CRM) integrations and the broader lead-to-revenue ecosystem
* Advanced certifications in NetSuite (SuiteCloud Developer) or Workday (Integration Consultant)
* Experience with data warehousing and BI tools (e.g., Snowflake, Tableau, or Looker)
