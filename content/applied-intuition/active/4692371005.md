## About the role

We are a rapidly scaling, late-stage startup delivering advanced software platforms and AI-powered toolchains for the development, testing, validation, and deployment of ADAS (Advanced Driver-Assistance Systems) and autonomous vehicle systems. As an Administrative Assistant supporting EMEA Finance & Operations, you will be an essential partner to our regional finance and operations teams across Europe, the Middle East, and Africa.

Based in Germany, you will own the day-to-day financial administration and operational support functions that keep our EMEA offices running smoothly — including budget tracking, vendor purchase order management, accounts payable oversight, and payment monitoring across all EMEA countries. This is a highly cross-functional role requiring strong attention to detail, comfort working across multiple currencies and regulatory environments, and the ability to manage competing priorities with professionalism and efficiency.

## At Applied Intuition, you will:

* Maintain EMEA budget trackers, reconcile against NetSuite cost centers, and support monthly variance analysis and quarterly forecasts
* Own the end-to-end EMEA PO process, including vendor onboarding, approvals, and tracking against delivery milestones
* Monitor the EMEA AP queue end-to-end and serve as the primary contact for vendor invoice inquiries through month-end close
* Coordinate and reconcile multi-currency vendor payments (EUR, GBP, SEK) with global treasury and support VAT documentation
* Own EMEA shipping operations and accounts (DHL, FedEx), including import/export taxation and cross-office logistics
* Provide administrative support to the EMEA teams and support office operations for the Germany office

## We’re looking for someone who has:

* Bachelor's degree in Business, Finance, Accounting, or equivalent experience
* 2+ years in finance operations, AP, or administrative roles, ideally at a multinational or tech company
* **Fluency in English and German**
* Hands-on mindset, comfortable operating from strategy to day-to-day execution
* Strong AI affinity and drive to automate and continuously improve processes
* Experience managing procurement, AP, and vendor relationships across multiple countries
* Highly organized, detail-oriented, and strong in Excel/Google Sheets
* Familiarity with multi-currency payments and VAT requirements across EMEA
* Proactive, self-directed, and comfortable in a fast-paced startup environment

## Nice to have:

* Experience with NetSuite or a comparable ERP
* Additional European languages (French, Swedish or other)
* Experience with international shipping operations and import taxation
* Familiarity with withholding tax documentation across EMEA jurisdictions
