## About Applied Intuition

Applied Intuition, Inc. is powering the future of physical AI. Founded in 2017 and now valued at $15 billion, the Silicon Valley company is creating the digital infrastructure needed to bring intelligence to every moving machine on the planet. Applied Intuition services the automotive, defense, trucking, construction, mining and agriculture industries in three core areas: tools and infrastructure, operating systems, and autonomy. Eighteen of the top 20 global automakers, as well as the United States military and its allies, trust the company’s solutions to deliver physical intelligence. Applied Intuition is headquartered in Sunnyvale, California, with offices in Washington, D.C.; San Diego; Ft. Walton Beach, Florida; Ann Arbor, Michigan; London; Stuttgart; Munich; Stockholm; Bangalore; Seoul; and Tokyo. Learn more at [applied.co](http://applied.co/).

*We are an in-office company, and our expectation is that employees primarily work from their Applied Intuition office 5 days a week. However, we also recognize the importance of flexibility and trust our employees to manage their schedules responsibly. This may include occasional remote work, starting the day with morning meetings from home before heading to the office, or leaving earlier when needed to accommodate family commitments.*

## About the role

We are seeking a detail-oriented and ambitious People Operations Generalist to join our team! This role is ideal for builders who thrive in a high-growth, fast-paced environment and want to play a key role in shaping the foundation of our operations. As a People Operations Generalist, you will optimize HR systems, implement scalable and efficient processes, and ensure data accuracy to support strategic decision-making.

## At Applied Intuition, you will:

* Lead the development, enhancement, and management of People data and reporting, making sure we have accurate and actionable insights for decision-making
* Optimize People systems and workflows across Rippling, Carta, Greenhouse, JIRA, PAVE, and more
* Own and manage our rotation and mobility program, shaping strategy, policies, and process improvements while addressing employee inquiries
* Ensure compliance with federal, state, and local employment laws
* Point of contact for employee questions and matters related to rotations, benefits, leaves of absence, and timeoff
* Generate comprehensive reporting and dashboards to support data-driven decision-making
* Other People or general operations responsibilities as required by the People team on an ad-hoc basis

## We're looking for someone who has:

* 3+ years of practical experience experience in a People or HR-related role
* Detail-oriented and data-driven mindset
* Intuition and ability to identify potential areas of improvement in our operational cadence
* Proactivity in problem-solving - you don’t just spot gaps; you fill them

## Nice to have:

* Bachelor's degree
* Experience in high-growth technology companies, particularly in automotive or autonomous vehicle industries
* Direct involvement in scaling startups from early-stage to larger organizational structures.
* Experience with HRIS systems, people analytics tools, and large datasets
