### **ABOUT THE ROLE:**

We are seeking a highly skilled Mechanical Commissioning Technician to support the buildout and startup of SpaceXAI's high-performance data centers. In this critical hands-on role, you will perform commissioning, testing, and troubleshooting of mechanical systems that provide cooling, air handling, and fluid distribution for our next-generation AI supercomputing infrastructure.

You will work closely with construction, engineering, and operations teams to ensure safe, reliable, and high-quality mechanical systems from initial installation through full system turnover. This is a full-time, onsite role with significant travel to SpaceXAI data center sites.

### **RESPONSIBILITIES:**

* Execute mechanical commissioning activities for HVAC systems, chillers, cooling towers, CRAC/CRAH units, pumps, heat exchangers, piping systems, and associated controls in data center environments.
* Perform functional testing, airflow and water flow verification, system balancing, and integrated system performance validation.
* Troubleshoot and resolve mechanical, controls, and hydronic issues during pre-commissioning, commissioning, and startup phases.
* Develop and execute detailed commissioning test procedures, checklists, and documentation in accordance with project specifications and industry standards.
* Collaborate with EPC contractors, OEM vendors, and SpaceXAI engineers to coordinate system energization, startup sequences, and integrated system testing.
* Ensure strict adherence to safety protocols, LOTO procedures, and quality standards during all commissioning activities.
* Maintain accurate as-built documentation, test records, and commissioning reports.
* Support training of operations and maintenance teams on commissioned mechanical systems.
* Participate in constructability reviews and provide field feedback to improve design and installation quality.

### **BASIC QUALIFICATIONS:**

* 3+ years of hands-on mechanical commissioning or field technician experience in data centers, power plants, or large industrial facilities.
* Strong working knowledge of data center cooling systems including chillers, cooling towers, pumps, CRAC/CRAH units, and precision air handling.
* Experience with commissioning test equipment (manometers, anemometers, flow meters, temperature/pressure calibrators, etc.).
* Familiarity with relevant codes and standards (ASHRAE, SMACNA, NFPA, ASME, IMC).
* Demonstrated ability to read and interpret mechanical drawings, P&IDs, and control schematics.
* Strong safety mindset with current OSHA 10/30 or equivalent training.
* Willingness to work extended hours, nights, and weekends during critical commissioning windows.

### **PREFERRED SKILLS AND EXPERIENCE:**

* Prior commissioning experience on hyperscale data center projects or mission-critical facilities.
* NEBB, AABC, or equivalent certification in testing, adjusting, and balancing (TAB).
* Experience with BMS/BAS controls troubleshooting and integration (BACnet, Modbus, etc.).
* Knowledge of chilled water systems, refrigerant systems, and high-density liquid cooling technologies.
* Proficiency with commissioning management software (e.g., CxPro, Procore, or similar).
* Technical degree or trade school certification in Mechanical Engineering Technology, HVAC, or related field.

### **ADDITIONAL REQUIREMENTS:**

* Ability to travel extensively to SpaceXAI construction sites and remain onsite during peak commissioning phases.
* Capability to work in industrial environments, climb ladders, lift moderate loads, and wear PPE for extended periods.
