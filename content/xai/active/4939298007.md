### ABOUT THE ROLE:

As a Facilities Maintenance Assistant at SpaceXAI Memphis, you will support the upkeep of our supercomputing data center facilities by assisting with routine non-critical maintenance tasks. Under close supervision, you'll help with basic operations and limited maintenance on critical equipment, contributing to a reliable and efficient environment that powers our AI-driven mission. This entry-level role emphasizes hands-on learning, teamwork, and attention to detail in a fast-paced setting.

### RESPONSIBILITIES:

* Assist in performing non-critical maintenance and repair work, including people door repairs, administrative area upkeep, building envelope maintenance, and/or landscaping
* Under direct supervision of an operator, assist as required in the operation of critical equipment.
* Assist in the performance of limited maintenance on offline critical equipment as directed, including: single chiller maintenance, single CDU filter changeouts, and/or leak detection verification in alarming racks
* Support daily inspections of facilities areas to identify and report potential issues.
* Help maintain spare parts inventory and assist with basic organization of tools and equipment.
* Follow standard operating procedures (SOPs) and safety protocols during all tasks.
* Collaborate with team members to ensure timely completion of maintenance activities.
* Provide support during scheduled system maintenance or minor repairs.
* Assist in documenting work performed and any observed issues.
* Participate in team training sessions and contribute to a collaborative work environment.

### BASIC QUALIFICATIONS:

* High school diploma or equivalent (relevant experience or technical certification preferred).
* 1+ years of experience in maintenance, facilities, or a related hands-on role (data center experience is a plus).
* Basic knowledge of mechanical, electrical, or plumbing systems.
* Familiarity with hand tools and power tools.
* Strong attention to detail and ability to follow instructions closely.
* Good communication skills and a team-oriented mindset.
* Ability to prioritize tasks in a dynamic environment.

### PREFERRED SKILLS AND EXPERIENCE:

* Experience in a data center or industrial facility setting.
* Basic understanding of HVAC or electrical systems.
* OSHA safety certification or equivalent.
