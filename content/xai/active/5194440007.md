### **ABOUT THE ROLE:**

We serve our community of users and customers by working tirelessly to preserve free expression and choice, create limitless interactivity, and maintain a safe digital marketplace. The Content Moderation Quality Lead will oversee the end-to-end Quality Assurance program for content moderation workflows, ensuring policy compliance, user safety, and operational excellence across global footprint operations.

You are an experienced quality management professional with a deep understanding of Trust and Safety ecosystems. You are skilled at leading teams, defining sampling methodologies, and leveraging cutting-edge automation to improve accuracy. You thrive in fast-paced environments and excel at managing cross-functional relationships between internal stakeholders and external vendor partners.

### RESPONSIBILITIES:

* Develop and maintain a comprehensive Quality program to ensure a consistent, safe user experience.
* Leverage AI-driven tools for quality monitoring, content moderation performance analysis, and predictive error detection.
* Oversee the content moderation quality frameworks across both captive and third-party vendor operations.
* Manage a high-performing team of Quality Analysts, including full-time employees and contractors.
* Collaborate with Operations Managers, Site Leads, and Vendor Operations to drive continuous quality improvements.
* Define precise audit sampling methodologies and construct robust quality processes for policy workflows.
* Design and implement scalable, standardized quality assessment processes globally.
* Lead process excellence frameworks to maximize community safety and operational accuracy.
* Reduce workflow complexity through process automation, standard operating procedures, and strategic mapping.
* Review and minimize policy escalations resulting from moderation errors across delivery sites.
* Recommend continuous enhancements to internal moderation tools, safety policies, procedures, and training programs.
* Drive targeted operational improvements through thorough root cause analysis.
* Build and sustain a data-driven Quality Program enabled by AI analytics to enhance policy accuracy and operational efficiency.
* Design specific quality assessment and feedback mechanisms tailored to high-volume content moderation operations.
* Create and manage regular quality audits, checklists, and comprehensive summary reports detailing compliance and edge-case exceptions.
* Maintain rigorous QA documentation, including action item logs and policy calibration trackers.
* Drive individual moderator audits, monitor process compliance, and execute targeted performance tracking for bottom-quartile agents.
* Conduct specialized root cause analysis studies on performance trends, presenting courses of action to stakeholders for policy calibration.
* Report routinely on quality program activities and leverage analytics to improve overall operational effectiveness.

### BASIC QUALIFICATIONS:

* Bachelor’s degree in a relevant field or equivalent practical experience.
* 4–5 years of quality management experience specifically within captive and vendor content moderation or trust and safety operations.
* Proven experience managing and leading teams of Quality Analysts.
* Deep expertise in designing, scaling, and running global alignment and calibration processes.
* Strong data analytics capabilities, including sampling methodology, coaching frameworks, and performance insights.
* Excellent project and program management skills with a track record of cross-functional collaboration.
* Skilled in executive-level communication and influencing operational stakeholders across multiple sites.
* Experience leveraging AI tools and automated platforms to enhance quality management workflows.

### PREFERRED SKILLS AND EXPERIENCE:

* Demonstrated ability to collect, analyze, and visualize complex trend data to inform strategic policy decisions.
* Proficiency with business intelligence and dashboard tools such as SQL, Looker, Tableau, or equivalent data visualization platforms.
* Familiarity with AI-powered moderation analytics, automated scoring, and operational optimization platforms.
