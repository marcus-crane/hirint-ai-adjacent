**ABOUT THE ROLE**

We are hiring skilled Electricians to join SpaceXAI’s internal construction and electrical teams supporting the rapid build-out of our data centers. This is a traveling role—you will mobilize with project teams to active construction sites, install and commission mission-critical electrical systems, and move with the work as new facilities come online.

You will work on high-voltage and medium-voltage power distribution, backup power systems, grounding, lighting, and process power in a fast-paced, high-stakes environment where uptime and safety are paramount. This hands-on position is ideal for experienced electricians who thrive in industrial or data-center settings, enjoy project-based travel, and want to contribute directly to groundbreaking AI infrastructure.

**RESPONSIBILITIES**

* Travel with internal construction and electrical teams to project sites to install, terminate, and test electrical systems including underground duct banks, conduits, cable pulling, switchgear, transformers, generators, UPS systems, PDUs, busways, and grounding.
* Perform maintenance, troubleshooting, and repairs on low- and medium-voltage power distribution systems across multiple active sites.
* Support commissioning activities: point-to-point checks, functional testing, megger testing, and system energization.
* Read and interpret electrical drawings, schematics, one-line diagrams, and specifications.
* Ensure full compliance with NEC, NFPA 70E, OSHA, and SpaceXAI safety & quality standards (including LOTO and arc flash protocols).
* Collaborate with contractors, engineers, and operations teams to deliver projects on time with zero incidents.
* Participate in constructibility reviews and provide field feedback to improve designs.
* Maintain accurate documentation, as-builts, and test records.
* Mobilize quickly between project locations as construction phases and priorities shift.

**BASIC QUALIFICATIONS**

* 2+ years of hands-on experience in industrial, commercial, or mission-critical electrical work (data centers, power plants, or large facilities preferred).
* Strong knowledge of electrical systems up to 480V (medium/high voltage experience a big plus).
* Proficiency with test equipment (multimeters, meggers, power quality analyzers, etc.).
* Ability to read blueprints and electrical diagrams.
* Current OSHA 10/30 training and strong safety mindset.
* Willingness and ability to travel extensively (often 80–100%) and work flexible hours, including nights, weekends, and extended shifts during critical builds or commissioning.
* Ability to lift 50+ lbs, climb ladders, and work in industrial environments with PPE.

**PREFERRED SKILLS AND EXPERIENCE**

* Journeyman Electrician license (or equivalent).
* Experience with data center or hyperscale power infrastructure (redundant UPS, generators, busway systems).
* Prior work on large-scale, multi-site construction projects that required frequent travel.
* NETA certification, NICET, or equivalent.
* Knowledge of PLC/controls, SCADA, or variable frequency drives.
