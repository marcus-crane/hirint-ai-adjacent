### **ABOUT xAI**

xAI’s mission is to create AI systems that can accurately understand the universe and aid humanity in its pursuit of knowledge. Our team is small, highly motivated, and focused on engineering excellence. This organization is for individuals who appreciate challenging themselves and thrive on curiosity. We operate with a flat organizational structure. All employees are expected to be hands-on and to contribute directly to the company’s mission. Leadership is given to those who show initiative and consistently deliver excellence. Work ethic and strong prioritization skills are important. All employees are expected to have strong communication skills. They should be able to concisely and accurately share knowledge with their teammates.

### **ABOUT THE ROLE:**

As a Project Manager for Power Generation at xAI, you will own the planning, execution, and delivery of large-scale power generation projects that support xAI’s rapidly expanding AI infrastructure. You will lead the development of power plants, substations, generation assets, and grid interconnection projects to ensure reliable, high-capacity power supply for our next-generation data centers. Working cross-functionally with engineering, construction, procurement, and energy teams, you will drive projects from concept through commissioning in a fast-paced, high-stakes environment.

### RESPONSIBILITIES:

* Lead full lifecycle project management for power generation initiatives, including gas turbines, reciprocating engines, renewables integration, and substation projects
* Develop and maintain detailed project schedules, budgets, and resource plans
* Manage project risks, issues, and change orders while ensuring on-time and on-budget delivery
* Coordinate with utility companies, regulatory agencies, and internal stakeholders for permitting and grid interconnection
* Track project progress, KPIs, and financial performance with clear reporting to leadership
* Collaborate closely with engineering teams on technical specifications and design reviews
* Drive procurement strategy and vendor/contractor management
* Ensure compliance with safety, quality, and environmental standards

### **BASIC QUALIFICATIONS:**

* 5+ years of project management experience in power generation, energy infrastructure, or large industrial projects
* Proven track record delivering complex, multi-million dollar energy projects on schedule and within budget
* Strong working knowledge of power generation technologies (gas, diesel, renewables) and electrical infrastructure
* Experience with project management tools (Primavera P6, MS Project, or equivalent)

### PREFERRED SKILLS AND EXPERIENCE:

* Background in data center power systems or hyperscale energy projects
* Professional certifications (PMP, PE, or equivalent)
* Experience with utility-scale generation, battery energy storage systems (BESS), or high-voltage substations
* Familiarity with regulatory environments, permitting processes, and grid compliance
* Bachelor’s degree in Engineering, Construction Management, or related technical field

### **ADDITIONAL REQUIREMENTS:**

* Ability to work onsite in Memphis, TN / Southaven, MS area
* Strong leadership and communication skills with the ability to influence across teams
* Comfortable operating in a dynamic, rapidly scaling organization with aggressive timelines
