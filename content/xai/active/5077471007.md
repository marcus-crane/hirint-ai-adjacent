### ABOUT THE ROLE:

We’re looking for a detail-oriented and proactive recruiting coordinator to support our fast-paced JAPAC Talent Acquisition team. This is a contract role ideal for someone who thrives in a dynamic environment and enjoys coordinating processes, managing systems, and creating a great candidate experience.

### RESPONSIBILITIES:

* Schedule interviews and coordinate logistics across multiple time zones
* Maintain accurate data in our Applicant Tracking System (ATS)
* Communicate with candidates throughout the interview process to ensure a smooth and positive experience
* Partner with recruiters and hiring managers to support end-to-end recruitment processes

### BASIC QUALIFICATIONS:

* 1–3 years of experience in a coordination, recruiting, or people ops/HR support role
* Strong organizational and time management skills; ability to manage multiple tasks with high attention to detail
* Excellent written and verbal communication skills in Japanese and English will be a plus
* Comfortable working with different time zones and managing scheduling complexity
* Familiarity with ATS (Greenhouse or Workday) and Google Suite products is a plus
