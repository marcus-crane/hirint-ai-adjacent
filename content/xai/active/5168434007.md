### **ABOUT xAI**

xAI’s mission is to create AI systems that can accurately understand the universe and aid humanity in its pursuit of knowledge. Our team is small, highly motivated, and focused on engineering excellence. This organization is for individuals who appreciate challenging themselves and thrive on curiosity. We operate with a flat organizational structure. All employees are expected to be hands-on and to contribute directly to the company’s mission. Leadership is given to those who show initiative and consistently deliver excellence. Work ethic and strong prioritization skills are important. All employees are expected to have strong communication skills. They should be able to concisely and accurately share knowledge with their teammates.

### **ABOUT THE ROLE:**

As an Associate Data Center Operations Technician at xAI, you will support the health and reliability of our server and network infrastructure for Data Centers and Global Points of Presence. You will help drive our two most important data center operations metrics: mean time to detect (MTTD) and mean time to repair (MTTR). You are great at following directions, a fast learner, super attentive to details, and eager to grow your skills in a fast-paced environment. You are accessible via cell phone and willing to get onsite when there are emergencies as needed. You will work closely with senior technicians and engineers while learning all aspects of the equipment to help improve our server intake and repair processes.

### **RESPONSIBILITIES:**

* Assist with troubleshooting and monitoring of servers and network equipment in our data centers and global points of presence.
* Support rack and stack of data center network equipment.
* Help maintain warehouse inventory and asset management using our internal application.
* Assist with labeling and troubleshooting of fiber/optics cables.
* Support power supply cabling, installation, troubleshooting, and basic repair activities.
* Participate in the installation of racks, servers, and switches (staging, cabling, power-up, and handoff to the provisioning team).
* Help manage, respond to, and resolve data center operations tickets via Jira.
* Create and maintain documentation of tasks and standard operating procedures.
* Assist with receipt and decommissioning of data center hardware.
* Support vendor returns for infrastructure under and out of warranty.
* Help manage spare parts inventory within the data center.
* Support the definition, design, and implementation of network layouts and solutions within our data centers.

* Position is subject to pre-employment drug and random drug and alcohol testing
