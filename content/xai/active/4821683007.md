### ABOUT THE ROLE:

As the Facilities Operations Supervisor at xAI Memphis, you will oversee the maintenance and performance of critical data center infrastructure, including power, cooling, and environmental systems. You will lead a team of Facilities Operations Technicians, driving hands-on technical operations, team leadership, process optimization, and cross-functional collaboration to ensure the reliability and efficiency of our data center. This role emphasizes fostering a team-first culture, prioritizing team focus, and promoting a collaborative work environment.

### RESPONSIBILITIES:

* Lead and mentor a team of Facilities Operations Technicians, fostering a culture of excellence, collaboration, and continuous improvement.
* Oversee maintenance, monitoring, and troubleshooting of data center infrastructure, including HVAC, power distribution, and backup systems.
* Develop, implement, and optimize preventive maintenance schedules, equipment calibration, and system performance monitoring.
* Create and enforce standard operating procedures (SOPs) to ensure operational consistency and efficiency.
* Ensure compliance with safety regulations and industry standards.
* Collaborate with engineering and procurement teams to streamline equipment intake, installation, and maintenance processes.
* Utilize internal applications for inventory and asset management, ensuring accurate tracking and reporting.
* Manage critical equipment maintenance and repairs, ensuring timely resolution and detailed documentation.
* Partner with cross-functional teams to support seamless data center operations.
* Lead initiatives to enhance operational efficiency and minimize downtime.
* Provide on-call support and respond promptly to critical facilities incidents.

### BASIC QUALIFICATIONS:

* Bachelor’s degree in Mechanical, Electrical, or a related engineering field (relevant experience may substitute for degree).
* 4+ years of experience managing critical facilities systems, including power, cooling, and environmental controls.
* In-depth knowledge of electrical, mechanical, and HVAC systems in data centers.
* 4+ years of experience troubleshooting and maintaining critical facilities infrastructure.
* Proficiency with building management systems (BMS) and environmental monitoring tools.
* Proven leadership experience in critical facilities or technical operations environments.
* Demonstrated ability to lead facilities infrastructure projects.
* Proficiency with hand tools and power tools.
* Strong prioritization and time management skills.
* Ability to thrive in a fast-paced environment while maintaining attention to detail.
* Must pass pre-employment and annual post-employment background checks.
* Physical Requirements:
  + Ability to lift up to 35 lbs. unassisted.
  + Comfortable working at heights up to 50 feet with appropriate safety gear.
  + Ability to work in environments with exposure to noise.
  + Physical dexterity to perform job functions in confined spaces.
* Availability to work evenings and weekends based on site operational needs; flexibility is required.
* Valid driver’s license.

### PREFERRED SKILLS AND EXPERIENCE:

* Experience programming PLC-based control systems or working with Building Management Systems.
* Project Management Professional (PMP) certification or equivalent.
* EPA Universal Refrigerant Certification (EPA 608 Technician).
