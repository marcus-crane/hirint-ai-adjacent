Scale AI is looking for an Internal Communications Senior Manager to join our Communications team. Reporting to the Internal Communications Lead, you will be a key driver in building and scaling a world-class internal comms function that connects every employee to Scale's mission, leadership vision, and business milestones. This is a high-impact, cross-functional role that will shape how nearly every team at Scale understands and rallies around company goals.

**You will:**

* Own and expand internal communications across all business units, building structured programs that help leaders engage and align their teams
* Identify gaps in the current internal comms landscape and design net-new programs, from concept through execution, that meet the evolving needs of a fast-growing, cross-functional organization.
* Partner closely with business unit leaders and chiefs of staff to develop and execute their communications strategies, including AMAs, leadership updates, and mission-critical announcements
* Drive the strategy and execution of company wide channels including all-hands meetings, AMAs, internal newsletters, and Slack channels with the goal of ensuring employees feel informed and connected to Scale's direction
* Develop a cohesive internal communications framework that ladders up to CEO and executive messaging, helping employees understand business performance, company milestones, and strategic priorities
* Act as a strategic advisor to senior leaders on how to communicate with and inspire their teams, improving leader visibility and employee engagement across the organization
* Build and maintain measurement frameworks (e.g., pulse surveys, engagement metrics) to assess program impact and continuously improve internal comms effectiveness
* Collaborate with the broader Communications team on crisis, policy, and corporate storytelling as needed, ensuring internal and external narratives are aligned

**Ideally you'd have:**

* 6+ years of experience in internal communications, with a proven track record of building and scaling internal comms programs at a fast-growing technology company
* Demonstrated experience advising and partnering with senior leaders and executives on communications strategy, with the ability to influence without authority
* Strong program management skills and the ability to build repeatable, scalable processes across a large, cross-functional organization
* Exceptional written and verbal communication skills, with the ability to craft compelling narratives that resonate with diverse internal audiences

**Nice to haves:**

* Experience working in a high-growth technology company
* Familiarity with internal comms tools, platforms (e.g., Slack, intranet systems, survey tools) programming, and data-driven approaches to measuring communication effectiveness
* Experience supporting CEO or executive-level communications in a fast-paced environment
