Tenstorrent is leading the industry on cutting-edge AI technology, revolutionizing performance expectations, ease of use, and cost efficiency. With AI redefining the computing paradigm, solutions must evolve to unify innovations in software models, compilers, platforms, networking, and semiconductors. Our diverse team of technologists have developed a high performance RISC-V CPU from scratch, and share a passion for AI and a deep desire to build the best AI platform possible. We value collaboration, curiosity, and a commitment to solving hard problems. We are growing our team and looking for contributors of all seniorities.

This is a 12 month contract position, with the possibility of a contract extension or conversion to a full time role based on performance and business needs.

As a Recruiting Coordinator at Tenstorrent, you'll focus on making the candidate experience seamless and positive. This role is all about managing interview logistics, keeping communication clear, and supporting the recruiting team so everything runs smoothly and efficiently.

This role is remote, based out of North America.

We welcome candidates at various experience levels for this role. During the interview process, candidates will be assessed for the appropriate level, and offers will align with that level, which may differ from the one in this posting.

#### 

#### **Who You Are**

* A highly organized multitasker who finds satisfaction in keeping things running smoothly.
* A clear, thoughtful communicator who keeps candidates and teammates informed and supported.
* Comfortable navigating G-Suite and applicant tracking systems like Greenhouse or Lever—or eager to learn quickly.
* Team-first, proactive, and energized by working in a fast-paced, collaborative environment.

#### **What We Need**

* Coordinate and schedule interviews—both virtual and in-person—with precision and attention to detail.
* Keep communication with candidates and hiring managers dialed in and timely.
* Maintain clean, organized candidate data in our applicant tracking system.
* Support team documentation, templates, and general admin tasks to keep everything running smoothly.

#### **What You Will Learn**

* What it takes to create a standout candidate experience from start to finish.
* How recruiting operations fuel a growing tech company—and how to scale them.
* Hands-on experience with tools and systems used by top recruiting teams.
* Ways to collaborate with recruiters, hiring managers, and interviewers to move fast without missing a beat.

*Compensation for all engineers at Tenstorrent ranges from $100k - $500k including base and variable compensation targets. Experience, skills, education, background and location all impact the actual offer made.*
