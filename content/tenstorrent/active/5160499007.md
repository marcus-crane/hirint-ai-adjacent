Tenstorrent is leading the industry on cutting-edge AI technology, revolutionizing performance expectations, ease of use, and cost efficiency. With AI redefining the computing paradigm, solutions must evolve to unify innovations in software models, compilers, platforms, networking, and semiconductors. Our diverse team of technologists have developed a high performance RISC-V CPU from scratch, and share a passion for AI and a deep desire to build the best AI platform possible. We value collaboration, curiosity, and a commitment to solving hard problems. We are growing our team and looking for contributors of all seniorities.

Tenstorrent is building a world-class technical organization at the intersection of hardware and software. As an Executive Assistant, you will operate as a strategic partner to senior leadership, ensuring time, focus, and execution are aligned with the most important priorities across the organization.

This role is hybrid, based out of Santa Clara, CA.

We welcome candidates at various experience levels for this role. During the interview process, candidates will be assessed for the appropriate level, and offers will align with that level, which may differ from the one in this posting.

**Who You Are**

* Proven Executive Assistant supporting senior leaders in fast-paced, technical or engineering-driven environments.
* Operates as a strategic partner, not just an administrator, with strong ownership and initiative.
* Highly organized with exceptional time management and ability to prioritize across competing demands.
* Strong communicator with sound judgment, professionalism, and discretion in handling sensitive information.
* Curious about AI and comfortable navigating ambiguity while adapting quickly as priorities shift.

**What We Need**

* Own and optimize a complex executive calendar, aligning time with organizational priorities and protecting focus while moving fast, anticipating needs, and executing with minimal direction.
* Serve as a central point of contact for internal and external stakeholders, managing communication and information flow.
* Coordinate travel, onsite visits, and events to maximize efficiency and strategic impact.
* Support operational and cross-functional initiatives including hiring, planning, and special projects.

**What You Will Learn**

* How senior leaders operate and prioritize in a high-growth, deeply technical organization.
* How to drive alignment and execution across multiple teams through effective coordination and communication.
* How to manage complex workflows and competing priorities at scale.
* How strategic decisions are translated into day-to-day execution.
* How to operate as a high-leverage partner within a fast-moving organization.
