Tenstorrent is leading the industry on cutting-edge AI technology, revolutionizing performance expectations, ease of use, and cost efficiency. With AI redefining the computing paradigm, solutions must evolve to unify innovations in software models, compilers, platforms, networking, and semiconductors. Our diverse team of technologists have developed a high performance RISC-V CPU from scratch, and share a passion for AI and a deep desire to build the best AI platform possible. We value collaboration, curiosity, and a commitment to solving hard problems. We are growing our team and looking for contributors of all seniorities.

As Manager, Finance COE, this role will build and lead Tenstorrent’s Bangalore finance hub from the ground up while serving as India Country Controller. Starting with entity accounting and close operations, this role will expand over time to include management reporting, data quality, and AI-driven finance automation—creating a scalable finance engine for a fast-growing global company.

This role ishybrid, based out of Bengaluru, India.

We welcome candidates at various experience levels for this role. During the interview process, candidates will be assessed for the appropriate level, and offers will align with that level, which may differ from the one in this posting.

**Who You Are**

* Experienced finance leader with a builder’s mindset and a track record of standing up teams, processes, and controls from scratch.
* Strong people manager who develops talent, sets a high bar, and creates a culture of accountability and continuous learning.
* Comfortable navigating complexity across global entities, multiple accounting standards, and evolving business needs.
* Curious about technology and excited to apply AI and automation to modernize core finance operations.

**What We Need**

* Ownership of entity accounting, close processes, statutory compliance, and audit support for global subsidiaries.
* Leadership of a growing Bangalore-based team, including hiring, coaching, and performance management.
* Development of scalable SOPs, controls, and data quality frameworks that ensure accuracy and audit readiness.
* Expansion of the COE to support management reporting, AI agent oversight, and finance automation initiatives.

**What You Will Learn**

* How to build and scale a world-class Finance Center of Excellence in a high-growth AI and semiconductor company.
* Deep exposure to global accounting, controllership, and cross-border finance operations.
* Hands-on experience integrating AI agents and automation into core finance workflows.
* Opportunities to shape the future of finance operations as the company prepares for its next stage of growth.
