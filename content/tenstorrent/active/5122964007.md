Tenstorrent is leading the industry on cutting-edge AI technology, revolutionizing performance expectations, ease of use, and cost efficiency. With AI redefining the computing paradigm, solutions must evolve to unify innovations in software models, compilers, platforms, networking, and semiconductors. Our diverse team of technologists have developed a high performance RISC-V CPU from scratch, and share a passion for AI and a deep desire to build the best AI platform possible. We value collaboration, curiosity, and a commitment to solving hard problems. We are growing our team and looking for contributors of all seniorities.

Tenstorrent is building next-generation AI hardware and software. This role focuses on the user-mode driver and interface layer that enables communication, resource management, and performance across our devices. You will play a key role in defining how our software stack interacts with both internal and third-party hardware.

This role ishybrid, based out of Belgrade, Serbia.

We welcome candidates at various experience levels for this role. During the interview process, candidates will be assessed for the appropriate level, and offers will align with that level, which may differ from the one in this posting.

**Who You Are**

* Deep systems engineer with 7+ years experience in user-mode drivers or low-level systems programming.
* Strong proficiency in C and C++ with solid understanding of hardware-software interfaces.
* Familiar with Linux kernel concepts and how kernel and user-space components interact.
* Experience working with external customers or partners to define and support driver or hardware interfaces.
* Exposure to safety-critical systems, automotive platforms, or Functional Safety standards such as ISO 26262 is a strong plus.

**What We Need**

* Design and evolve the user-mode driver layer that provides high-performance APIs for Tenstorrent devices.
* Define driver interfaces and specifications that enable the broader software stack to run across internal and third-party hardware.
* Collaborate with kernel and hardware teams to ensure efficient cross-layer design and performance.
* Drive performance engineering efforts, including benchmarking, profiling, and optimization of APIs.
* Support external integrations by defining hardware-software boundaries and enabling reliable deployment in customer environments, including safety-critical use cases where applicable.

**What You Will Learn**

* How user-mode and kernel-level drivers are designed for high-performance AI hardware.
* How hardware-software interfaces are defined to support scalable and portable systems.
* How performance is optimized across memory, compute, and communication layers.
* How third-party hardware integrations are enabled through well-defined driver and interface layers.
* How safety and reliability requirements influence modern driver architecture, including in automotive and mission-critical systems.
