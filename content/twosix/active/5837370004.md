**Overview of Opportunity**

**Two Six Technologies** is actively seeking a **Lead Software Reverse Engineer** to join our **Trusted Electronics & Effects** team. The team comprises highly skilled individuals passionate about low-level programming, firmware analysis, and vulnerability research. The team is growing and looking for someone with a strong C/C++ and assembly background who understands how to reverse-engineer hardware and software across various architectures to detect zero-day vulnerabilities.

**What you will do**

* Lead the planning and execution of technical research tasks, providing direction to team members on task prioritization and schedule management to meet mission-critical objectives.
* Utilize exemplar techniques to reverse engineer wireless and embedded systems, identifying deep-seated vulnerabilities and exercising sound judgment to assess their security impact.
* Develop organized and maintainable proof-of-concept exploits while breaking down complex deliverables into manageable technical milestones for the research team.
* Operate with significant autonomy to analyze firmware, software protections, and wireless protocols, often exploring new principles where limited references exist.
* Serve as a technical lead in a fast-paced environment, collaborating with CNO developers and hardware engineers.

**What you will need (basic qualifications)**

* Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Computer/Electrical Engineering, or a related field with typically 7+ years of experience in a similar technical role.
* Extensive experience with programming and scripting languages including C/C++, Python, and Assembly within Linux command-line environments.
* Proven expertise in reverse engineering and vulnerability research using industry-standard tools such as IDA Pro, Binary Ninja, or Ghidra.
* Demonstrated experience leading a team of engineers or researchers, including the ability to provide technical direction and manage project schedules.
* Technical proficiency in firmware analysis (ARM, MIPS, PowerPC, RTOS), firmware rehosting (QEMU), or advanced exploit development.
* Experience reverse engineering network communication protocols using Wireshark, tcpdump, or other hardware-level tools.

**Nice to have (preferred)**

* Experience scripting with the Binary Ninja API or Ghidra to automate complex reverse engineering workflows.
* Hands-on experience with hardware debugging tools such as JTAG/SWD or software-defined radio (SDR) frameworks.
* Knowledge of cryptographic security, secure boot mechanisms, or side-channel analysis.
* Familiarity with digital signal processing, RF protocols, and wireless security standards.
* Knowledge of wireless communication protocols, such as Bluetooth, WiFi, 2G/3G/4G/5G, or similar.
* Active TS/SCI with Polygraph.

**Security Clearance**

* Active Top Secret clearance required with eligibility/willingness to obtain a TS/SCI.
