# **About the Team**

The Autonomy & Safety Engineering team is responsible for ensuring 1X robots operate safely, reliably, and predictably in real-world human environments. We work at the intersection of robotics, autonomy, controls, perception, and systems safety to develop the frameworks, tooling, and validation methodologies that enable safe deployment of humanoid robots at scale.

This team plays a critical role in defining how embodied AI systems safely interact with people, homes, and dynamic real-world environments.

# **Your Charter**

Lead the development and implementation of autonomy safety systems, risk mitigation strategies, and robot safety validation frameworks for 1X humanoid robots. This role is critical to ensuring our robots can safely operate alongside humans while maintaining reliability, scalability, and compliance across increasingly complex autonomous behaviors and real-world deployments.

# **Key Outcomes**

* Design and implement scalable robot safety architectures and autonomy validation systems across humanoid robotics platforms
* Establish safety evaluation methodologies, simulation frameworks, and operational risk mitigation strategies for autonomous robot behaviors
* Partner cross-functionally with Autonomy, Controls, Perception, Hardware, and Systems teams to define and enforce safety requirements across the robotics stack
* Improve operational reliability and incident prevention through safety analysis, testing frameworks, and continuous validation processes
* Develop scalable processes and tooling for identifying, tracking, and mitigating autonomy-related risks during development and deployment

# **Key Competencies**
