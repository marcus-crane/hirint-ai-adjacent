# **About the Team**

The Hands team is responsible for developing one of the most complex and important subsystems in humanoid robotics: dexterous manipulation. We design, prototype, test, and iterate rapidly to create robotic hands capable of interacting with the world safely, reliably, and with human-like precision.

Working across mechanical, electrical, firmware, controls, and software disciplines, the team moves quickly from concept to hardware, relying on rapid prototyping and hands-on experimentation to accelerate development.

# **Your Charter**

Build, debug, and validate the prototypes that enable the Hands team to move faster. You will support the development of robotic hand systems by fabricating mechanical hardware, bringing up electronics, testing integrated assemblies, and helping engineers rapidly iterate on new designs.

As a Hardware Technician, you'll play a critical role in turning engineering concepts into functional prototypes, helping bridge the gap between design and real-world performance.

# **Key Outcomes**

* Accelerate development cycles by delivering high-quality prototypes, assemblies, and hardware modifications on aggressive timelines
* Support successful PCB and electromechanical bring-up through effective debugging, testing, and validation activities
* Improve prototype quality and reliability by identifying issues early and providing actionable feedback to engineering teams
* Build and maintain documentation that enables repeatable assembly, testing, and future design iterations
* Support cross-functional teams by ensuring mechanical, electrical, and firmware systems integrate smoothly into robotic hand platforms

# **Key Competencies**

* Strong hands-on fabrication and assembly skills across mechanical and electrical systems
* Excellent troubleshooting abilities with the confidence to diagnose issues across hardware and integrated systems
* Ability to move quickly while maintaining high standards for quality and attention to detail
* Strong collaboration skills and comfort working closely with engineers across multiple disciplines
* Ownership mindset with a willingness to take initiative and solve problems independently

# **Minimum Requirements**

* 5+ years of experience supporting hardware development in robotics, automotive, consumer electronics, or related industries
* Experience using electrical test equipment including oscilloscopes, power supplies, multimeters, and data acquisition tools
* Proficiency with CAD software such as Onshape, SolidWorks, Fusion 360, or equivalent
* Strong hands-on experience with 3D printing, CNC machining, laser cutting, manual machining, and general fabrication tools
* Experience documenting assembly processes, test procedures, and development activities

# **Preferred Skills**

* Experience working with robotic systems, actuators, or mechatronic assemblies
* Experience performing PCB bring-up, firmware flashing, component rework, and hardware validation
* Experience designing and building custom test fixtures or validation rigs
* Familiarity with data acquisition systems, automated testing, and scripting tools
* Experience supporting rapid prototyping efforts in fast-paced product development environments
