To do this right, we have to solve robotics, AI, manufacturing - at the same time, at scale, in a form factor that has to be safe enough to live with your family. If you’re inspired by this, you’ll thrive here. We’ve been at this since 2014 and we’re at the point where the hard problems are behind us and the hard work is in front of us.

# About the Team

The IT & Business Systems team builds and operates the internal platforms that enable 1X to scale globally across engineering, manufacturing, operations, and corporate functions. We focus on creating secure, reliable, and high-signal workplace systems that reduce operational friction, automate workflows, and support high-velocity execution across the company.

As 1X grows, this team plays a critical role in building the operational backbone that allows employees, robots, infrastructure, and business systems to function together seamlessly and securely.

# Your Charter

Own the architecture, governance, and operational excellence of 1X’s workplace platforms and business systems ecosystem. This role is responsible for designing scalable workflows, improving system interoperability, strengthening access governance, and enabling operational efficiency across a rapidly growing robotics organization. Your work will directly impact the speed, reliability, and scalability of how teams collaborate and execute across the company.

# Key Outcomes

* Design and scale workplace platform infrastructure supporting rapid organizational growth across engineering, manufacturing, and operations teams
* Improve operational efficiency by automating workflows, reducing manual processes, and increasing system reliability across business-critical platforms
* Establish scalable governance, RBAC, and access management standards across workplace and collaboration systems
* Create high-signal reporting and operational dashboards that improve visibility, prioritization, and decision-making for technical and business leadership
* Partner cross-functionally with Security, IT, Engineering, and Operations teams to ensure platform scalability, compliance, and operational readiness
