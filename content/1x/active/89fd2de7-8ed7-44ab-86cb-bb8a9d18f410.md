# About the Team

The IT & Infrastructure Engineering team builds and operates the foundational systems that power 1X employees, robots, manufacturing environments, and operational infrastructure. We focus on creating secure, scalable, and highly automated environments that enable engineering and operations teams to move quickly and reliably.

Linux systems and endpoint infrastructure are critical to how 1X develops, deploys, and operates robotics systems across engineering labs, manufacturing floors, and corporate environments.

# Your Charter

Own and scale the Linux device management infrastructure across 1X’s rapidly growing robotics organization. This role is responsible for building secure, automated, and reliable Linux fleet management solutions that support engineering productivity, operational scalability, and strong security posture across development and production environments.

# Key Outcomes

* Design and scale Linux device management systems supporting engineering, robotics, and operational environments across the company
* Improve fleet reliability, security, and operational efficiency through automation, centralized management, and configuration standardization
* Develop scalable provisioning, patch management, compliance, and monitoring workflows across Linux endpoints and infrastructure
* Partner cross-functionally with Security, IT, Robotics, and Infrastructure teams to improve endpoint security and operational readiness
* Reduce manual operational overhead by implementing automation tooling, self-service workflows, and infrastructure-as-code methodologies

# Key Competencies

* Deep expertise managing Linux endpoints and infrastructure at scale
* Strong automation and scripting skills using Python, Bash, or similar tooling
* Experience with configuration management and device orchestration platforms such as Ansible, Puppet, Chef, or Salt
* Strong understanding of endpoint security, patch management, compliance, and systems hardening best practices
* Excellent troubleshooting and operational problem-solving skills across distributed systems environments

# Minimum Requirements

* 7+ years of experience in Linux systems engineering, device management, infrastructure engineering, or related technical domains
* Strong hands-on experience administering Linux systems in enterprise or production environments
* Experience with device provisioning, endpoint management, systems automation, and configuration management tooling
* Strong scripting/programming experience using Python, Bash, or equivalent languages
* Experience supporting cloud infrastructure environments such as AWS, GCP, or Azure

# Preferred Skills

* Experience supporting robotics, manufacturing, operational technology (OT), or hardware engineering environments
* Familiarity with endpoint detection and response (EDR), identity management, and device trust frameworks
* Experience with CI/CD systems, developer infrastructure, or Kubernetes environments
* Exposure to zero trust security principles and infrastructure compliance frameworks
* Experience operating in high-growth startup environments
