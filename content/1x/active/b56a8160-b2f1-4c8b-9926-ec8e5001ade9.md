# **About the Team**

The Autonomy & Safety Engineering team is responsible for ensuring 1X robots operate safely, reliably, and predictably in real-world human environments. We develop the systems, methodologies, and operational frameworks that validate the safety of humanoid robots across autonomy, controls, perception, hardware, and deployment operations.

The Safety Assurance function plays a critical role in defining how safety is measured, documented, validated, and operationalized as 1X scales embodied AI systems into homes and real-world environments.

# **Your Charter**

Lead the development and execution of safety assurance methodologies, validation frameworks, and operational safety processes for 1X humanoid robots. This role is responsible for ensuring that robot behaviors, autonomy systems, and operational deployments meet rigorous safety standards while enabling rapid product iteration and large-scale deployment of embodied AI systems.

# **Key Outcomes**

* Establish scalable safety assurance frameworks across autonomy, controls, perception, hardware, and operational robotics systems
* Define and operationalize hazard analysis, risk assessment, validation, and safety case methodologies across robot development lifecycles
* Partner cross-functionally with Engineering, Product, Operations, and Safety teams to ensure safety requirements are integrated into system architecture and deployment readiness
* Improve operational safety readiness through structured incident analysis, validation testing, and continuous risk mitigation processes
* Develop scalable documentation, governance, and auditability processes supporting internal safety reviews and future regulatory readiness

# **Key Competencies**

* Deep expertise in safety assurance, systems safety engineering, robotics safety, or other safety-critical engineering domains
* Strong systems-level thinking with the ability to evaluate complex interactions across software, hardware, autonomy, and operational environments
* Experience developing hazard analysis, risk assessment, safety validation, or assurance case methodologies
* Strong technical communication skills with the ability to align engineering, operations, and leadership stakeholders around safety objectives
