Agility’s commercially deployed humanoids operate alongside teams in warehouses, manufacturing facilities, and distribution centers—tackling physically demanding and repetitive tasks while enabling workers to focus on higher-value work. With industry-leading safety standards and years of proven deployment data, we're pioneering a new era of automation that enhances human potential.

### **About the Work**

As the Senior Product Marketing Manager, you will bridge the gap between our industry-leading humanoid robotics technology and the audiences evaluating our solutions. Your job is to own positioning and messaging for Agility’s product and technology portfolio—including our humanoid robot Digit, Arc automation software platform, and the broader ecosystem of tools and technology that keep Digit working.

You will be the ultimate connective tissue between masterful engineering and the market, translating deep technical concepts into crisp, customer-focused value propositions. The ideal candidate blends deep technical curiosity with strategic marketing muscle and a hands-on passion for storytelling and content creation.

### **What You’ll Do**

* **Portfolio Positioning & Messaging:** Define and evolve the overarching value proposition for Agility’s product portfolio. Develop differentiated messaging frameworks that connect technical capabilities with customer needs.
* **Drive Go-To-Market & Launches:** Lead GTM planning and high-profile product announcements. You will own audience definition, naming, positioning, and marketing requirements to drive awareness and demand in priority industries and use cases.
* **Technical Content & Enablement:** Serve as a hands-on creator and overseer of high-impact sales enablement assets (spec sheets, presentations, case studies, and videos). Ensure absolute technical accuracy while accelerating the buyer's journey.
* **Know the Customer Deeply:** Map the decision-making landscape for automation buyers and operators. Understand their operational pain points, tech stacks, buying processes, and KPIs to create persona frameworks that ground all demand generation and marketing execution.
* **Synthesize Market Intelligence:** Act as the strategic loop between the market and the company. Gather and translate analyst research, win/loss data, competitive intelligence, and customer feedback into actionable insights that shape our go-to-market.
* **Cross-Functional Collaboration:** Partner closely with Product Management and Engineering to become a subject matter expert on Agility’s hardware and software applications. Collaborate tightly with a world class creative team to bring product storytelling to life across all marketing channels.
* **Set Demand Generation Priorities:** Use data-driven insights and market trends to establish clear priorities for demand generation, ensuring campaigns align with current product capabilities and priority industries.

### **About You**

* **8+ years of experience** in product marketing or product management, with a proven track record of driving high-profile product launches for robotics, AI, or adjacent deep-tech hardware.
* **A master storyteller and writer** who can translate complex technical functionality into clear, relatable narratives for both highly technical engineering buyers and non-technical executives.
* **Industrial market fluency:** Experience marketing to manufacturing, logistics, or warehousing industries is a strong plus. You should be comfortable speaking the language of industrial customers.
* **A strong bias for action**, backed by incisive strategic instincts. You balance big-picture strategy with execution, and you thrive when bringing clarity and direction to fast-paced, ambiguous environments.
* **Highly collaborative and pragmatic**, with a demonstrated ability to partner closely with Product Management, Sales, and creative teams to influence outcomes without formal authority.

### **The Agility Fit**

* **Ownership:** You proactively identify opportunities, take initiative on solutions, and follow through on commitments.
* **Trust:** You listen as much as you talk and take responsibility for collective success.
* **Adaptability:** You’re smart with resources, focus on outcomes, and can pivot effectively as our industry evolves.

This is a fully remote role with the option to work hybrid if a commutable distance from our Salem, OR, Pittsburgh, PA, or Fremont, CA offices.
