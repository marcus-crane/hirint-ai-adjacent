Agility’s commercially deployed humanoids operate alongside teams in warehouses, manufacturing facilities, and distribution centers—tackling physically demanding and repetitive tasks while enabling workers to focus on higher-value work. With industry-leading safety standards and years of proven deployment data, we're pioneering a new era of automation that enhances human potential.

**Overview**

Agility Robotics is pioneering the humanoid robotics segment, developing systems that bridge advanced AI, hardware, and real-world deployment at scale. As the company accelerates commercialization of its next-generation humanoid platform, the **Chief of Staff to the CEO** plays a critical leadership role in shaping and delivering the company’s most high-impact strategic programs.

Reporting directly to the CEO, this role leads a high-caliber team responsible for driving alignment, executional speed, and enterprise-level value creation. The Chief of Staff functions as a strategic accelerator—a highly focused, agile executive translating strategy into measurable outcomes that position Agility Robotics as the clear leader in humanoid robotics.

### **Purpose of the Chief of Staff to CEO**

To ensure Agility Robotics maintains strategic focus, executional speed, and disciplined alignment across the initiatives that most directly determine leadership in the humanoid robotics category.

### **Core Responsibilities**

#### **Leadership & Execution**

* **Own a Strategic Portfolio:** Manage a portfolio of strategic programs that enable Agility’s humanoid leadership, ensuring executional discipline, risk mitigation, and milestone delivery.
* **Operational Cadence:** Establish operating cadence, program reviews, and executive reporting frameworks in partnership with the Executive Leadership Team (ELT) and program teams.
* **Collaboration with Engineering:** Provide strategic oversight for a portfolio of engineering programs and work collaboratively with Technical Program Managers (TPMs) that report into the engineering organization to ensure efforts are synchronized with enterprise-level strategic priorities.
* **Governance Frameworks:** Establish program governance frameworks, milestones, and KPIs to ensure delivery of high-priority initiatives on time, within scope, and at quality.

#### **Cross-Functional Leadership & Alignment**

* **ELT Engagement:** Directly engage with the ELT on a regular basis to ensure initiatives remain on track, providing high visibility and accountability for results.
* **OKR Management:** Drive OKR alignment and provide visibility across the ELT to support and drive achievement across the entire organization.
* **Internal Communication:** Lead cross-functional teams across org-wide meetings to ensure the organization is advised on progress against OKRs and educated on key company initiatives.

#### **Thought Partnership & Culture**

* **Senior Advisor:** Act as a thought partner to senior leadership, synthesizing data, insights, and trade-offs into clear recommendations and decision frameworks.
* **Performance Culture:** Foster a culture of analytical rigor, accountability, and high-bar output within the team and across all program teams.
* **Risk Mitigation:** Anticipate and mitigate risks in complex technical programs while maintaining agility in execution.

### **Qualifications**

* **Experience:** 15+ years of experience in operations or leadership within emerging technology, robotics, or high-growth sectors.
* **Track Record:** Proven ability to drive large-scale, complex, cross-functional initiatives from concept through commercialization with executive-level accountability.
* **Technical Acumen:** Deep strategic and analytical skills with significant exposure to complex hardware-software systems; experience in robotics or automation environments is strongly preferred.
* **Education:** Bachelor’s degree in engineering, computer science, or related technical field required; Top-tier MBA or Master’s in a technical field strongly preferred.
* **Leadership:** Strong executive presence, communication, and leadership skills capable of influencing at C-suite levels.

This a hybrid position based out of one of our Fremont, CA office.
