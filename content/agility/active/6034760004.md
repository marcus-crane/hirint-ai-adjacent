## About the Role

As a Buyer II, you will independently manage procurement execution across assigned suppliers and commodities while ensuring material availability to support engineering builds and production. You will serve as the primary point of contact for supplier execution activities and proactively manage delivery performance, shortages, and supply continuity.

This role will primarily support electronics commodities including custom printed circuit board assemblies (PCBAs), cable harnesses, sensors, connectors, and related electronic assemblies. You will work closely with Global Supply Managers (GSMs), Planning, Engineering, Manufacturing, and Finance to ensure procurement activities are executed effectively and supplier commitments align with business needs.

This role requires strong procurement execution skills, supplier management experience, and the ability to independently manage procurement priorities and supplier performance in a fast-paced hardware environment.

## About the Work

* Manage all purchase order activity for assigned suppliers, including creation, revisions, confirmations, and delivery management.
* Own supplier communications related to delivery schedules, shortages, push-in/push-out requests, engineering changes, and recovery plans.
* Monitor supplier performance and proactively identify supply risks before they impact engineering builds or production schedules.
* Maintain ERP/MRP data accuracy including pricing, lead times, supplier records, order status, sourcing information, and procurement parameters.
* Support request for quotation (RFQ) activities and supplier quote analysis to assist Global Supply Managers (GSMs) with sourcing decisions and cost reduction initiatives.
* Drive tactical cost savings through supplier engagement, quote comparisons, pricing analysis, and identification of commercial opportunities.
* Coordinate material readiness for prototype, engineering validation, and production builds.
* Partner with Planning and Manufacturing teams to resolve shortages and material availability issues.
* Track long-lead materials and work closely with suppliers to ensure supply continuity and schedule alignment.
* Support supplier onboarding activities and ensure suppliers meet procurement process requirements.
* Resolve invoice discrepancies, shipment issues, receiving concerns, and supplier-related execution challenges.
* Provide timely updates to cross-functional teams regarding supplier commitments, shortages, material availability, and delivery risks.
* Support new product introduction (NPI) activities through proactive procurement planning and supplier coordination.

## About You

* Experience working with electronics manufacturing services (EMS) providers and contract manufacturers preferred.
