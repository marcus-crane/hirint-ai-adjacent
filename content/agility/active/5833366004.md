Agility’s commercially deployed humanoids operate alongside teams in warehouses, manufacturing facilities, and distribution centers—tackling physically demanding and repetitive tasks while enabling workers to focus on higher-value work. With industry-leading safety standards and years of proven deployment data, we're pioneering a new era of automation that enhances human potential.

**About the Team:**

Our mechanical engineering team is agile and has grown rapidly, and we are looking for a talented mechanical design drafter, to join us in the San Francisco Bay Area. We are responsible for delivering advanced and reliable designs that meet a complex set of requirements to our customers, including software developers, applications engineers, manufacturing engineers and ultimately to fleet deployment managers. We work as a group to develop new technologies, engineer new products, and refine/maintain existing designs. The team is composed of engineers from many backgrounds and levels of experience.

**About The Role:**

We are currently designing the next generation of Digit for commercial scale. Highly reliable and robust designs are critical to successful deployment of our robots in large quantities. Digit works in demanding environments, performing highly repetitive and heavily loaded tasks. The mechanical designer will work closely with mechanical engineers and play a key role in ensuring that we have well documented robot designs that are production-ready.

This role is based in the San Francisco Bay Area.

**About the work:**

* Creo CAD model and drafting creation and management to Agility standards

+ Sourcing OTS component models and integrating them in the assembly
+ Updating CAD based on engineering analysis inputs
+ Detail drafting of parts and assemblies
+ CAD Library management
+ CAD template management

* CAD data management in Windchill

+ BOM creation and management
+ Submission and herding of Promotion Requests

* Identification of OTS components that meet engineering specifications
* Product design in support of engineering requirements
* Support cross-functional design reviews through production of quality CAD screenshots, renderings, and creation of presentation slides

**About you:**

* Expert in Creo, including top down modeling, drafting, and library management
* Expert in Windchill, promotion requests, file management, BOM management
* Fluent in the application of GD&T to mechanical parts and assemblies
* Expert knowledge in several of the following manufacturing processes: sheet metal, machining, casting, injection molding
* Bonus: Altuim CoDesigner experience
* 5+ years of mechanical designer experience

**Preferred Skills and Experience:**

* Experience with tolerance analysis
* Windchill PDM Administration experience
* Jira/Confluence experience
* A.S. Electro/Mechanical Design Drafting

This a hybrid position based out of one of our Fremont office.
