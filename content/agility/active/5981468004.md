Agility’s commercially deployed humanoids operate alongside teams in warehouses, manufacturing facilities, and distribution centers—tackling physically demanding and repetitive tasks while enabling workers to focus on higher-value work. With industry-leading safety standards and years of proven deployment data, we're pioneering a new era of automation that enhances human potential.

**About The Role**

Agility Robotics is pioneering the humanoid robotics segment, developing systems that bridge advanced AI, hardware, and real-world deployment at scale. As the company accelerates toward commercialization of its next-generation humanoid platforms, the Vice President of Strategy & Corporate Development plays a critical leadership role in shaping and delivering the company’s most high-impact strategic programs.

Reporting directly to the CBO, this role leads a small, high-caliber team responsible for driving alignment, strategic acceleration, and enterprise-level value creation and directly engages with the Executive Leadership Team in a meaningful way.  

The VP, Strategy & Corporate Development functions as a strategic accelerator, a highly focused, agile executive translating strategy into measurable outcomes that position Agility Robotics as the clear leader in humanoid robotics.

**Purpose of Strategy & Corporate Development**

To ensure Agility Robotics maintains strategic focus, executional speed, and disciplined alignment across the initiatives that most directly determine leadership in the humanoid robotics category.

**About The Work**

#### **Growth Focused Strategic Programs**

* Own a portfolio of strategic programs that enable Agility’s humanoid leadership focused on sustainable growth.
* Strategic pathway identification to support and drive Agility’s growth ambition and valuation performance.
* Drive executional discipline, risk mitigation, and milestone delivery across strategic programs under the Chief Business Officer.

**Business Development & Corporate Development**

* Lead strategic identification and evaluation of potential M&A and partnership opportunities aligned with Agility’s roadmap, particularly in areas of AI, safety, and humanoid platform technologies for integration, and commercial acceleration.
* Drive due diligence, financial modeling, and integration planning for targeted opportunities that accelerate market position or technological advantage.
* Support investor and Board engagement with analysis and recommendations tied to growth initiatives.
* Build and maintain an external network of partners to accelerate product adoption and category leadership.

**Strategic Growth & Market Expansion**

* Lead CBO-sponsored initiatives that drive organic and inorganic growth, including new market activations, geographic expansion, and industry adjacency explorations.
* Partner with leaders to identify, validate, and operationalize new channels of value creation.
* Partner with Product organization to support strategy development for key verticals, including logistics, manufacturing, automotive, defense, and others, ensuring Agility’s humanoid platform aligns with the highest-value use cases.

* Evaluate and design partnership frameworks that extend beyond commercial contracts, like ecosystem integration and platform standardization.

**Leadership & Organizational Impact**

* Directly engage with the Executive Leadership Team (ELT) on a regular basis to ensure initiatives remain on track, ensuring visibility and accountability.
* Drive OKR alignment and provide visibility across the ELT to support and drive achievement
* Foster a culture of analytical rigor, high-bar output, and rapid thinking within the team.
* Establish operating cadence, program reviews, and executive reporting frameworks in partnership with the ELT.
* Partner across the ELT to support enterprise problem solving, prioritization, and alignment on strategic imperatives.
* Leads cross functional teams across org-wide employee meetings and cadences to ensure the entire organization is advised on the company’s progress against OKRs, educated on initiatives within the company, and informed on the latest cross-functional updates.

**About You**

* 15+ years of experience in strategy, operations, corporate development, or program leadership within emerging technology, robotics, or high-growth sectors.
* Top-tier MBA or Engineering Degree
* Prior experience at a top-three management consulting firm (McKinsey, Bain, or BCG), ideally with exposure to technology and industrial clients.
* Proven track record of leading complex, cross-functional initiatives with executive-level accountability.
* Deep strategic, analytical, and financial acumen with the ability to navigate high-stakes corporate development and investor discussions.
* Strong executive presence, communication, and leadership skills capable of influencing at Board and C-suite levels.
* Experience working in or advising robotics, automation, or complex hardware-software systems preferred.

**Success Metrics**

* Deliver clear, data-backed strategic pathways that unify market opportunity, product direction, and commercial strategy to accelerate decision-making.
* Build and advance a prioritized pipeline of strategic partnerships and M&A opportunities that strengthen Agility’s competitive moat and long-term valuation.
* Define and operationalize strategic growth initiatives,  including vertical pathways, new markets, and adjacencies that expand Agility’s TAM capture.
* Establish ecosystem-level frameworks and partnerships that deepen platform integration, enhance partner adoption, and reinforce Agility’s position in the humanoid category.
* Drive cross-functional alignment and cohesion across Commercial, Product, Operations, Marketing, and Finance through strategic alignment and prioritization mechanisms.
* Build and lead a high-performing team that delivers high-quality analysis, structured outputs, and measurable acceleration for initiatives.
