Agility’s commercially deployed humanoids operate alongside teams in warehouses, manufacturing facilities, and distribution centers—tackling physically demanding and repetitive tasks while enabling workers to focus on higher-value work. With industry-leading safety standards and years of proven deployment data, we're pioneering a new era of automation that enhances human potential.

**About The Role**

We are seeking a UX/UI Designer to join our growing team. In this role, you will be responsible for creating intuitive, visually compelling, and user-friendly digital experiences that support the operation, monitoring, and management of our robotic systems. You will work closely with engineers, product managers, and researchers to translate complex technical workflows into elegant, efficient interfaces.

Robotics experience is helpful but not required—what matters most is your ability to simplify complexity, advocate for the user, and bring a strong design perspective to multidisciplinary collaboration. This is a remote-friendly role with a preference for candidates located near one of our offices for in-person collaboration, meetings, and events.

**About The Work**

* Lead the end-to-end design process for robot and web based UI: e.g. create wireframes, prototypes, and final UI assets ready for implementation by our development team
* Improve the UX/UI of features implemented by our development teams
* Design industry leading user interfaces and experiences for robot operator tools, dashboards, and mobile/web/VR applications.
* Develop and maintain design systems and style guides to ensure consistency across platforms.
* Collaborate with product and engineering teams to align design solutions with technical feasibility and business needs.
* Assist our HRI/UX Researcher in conducting user research and usability testing, and then iterate based on feedback.
* Create intuitive visualizations for real-time data, robot status, and system controls.
* Stay current with UX/UI best practices and emerging design trends.

**About You**

* Bachelor’s degree in Design, HCI, or related field—or equivalent practical experience.
* 3+ years of professional UX/UI design experience.
* Strong portfolio demonstrating user-centered design across web or software applications.
* Experience designing complex systems, dashboards, or technical tools for enterprise software.
* Proficiency with design tools (e.g., Figma, Sketch, Adobe XD).
* Experience with prototyping and usability testing.
* Strong communication and collaboration skills.
* Authorization to work in the USA

**Bonus Points**

* Familiarity with robotics, IoT, or industrial automation interfaces.
* Knowledge of front-end development basics (HTML/CSS/JavaScript) for better design-engineering collaboration

This a hybrid position based out of one of our Fremont, CA office.
