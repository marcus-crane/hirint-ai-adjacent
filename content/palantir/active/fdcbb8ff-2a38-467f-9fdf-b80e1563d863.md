**A World-Changing Company**

Palantir builds the world’s leading software for data-driven decisions and operations. By bringing the right data to the people who need it, our platforms empower our partners to develop lifesaving drugs, forecast supply chain disruptions, locate missing children, and more.

**The Role**

Talent Strategists at Palantir don't work from a boilerplate requisition list. They embed with their teams, learn what exceptional actually looks like for a given role, and hire the Engineers building the future.   
  
As a Technical Sourcer, you'll support that work. You'll partner with Talent Strategists and Hiring Managers to build the pipelines and manage the process for some of our hardest-to-fill roles. The job is equal parts research, relationship-building, and attunement — figuring out where the right people are, what's going to resonate with them, and how to get them in the door.  
  
This is an 8-month contract role. It moves fast, the priorities shift, and the standard is high. You will start with two weeks of onboarding before jumping into the deep end.

### Core Responsibilities

* Source passive candidates through direct outreach, referral networks, research, and sourcing tools. Continuously reassess what's working. The talent we need does not sit in obvious places, and the methods that worked last quarter may not work today.
* Review inbound applicants and referrals, screening candidates with a genuine eye for fit, not just credentials.
* Own the candidate experience from first outreach through to pre-offer stage. You're their main point of contact, and how you manage that relationship matters.
* Maintain meticulous candidate records within our ATS.
* Share pipeline data and market insights with the broader Talent team proactively — not just when asked. Sourcing in a silo is sourcing that fails.

### What We Value

* You generate solutions, not excuses. When the search stalls, you rebuild the approach, not the timeline.
* You are data-driven about your pipeline and honest about what's working. Intuition matters. So does evidence.
* You communicate with clarity and conviction to candidates and internal stakeholders.
* You operate without supervision and without complaint in an environment where priorities shift and the bar does not.
* You are genuinely curious about technology and the people who build it.

### What We Require

* 1+ years of experience in recruiting, sourcing, talent-spotting, or a recruiting-adjacent role (we are open to a range of backgrounds).
* Experience using an ATS to source, track, and manage candidates.
* Extraordinary interpersonal skills and an instinct for building relationships quickly.
