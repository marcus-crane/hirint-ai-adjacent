## About ElevenLabs

ElevenLabs is an AI research and product company transforming how we interact with technology.

We launched in January 2023 with the first human-like AI voice model. Today, we serve millions of users and thousands of businesses - from fast-growing startups to large enterprises like Deutsche Telekom and Meta. Our investors are some of the world's most prominent, including Andreessen Horowitz, ICONIQ Growth and Sequoia. We've raised $781M in funding and our last valuation was $11B - multiples of 11, always.  
  
We have expanded from voice into three main platforms:

* ElevenAgents enables businesses to deliver seamless and intelligent customer experiences, with the integrations, testing, monitoring, and reliability necessary to deploy voice and chat agents at scale.
* ElevenCreative empowers creators and marketers to generate and edit speech, music, image, and video across 70+ languages.
* ElevenAPI gives developers access to our leading AI audio foundational models.

Everything we do is the result of the creativity and commitment of our team - builders doing the best work of their lives. We are researchers, engineers, and operators. IOI medalists and ex-founders. If you want to work hard and create lasting positive impact, we want to hear from you.

**About the role**

Brazil is one of our fastest-growing markets globally, and we're looking for a highly motivated Events Marketer to lead planning and execution of impactful events across the country and broader Brazil-focused GTM motion. Your work will deepen ElevenLabs' presence, drive brand awareness, and generate high-quality pipeline in partnership with our Brazil GM and GTM teams.

You'll own the full lifecycle of in-person owned events, third-party tech conferences, executive dinners, workshops, webinars, sponsorships, and strategic partnerships. You'll adapt and localize successful global playbooks while identifying new, high-impact opportunities unique to the Brazilian market.

**In this role, you will:**

* Develop and execute event plans aligned with business objectives across São Paulo, Rio de Janeiro, and key Brazilian markets
* Organise third-party conferences, owned branded events, and strategic dinners with key customers and prospects
* Collaborate cross-functionally with sales, marketing, and GTM teams for seamless execution
* Create compelling event messaging that resonates with Brazilian business audiences
* Manage event budgets and timelines end to end
* Analyze event performance and provide recommendations for future improvements
* Build and maintain relationships with sponsors, partners, and vendors across Brazil

**What you bring:**

* 5+ years events experience, with at least three in tech event marketing
* Native Portuguese speaker, English fluency required
* Deep knowledge of the Brazilian tech ecosystem and key industry events including events in São Paulo and beyond
* Experience working with Brazilian event agencies and production partners
* Based in São Paulo preferred
