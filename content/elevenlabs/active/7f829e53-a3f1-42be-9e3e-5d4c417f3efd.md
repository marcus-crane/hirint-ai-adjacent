## About ElevenLabs

ElevenLabs is an AI research and product company transforming how we interact with technology.

We launched in January 2023 with the first human-like AI voice model. Today, we serve millions of users and thousands of businesses - from fast-growing startups to large enterprises like Deutsche Telekom and Meta. Our investors are some of the world's most prominent, including Andreessen Horowitz, ICONIQ Growth and Sequoia. We've raised $781M in funding and our last valuation was $11B - multiples of 11, always.  
  
We have expanded from voice into three main platforms:

* ElevenAgents enables businesses to deliver seamless and intelligent customer experiences, with the integrations, testing, monitoring, and reliability necessary to deploy voice and chat agents at scale.
* ElevenCreative empowers creators and marketers to generate and edit speech, music, image, and video across 70+ languages.
* ElevenAPI gives developers access to our leading AI audio foundational models.

Everything we do is the result of the creativity and commitment of our team - builders doing the best work of their lives. We are researchers, engineers, and operators. IOI medalists and ex-founders. If you want to work hard and create lasting positive impact, we want to hear from you.

## About the role

We're looking for a senior B2B Marketing Generalist to build and scale demand generation across multiple channels. This is a hands-on role focused on generating enterprise pipeline, growing our brand with technical buyers, and creating programs that become benchmarks for B2B marketing in AI.

Your work will shape how the Indian businesses discover, evaluate, and adopt ElevenLabs. The goal is to build repeatable marketing engines that make the top choice for every enterprise evaluating voice AI, whether for conversational agents, or creative applications.  
  
**What you’ll do:**

* Build integrated B2B marketing campaigns end to end across priority industries, from strategy and messaging through execution and measurement.
* Build and execute programs across ABM, content, webinars, partner/customer marketing, executive branding, product launches, and outbound, choosing the right channel for the problem rather than owning a single function.
* Partner closely with sales, product marketing, growth, design, and global marketing teams to keep messaging consistent and execute high-impact campaigns.
* Own pipeline generation and campaign performance, using data to measure results, run experiments, and continuously improve what works.

**Requirements:**

* 5 to 7 years of experience at a high-growth technology company - built and scaled B2B demand generation programs that created measurable enterprise pipeline, and know what moved the needle.
* Work well without much process. This role spans multiple marketing functions and needs someone who sets priorities, spots opportunities, and moves quickly.
* Relentless commitment to excellence - holds a high bar for quality, clarity, and impact
* Is curious about AI, follows the technology ecosystem closely, and has exceptional judgment on what makes marketing stand out.

**Growth Potential**

We believe in hiring the best talent in the world to make an impact in the right role at the right time. At ElevenLabs, strong performance creates opportunities for growth. Here are some of the career paths you can expect after excelling in this role:

* Lead B2B demand generation for the region and channels as ElevenLabs continues to scale globally.
* Own a major marketing function, such as enterprise marketing, events, or product marketing.
* Grow into a broader marketing leadership role, helping define the GTM strategy for new products and markets.

**Location**

This role is remote-first and can be executed from anywhere in India.
