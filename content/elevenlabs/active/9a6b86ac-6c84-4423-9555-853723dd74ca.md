## About ElevenLabs

ElevenLabs is an AI research and product company transforming how we interact with technology.

We launched in January 2023 with the first human-like AI voice model. Today, we serve millions of users and thousands of businesses - from fast-growing startups to large enterprises like Deutsche Telekom and Meta. Our investors are some of the world's most prominent, including Andreessen Horowitz, ICONIQ Growth and Sequoia. We've raised $781M in funding and our last valuation was $11B - multiples of 11, always.  
  
We have expanded from voice into three main platforms:

* ElevenAgents enables businesses to deliver seamless and intelligent customer experiences, with the integrations, testing, monitoring, and reliability necessary to deploy voice and chat agents at scale.
* ElevenCreative empowers creators and marketers to generate and edit speech, music, image, and video across 70+ languages.
* ElevenAPI gives developers access to our leading AI audio foundational models.

Everything we do is the result of the creativity and commitment of our team - builders doing the best work of their lives. We are researchers, engineers, and operators. IOI medalists and ex-founders. If you want to work hard and create lasting positive impact, we want to hear from you.

**About the role**

We're looking for an experienced, driven Strategic Account Executive to drive ElevenLabs' growth across the Netherlands' largest enterprises, with a specific focus on Government, Healthcare & Utilities. Our ideal candidate has a well-established network and a proven track record of selling to C-level and senior decision-makers at major Dutch public sector bodies, healthcare organizations, and utilities providers — and is passionate about the transformative possibilities of AI voice technology.

In this role you'll act as a strategic partner and trusted advisor, enabling clients to leverage our industry-leading models and ElevenAgents — our end-to-end platform for building and deploying AI voice agents — to reimagine their customer experience, internal workflows, and monetization strategies.

**In this role you will:**

1. Build and manage a growing portfolio of strategic accounts across the Netherlands, with a primary focus on Government, Healthcare & Utilities, to help ElevenLabs meet its revenue goals.
2. Identify new business opportunities where ElevenLabs' conversational AI capabilities, including ElevenAgents, can drive customer engagement, contact center automation, and operational efficiency for government bodies, healthcare organizations, and utilities providers.
3. Lead consultative, multi-stakeholder sales cycles, building compelling business cases that translate AI voice technology into measurable business value for senior executives and economic buyers.
4. Develop and maintain a deep understanding of the conversational AI landscape, including customer use cases, competitive solutions, and emerging trends, particularly as they apply to regulated Dutch government, healthcare, and utilities organizations.
5. Demonstrate expertise, or a strong willingness to learn, about conversational AI and how ElevenLabs' voice technology can unlock value across customer support, virtual agents, IVR modernization, and in-app assistants.
6. Develop and execute account strategies to expand ElevenLabs' presence within the Dutch government, healthcare, and utilities sectors, navigating complex procurement and organizational structures, including public tender processes.
7. Partner closely with Customer Success, FDEs and Solutions Engineering to ensure smooth onboarding, deployment, and expansion of accounts.
8. Serve as a trusted advisor to clients, educating C-suite and senior operations leaders on emerging trends in generative AI, voice interfaces, and conversational agents.

**Requirements**

1. 7+ years of quota-carrying strategic sales experience in SaaS or technology, ideally with exposure to AI, generative AI, LLM-based products, or API-driven platforms.
2. Proven success closing deals and managing long, complex sales cycles with multiple stakeholders across business, IT, legal, and procurement.
3. Well-connected in the Dutch government, healthcare, and utilities landscape, with an existing network of senior individuals and strong executive presence and ability to build relationships at the C-suite and board level.
4. Experience selling technical solutions to product and engineering leaders; ability to translate complex technology into business value.
5. Deep understanding of enterprise and public sector procurement and legal processes in the Netherlands, including tender and RFP processes, with the ability to accelerate deal velocity within complex organizational structures.
6. Comfort operating in an early-stage, high-growth environment, including building new playbooks and iterating quickly.
7. Passion for voice and audio AI and how it can unlock transformative value for customers.
8. A hybrid of customer and product-driven mentality that prioritizes client satisfaction and scale.
9. Native or full professional proficiency in Dutch; strong English for internal collaboration.

**Location**

This role is remote-first, so it can be executed from anywhere in the Netherlands with the ability to travel to client sites when required.
