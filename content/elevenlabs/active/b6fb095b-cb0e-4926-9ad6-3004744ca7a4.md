## About ElevenLabs

ElevenLabs is an AI research and product company transforming how we interact with technology.

We launched in January 2023 with the first human-like AI voice model. Today, we serve millions of users and thousands of businesses - from fast-growing startups to large enterprises like Deutsche Telekom and Meta. Our investors are some of the world's most prominent, including Andreessen Horowitz, ICONIQ Growth and Sequoia. We've raised $781M in funding and our last valuation was $11B - multiples of 11, always.  
  
We have expanded from voice into three main platforms:

* ElevenAgents enables businesses to deliver seamless and intelligent customer experiences, with the integrations, testing, monitoring, and reliability necessary to deploy voice and chat agents at scale.
* ElevenCreative empowers creators and marketers to generate and edit speech, music, image, and video across 70+ languages.
* ElevenAPI gives developers access to our leading AI audio foundational models.

Everything we do is the result of the creativity and commitment of our team - builders doing the best work of their lives. We are researchers, engineers, and operators. IOI medalists and ex-founders. If you want to work hard and create lasting positive impact, we want to hear from you.

## About the role

We’re hiring a Developer Marketing Lead to make ElevenLabs the default audio AI platform for builders. You will own the strategy, budget, and narrative that attract qualified developers and turn intent into self-serve revenue—through positioning, launches, partnerships, content, and high-performing marketing surfaces (developer landing pages and campaigns), and later into enterprise leads. Your north star is developer sign-ups. This is a unique blend of product marketing and growth marketing, focused on developers.

Day to day, you’ll craft crisp developer messaging, orchestrate launches, continuously improve developer landing experiences with a disciplined test cadence, and spin up and test new growth channels (such as developer content, partnerships, SEO and paid). You’ll work cross-functionally with paid, SEO, and comms/PR to scale what works. This is not DevRel or in-product (no docs/SDKs/onboarding). This can be an IC role or a hybrid of IC and manager.

## Requirements

* 5+ years doing growth/marketing at fast-growing companies, or selling your own products.
* Deeply understand developers - what resonates and doesn’t, where they show up, how to position products for them.
* Analytical skills - you can use Google Sheets and/or SQL to understand the impact of the work you’re doing.
* Strong at copywriting.
* Experience in multiple channels including at least one being developer content, SEO, paid, and partnerships.
* Ownership, autonomy, bias to action.
* Know how to (vibe) code.
