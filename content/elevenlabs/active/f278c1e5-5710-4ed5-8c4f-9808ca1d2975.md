## **About the role**

We’re hiring a Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO) Manager to own the experimentation program that turns our paid traffic into signups, activations, and revenue. This role owns strategy and execution across the on-site funnel—from the marketing site and landing pages to signup and partnering with onboarding—and is expected to find compounding conversion gains through a disciplined, data-driven testing program.

The ideal candidate is equally comfortable with the strategy and the execution: forming sharp hypotheses, designing statistically sound tests, building and QA’ing variants, reading results without fooling themselves, and engineering skills to build on-page experiences. You obsess over funnel friction, statistical rigor, and velocity, and you know how to build an experimentation roadmap that ships. This role is highly cross-functional and will partner closely with design, engineering, copy, performance marketing, lifecycle, and product to drive impact.

## **What you will do**

* Own the CRO strategy and experimentation roadmap across the marketing site, landing pages, and signup/onboarding funnels
* Form hypotheses from analytics, session data, and user research; prioritize tests by expected impact and effort
* Design, build, QA, and launch A/B and multivariate tests; ensure results are statistically sound
* Partner with design, engineering, and copy to spec and ship high-quality variants quickly
* Analyze results, document learnings, and turn wins into durable improvements across the funnel
* Instrument funnel analytics and tracking so conversion impact is measurable end-to-end
* Align on-site conversion with paid and lifecycle traffic in partnership with performance and lifecycle marketing
* Raise experimentation velocity and rigor as the program scales

## **Requirements**

* Proven track record owning a CRO/experimentation program that delivered measurable conversion and revenue gains
* Hands-on expertise with A/B testing and CRO tools (Optimizely, VWO, Google Optimize successors, Statsig, GrowthBook, or similar), web analytics (GA4, Amplitude, Mixpanel, or similar), and front-end development (HTML, CSS, and JavaScript)
* Strong analytical rigor—you understand statistical significance, sample size, and how to avoid false positives without a second opinion
* Experience optimizing signup, onboarding, and landing-page funnels for both self-serve and lead-gen motions
* Comfortable working closely with design, engineering, and copy to ship high-quality test variants
* Clear communicator who can turn data into decisions and bring the team along
* You thrive in scrappy, high-ownership environments and are confident building a program from the ground up

## **Bonus**
