## About ElevenLabs

ElevenLabs is an AI research and product company transforming how we interact with technology.

We launched in January 2023 with the first human-like AI voice model. Today, we serve millions of users and thousands of businesses - from fast-growing startups to large enterprises like Deutsche Telekom and Meta. Our investors are some of the world's most prominent, including Andreessen Horowitz, ICONIQ Growth and Sequoia. We've raised $781M in funding and our last valuation was $11B - multiples of 11, always.  
  
We have expanded from voice into three main platforms:

* ElevenAgents enables businesses to deliver seamless and intelligent customer experiences, with the integrations, testing, monitoring, and reliability necessary to deploy voice and chat agents at scale.
* ElevenCreative empowers creators and marketers to generate and edit speech, music, image, and video across 70+ languages.
* ElevenAPI gives developers access to our leading AI audio foundational models.

Everything we do is the result of the creativity and commitment of our team - builders doing the best work of their lives. We are researchers, engineers, and operators. IOI medalists and ex-founders. If you want to work hard and create lasting positive impact, we want to hear from you.

## **About the role**

We’re hiring a Paid Media Manager to scale our paid acquisition across the full funnel and every segment—self-serve, developer, and enterprise–for emerging channels (e.g. Reddit, Snap, programmatic, et al). This role owns strategy and execution across the paid funnel, from channel mix and budget to creative testing and landing-page alignment, and is expected to find profitable, scalable growth through experimentation and data-driven decision making.

The ideal candidate is platform agnostic, with proven, hands-on experience across multiple paid channels. You should be as comfortable optimizing for self-serve signups as you are for demo requests and lead quality, and as fluent in creative testing as you are in attribution and CAC math. This role is highly cross-functional and will partner closely with creative, lifecycle, landing-page, product marketing, and sales to drive impact.

## **What you will do**

* Own full-funnel paid media strategy and execution across self-serve, developer, and enterprise segments
* Build scalable systems for paid acquisition, with strong attention to CAC, payback, and pipeline/conversion quality
* Manage channel mix and budget allocation across Google, Meta, LinkedIn, and emerging channels
* Partner with creative on ad concepts and run a high-velocity creative testing program
* Work with landing-page, lifecycle, and sales teams to align full-funnel journeys
* Move fast: run A/B tests, analyze performance, iterate quickly, and document learnings
* Instrument tracking, attribution, and reporting so paid impact is measurable end-to-end
* Elevate experimentation velocity and platform diversity to uncover untapped growth

## **Requirements**

* Proven track record of scaling paid programs profitably across Google, Meta, LinkedIn, and other channels
* Hands-on platform expertise—you’ve personally built and optimized campaigns, not just managed an agency
* Strong analytical chops—you understand attribution, tracking, and LTV vs CAC math without needing a second opinion
* Experience running creative testing and collaborating closely with creative teams on high-performing ads
* Experience aligning campaigns to full-funnel journeys across self-serve and sales-led motions
* Clear communicator with the ability to write high-intent copy and brief creative
* You thrive in scrappy, high-ownership environments and are confident building systems from the ground up

## **Bonus**

* Experience marketing to technical audiences (e.g., developers, APIs, infrastructure)
* Familiarity with both self-serve and sales-led motions
* Experience standing up reporting systems or collaborating with data/BI teams
