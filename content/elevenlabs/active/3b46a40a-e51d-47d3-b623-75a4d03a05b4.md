## About ElevenLabs

ElevenLabs is an AI research and product company transforming how we interact with technology.

We launched in January 2023 with the first human-like AI voice model. Today, we serve millions of users and thousands of businesses - from fast-growing startups to large enterprises like Deutsche Telekom and Meta. Our investors are some of the world's most prominent, including Andreessen Horowitz, ICONIQ Growth and Sequoia. We've raised $781M in funding and our last valuation was $11B - multiples of 11, always.  
  
We have expanded from voice into three main platforms:

* ElevenAgents enables businesses to deliver seamless and intelligent customer experiences, with the integrations, testing, monitoring, and reliability necessary to deploy voice and chat agents at scale.
* ElevenCreative empowers creators and marketers to generate and edit speech, music, image, and video across 70+ languages.
* ElevenAPI gives developers access to our leading AI audio foundational models.

Everything we do is the result of the creativity and commitment of our team - builders doing the best work of their lives. We are researchers, engineers, and operators. IOI medalists and ex-founders. If you want to work hard and create lasting positive impact, we want to hear from you.

## **About the role**

We're looking for an iOS Engineer to join the Mobile team. You'll work across the full product cycle - from ideation and experimentation through to shipping - with a focus on building features that drive real engagement and a great user experience.

You'll also play a key role in shaping new apps in the audio AI space, bringing your technical expertise to products built from the ground up.

* Drive the development of innovative features through data analysis, experimentation, and product ideation
* Own and evolve the mobile platform infrastructure needed to build, test, and ship at pace
* Contribute to architecture decisions and technical direction for the app
* Collaborate closely with design, product, research, and data science teams

## **Requirements**

* Strong native iOS engineering background, with a track record of shipping features that improve user experience and drive engagement
* Highly analytical, with hands-on experience designing and running A/B tests and data-driven experiments
* Comfortable with ambiguity and fast-moving environments
* A genuine drive to learn and a talent for sharing knowledge clearly with those around you

This role is remote-first, so it can be executed from anywhere with the ability to operate between GMT +3 and GMT-7 preferred, and during our core hours 14:00 to 18:00 GMT+1 required.
