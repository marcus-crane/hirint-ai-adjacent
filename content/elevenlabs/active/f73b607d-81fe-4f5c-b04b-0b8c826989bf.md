## About ElevenLabs

ElevenLabs is an AI research and product company transforming how we interact with technology.

We launched in January 2023 with the first human-like AI voice model. Today, we serve millions of users and thousands of businesses - from fast-growing startups to large enterprises like Deutsche Telekom and Meta. Our investors are some of the world's most prominent, including Andreessen Horowitz, ICONIQ Growth and Sequoia. We've raised $781M in funding and our last valuation was $11B - multiples of 11, always.  
  
We have expanded from voice into three main platforms:

* ElevenAgents enables businesses to deliver seamless and intelligent customer experiences, with the integrations, testing, monitoring, and reliability necessary to deploy voice and chat agents at scale.
* ElevenCreative empowers creators and marketers to generate and edit speech, music, image, and video across 70+ languages.
* ElevenAPI gives developers access to our leading AI audio foundational models.

Everything we do is the result of the creativity and commitment of our team - builders doing the best work of their lives. We are researchers, engineers, and operators. IOI medalists and ex-founders. If you want to work hard and create lasting positive impact, we want to hear from you.

## **About the role**

We are looking for a North America Strategic Partner Manager to build and lead our highest-impact partnerships across the region. This is a rare opportunity to define the playbook from scratch: identifying, recruiting, and activating a network of GSIs and Hyperscalers, with a strong preference for experience in Google Cloud who will embed ElevenLabs voice AI into enterprise workflows and customer experiences at scale.

You'll work directly with some of the most influential players in the cloud and services ecosystem to build joint GTM motions, accelerate enterprise pipeline, and position ElevenLabs as the preferred voice AI platform for GSI and hyperscaler-led deals. This is a builder role. You'll own partner strategy, recruitment, enablement, and co-sell execution across North America.

The ideal candidate has deep relationships within the GSI and hyperscaler ecosystems, understands how to structure partner programs that drive real revenue outcomes, and thrives in an environment where they're creating structure rather than inheriting it.

## **What you bring**

* 10+ years building and managing successful partnerships in North America, with a proven track record scaling GSI and/or hyperscaler relationships (Google Cloud, AWS, or Azure) that generated measurable enterprise revenue
* Experience recruiting net-new partners and taking them from zero to production (not just managing existing relationships) with the ability to build joint GTM plans, co-sell motions, and partner business cases
* Commercial mindset with demonstrated success driving partner-sourced and partner-influenced revenue; strong understanding of API-driven products and the ability to work closely with engineering, product, and solutions teams
* Strong cross-functional operator: comfortable navigating complex partner org structures while aligning internal sales, marketing, and product teams behind shared outcomes
* Highly organized and data-driven, with excellent communication skills and the ability to influence without authority in a fast-paced, high-growth environment
* Genuine passion for emerging AI technologies and their real-world applications, paired with a collaborative, proactive approach and a high level of ownership and accountability

**Bonus points:**

* Existing relationships within Google Cloud's partner ecosystem
* Experience in AI, developer tools, or voice/speech technology
* Prior experience as a founding or first-in-function partner hire
