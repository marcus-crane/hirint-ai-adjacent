## About ElevenLabs

ElevenLabs is an AI research and product company transforming how we interact with technology.

We launched in January 2023 with the first human-like AI voice model. Today, we serve millions of users and thousands of businesses - from fast-growing startups to large enterprises like Deutsche Telekom and Meta. Our investors are some of the world's most prominent, including Andreessen Horowitz, ICONIQ Growth and Sequoia. We've raised $781M in funding and our last valuation was $11B - multiples of 11, always.  
  
We have expanded from voice into three main platforms:

* ElevenAgents enables businesses to deliver seamless and intelligent customer experiences, with the integrations, testing, monitoring, and reliability necessary to deploy voice and chat agents at scale.
* ElevenCreative empowers creators and marketers to generate and edit speech, music, image, and video across 70+ languages.
* ElevenAPI gives developers access to our leading AI audio foundational models.

Everything we do is the result of the creativity and commitment of our team - builders doing the best work of their lives. We are researchers, engineers, and operators. IOI medalists and ex-founders. If you want to work hard and create lasting positive impact, we want to hear from you.

**About the role**

India is one of our fastest-growing markets, and we're looking for a highly motivated, self-starter Events Marketer to lead the planning and execution of impactful events across India and the wider APAC region. Your work will play a key role in deepening ElevenLabs' presence, driving brand awareness, and generating high-quality pipeline in partnership with our India marketing lead and our GTM teams.

In this role, you will own the full lifecycle of in-person owned events, third-party tech conferences, executive dinners, workshops, webinars, sponsorships, and strategic partnerships. You'll adapt and localize successful playbooks from our US and EU programs for the market, while also identifying new, high-impact opportunities unique to the region.

You will collaborate closely with sales to target the right audiences, build invite strategies, and ensure strong event follow-through. You'll partner with marketing and develop event toolkits, content, and drive post-event analysis to measure success and optimize future activations.

We're looking for someone who thrives in fast-paced environments, is comfortable managing multiple projects simultaneously, and brings creativity, operational excellence, and problem-solving to everything they touch. This is a high-ownership, high-autonomy role with the opportunity to build and scale ElevenLabs' event presence across India and APAC — and to create memorable experiences that deliver real business impact.

In this role, you will:

* Develop and execute event plans that align with business objectives and drive attendance and engagement
* Organise third-party conferences, owned branded events, and strategic breakfasts/dinners/lunches with key customers or potential clients
* Collaborate with cross-functional teams to ensure seamless execution of events, including venue selection, vendor management, and logistics, managing the full end to end planning process
* Create compelling event messaging and content that resonates with target audiences and drives interest and attendance
* Manage event budgets and timelines, ensuring all deliverables are completed on time and within budget
* Analyze event performance metrics and provide recommendations for future improvements and optimizations
* Follow up with participants in events in collaboration with the sales & growth teams
* Build and maintain relationships with key stakeholders, including sponsors, partners, and vendors
* Stay up-to-date on industry trends and best practices, and incorporate them into event marketing strategies to stay ahead of the competition
