## About ElevenLabs

ElevenLabs is an AI research and product company transforming how we interact with technology.

We launched in January 2023 with the first human-like AI voice model. Today, we serve millions of users and thousands of businesses - from fast-growing startups to large enterprises like Deutsche Telekom and Meta. Our investors are some of the world's most prominent, including Andreessen Horowitz, ICONIQ Growth and Sequoia. We've raised $781M in funding and our last valuation was $11B - multiples of 11, always.  
  
We have expanded from voice into three main platforms:

* ElevenAgents enables businesses to deliver seamless and intelligent customer experiences, with the integrations, testing, monitoring, and reliability necessary to deploy voice and chat agents at scale.
* ElevenCreative empowers creators and marketers to generate and edit speech, music, image, and video across 70+ languages.
* ElevenAPI gives developers access to our leading AI audio foundational models.

Everything we do is the result of the creativity and commitment of our team - builders doing the best work of their lives. We are researchers, engineers, and operators. IOI medalists and ex-founders. If you want to work hard and create lasting positive impact, we want to hear from you.

We're looking for an experienced partnerships leader to build and scale our Commercial Partners Programme in France — bringing together resellers, systems integrators, and consulting firms who help deploy ElevenLabs' technology to their clients across the French market.

You'll own the reseller, integrator, and consulting partner strategy end-to-end — from building relationships with leading SIs, consultancies, and resellers, to onboarding, enabling, and activating them to sell and deliver ElevenLabs products.

This is a 0→1 build: you'll design and pilot partner incentive and enablement models, generate qualified pipeline through partner-led opportunities, and scale the programme against regional growth targets, while owning day-to-day execution and partner performance.

**What Success Looks Like (First 6–9 Months)**

* Partner-Sourced & Influenced Revenue: closed-won ARR through GSIs, RSIs, cloud providers, VARs, and technology partners reaching at least 20% of regional revenue
* Partner-Sourced Pipeline: qualified pipeline created through partners, with a focus on enterprise opportunities in target industries
* Revenue-Producing Partners: number of partners actively generating pipeline, opportunities, or revenue each quarter
* Time-to-First Revenue: speed from partner onboarding to first sourced opportunity and first closed-won deal
* Strategic Account Penetration: new enterprise accounts and executive relationships unlocked through partners that ElevenLabs would've struggled to access directly

**Requirements**

* 12+ years building and scaling an in-country SaaS/Cloud solution, with a strong network you can leverage from day one
* 4+ years owning RSI, GSI, and hyperscaler partnerships, with proven experience driving joint account planning, co-sell, and revenue — not just relationship management
* A builder mindset — experience creating partner motions from 0→1, rather than inheriting a mature programme
* Comfortable working with technical, API-driven products, and able to explain complex concepts to partners and customers
* Bonus: Mix of 3-4 years in sales + 3-4 years in partnerships/alliances; mix of 3-4 years at a large company + 3-4 years at a smaller/less established company

**Location**

This role is remote-first but Paris is preferred.
