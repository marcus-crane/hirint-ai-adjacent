## About ElevenLabs

ElevenLabs is an AI research and product company transforming how we interact with technology.

We launched in January 2023 with the first human-like AI voice model. Today, we serve millions of users and thousands of businesses - from fast-growing startups to large enterprises like Deutsche Telekom and Meta. Our investors are some of the world's most prominent, including Andreessen Horowitz, ICONIQ Growth and Sequoia. We've raised $781M in funding and our last valuation was $11B - multiples of 11, always.  
  
We have expanded from voice into three main platforms:

* ElevenAgents enables businesses to deliver seamless and intelligent customer experiences, with the integrations, testing, monitoring, and reliability necessary to deploy voice and chat agents at scale.
* ElevenCreative empowers creators and marketers to generate and edit speech, music, image, and video across 70+ languages.
* ElevenAPI gives developers access to our leading AI audio foundational models.

Everything we do is the result of the creativity and commitment of our team - builders doing the best work of their lives. We are researchers, engineers, and operators. IOI medalists and ex-founders. If you want to work hard and create lasting positive impact, we want to hear from you.

## What we offer

* **Innovative culture:** You’ll be part of a generational opportunity to define the trajectory of AI, surrounded by a team pushing the boundaries of what’s possible.
* **Growth paths:** Joining ElevenLabs means joining a dynamic team with countless opportunities to drive impact - beyond your immediate role and responsibilities.

ElevenLabs is growing fast across EMEA, and our events programme needs to keep pace. We're looking for a contractor to take ownership of regional field events - trade shows, brand activations, and partner experiences - across multiple European markets.

This is a hands-on execution role. You'll work autonomously, move quickly, and hold a high bar for what a world-class event looks like. You'll be embedded in a lean team, so you'll need to be comfortable making decisions, managing vendors, and adapting on the fly without heavy oversight.

The right person for this role has done this before — ideally at a fast-moving tech or AI company — and knows what separates a forgettable event from one people talk about.

* Own end-to-end logistics and delivery for field events and brand activations across EMEA
* Manage third-party vendors, agencies, and venue relationships across multiple markets
* Support trade show presence including booth builds, sponsorship fulfilment, and on-site execution
* Coordinate with internal stakeholders across marketing, sales, and leadership to align on goals and messaging
* Identify and flag gaps - from branding and signage to attendee experience - and solve them proactively

**Requirements**

* Proven track record running events across multiple European markets (not just the UK)
* Comfortable owning both owned events and third-party sponsorships end-to-end
* Experienced working in lean, fast-moving environments where you set your own pace
* Strong vendor and agency management skills across different geographies
* Available to work on a contract basis with flexibility to travel across EMEA as needed

**Bonus**

* Experience in AI, developer tooling, or enterprise tech audiences
* Experience on Splash and the guest registration process
* Background in events agencies with brand-side account experience

**Location**

This role is remote-first, so it can be executed from anywhere in EMEA, with the ability to operate in CET/GMT timezones. If you prefer, you can work from our offices in Dublin, London, or Warsaw.
