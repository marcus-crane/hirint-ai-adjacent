Our mission is to automate coding. The first step in our journey is to build the best tool for professional programmers, using a combination of inventive research, design, and engineering. Our organization is very flat, and our team is small and talent dense. We particularly like people who are truth-seeking, passionate, and creative. We enjoy spirited debate, crazy ideas, and shipping code.

**About the Role**

Cursor's Business Development team drives our alternative routes to market: executive briefings, investor-convened roadshows, and zero-to-one market entry. This motion has opened enterprise markets across continents, converting executive access into qualified pipeline at a measured, proven rate.

We're looking for a Regional Vice President of Business Development to accelerate this motion in your region, working closely with our go-to-market leaders, customers, and partners to turn executive relationships into sourced pipeline for the account and partnership teams who carry those relationships forward.

**What You'll Do:**

* **Own our executive briefing network in-region.** Build and maintain relationships across our largest customers and prospects.
* **Plan and lead executive engagement.** Organize and facilitate meetings and multi-day trips for Cursor's executives in-region, directly owning the relationship until an account owner is assigned.
* **Drive new market entry and intelligence.** Identify sub-regions, industries, and strategies for net-new customer acquisition, and help establish our presence in them.
* **Execute our regional enterprise growth strategy.** Manage relationships across the full partner and customer lifecycle — from planning trips and roadshows to supporting account executives through close.
* **Forge new relationships.** Build connections with partners, customers, and stakeholders in the region that contribute to our growth.

**You May Be a Fit If You:**

* 10+ years of experience in business development, partnerships, venture capital, or sales

* Comfortable operating in ambiguity, with a track record of hitting goals without a playbook
* Have worked directly with customers and cross-functionally to get things done
* Self-starter with clear evidence of professional initiative
* Exceptional communicator with strong executive presence
* Take pride in being a "super IC,” someone who does exceptional individual work
* Familiar with the developer tooling ecosystem and current trends in AI
