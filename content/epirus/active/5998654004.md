## **About Epirus**

Epirus is a high-growth technology company dedicated to overcoming the asymmetric challenges inherent to the future of national security. Epirus' flagship product, Leonidas, is a software-defined system built using intelligent power management techniques which allow power-hungry systems to do more with less.  

**Job Summary:**

This is an exciting role in a growing company. Epirus designs and builds cutting-edge, high-powered microwave (HPM) systems transforming the defense industry. As we scale our operations, we're seeking a skilled Palantir Developer with a finance background to support data integration, reporting, and decision-support tooling across our business. This is a 3-month contract engagement with potential for extension based on business need.

## **Responsibilities:**

* Build, configure, and maintain Palantir Foundry pipelines, ontologies, and dashboards to support finance use cases
* Partner with Finance, FP&A, and Operations teams to translate business requirements into scalable, actionable data products
* Develop and maintain financial reporting workflows including budgeting, forecasting, cost tracking, and variance analysis applications
* Integrate data from ERP and other enterprise systems (Oracle, SAP, or similar) into Palantir Foundry
* Support ad hoc analyses and build executive-level dashboards for leadership visibility into financial performance and operational KPIs
* Support end-user training and adoption of newly developed Foundry applications

## **Basic Qualifications:**

* Bachelor’s degree in related field and a minimum 5 years of experience in Accounting, Finance or a related experience with minimum 3 years of hands-on experience with Palantir Foundry (Pipeline Builder, Ontology, Workshop/Slate)
* Background in finance, accounting, FP&A, or financial operations
* Experience integrating ERP or financial systems data (Oracle, SAP, or similar)
* Ability to communicate technical concepts clearly to non-technical stakeholders
* US Citizen and/or Permanent Resident (security clearance may be required)

 

## Preferred Skills and Experience:

* Familiarity with government cost accounting or program financials
* Proficiency in Python or PySpark within Foundry
* Experience building KPI frameworks and executive reporting tools
* Ability to work cross-functionally across finance, operations, and IT
* Comfortable operating independently in a fast-paced, high-growth environment
