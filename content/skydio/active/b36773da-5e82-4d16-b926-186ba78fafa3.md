Skydio is the leading US drone company and the world leader in autonomous flight, the key technology for the future of drones and aerial mobility. The Skydio team combines deep expertise in artificial intelligence, best-in-class hardware and software product development, operational excellence, and customer obsession to empower a broader, more diverse audience of drone users, from [utility inspectors](https://www.skydio.com/solutions/energy-and-utilities) to [first responders](https://www.skydio.com/solutions/public-safety), [soldiers in battlefield scenarios](https://www.skydio.com/solutions/national-security/tactical-isr), and [beyond](https://www.skydio.com/solutions).

**About the Role:**

Skydio is seeking a highly motivated Automation / Controls Engineer to design, implement, and support automated manufacturing systems for our drone production lines. This role will focus on integrating and optimizing control systems, robotics, and SCADA platforms to improve throughput, quality, and reliability.

**How You’ll Make an Impact:**

* Design, develop, and maintain control systems for automated manufacturing equipment
* Program and troubleshoot PLCs, HMIs, and SCADA systems (e.g., Ignition)
* Integrate and support industrial robotics used in:

  + Precision dispensing (adhesives, coatings, etc.)
  + Fastening (torque-controlled assembly systems)
  + Soldering (selective or robotic soldering systems)
* Develop and maintain system architectures, electrical schematics, and documentation
* Diagnose and resolve controls and automation issues on the production floor
* Collaborate with manufacturing, process, and quality engineering teams to optimize system performance
* Implement data collection and monitoring solutions to improve visibility and decision-making
* Ensure systems meet safety standards and comply with relevant regulations

**What Makes You a Good Fit:**

### **Required Qualifications**

* Bachelor’s degree in Electrical Engineering, Mechatronics, Controls Engineering, or related field
* 4-5+ years of experience in industrial automation or controls engineering
* Hands-on experience with SCADA systems (Ignition strongly preferred)
* Ability to troubleshoot complex electromechanical systems in a production environment

### **Preferred Qualifications**

* Experience with robotic systems for:

  + Dispensing applications
  + Automated fastening systems
  + Robotic or selective soldering
* Familiarity with machine vision systems
* Experience in high-volume or high-mix manufacturing environments
* Knowledge of MES integration and data systems
* Experience with motion control systems and servo drives
* Strong programming skills (Python, structured text, or similar)
* Obtaining FAA Part 107 certification within the first 60 days of employment is strongly encouraged for all Skydio employees and required for certain positions.
