Skydio is the leading US drone company and the world leader in autonomous flight, the key technology for the future of drones and aerial mobility. The Skydio team combines deep expertise in artificial intelligence, best-in-class hardware and software product development, operational excellence, and customer obsession to empower a broader, more diverse audience of drone users, from [utility inspectors](https://www.skydio.com/solutions/energy-and-utilities) to [first responders](https://www.skydio.com/solutions/public-safety), [soldiers in battlefield scenarios](https://www.skydio.com/solutions/national-security/tactical-isr), and [beyond](https://www.skydio.com/solutions).

**Position Overview**

We are seeking a reliable and detail-oriented Equipment & Inventory Specialist to support the Solutions Engineering team with daily inventory tracking, equipment preparation, logistical support, and operational readiness for a growing technical operations team.

This role is ideal for someone who enjoys hands-on work, organization, and supporting fast-paced field operations.

**This position will begin as a part-time 1099 contractor role with the opportunity to grow based on operational needs and performance.**

**Key Responsibilities**

* Inventory & Asset Management
* Track and maintain equipment inventory across multiple locations
* Update inventory spreadsheets and asset databases
* Monitor equipment movement between facilities and field teams
* Forecasting and ordering inventory with manufacturing and vendors
* Assist with receiving, organizing, and staging incoming equipment
* Equipment Preparation & Logistics
* Prepare equipment kits for shipment and deployment
* Perform basic inspections and readiness checks
* Label and organize equipment, cases, and accessories
* Assist with packing, shipping, LTL booking, and receiving operations
* Coordinate shipments between various teams at multiple locations
* Operational Support
* Maintain clean and organized storage/work areas
* Coordinate with operations leadership on equipment status
* Support documentation and process improvement efforts
* Assist with inventory audits and cycle counts
* Supporting ad hoc projects and demands at HQ as they arise

**Preferred Qualifications**

* Strong organizational skills and attention to detail
* Experience with logistics, warehouse operations, technical equipment, or inventory

  management.
* Comfortable working with spreadsheets and inventory tracking systems
* Ability to lift and move equipment cases and boxes as needed
* Self-motivated and dependable
* Familiarity with electronics or technical hardware is a plus
* Position Details
* Employment Type: Part-Time, 1099 Contractor
* Schedule: Flexible; approximately 25 hours per week initially
* Location: San Mateo, CA

**Ideal Candidate**

The ideal candidate is proactive, organized, and comfortable supporting operational workflows

in a dynamic technical environment. We’re looking for someone who can take ownership of

equipment organization and help improve overall operational efficiency.
