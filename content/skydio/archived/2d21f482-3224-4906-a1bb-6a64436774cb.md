Skydio is the leading US drone company and the world leader in autonomous flight, the key technology for the future of drones and aerial mobility. The Skydio team combines deep expertise in artificial intelligence, best-in-class hardware and software product development, operational excellence, and customer obsession to empower a broader, more diverse audience of drone users, from [utility inspectors](https://www.skydio.com/solutions/energy-and-utilities) to [first responders](https://www.skydio.com/solutions/public-safety), [soldiers in battlefield scenarios](https://www.skydio.com/solutions/national-security/tactical-isr), and [beyond](https://www.skydio.com/solutions).

**About the role:**

We are seeking a motivated and detail-oriented Supply Chain Intern to support our supply chain and operations team. This internship offers hands-on experience across supply chain and cross-functional collaboration. The intern will gain exposure to real-world supply chain challenges and contribute to projects that improve efficiency, visibility, and performance.  
  
**How you'll make an impact (example projects below):**

* Support supplier performance management by creating dashboards of on-time-delivery, quality, and cost
* Develop supply chain strategy to optimize for mitigating geo-political risks by mapping existing supply chain as-is, derive insights, make recommendations for the to-be supply chain architecture.
* Establish product costing process to manage standard costing, risks and opportunities and provide ability to see cost trends

**What makes you a good fit:**

* Currently pursuing a degree in Supply Chain, Operations, Industrial Engineering, Business, or related field
* Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
* Proficiency in Excel (pivot tables, VLOOKUP/XLOOKUP, basic formulas)
* Ability to work with data and draw meaningful insights
* Strong attention to detail

**Compensation Range:** The hourly base salary range for this position is $41/hr for Undergrad, $47/hr for Graduate students, and $53 for PhD students\*. Compensation will vary based on factors, including skill level, proficiencies, transferable knowledge, and experience. In addition to base salary, Skydio full-time employees are eligible to enroll in our benefit plans and take advantage of a variety of incentives and stipends.

\*For some positions the pay may be dependent upon the individual's regional location.
