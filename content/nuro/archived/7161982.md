**Who We Are**

Nuro is a self-driving technology company on a mission to make autonomy accessible to all. Founded in 2016, Nuro is building the world’s most scalable driver, combining cutting-edge AI with automotive-grade hardware. Nuro licenses its core technology, the Nuro Driver™, to support a wide range of applications, from robotaxis and commercial fleets to personally owned vehicles. With technology proven over years of self-driving deployments, Nuro gives the automakers and mobility platforms a clear path to AVs at commercial scale—empowering a safer, richer, and more connected future.

**About the Role**We are looking for an experienced mechanical designer to join our world class team of engineers in our Autonomous Vehicle Hardware group. In this role you will be responsible for the research, design, and delivery of our autonomous hardware solutions that enable Nuro’s autonomous systems. This work spans electronics and sensor packaging, technology development, and vehicle integrations to support customer programs. You’ll be developing the hardware solutions that safely and reliably enables our L4 autonomy system across platforms, use cases, and operational conditions..

**About the Work**

* Own the engineering design, development, and release of autonomous hardware necessary to enable the Nuro Driver system across customer platforms.
* Lead the specification and integration in collaboration with our vehicle partners to ensure platform integrations meet the performance needs of our Nuro Driver system and satisfy customer product requirements.
* Work collaboratively across cross-functional teams to develop and execute sensing and electronics architecture for self-driving robots designed for mass deployment on public roads.
* Lead efforts to benchmark performance, integration, and reliability of available electronics technologies that drives internal make vs. buy decisions.
* Own the identification and key suppliers to bring ideas from concept to production readiness.
* Manage designed parts and assemblies through proof-of-concept validation up through production readiness.
* Drive part quality improvements, cost reductions, and efficiency improvements.
* Communicate technical decisions, development plans, and ongoing issues to management.

**About You**

* 4+ years of experience in mechanical design with a proven track record on design, build, and test of electro-mechanical hardware.
* Experience working across the entire production pipeline from personally hand building prototypes to working with quality and manufacturing teams in mass production factory environments.
* Experience using a wide range of manufacturing processes from small scale to high volume, such as rapid prototyping, sheet metal, injection molding, machining, and casting.
* Experience designing for a wide range of materials with ability to create optimal geometry based on cost, weight, strength/durability, and thermal analysis, using both FEA and hand calculations.
* Experience working with reliability teams to create/execute test plans that validate your designs.
* Experience working with fabrication and module vendors to communicate design requirements, incorporate DFM feedback, manage initial production runs, and oversee validation testing.
* Self-starter with strong communication and organizational skills — relentlessly resourceful, with a bias towards action.
* Proficiency with CAD is a must and familiarity with UG NX is a plus.
* Willing and able to support infrequent domestic and international travel.
