**Who We Are**

Nuro believes self-driving vehicles are the most immediate and profound opportunity for AI to drive positive change in the physical world. Safer streets, more time for what matters, and easier access to the world around us—that’s why we’re building a universal autonomy platform: self-driving for all roads and all rides.

Founded in 2016, Nuro is a physical AI company developing Level 4 autonomous driving technology for a wide range of vehicles, use cases, and markets. Powered by the Nuro Driver™, our universal autonomy platform enables the global mobility ecosystem to deploy autonomy at scale—from robotaxis and logistics fleets to personal vehicles.

With years of real-world deployment experience and a flexible, partner-led business model, Nuro is working toward a future where millions of autonomous vehicles powered by our technology help make everyday life safer, easier, and more connected.

Nuro has raised over $2B in capital from Uber, NVIDIA, Google, Softbank, Fidelity, T. Rowe Price, and other leading investors.

**About the Role**We're looking for a Product Manager to own the data infrastructure and labeling systems that power our autonomous vehicle development. Data quality is one of the most critical inputs to our ML and Behavior teams, and this role will build the systems and workflows that give those teams confidence in the data they depend on—shipping dashboards that eliminate manual reconciliation, defining labeling standards that prevent costly rework, and creating infrastructure that scales as our fleet grows. This is a high-ownership role where you'll work across Engineering, Operations, ML, and external vendors to turn processes into reliable, measurable systems.

**About the Work**

* **Build visibility into data.** Ship dashboards that reconcile mileage across sources, show collection coverage, and let ML and Behavior teams see what data exists and what's missing.
* **Optimize data collection.** Define requirements for smarter data collection routing to improve targeting efficiency—fewer miles driven, higher-value data captured.
* **Scale the infrastructure.** Develop tooling to support 150+ vehicles operating across multiple cities.
* **Raise the bar on labeling quality.** Own labeling specs approval, define pass/fail quality thresholds, and design a vendor audit framework that gives us quantified confidence in output.
* **Own the labeling tooling roadmap.**Prioritize engineering investment in labeling infrastructure and identify cost reduction opportunities (pre-labeling, smarter task routing, reducing redundant QA).

**About You**

* **You take ownership of messy problems.** You're energized, not discouraged, by ambiguity. You'll inherit fragmented processes and incomplete systems—and you'll build structure where none exists.
* **You ship.** You bias toward action and delivering working solutions over perfect plans. You know when "good enough now" beats "great later."
* **You're deeply analytical.** You're comfortable digging into data pipelines, writing SQL, and building your own visibility when dashboards don't exist yet.
* **You operate at both altitude and ground level.** You can define a long-term infrastructure vision and also roll up your sleeves to document acceptance criteria or audit vendor output.
* **You communicate crisply across technical and non-technical audiences.** You'll work daily with engineers, ML researchers, operations teams, and external vendors—and need to translate between all of them.
* **You hold systems accountable.** You don't just launch processes; you measure them, find where they break, and fix them.
* **You have 5+ years of product management experience**, ideally with exposure to data platforms, ML infrastructure, operations tooling, or vendor management.
