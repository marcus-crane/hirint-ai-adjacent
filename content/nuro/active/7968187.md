**Who We Are**

Nuro is a self-driving technology company on a mission to make autonomy accessible to all. Founded in 2016, Nuro is building the world’s most scalable driver, combining cutting-edge AI with automotive-grade hardware. Nuro licenses its core technology, the Nuro Driver™, to support a wide range of applications, from robotaxis and commercial fleets to personally owned vehicles. With technology proven over years of self-driving deployments, Nuro gives the automakers and mobility platforms a clear path to AVs at commercial scale, empowering a safer, richer, and more connected future.

**About the Role**

The Operations team is looking for a Shift Supervisor to build a best-in-class teleoperator team that successfully executes on our operational missions and meets Nuro's challenging goals. As a front-line manager embedded in Command Center Operations (CCO), you will be responsible for hiring and onboarding operators, actively managing their growth and performance, and leading continuous improvement processes and projects.

You'll work closely with cross-functional stakeholders within operations and with other teams to ensure the team's work remains a valuable part of the company's metrics. Guardian Operations is Nuro's teleoperation platform enabling remote human operators to supervise and intervene in autonomous vehicle behavior via Indirect Control (IC) — supporting vehicles operating on public roads across Bay Area, Houston, LA, and LVMS.

**About the Work**

**People Management:**

* Run the end-to-end interview process and successfully execute onboarding for new hires in line with the plan, in partnership with L&D
* Lead and manage a team of 6-10 teleoperators, providing feedback, guidance, training oversight, and coaching/mentoring
* Own performance reviews in line with established evaluation metrics and collaborate with stakeholders to update them as needed
* Manage Day 1 onboarding logistics including account creation, orientation meetings, lunch scheduling, and timecard adjustments

**Execution Management:**

* Lead Start-of-Shift and End-of-Shift activities including operator attendance, performance tracking, and project management
* Work closely with the Planning team to ensure operators are trained and ready when new features and capabilities roll out
* Understand weekly goals and allocation strategy; provide visibility into operator availability on a periodic basis
* Respond to operator and on-site incidents per internally established procedures
* Actively review plan vs. actual performance weekly to understand deviation drivers and identify action items  
  Ability to work cross-functionally with engineers and product teams to provide feedback for continuous improvement
* Ensure all operators complete Fleetio checklists and OBS recording requirements for every assigned mission
* Ensure mission summaries are completed and posted to #guardian\_mission\_summaries after each mission
* Drive continuous improvement processes and projects to enhance efficiency and throughput
* Engage peers across operating locations to share best practices and drive standardization
