### **Who We Are**

Nuro is a self-driving technology company on a mission to make autonomy accessible to all. Founded in 2016, Nuro is building the world’s most scalable driver, combining cutting-edge AI with automotive-grade hardware. Nuro licenses its core technology, the Nuro Driver™, to support a wide range of applications, from robotaxis and commercial fleets to personally owned vehicles. With technology proven over years of self-driving deployments, Nuro gives the automakers and mobility platforms a clear path to AVs at commercial scale, empowering a safer, richer, and more connected future.

### **About the Role**

As a member of Nuro’s Technical Recruiting team, you will identify, engage, and close world-class talent across Machine Learning Infrastructure, Data Infrastructure, Data Platform, and Core Infrastructure Engineering. You will partner closely with our engineering leaders to build high-impact teams that drive Nuro’s AI-first strategy and long-term technical vision.

### **About the Work**

* Manage the full-cycle technical recruiting process, including top-of-funnel strategy, proactive sourcing, driving searches, and leading offers and closing.
* Partner with engineering leaders across ML Infrastructure, Data Platform, Developer Productivity, and Core Infrastructure to shape hiring strategy, define role profiles, and ensure a highly effective and structured interview process.
* Use data and analytics to influence hiring decisions, identify bottlenecks, and continuously improve recruiting velocity and quality.
* Maintain our high hiring bar by assessing both technical strength and cultural alignment, ensuring each hire elevates the team.
* Collaborate with sourcing and recruiting partners to build strong pipelines, implement new tools and workflows, and increase the overall efficiency of our recruiting engine.

### **About You**

* You are a hunter with a track record of creatively identifying and engaging deeply technical, high-caliber talent without relying on job boards or databases.
* You have a minimum of 5 years of in-house technical recruiting experience at high-growth technology companies or start-ups, ideally with a focus on infrastructure or platform engineering.
* You thrive in environments with a very high hiring bar and are discerning in evaluating engineering talent across systems, data, platform, and ML infrastructure domains.
* You are an engaging communicator who builds trust quickly with both candidates and hiring partners.
* You are highly self-motivated with exceptional prioritization skills, capable of driving multiple searches in parallel with urgency and clarity.
* You are intellectually curious, able to quickly grasp complex technical concepts, and comfortable partnering with senior engineering leaders.
* You operate with integrity, sound judgment, and a strong work ethic.
* You are passionate about building world-class engineering organizations and excited by the impact of highly scalable infrastructure.

### **Bonus Points**

* Experience recruiting ML infrastructure, distributed systems, data platform, or large-scale infrastructure engineers.
* Experience supporting diversity and inclusion initiatives.
* Familiarity with Greenhouse ATS.
