**Who We Are**

Nuro is a self-driving technology company on a mission to make autonomy accessible to all. Founded in 2016, Nuro is building the world’s most scalable driver, combining cutting-edge AI with automotive-grade hardware. Nuro licenses its core technology, the Nuro Driver™, to support a wide range of applications, from robotaxis and commercial fleets to personally owned vehicles. With technology proven over years of self-driving deployments, Nuro gives the automakers and mobility platforms a clear path to AVs at commercial scale, empowering a safer, richer, and more connected future.

**About the Role**We're looking for an execution-focused People & Talent Partner to join our People Success team. You'll own delivery of people programs spanning performance, engagement, onboarding, and L&D, while serving as a reliable operational resource for our HR Business Partners.

**About the Work**

* Support end-to-end execution of bi-annual performance review cycles, including system configuration, training, communications, and calibration support
* Support the annual employee engagement survey from setup through results analysis and action planning
* Run quarterly Cultural Champions and bi-monthly AI Coding Contest recognition programs
* Partner with the People Success team to develop and scale AI fluency and L&D programs
* Drive the new hire experience through orientation, quarterly events, new hire spotlights, and early-tenure 360-degree feedback check-ins
* Identify and implement AI-driven automation across people workflows to reduce manual lift and surface program insights
* Partner with the People Success team to explore and prototype net-new possibilities enabled by AI tooling, bringing structure and follow-through to early-stage ideas before they become formal initiatives
* Tools: Workday, Lattice (or equivalent platforms), and comfort navigating AI tools (e.g., Claude, Claude Code, AI Studio or similar) in day-to-day work
* Support HRBPs on org and employee changes: reorgs, transfers, mobility, manager transitions, exits, and Workday headcount updates
* Manage compliance processes including leave of absence, accommodations, and related documentation
* Own RTO remote exception requests and non-compliance triage, escalating to HRBPs as needed
* Serve as Tier 1 point of contact for employee HR questions, routing complex matters to the appropriate HRBP

**About You**

* 3+ years in a Talent Management/Program Management/HR Generalist role, preferably in a fast-paced or tech-driven environment.
* Excellent organizational skills with strong attention to detail.
* Ability to prioritize and manage multiple tasks in a dynamic and ambiguous environment.
* Strong verbal and written communication skills to effectively collaborate across teams.
* Proactive, solution-oriented mindset with the ability to identify trends.
* Genuine curiosity about AI and emerging HR technology, you follow what's changing, experiment with new tools, and bring ideas about what's newly possible
* Demonstrated integrity, professionalism, and discretion with sensitive information.
* Program management skills with a structured, solutions-focused approach.
* Proficiency in Google Suite and HRIS platforms
* Bachelor’s degree in Human Resources, Business Administration, or a related field.

**Bonus Points** 

* Background in Recruiting, Learning and Development (L&D), or Talent Management.
* Professional HR certification (PHR, SPHR, etc.).
