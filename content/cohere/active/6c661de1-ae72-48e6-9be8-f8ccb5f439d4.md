**About this role:**

We are seeking a high-caliber leader to stand up a top-notch Finance PMO (Project Management Office). This is a high-visibility, high-impact role designed for a strategic operator who thrives in the "gray space." You will be the glue between Finance, Legal, Engineering and Product, ensuring our most critical corporate milestones are executed with precision.

**As our head of Finance PMO you will manage:**

**M&A Project Management Office**

* Establish the ‘M&A Playbook’ for the company, covering everything from initial due diligence to post-merger integration.
* Functional Due Diligence: Coordinate across Engineering, HR, Legal, and Finance to identify risks and synergies during the deal phase.
* Integration Lead: Drive the 100-day integration plan for newly acquired entities, ensuring culture, systems, and financial reporting are unified seamlessly.

**Strategic Planning Cycle Management**

* AOP Orchestration: Architect and lead the end-to-end Annual Operating Plan (AOP) process, including the design of integrated planning timelines, standardized financial templates, and executive stage-gate reviews to ensure organizational alignment on fiscal goals.
* Resource Capacity Validation: Drive strategic resource planning by performing gap analyses between proposed Product/Engineering roadmaps and current headcount constraints, ensuring the company has the necessary headcount to execute its highest-priority initiatives.

**Special Projects**

* Act as the "special ops" lead for finance leadership on high-priority projects that don't have a natural home.
* Process Optimization: Identify bottlenecks in our current financial operations and design scalable, automated solutions.

**This career opportunity may be a good match for you if you have:**

* 8-10+ year progressive work experience Management Consulting, Strategic Finance/Planning, or a PMO lead role within a high-growth AI or SaaS environment
* Experience with M&A cycles preferred (from DD to integration)
* Strong interpersonal and communication skills, including effective communication skills at all levels within the company and the ability to collaborate across functions.
* Proven ability to deliver change, improve and streamline processes, drive efficiency, and influence stakeholders.
* Ability to thrive in fast-paced, constantly involving environments. Startup/scaleup experience is a plus.

If some of the above doesn’t line up perfectly with your experience, we still encourage you to apply!
