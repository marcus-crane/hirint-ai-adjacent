**Who are we?**

Cohere is at the forefront of AI innovation, building cutting-edge language models and AI systems. Our Finance team plays a critical role in supporting our rapid growth and ensuring operational excellence. We value technical expertise, collaborative problem-solving, and a commitment to accuracy and compliance. This role offers the opportunity to make a significant impact on our financial infrastructure while working at the intersection of finance and transformative technology. As we scale our business and navigate an evolving financial landscape, you'll play a pivotal role in driving operational excellence and serving as a key partner on accounting implications of major business decisions.

**In this role, you will:**

* Own the full lifecycle management of our NetSuite ERP environment, including architecture design, configuration optimization, integration development, and strategic roadmap planning to support General Ledger, AP, AR, Procure-to-Pay, Fixed Assets, Revenue Recognition, Audit, and International Consolidations & Reporting
* Drive end-to-end process excellence across Order-to-Cash and Procure-to-Pay workflows, identifying and resolving gaps between upstream systems and ERP through disciplined integration design and implementation
* Identify and implement automation opportunities across finance workflows, with specific focus on deploying AI agents to replace manual processes and enhance operational efficiency
* Establish and maintain robust operational controls, including disciplined change management, comprehensive data governance frameworks, and measurable SLAs across all financial systems to ensure audit readiness and SOX compliance
* Partner with cross-functional teams including Finance, Operations, IT, and Engineering to translate business requirements into scalable system solutions and drive continuous improvement
* Lead system enhancements, upgrades, and third-party integrations while ensuring data integrity, scalability, and operational reliability across the broader application landscape
* Develop and maintain documentation, training materials, and user support processes to champion best practices across finance teams
* Monitor system performance, troubleshoot issues, and implement proactive measures to maintain system stability and security

**You may be a good fit if you:**

* Have 7+ years of hands-on experience with NetSuite ERP administration and configuration across multiple financial modules
* Possess deep expertise in end-to-end financial workflows including AP, AR, GL, month-end close, and consolidation processes
* Have experience with AI/LLM technologies and have successfully implemented automation solutions in finance contexts
* Bring excellent analytical and problem-solving skills with attention to detail and commitment to data accuracy
* Have strong stakeholder management abilities and can build trusted relationships with finance leadership and cross-functional teams
* Are passionate about leveraging technology to transform manual processes and create exceptional operational efficiency
* Possess a bachelor's degree in Information Systems, Computer Science, Accounting, Finance, or related field
* Have experience working in fast-paced, dynamic environments with evolving requirements and priorities
