## **About the Role**

We’re seeking an experienced Manager, Real Estate & Facilities to join our team at Cohere. This position is perfect for someone who wants to take over a comprehensive operational portfolio that combines strategic real estate management with hands-on facilities oversight. You’ll be the point person for our global broker relationship (JLL), lease negotiations, and overall workplace strategy & operations.

## **Key Responsibilities**

### **Strategic Real Estate Management**

* Lead all lease negotiations, renewals, and terminations for our global portfolio
* Manage and optimize our relationship with JLL as our global brokerage partner
* Align headcount vs. office space strategy with FP&A and HR on budget parameters, ensuring offices are managed accordingly
* Conduct market analysis and site selection in conjunction with the Head of Procurement, HR and Leadership for new locations and expansions
* Oversee portfolio performance metrics and financial reporting
* Manage real estate budget for the company

### **Facilities Operations & Vendor Management**

**Vendor Management & Negotiation**

* Negotiate contracts with vendors for cleaning, security, maintenance, and other facility services
* Manage vendor relationships (including JLL as a key vendor partner), performance evaluations, and continuous improvement initiatives

**Operations, Compliance & Implementation**

* Consolidate and streamline FM services across all office locations
* Direct physical security operations and compliance across all sites
* Develop and implement facilities policies, procedures, and sustainability initiatives
* Manage facility budgets, capital planning, and expense optimization
* Oversee facility moves, reconfigurations, and workplace experience enhancements
* Ensure compliance with building codes, safety regulations, and lease agreements

**This role may require occasional travel to visit office locations and conduct operational reviews.**

## **Qualifications & Experience**

### **Required**

* **Experience:** 8-10 years in combined real estate and facilities management
* **Broker/Vendor Management:** Demonstrated success managing relationships with global brokerage firms and vendors
* **Lease Expertise:** Strong background in commercial lease negotiation and administration
* **Strategic Thinking:** Ability to link operational decisions to business strategy and growth objectives
* **Financial Partnership:** Experience managing budgets in partnership with FP&A to ensure budget control and optimization

### **Preferred**

* **Multi-site Portfolio:** Experience managing real estate/facilities across multiple locations
* **CRE/FM Certifications:** CPM, CFM, or similar professional designations
* **Technology Proficiency:** Experience with FM software, lease management systems, and data analytics
