## 工作内容

1. Strategic Business Development

Identify, incubate, and convert medium to long term AI opportunities (LLM, CV, AI platforms, AIDC related services).

Shape client demand by translating policy, operational, and industry challenges into scalable AI solution roadmaps.

Own the full opportunity lifecycle from early engagement, PoC structuring, and commercial negotiation to contract closure.

2. Government & Institutional Account Leadership

Lead strategic relationships with Government departments, statutory bodies, and large public institutions.

Navigate public procurement processes, tenders, framework agreements, and phased deployment models.

Work closely with clients to align AI initiatives with regulatory, security, and governance requirements.

3. Solution & Platform Selling

Position SenseTime’s AI platforms (e.g., LLMs, vision platforms, AI infrastructure services) as long term capability enablers rather than standalone projects.

Collaborate with presales, product, and delivery teams to design technically and commercially viable solutions.

Ensure solution proposals balance customer value, delivery feasibility, and margin sustainability.

4. Ecosystem & Partner Enablement

Develop and manage partnerships with system integrators, telecom operators, cloud/hardware providers, and local solution partners.

Structure joint go-to-market models and partner-led delivery strategies where appropriate.

Act as the commercial lead in multi-party engagements and consortium-style opportunities.

5. Revenue & Pipeline Ownership

Deliver annual revenue targets while building a qualified multi-year pipeline aligned with strategic priorities.

Provide accurate forecasting, deal risk assessment, and pipeline visibility to management.

Support internal planning by feeding market intelligence and customer feedback into product and strategy discussions.

## 任职要求

1. 8–12+ years of experience in enterprise, government, or solution-based B2B sales, preferably in AI, software, cloud, or ICT sectors.

2. Proven track record managing complex, high-value deals with long sales cycles.

3. Strong understanding of Government & Public Sector procurement and stakeholder environments in Hong Kong and/or Macau.
