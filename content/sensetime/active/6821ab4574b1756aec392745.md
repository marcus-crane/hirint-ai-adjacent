## 工作内容

• Own the strategy, roadmap, and delivery of B2C AI-powered software products with a strong focus on user value and business impact.

• Define product goals and success metrics aligned with company’s focused local strategy and ensure execution through effective planning and cross-functional collaboration.

• Work closely with R&D teams to translate high-level product vision into clear, technically feasible development plans.

• Drive product prioritization based on business value, technical complexity, and user impact.

• Continuously analyze user data, market trends, and competitive landscape to identify new opportunities and areas for product evolution.

• Manage the end-to-end product lifecycle, including launch strategies, performance tracking, and iteration based on feedback and analytics.

• Collaborate with stakeholders across engineering, business, operations, and compliance to ensure delivery excellence and cross-team alignment.

## 任职要求

• Must be based in Riyadh; remote work is not supported.

• 5+ years of product management experience, preferably in AI, mobile/web platforms, or technology startups.

• English as working language. Great communication skills in English(spoken and written) are required.

• Proven experience delivering consumer-facing software products, ideally involving AI capabilities. Ability to lead product initiatives across cross-functional teams in a complex, fast-growing or start-up environment.

• Strong business acumen with a clear understanding of product-market fit, ROI, and growth levers.

• Familiarity with innovative product development, data-driven iteration, and stakeholder management.

• Experience working closely with engineering teams and understanding technical trade-offs.

• Bachelor or master’s degree or above in Computer Science, Engineering, Business, or a related field.
