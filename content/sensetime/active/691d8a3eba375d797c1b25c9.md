## 工作内容

Conduct strategic and market research in AI and technology sectors to identify growth opportunities in Middle East.

Evaluate, structure, and execute corporate investment and M&A opportunities.

Support fundraising activities and investor relations, working closely with the CEO to engage existing and potential investors.

Collaborate with cross-functional teams (product, sales, legal, finance) to ensure alignment of corporate development initiatives with company goals.

## 任职要求

12+ years of relevant experience in investment firms (PE/VC/IBD) or in corporate strategy/corporate development roles within leading technology or diversified companies.

Proven track record in AI/technology-related investment, M&A, or corporate strategy projects.

Strong financial analysis, deal execution, and strategic planning skills.

Excellent English communication skills (both written and spoken)

Overseas education or work experience required.
