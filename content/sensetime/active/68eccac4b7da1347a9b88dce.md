## 工作内容

Establish, plan and execute the engagement strategies with key public-sector stakeholders with a view of enhancing the Company’s corporate positioning.

Strategize and execute effective public affairs programmes, and manage the relationship and communication with all stakeholders.

Forge and maintain partnerships with institutions such as universities, research institutes, industry associations, and industry alliances to foster greater cooperation within the ecosystem and maintain network of industry leaders.

Collect, organize, and analyze national/regional and industry policies and regulations, providing policy support for the company's business development and high-level decision-making.

Assist and perform other corporate development affairs duties as required.

and publicity.

## 任职要求

Degree in Business, Mass Communications, Language Studies, Political Science or related disciplines.

5 years’ experience in Management Consulting, Government Relations in large-scale organisations or with proven record of collaboration with government departments.

Excellent communication, writing and organisational skills.

Excellent problem solving skills, ability to perform under pressure.

Excellent written and verbal communication skills in English and Chinese (both Putonghua and Cantonese).

Strong sensitivity in current affairs and political landscape, and versatile in issues management, stakeholder engagement and publicity.
