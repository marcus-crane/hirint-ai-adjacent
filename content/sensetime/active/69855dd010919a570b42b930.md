## 工作内容

1.Project Delivery & Implementation

Execute the technical deployment and configuration of software solutions according to project plans and client requirements.

Collaborate with Project Managers and Service Delivery Managers to track milestones, manage risks, and ensure on-time delivery.

Create and maintain comprehensive technical documentation, including deployment guides, runbooks, and handover materials for clients and support teams.

Provide technical support during the UAT (User Acceptance Testing) and go-live phases.

2.DevOps

Containerize applications using Docker and manage container orchestration with Kubernetes.

Automate routine operational tasks to improve efficiency and reduce manual errors.

3.Operations & Monitoring

Monitor system performance, availability, and security metrics.

Act as a primary responder for incidents, performing troubleshooting, root cause analysis, and implementing fixes to ensure SLAs are met.

Perform system maintenance, including patching, upgrades, and backups.

Implement and improve monitoring, alerting, and logging strategies.

4.Collaboration & Support

Work closely with development teams to ensure applications are designed for scalability, reliability, and operability.

Serve as a liaison between project delivery teams and clients for technical queries and status updates during the delivery and early support phases.

Participate in overnight deployment or support for critical production systems (if required).

## 任职要求

Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Systems, Engineering, or a related field.

3+ years of experience in a DevOps, SRE, System Administration, or technical project delivery role.

Proven experience in deploying and supporting enterprise-level applications.

Strong knowledge of Linux/Unix administration and shell scripting (Bash).

Proficiency in at least one scripting language (Python, Go, or similar).

Practical experience with containerization and orchestration (Docker, Kubernetes).

Understanding of networking fundamentals, security principles, and monitoring concepts.

Excellent problem-solving and analytical skills.

Strong sense of ownership and a proactive, automation-focused mindset.

Ability to manage multiple tasks and projects in a fast-paced environment.

Effective communication skills to collaborate with both technical and non-technical stakeholders.

Customer-oriented attitude with a focus on delivering high-quality service.

Excellent command of Chinese, understanding in Cantonese is a plus.

Professional proficiency in English (reading and writing), with the ability to handle English technical documentation.
