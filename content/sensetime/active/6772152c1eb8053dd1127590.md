## 工作内容

Develop and implement a comprehensive sales strategy targeting specific industries.

Drive business development by identifying new market opportunities, developing strong relationships with key stakeholders, and converting leads into strategic accounts.

Implement a data-driven approach to managing and prioritizing sales pipelines, ensuring that sales targets are met or exceeded consistently.

Coordinate and collaborate with cross-functional teams (e.g., product, engineering, and marketing) to ensure alignment on sales goals, customer needs, and product offerings.

Build and maintain long-term relationships with decision-makers in transportation authorities, entertainment companies, hospitality providers, and city planners.

## 任职要求

15+ years of experience in sales or business development with at least 5 years in a leadership role within required industries

Proven track record of sales success in complex B2B environments, ideally related to technology, smart infrastructure, IoT, and urban solutions.

Excellent negotiation, presentation, and communication skills, with the ability to engage executives and key decision-makers.
