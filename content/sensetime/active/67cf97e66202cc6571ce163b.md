## 工作内容

Oversee the successful delivery of the projects to ensure projects are delivered on time, within budget and to the required standards. Focuses on:

- Internal R&D resource management: follow through from requirements gathering to development, SIT, UAT and production. Make

- External vendor management: cost / quality / timeline management

- Client satisfaction management.

## 任职要求

Extensive experience (15+ years) in software development, project management, or IT services.

At least 5+ years in a leadership role managing large teams, complex projects, and client relationships.

Experience in managing multiple delivery projects at the same time, often with varying timelines and client requirements.

Proven track record of successfully delivering software solutions on time and within budget
