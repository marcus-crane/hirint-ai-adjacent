**📐 About this role**

WRITER is growing rapidly in our New York office, one of our three major global hubs. The Workplace experience manager for our New York office will ensure that our NYC colleagues (100 and growing!) have an incredible daily experience that helps them do their best work.

From the physical environment and layout to food & beverage (daily lunch, snacks) to internal and external events, you will have a wide and creative canvas to support, surprise, and delight our NYC WRITER colleagues. This role is office-based in New York City at 1 Pennsylvania Plaza, with 5 days in the office every week. This role will also provide structure remote support for our Chicago, Illinois office, and secondary support for our London, UK office.

**🦸🏻‍♀️ Your responsibilities:**

* Create an amazing daily experience for our WRITER colleagues and their guests
* Manage inventory to ensure the office is well equipped with snacks, beverages, supplies
* Maintain office budget and ensure financial stewardship and high ROI for our spend
* Organize and support internal and external company events and meetings including our new hire onboarding program and our weekly global All Hands
* Own space and capacity planning and continually enhance office layout and seating plan
* Manage mail, shipping & receiving, and other office logistics
* Work with property management and vendors for various services, repair, and other support
* Create office policies and processes to drive efficiency and better employee experiences

**⭐️ Is this you?**

* Previous experience as an office manager, administrator, or similar role

* Experience in fast-growing, fast-changing tech companies a strong plus

* Great customer service and hospitality mindset, friendly and approachable
* Strong attention to details that impact employee experience
* Proactive problem solver who anticipates and resolves issues
* Excellent at prioritization, time management, and juggling of many tasks
* Drawn to and already live by WRITER’s company values of Connect, Challenge, Own
