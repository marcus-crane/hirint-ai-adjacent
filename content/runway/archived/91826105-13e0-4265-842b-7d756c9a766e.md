We are building AI to simulate the world through merging art and science.  
  
We believe that world models are at the frontier of progress in artificial intelligence. Language models alone won’t solve the world’s hardest problems – robotics, disease, scientific discovery. Real progress requires models that experience the world and learn from their mistakes, the same way that humans do. And this kind of trial and error can be massively accelerated when done in simulation, rather than in the real world.  
  
World models offer the most clear path to general-purpose simulation, changing how stories are told, how scientific progress is made and how the next frontiers of humanity are reached.

Our team consists of creative, open minded, caring and ambitious people who are determined to change the world. We aspire to continuously build impossible things and our ability to do so relies on building an incredible team. If you are driven to do the same, we'd love to hear from you.

## About the role

\**Open to hiring remote across North America and EU — we also have offices in NYC, San Francisco, London and Seattle.*

We're looking for a multi-faceted creative to join our social media team helping to evolve Runway's presence across Instagram, YouTube, LinkedIn, TikTok and X. You'll help evolve and mature our channel-specific strategies, build out content pillars and the systems that drive how Runway shows up on social.

## What you’ll do

* Create and publish content for our social channels — short-form video and platform-native formats
* Work with our Growth team to support paid influencer partnerships
* Collaborate with creative, product marketing and comms to align around launches, campaigns and cultural moments
* Make bold and ambitious work that pushes the space forward

## What you’ll need

* 3-5 years in social media
* Deep platform fluency across Instagram, YouTube, TikTok, LinkedIn, and X — you know what works natively on each and why
* Strong editorial and generative skills with a high proficiency in Runway
* A portfolio of work that shows both strategic thinking and hands-on content craft
* An insatiable creative curiosity and the ability to operate autonomously in a fast-paced and ever changing environment. Owning work end-to-end
* Live and breathe the generative AI space and have a passion for using the tools
