We are building AI to simulate the world through merging art and science.  
  
We believe that world models are at the frontier of progress in artificial intelligence. Language models alone won’t solve the world’s hardest problems – robotics, disease, scientific discovery. Real progress requires models that experience the world and learn from their mistakes, the same way that humans do. And this kind of trial and error can be massively accelerated when done in simulation, rather than in the real world.  
  
World models offer the most clear path to general-purpose simulation, changing how stories are told, how scientific progress is made and how the next frontiers of humanity are reached.

Our team consists of creative, open minded, caring and ambitious people who are determined to change the world. We aspire to continuously build impossible things and our ability to do so relies on building an incredible team. If you are driven to do the same, we'd love to hear from you.

### **About the role**

Runway is rapidly scaling to users and enterprises around the world. We are looking for a senior IC to operationalize, activate, and scale relationships across Runway's most strategic partnerships. This is a role for someone who wants to own whatever needs owning, take ambiguous and high-stakes opportunities from zero to launched, and write the playbook where none exists yet. You will work closely across Product, Research, Commercial, and Creative.

### **What you’ll do**

* Run point on some of Runway's most strategic partnerships across product, GTM, ecosystem, and bespoke strategic deals that don't fit any single bucket
* Take complex, ambiguous opportunities from zero to launched — sourcing, structuring, negotiating, executing, and operationalizing — without waiting for a playbook to be handed to you
* Foster deep, trusted relationships with executives and senior leadership at our most important partners, from large technology companies and Fortune 100 enterprises to the content platforms shaping the next decade of AI
* Conceptualize, document, and articulate novel partnership models for Runway; think creatively beyond what exists today to imagine the structures and motions that should
* Develop a deep, functional and technical understanding of our products and future roadmap, with a keen sense of where partnerships can compound product velocity and reach
* Partner closely with Product, Creative, Research, and Commercial teams to share partner feedback and shape opportunities for further product development
* Build the early operating muscle for partnerships at Runway — frameworks, deal templates, governance, reporting — so the function scales with us
* Systematically track activity, deal performance, and market feedback to refine strategy, identify patterns, and enable Runway's competitive advantage and market growth

### **What you’ll need**

* 8+ years of overall experience across some combination of strategic partnerships, business development, GTM, corporate development, consulting, or founding/operating — with a track record of having owned outcomes end-to-end
* Are motivated by the opportunity to build new systems in a high-growth environment, with a focus on quality, scale, and automation
* Strong analytical capabilities with demonstrated ability to convert complex data and information into actionable insights and strategy
* Demonstrated range: you've done the deal *and* the integration *and* the launch.
* Exceptional executive presence and communication, with the ability to hold your own with a studio chief, a CTO, and a Runway research lead in the same week
* Ability to work in a fast-paced, dynamic environment and manage multiple priorities with expansive ownership
* A strong bias towards action and belief that no task is too small for you
* High technical aptitude for understanding complex technologies, tools, systems, and issues
* Mindset to think creatively and think beyond past checklists and playbooks
* Highly resourceful with the ability to create structure and work autonomously amidst ambiguity
* New York or SF-based preferred
