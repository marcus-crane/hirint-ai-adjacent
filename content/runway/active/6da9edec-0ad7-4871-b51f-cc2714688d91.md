We are building AI to simulate the world through merging art and science.  
  
We believe that world models are at the frontier of progress in artificial intelligence. Language models alone won’t solve the world’s hardest problems – robotics, disease, scientific discovery. Real progress requires models that experience the world and learn from their mistakes, the same way that humans do. And this kind of trial and error can be massively accelerated when done in simulation, rather than in the real world.  
  
World models offer the most clear path to general-purpose simulation, changing how stories are told, how scientific progress is made and how the next frontiers of humanity are reached.

Our team consists of creative, open minded, caring and ambitious people who are determined to change the world. We aspire to continuously build impossible things and our ability to do so relies on building an incredible team. If you are driven to do the same, we'd love to hear from you.

## About the role

*\*Open to hiring remote across North America (west coast only) and the UK — we also have offices in San Francisco, Seattle, and London.*

We’re looking for a creative individual to join our dynamic team of creatives, engineers, business folks, and more.

In this role you will be an early member of a fast growing creative team. You’ll work closely with our Sales and Account Management teams to convey the value of Runway to Enterprise prospects and customers. As a user of Runway yourself and a content creator, you will handle pre and post-sale work, ensuring that customers are using Runway to it’s full potential, as well as any creative work in the product, inclusive of media content, presets, featured workflows, etc. You’ll also have a hand in any marketing sprints for model and feature releases.

Things are always moving quickly at Runway. We need team members with an agile and dynamic mentality who are comfortable with change and always willing to go above and beyond to ensure a job is done optimally.

## What you’ll do

* Creatively push the models available on Runway further using workflows and custom pipelines
* Craft content and lead demonstrations to show Enterprise prospects and customers how to leverage and utilize Runway most strategically
* Partner with our Sales and Creative teams to provide guidance and training to Enterprise prospects and customers.
* Highlight and explain common use cases and workflows for Enterprise organizations
* Serve as the day-to-day product expert for both pre and post sales conversations; help businesses understand the potential of utilizing Runway in their creative pipeline
* Be a champion for our customers and work with our internal teams to translate customer feedback into product insights; liaise between customers and our internal teams in order to provide the best support and experience possible

## What you’ll need

* Fluent in both Japanese and English
* Experience & expertise creating content from end to end
* Experience working with Japanese enterprises a plus
* Passion for experimentation when it comes to content creation and creative workflows- you care more about “how we get there” than “the destination”
* Comfort working with creative tools in addition to Runway (Adobe, DaVinci Resolve etc), as well as an understanding of our technical and competitive landscape
* You love sharing and presenting the things you’ve built
* Ability to deliver complex information to customers in a clear and concise way
* Ability to thrive in a constantly changing and growing environment
* Excitement about AI and motivation to continuously learn more about this new and exciting space!
