### **Job Description:**

Shield AI is building the world’s best AI pilot. As a fast-scaling defense aerospace company, we develop autonomous systems that operate in complex, contested environments to protect service members and civilians. Our mission sits at the intersection of artificial intelligence, advanced hardware, and national security. We are expanding our international go-to-market footprint and investing in strategic growth markets across Europe.

The Senior Field Marketing Manager, Europe will establish and execute field marketing across priority defense and government accounts in the United Kingdom, Netherlands, Norway, Poland, Romania, and Greece. This person will drive account-based marketing programs, support strategic capture efforts, and generate measurable pipeline impact in close partnership with regional sales and business development teams.

This is a hands-on role in a high-growth environment. The ideal candidate is equally comfortable developing account strategies and executing field programs, thrives in complex multinational government sales environments, and understands how to align marketing efforts with long-cycle defense procurement processes across multiple European markets.

### What you'll do:

* Lead and execute field marketing across priority defense and government accounts in the UK, Netherlands, Norway, Poland, Romania, and Greece, tightly aligned with regional Go-to-Market priorities.
* Develop and execute account-based marketing strategies for primary accounts, mapping buying centers and orchestrating multi-touch engagement across executive briefings, targeted campaigns, industry events, and owned customer programs.
* Demonstrate deep understanding of European defense procurement environments, acquisition cycles, NATO frameworks, and country-specific stakeholder landscapes, tailoring engagement strategies accordingly.
* Engage key ministries and agencies including, but not limited to:
* UK Ministry of Defence (MOD) and associated commands
* Netherlands Ministry of Defence
* Norwegian Ministry of Defence and defense materiel agencies
* Polish Ministry of National Defence
* Romanian Ministry of National Defence
* Hellenic Ministry of National Defence (Greece)
* Own strategy and execution for major defense and aerospace events across the region, prioritizing high-value account engagement and executive-level meetings over brand presence alone.
* Plan and execute owned customer events, private briefings, and targeted engagements in partnership with sales and capture teams to accelerate opportunity progression.
* Align marketing programs with regional sales objectives, pipeline targets, and account plans to drive measurable revenue impact across all focus markets.
* Own Salesforce campaign execution, including creating and managing Campaign IDs, ensuring proper campaign structure, tracking member status, and maintaining data integrity in partnership with sales.
* Partner closely with sales to ensure correct opportunity association, contact mapping, and required CRM field completion to support accurate pipeline reporting and attribution.
* Build and execute programs within Marketo, including campaign flows, audience segmentation, nurture programs, performance tracking, and marketing-to-sales workflow alignment.
* Establish KPIs aligned to European government sales realities, including account penetration, stakeholder engagement depth, opportunity influence, and deal acceleration.
* Bring structure, accountability, and operational rigor to field marketing execution across strategically important European defense markets.

### Required qualifications:

* 8–12 years of experience in field marketing, demand generation, or integrated marketing roles
* Proven expertise in **account-based marketing (ABM)** within complex enterprise or government accounts
* Demonstrated ability to **drive pipeline, influence opportunities, and support deal progression**
* Hands-on experience executing **events, executive engagements, and targeted field programs**
* Strong experience working cross-functionally with **Sales, BD, and capture teams**
* **Hands-on Salesforce (SFDC) experience**, including campaign creation, Campaign ID management, opportunity alignment, and pipeline reporting
* Experience using **marketing automation platforms (e.g., Marketo)** for campaign execution, segmentation, and nurture flows
* Ability to operate in a **hands-on, high-growth environment** with ownership and accountability

### Preferred qualifications:

* Experience marketing into **European government, Ministry of Defence, or defense/aerospace sectors**
* Familiarity with **UK MOD and other European defense ministries** (Netherlands, Norway, Poland, Romania, Greece)
* Understanding of **European procurement cycles, NATO frameworks, and multi-country acquisition environments**
* Experience aligning marketing to **capture efforts in long-cycle government deals**
* Additional **European language proficiency**
