The Senior Field Marketing Manager, Eastern Europe will establish and execute field marketing across the region’s defense, security, and government ecosystem. This leader will drive account-based marketing programs, support strategic capture efforts, and generate measurable pipeline impact in close partnership with regional sales and business development teams.

This is a hands-on role in a high-growth environment. The ideal candidate is equally comfortable developing account strategies and executing field programs, thrives in complex government sales environments, and understands how to align marketing efforts with long-cycle defense procurement processes. This role reports to the VP of Global Field Marketing and Events.

### What you'll do:

* Lead and execute field marketing across Eastern European defense ministries, armed forces, NATO-aligned organizations, and strategic aerospace and security accounts, tightly aligned with regional go-to-market priorities.
* Develop and execute account-based marketing (ABM) strategies for priority government and defense accounts across Eastern Europe, mapping buying centers and orchestrating multi-touch engagement across events, executive briefings, targeted campaigns, and owned customer programs.
* Demonstrate deep understanding of regional defense procurement environments, acquisition cycles, and stakeholder landscapes, tailoring engagement strategies accordingly.
* Own strategy and execution for major Eastern European defense and aerospace events, prioritizing high-value account engagement and executive-level meetings over brand presence alone.
* Plan and execute owned customer events, private briefings, and targeted engagements in partnership with sales and capture teams to accelerate opportunity progression.
* Align marketing programs with local sales objectives, pipeline targets, and account plans to drive measurable revenue impact.
* Own Salesforce campaign execution, including creating and managing Campaign IDs, ensuring proper campaign structure, tracking member status, and maintaining data integrity in partnership with sales.
* Partner closely with sales to ensure correct opportunity association, contact mapping, and required CRM field completion to support accurate pipeline reporting and attribution.
* Build and execute programs within Marketo, including campaign flows, audience segmentation, lead scoring alignment, and performance reporting.
* Establish KPIs aligned to regional government sales realities, including account penetration, stakeholder engagement depth, opportunity influence, and deal acceleration.
* Bring structure, accountability, and operational rigor to field marketing execution within a fast-growing international market.

### Required qualifications:

+ 8–12 years of experience in field marketing, demand generation, or integrated marketing roles
+ Proven expertise in account-based marketing (ABM) within complex enterprise or government accounts
+ Demonstrated ability to drive pipeline, influence opportunities, and support deal progression
+ Hands-on experience executing events, executive engagements, and targeted field programs
+ Strong experience working cross-functionally with Sales, BD, and capture teams
+ Hands-on Salesforce (SFDC) experience, including campaign creation, Campaign ID management, opportunity alignment, and pipeline reporting
+ Experience with marketing automation platforms (e.g., Marketo) for campaign execution, segmentation, and nurture programs
+ Ability to operate in a hands-on, high-growth environment with strong ownership and execution focus

### Preferred qualifications:

* Experience marketing into Eastern European governments, Ministries of Defence, armed forces, NATO organizations, or defense/aerospace sectors
* Strong understanding of regional defense procurement processes and long-cycle acquisition environments
* Experience aligning marketing programs to capture efforts for large, complex government deals
* Familiarity with key Eastern European defense stakeholders and decision-making structures
* Proficiency in one or more Eastern European languages (e.g., Polish, Romanian, Czech, Ukrainian, or Hungarian)
