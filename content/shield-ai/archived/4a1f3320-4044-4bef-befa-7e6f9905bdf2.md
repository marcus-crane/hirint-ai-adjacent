Founded in 2015, Shield AI is a venture-backed deep-tech company with the mission of protecting service members and civilians with intelligent systems. Its products include the V-BAT and X-BAT aircraft, Hivemind Enterprise, and the Hivemind Vision product lines. With offices and facilities across the U.S., Europe, the Middle East, and the Asia-Pacific, Shield AI’s technology actively supports operations worldwide. For more information, visit [www.shield.ai](http://www.shield.ai/). Follow Shield AI on [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/company/shield-ai), [X](https://x.com/shieldaitech), [Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/shield_ai), and [YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/@Shield_AI).

We are looking for a highly strategic and execution-focused Staff Software Recruiter to help scale some of the most critical engineering organizations within the company. This person will own full-cycle recruiting for complex software engineering functions with a particular emphasis on Autonomy, Robotics, Embedded Software, Perception, Planning, Controls, Distributed Systems, and other highly specialized technical domains supporting next-generation aerospace and autonomous systems development.

This is not a traditional recruiting role. We are looking for someone who can operate as a true talent partner to engineering leadership, helping shape hiring strategy, influence workforce planning, drive prioritization discipline, and build scalable pipelines for some of the hardest-to-hire technical talent in the industry.

The ideal candidate thrives in high-growth, high-ambiguity environments and brings a strong track record of closing senior and niche software engineering talent while balancing speed, quality, and stakeholder partnership.

### WHAT YOU’LL DO:

* Own full-cycle recruiting for highly specialized software engineering roles across Autonomy, Robotics, Perception, Planning, Embedded Software, Distributed Systems, Simulation, Machine Learning, Infrastructure, and related technical domains
* Build and execute proactive sourcing strategies for difficult-to-find technical talent across aerospace, defense, robotics, autonomous systems, automotive, AI/ML, and adjacent industries
* Act as a strategic advisor to leadership on market trends, talent availability, compensation competitiveness, and hiring risks
* Drive strong operational rigor across pipeline management, funnel health, data accuracy, and stakeholder communication
* Help influence workforce planning and ensure hiring activity remains aligned to approved business priorities
* Improve recruiting scalability through process improvements, documentation, interview calibration, and tooling optimization
* Deliver an exceptional candidate experience while maintaining urgency and high hiring quality standards
* Mentor junior recruiters and sourcers while helping elevate recruiting best practices across the organization

### REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS:

* 10+ years of full-cycle technical recruiting experience, including significant ownership of complex software engineering searches
* Proven success recruiting Software Engineers, Autonomy Engineers, Robotics Engineers, Embedded Software Engineers, Perception Engineers, Infrastructure Engineers, Machine Learning Engineers, or similar technical talent
* Strong understanding of software engineering concepts, technical architectures, and engineering organizational structures
* Experience partnering with senior engineering leadership in fast-paced or high-growth environments
* Demonstrated ability to manage multiple complex searches simultaneously while maintaining strong hiring outcomes
* Strong sourcing and market-mapping capabilities across highly competitive technical domains
* Excellent communication, stakeholder management, and operational execution skills
* Experience using ATS systems, sourcing platforms, and recruiting analytics to drive decisions and pipeline visibility
* Ability to operate strategically while remaining highly execution-oriented
