### Job Description:

Own the financial performance of novel autonomous aircraft programs, partnering directly with Program Management, Engineering, and Operations to drive execution, cost control, and informed decision-making. This role sits at the center of program delivery—shaping forecasts, influencing trade-offs, and ensuring programs scale efficiently from development through production.

### What you'll do:

* Lead program financials end-to-end: forecasting, budgeting, EAC development, and variance analysis across development and production phases
* Partner with Program Managers to drive program execution, quantify risks/opportunities, and influence cost, schedule, and scope decisions
* Build and maintain integrated financial models linking labor, material, and manufacturing inputs to program outcomes
* Deliver actionable insights to senior leadership on program health, margin performance, and resource allocation
* Establish and scale KPI reporting (cost, schedule, throughput, unit economics) using Power BI, Excel, and data pipelines
* Support month-end and quarter-end close with accurate accruals, revenue recognition inputs, and variance narratives
* Collaborate with Supply Chain and Manufacturing to track material usage, inventory movement, and cost drivers
* Drive automation and process improvements across financial planning, reporting, and program analytics

### Required qualifications:

* 6+ years of experience in program finance or similar role within aerospace and defense environments
* Proven ownership of program financials with direct impact on EAC accuracy, cost control, and margin improvement
* Advanced financial modeling skills (Excel) with the ability to integrate operational drivers into forecasts
* Strong understanding of government contracting (FFP, CPFF, T&M) and practical application of US GAAP in a project-based environment
* Experience partnering cross-functionally with Program Management, Engineering, and Operations to influence decisions
* Demonstrated ability to translate complex financial data into clear, actionable insights for senior stakeholders
* Hands-on experience with ERP systems (e.g., Costpoint) and financial planning/reporting tools (e.g., Anaplan)
* Track record of operating independently in fast-paced, ambiguous environments with high accountability

### Preferred qualifications:

* Experience supporting autonomous systems, aerospace platforms, or complex hardware programs from development through production
* Deep expertise in EAC modeling, cost structure optimization, and program margin expansion
* Advanced data modeling and visualization capabilities (e.g., PowerBI)
* Experience building scalable financial infrastructure (dashboards, automated reporting, data pipelines)
* Strong grasp of finance in a production and manufacturing environment, including labor efficiency, yield, and unit cost drivers
* MBA or Master’s degree in Finance, Accounting, or a related field
* Demonstrated ability to influence program strategy and investment decisions at the leadership level
* High ownership mindset with a bias for action and a history of driving measurable business outcomes
