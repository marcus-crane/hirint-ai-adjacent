### Job Description:

Shield AI is seeking a Product Lifecycle Management (PLM) Systems Administrator to support and maintain our PLM ecosystem, with a primary focus on Siemens Teamcenter. This role will be responsible for the day-to-day administration, support, and continuous improvement of PLM systems that enable engineering and manufacturing workflows.

This is a hands-on role focused on system administration, user support, and operational excellence. The ideal candidate brings experience supporting Teamcenter or a similar PLM platform and has a strong foundation in PLM concepts and engineering system integrations.

This role is intended to be based in Dallas, TX.

### What you'll do:

* Administer, support, and maintain PLM systems, primarily Siemens Teamcenter
* Perform system configuration, upgrades, patching, and routine maintenance across PLM environments (production and test)
* Support multi-tier PLM architecture including web, enterprise, and database layers
* Monitor system performance, troubleshoot issues, and resolve incidents impacting engineering users
* Support environment management, including refreshes, deployments, and basic automation of repeatable tasks
* Assist with implementation of new features, modules, and system enhancements
* Provide day-to-day support to engineering users, including issue resolution, training, and documentation
* Collaborate with infrastructure, engineering, and business systems teams to support integrations with ERP, MES, and other enterprise systems
* Maintain system documentation, runbooks, and standard operating procedures
* Work with vendors and internal stakeholders to resolve issues and improve system capabilities

### Required qualifications:

* Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Information Systems, Engineering, or related field, or equivalent practical experience
* 3–5 years of experience administering or supporting PLM systems (Teamcenter preferred; other PLM platforms acceptable)
* Working knowledge of PLM concepts, including product structures, workflows, and lifecycle management
* Experience supporting Windows and/or Linux server environments
* Familiarity with system administration fundamentals (patching, monitoring, backups, troubleshooting)
* Basic understanding of database-backed applications and multi-tier architectures
* Strong problem-solving and analytical skills with a customer-focused mindset
* Effective communication skills and ability to work cross-functionally

### Preferred qualifications:

* Hands-on experience with Siemens Teamcenter (installation, configuration, or support)
* Experience with CAD integrations (e.g., NX, SolidWorks, CATIA)
* Familiarity with scripting or automation (PowerShell, Python, or similar)
* Exposure to cloud or virtualization platforms (Azure, VMware, or similar)
* Experience supporting engineering or manufacturing environments
* Familiarity with system integrations across PLM, ERP, or MES platforms
* Experience working in a fast-paced, high-growth or defense-related environment
