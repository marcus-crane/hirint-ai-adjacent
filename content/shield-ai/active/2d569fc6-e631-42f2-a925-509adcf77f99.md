### Job Description:

We are seeking a highly skilled and detail-oriented Senior Accountant to join our International Accounting team. This role is pivotal in ensuring accurate financial reporting across global entities, maintaining compliance with international accounting standards, and supporting cross-border financial operations.

### What you'll do:

* Prepare and review monthly, quarterly, and annual financial statements for international subsidiaries/branches.
* Ensure compliance with IFRS, US GAAP, and local statutory requirements.
* Manage intercompany transactions, reconciliations, and eliminations.
* Coordinate with external firm/regional finance teams to consolidate global financial data.
* Assist in foreign currency translation and hedging activities.
* Support external audits and liaise with international auditors.
* Monitor changes in international accounting regulations and assess impact.
* Lead process improvements and automation initiatives across global accounting operations.

### Required qualifications:

* Bachelor’s degree in Accounting, Finance, or related field.
* 5+ years of accounting experience, with at least 2 years in international accounting.
* Strong knowledge of IFRS and US GAAP.
* Demonstrated ability to work independently and collaborate cross-functionally in a fast-moving environment.
* Excellent analytical, organizational, and communication skills.
* Strong attention to detail and problem-solving mindset.

### Preferred qualifications:

* CPA.
* Experience in multinational corporations or global shared services.
* Advanced proficiency in Excel and data analysis.
* Familiarity with tax implications of international operations.
