### Job Description:

We are seeking a **Staff Propulsion Systems Manufacturing Engineer** to lead manufacturing engineering activities for advanced propulsion and fluid systems used in aerospace and defense applications. This role supports engine integration, fuel systems, oil systems, pumps, and related propulsion hardware from development through production.

The ideal candidate has deep experience with propulsion-related manufacturing processes, fluid systems integration, and production support for mission-critical hardware.

### What you'll do:

* Lead manufacturing engineering support for propulsion and fluid system assemblies
* Develop and optimize manufacturing processes, tooling, work instructions, and production flows for:
* Engines and propulsion systems
* Fuel systems and fuel tanks
* Oil and lubrication systems
* Pumps and fluid control hardware
* Aerospace vector and thrust systems
* Partner with design engineering to improve manufacturability, reliability, testability, and producibility
* Drive root cause investigations and corrective actions for propulsion manufacturing issues
* Develop work instructions, quality standards, and inspection methods
* Support supplier qualification, process validation, and production readiness reviews
* Lead DFM/DFA reviews and transition new products into manufacturing
* Support system integration, validation, and troubleshooting activities
* Define manufacturing standards, process controls, and acceptance criteria
* Support failure analysis and reliability improvement initiatives
* Collaborate with production, quality, supply chain, and test engineering teams
* Mentor junior manufacturing engineers

### Tools & skills:

* MES & QMS systems
* 3DEXPERIENCE / Teamcenter (PLM) systems
* Supplier manufacturing processes
* 5S/visual management
* Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs)

### Required qualifications:

* 7+ years of manufacturing engineering experience supporting propulsion or fluid systems
* Bachelor’s degree in Mechanical Engineering, Aerospace Engineering, Manufacturing
* Experience in prototype manufacturing in a startup or rapid-growth environment.
* Experience with:
* Engine assembly and integration
* Fuel and oil systems
* Precision fluid system manufacturing
* Aerospace propulsion hardware
* Demonstrated ability to work in a fast-paced, high-pressure environment and adapt to changing priorities.
* Knowledge of manufacturing process development and production scaling
* Strong understanding of DFM/DFA principles
* Experience with root cause analysis and corrective action methodologies

### Preferred qualifications:

* Aerospace or defense industry experience
* Experience supporting turbine, piston, hybrid-electric, or UAV propulsion systems
* Knowledge of pressure systems, leak testing, and fluid handling
* Familiarity with lean manufacturing and production optimization
* Experience with ERP/MES systems and manufacturing data analysis
* Strong project management skills, including experience managing multiple priorities and deadlines
* Knowledge of quality standards, such as AS9100 or ISO 9001
* Proficiency in CAD software
