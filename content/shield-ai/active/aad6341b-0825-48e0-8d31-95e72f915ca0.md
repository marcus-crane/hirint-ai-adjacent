**Job Description:**

We are growing the Avionics Engineering team to support development of the X-BAT aircraft and the Launch and Recovery Vehicle. Our team is designing a flight critical, custom avionics system to support communications, compute, AI, power, telemetry, and all other needed aircraft systems.

This role is an exciting opportunity to be responsible for integrating avionics systems, sensors, actuators, and payloads onto the XBAT and Launch and Recovery vehicle ensuring from concept through production.

### What you'll do:

* Support integration of avionics systems, sensors, actuators, and payloads into the aircraft and launch/recovery vehicle
* Assist in harness design
* Support hardware bring-up, integration testing, and debugging
* Participate in root cause investigations and corrective actions
* Create and maintain integration documentation, test procedures, and verification reports
* Work closely with mechanical, embedded, software, and manufacturing teams to resolve integration challenges
* Support environmental and qualification testing of flight hardware
* Contribute to continuous improvement efforts across hardware development projects

### Required qualifications:

* Bachelor’s degree in Electrical, Mechanical, Aerospace, or Systems Engineering (or equivalent experience)
* 0–3 years of relevant industry or internship experience
* Strong fundamentals in electrical systems and hardware integration
* Hands-on experience working with embedded systems, sensors, or power systems (academic projects acceptable)
* Comfort working with lab equipment (oscilloscope, DMM, power supplies, etc.)
* Strong troubleshooting mindset and willingness to work hands-on with hardware
* Ability to work in a fast-paced, iterative development environment
* Ability to obtain a S//SAR level security clearance desired.
