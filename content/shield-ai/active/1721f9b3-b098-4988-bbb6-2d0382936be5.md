### Job Description:

Join a 10-week internship at the center of strategy, execution, and company-building inside one of the fastest-growing companies in AI, autonomy, and aerospace. This is a high-ownership role for a top-performing student who wants direct exposure to executive decision-making, fundraising, strategic partnerships, IPO readiness, and mission-critical initiatives. You’ll work closely with senior leaders to solve ambiguous problems, drive cross-functional execution, and help scale a company operating at the frontier of defense and autonomous systems.

### What you'll do:

* Drive high-priority projects across AI/autonomy, aerospace systems, international expansion, and strategic growth initiatives
* Break down ambiguous business and operational problems into structured analyses, actionable plans, and measurable outcomes
* Conduct market, competitive, and partnership analysis across commercial, defense, and international sectors
* Partner cross-functionally with engineering, product, finance, operations, and business development teams to drive execution on critical initiatives
* Take ownership of high-impact “important but unowned” problems that improve company execution, communication, and operating cadence

### Required qualifications:

* Currently pursuing a Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Business, Economics, Engineering, Finance, Computer Science, or a related field
* Proven record of high performance in demanding environments, including internships, research, startups, student leadership, consulting, finance, engineering, or operating roles
* Exceptional analytical and structured problem-solving skills with the ability to synthesize complex information quickly
* Strong written and verbal communication skills, including experience building presentations, executive summaries, or analytical deliverables
* Demonstrated ability to manage multiple priorities and deliver high-quality work under tight timelines
* Ability to operate independently in ambiguous, fast-moving environments with strong judgment and attention to detail
* Proficiency in Excel/Google Sheets and presentation tools such as PowerPoint or Google Slides
* Authorized to work in the United States and available full-time for a 10-week summer internship

### Preferred qualifications:

* Prior internship experience in strategy, consulting, investment banking, venture capital, private equity, startups, aerospace, defense, or high-growth technology companies
* Experience building financial models, conducting market analysis, or supporting strategic transactions or fundraising efforts
* Demonstrated ability to lead complex cross-functional projects with measurable outcomes
* Strong interest in AI, autonomy, aerospace, defense technology, or national security
* Evidence of exceptional achievement, including founder experience, nationally competitive programs, published research, top academic performance, or elite leadership roles
* Ability to communicate effectively with both technical and non-technical stakeholders, including senior executives
* Track record of identifying problems proactively and driving execution without needing detailed direction
