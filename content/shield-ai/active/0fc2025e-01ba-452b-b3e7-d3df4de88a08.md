**Job Description:**

We are growing the Avionics Engineering team to support development of the X-BAT aircraft and the launch/recovery vehicle. Our team is designing a flight critical, custom avionics system to support communications, compute, AI, power, telemetry, and all other needed aircraft systems.

A Senior Electrical Engineer will be given ownership over part of the flight critical system design and work with all cross-functional teams to execute.

### What you'll do:

* Circuit and sub-system design for X-BAT and its support equipment. This includes cradle to grave ownership of architecture, design, analysis, schematic capture, layout, verification, qualification, integration, and manufacturing
* Work with mixed signal systems that include high-speed digital, FPGA, compute, analog, power regulation, EMI/EMC, RF, etc.
* Execute test and qualification campaigns. Including environmental test for vibration, altitude, radiation, thermal, humidity, etc.
* Lead system integration of your design. Including troubleshooting and root cause analysis
* Lead and mentor teammates
* Work closely with cross-functional teams (mechanical, embedded, software, manufacturing, thermal, supplier development, reliability, supply chain, etc.)
* Help automate manufacturing test solutions for volume production

### Required qualifications:

* Bachelor’s degree in electrical engineering; or equivalent
* A minimum of 3+ years of experience as an Electrical Engineer
* Strong technical fundamentals
* Contributed to and led multiple product design cycles involving EE, ME, and SW engineering
* Familiar with CAD software such as Altium or equivalent
* Familiar with a PLM system such as Arena or equivalent
* Demonstrated track record of extreme ownership, working hard, and delivering outstanding outcomes
* Ability to obtain a S//SAR level security clearance desired.
