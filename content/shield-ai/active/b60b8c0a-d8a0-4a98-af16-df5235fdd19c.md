### Job Description:

The Senior Field Marketing Manager, Singapore will establish and execute field marketing across Singapore, Taiwan, and Korea’s defense and government ecosystems. This leader will drive account-based marketing programs, support strategic capture efforts, and generate measurable pipeline impact in close partnership with regional sales and business development teams.

This is a hands-on role in a high-growth environment. The ideal candidate is equally comfortable developing account strategies and executing field programs, thrives in complex government sales environments, and understands how to align marketing efforts with long-cycle defense procurement processes across multiple Indo-Pacific markets.

### What you'll do:

+ Lead and execute field marketing across Singapore, Taiwan, and Korea defense and government accounts, tightly aligned with regional Go-to-Market priorities.
+ Develop and execute account-based marketing strategies for priority accounts, mapping buying centers and orchestrating multi-touch engagement across executive briefings, targeted campaigns, industry events, and owned customer programs.
+ Demonstrate deep understanding of regional defense procurement environments, acquisition cycles, and stakeholder landscapes, tailoring engagement strategies accordingly.
+ Engage key ministries and agencies including, but not limited to:
+ - Singapore Ministry of Defence (MINDEF) and Singapore Armed Forces (SAF)
  - Taiwan Ministry of National Defense and affiliated military branches and research organizations
  - Republic of Korea Ministry of National Defense (MND), Defense Acquisition Program Administration (DAPA), and Republic of Korea Armed Forces
  - Own strategy and execution for major defense and aerospace events across the region, prioritizing high-value account engagement and executive-level meetings over brand presence alone.
  - Plan and execute owned customer events, private briefings, and targeted engagements in partnership with sales and capture teams to accelerate opportunity progression.
  - Align marketing programs with regional sales objectives, pipeline targets, and account plans to drive measurable revenue impact across all three markets.
  - Own Salesforce campaign execution, including creating and managing Campaign IDs, ensuring proper campaign structure, tracking member status, and maintaining data integrity in partnership with sales.
  - Partner closely with sales to ensure correct opportunity association, contact mapping, and required CRM field completion to support accurate pipeline reporting and attribution.
  - Build and execute programs within Marketo, including campaign flows, audience segmentation, nurture programs, performance tracking, and marketing-to-sales workflow alignment.
  - Establish KPIs aligned to regional government sales realities, including account penetration, stakeholder engagement depth, opportunity influence, and deal acceleration.
  - Bring structure, accountability, and operational rigor to field marketing execution across strategically important APAC defense markets.
