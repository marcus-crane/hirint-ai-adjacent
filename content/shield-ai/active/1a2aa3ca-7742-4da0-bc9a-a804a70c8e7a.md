**Job Description:**

Shield AI is redefining autonomous aviation with cutting-edge AI and GPS/RF-denied navigation technologies. Now, we’re building a next-generation aircraft like no other—one that will push the boundaries of innovation and performance.

Shield AI is seeking a Communications Systems Engineer to lead the design, integration, and optimization of advanced communication systems for our next-generation autonomous aircraft.

This is a high-impact role where you will be responsible for mission-critical data links, encryption, and secure communications, ensuring seamless interoperability between airborne, sensor, ground, and satellite systems. You will work across sensor fusion, RF systems, SATCOM, and AI-driven data processing to deliver an aircraft capable of operating in contested environments with low latency and high reliability.

### What you'll do:

* Lead the development and integration of data links, encryption, and secure communication architectures into the aircraft platform.
* Define system-level requirements and architectures for RF, SATCOM, optical, and software-defined radio (SDR) communication systems.
* Optimize low-latency, high-bandwidth data transfer and encryption to ensure mission-critical performance.
* Ensure seamless connectivity between the aircraft, command centers, and satellite networks, enhancing real-time data transmission and autonomy.
* Develop AI-driven sensor fusion algorithms to enhance payload performance in high-threat environments.
* Collaborate with avionics, software, and autonomy teams to ensure robust and secure system integration.
* Validate performance through simulation, flight testing, and field deployments.
* Work with DoD stakeholders and compliance teams to ensure mission readiness and adherence to cybersecurity standards

### Required qualifications:

* Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Electrical Engineering, Aerospace Engineering, or related field.
* 8+ years of experience in RF communications or mission systems integration for UAVs or military aircraft.
* Strong expertise in RF systems, satellite communications (SATCOM), tactical data links (Link-16, CDL), and sensor integration.
* Proficiency in software-defined radio (SDR) technologies and secure, encrypted data transmission.
* Experience writing specifications for communications subsystems and architectures.
* Knowledge of MIL-STD-1553, ARINC 429, and other aerospace data bus standards.
* Experience working with classified communication protocols and DoD compliance.
* Active Secret clearance.
* Ability to obtain a S//SAR level security clearance desired.

### Preferred Qualifications:

* Experience working with AI-enhanced signal processing and sensor fusion for ISR missions.
* Background in low-observable (stealth) communications architectures.
* Familiarity with electronic warfare (EW) and RF threat mitigation techniques.
* Experience supporting DoD or intelligence community mission operations.
* Active Top Secret clearance or eligible to achieve, as well as SCI eligible.
