### Job Description:

Be at the center of delivering complex, mission-critical programs that bring advanced technology into real-world operation. In this role, you will drive planning, coordination, and execution across cross-functional teams—ensuring seamless demonstrations, efficient logistics, and sustained customer success. Working in a fast-paced, dynamic environment, you’ll turn strategy into action, keep teams aligned, and play a key role in delivering high-impact solutions on time and at scale.

### What you'll do:

Demo Project Manager Tasks:

* Generate demonstration plans
* Conduct demonstration event logistics planning
* Coordinate with Go-To-Market leads and customers
* Establish and maintain project action plans and risk metrics
* Generate and maintain project schedules
* Coordinate participation of personnel for demonstration events
* Establish and lead a consistent cadence of well-organized, well-facilitated, and effective meetings, including driving all preparation and follow up activities
* Manage project budgets
* Manage and organize customer correspondence
* Coordinate import/export regulatory compliance – ITAR and EAR
* Facilitate fuel availability and procurement
* Review, understand, and comply with proposals and contracts
* Drive subcontractor performance as applicable

Assistant Program Manager for Logistics Tasks:

* Act as the key point of contact for the sustainment portion of programs
* Drive program execution of Engineering Change Order (ECO) implementation tasks in the company’s Product Lifecycle Management Software
* Ensure Service Bulletins are released and disseminated to customers, and track their incorporation into fielded hardware.
* Provide continuous, real-time readiness tracking of customer hardware (aircraft, payloads, ground support equipment, etc)
* Serve as the programmatic point of contact for customer requested warranty and non-warranty repairs, to include RMAs to vendors.
* Serve as Deployed Operations Field Service Representatives primary point of contact within the program office
* Represent customer programs in managing Diminishing Manufacturing Sources and Material Shortage issues.
* Maintain real-time awareness of customer hardware inventory levels, both within Shield AI’s facilities as well as at customer sites.  Coordinate replenishment at intervals appropriate to customer usage rates.
* Ensure all customers have access to the most up-to-date technical manuals and bulletins applicable to their hardware configuration.
* Provide customer feedback to internal teams regarding maintainability and sustainability issues.

General Tasks:

* Deliver proven, exceptional, and reliable performance in support of customer/end-user’s mission through flawless project management.
* Effectively collaborate, motivate, and lead relevant stakeholders to successfully deliver early, under budget, and with exceptional technical performance.
* Complete critical path analyses including the evaluation of potential means to accelerate the pace or mitigate identified risks
* Identify potential bottlenecks or delays in projects and drive efforts to ensure projects stay on schedule
* Partner with leadership to guide prioritization of projects and utilization of resources
* Support an environment where accountability and program execution excellence are core.
* Report directly to Shield AI’s Senior Director of V-BAT Program Management

### Required qualifications:

* 4+ years of Project Management experience, including managing schedules, budgets, resources, and events
* Experience with scheduling tools such as Microsoft Project or Smartsheet
* Significant experience and excellent skills with Microsoft Office suite
* Excellent skills in leadership, time management, organization, and team management.
* Theoretical and practical project management knowledge
* Application of project management techniques and tools
* Excellent verbal and written communications skills
* Willingness and ability to travel internationally and domestically as needed
* A demonstrated record of working hard, being a trustworthy teammate, holding yourself and others to a high standard, and being kind to others

### Preferred qualifications:

* Experience in the field of unmanned aerial vehicles
* Familiarity with aviation product delivery, maintenance, sustainment, and field support
* Undergraduate degree in engineering or business
* Project Management Professional (PMP) certification.
