### Job Description:

Help shape the supply chain that powers the future of autonomous systems. In this role, you will lead the development and execution of strategic sourcing initiatives, building resilient, high-performing supplier networks that support growth and innovation. You'll drive sourcing strategies that balance cost, technology, capacity, and risk while influencing key make-versus-buy decisions and establishing the processes, metrics, and governance needed to deliver long-term operational success.

### What you'll do:

* Identify, qualify, and develop a strategic supplier base capable of supporting high-mix, low-to-medium volume production for aerospace and defense applications.
* Negotiate and manage long-term agreements (LTAs), supplier performance reviews, and cost-reduction initiatives.
* Lead supplier business reviews and quarterly operating reviews (QBRs) focusing on delivery, quality, cost, and technical capability.
* Partner with Supplier development and Engineering to ensure AS9100 compliance, FAI completion, and audit readiness.

Cross-Functional Execution:

* Collaborate with Engineering to ensure early supplier involvement (ESI) during product development and NPI phases.
* Work closely with Operations and Program Teams to align material readiness with production schedules and customer milestones.
* Partner with Finance and Legal to structure commercial terms, manage supplier liabilities, and ensure compliance with DFARS, ITAR, and export regulations.
* Support supply risk management and business continuity planning across all electronics categories.

### Required qualifications:

* Bachelor’s degree in Supply Chain Management, Electrical Engineering, Business, or related field (Master’s preferred) or equivalent relevant experience
* 7+ years of experience in strategic sourcing or supply chain management with a focus on electronics, PCBA, or electromechanical commodities
* Proven experience in defense, aerospace, or high-reliability electronics manufacturing environments
* Demonstrated success in supplier negotiations, cost reduction initiatives, and long-term contract management
* Deep understanding of electronics manufacturing processes, including PCB fabrication, SMT assembly, and harness production
* Strong business acumen with ability to translate technical and commercial data into strategic sourcing actions
* Excellent communication, influencing, and cross-functional leadership skills
