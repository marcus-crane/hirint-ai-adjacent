### **Job Description:**

We are seeking an experienced Material Planner to enhance our materials planning and procurement processes. In this role, you will play a pivotal part in optimizing material usage, driving efficiency, and ensuring seamless collaboration across teams to support operational success.

### What you'll do:

Leadership and Execution (Individual Contributor):

* Own the material planning function and ensure day-to-day processes align with company objectives.
* Develop and execute strategies to optimize material usage and enhance procurement efficiency.
* Demonstrate subject-matter expertise and provide informal knowledge sharing and best-practice guidance to peers.

Materials Analysis and Planning:

* Analyze material usage and consumption versus planned metrics and deliver actionable insights to stakeholders.
* Identify trends and recommend improvements to materials planning parameters to generate accurate procurement signals.
* Ensure materials planning activities align with operational and production requirements.

Cross-Functional Collaboration:

* Partner closely with engineering to integrate new products and materials into planning systems seamlessly.
* Serve as a primary point of contact to facilitate clear communication between engineering and procurement functions.
* Adapt materials planning approaches to accommodate product changes and engineering feedback in a timely manner.

System Improvements:

* Recommend and implement enhancements to materials planning systems to streamline procurement processes.
* Leverage advanced tools, analytics, and data-driven methodologies to improve planning efficiency.
* Monitor outcomes and continuously refine system configurations to support sustained performance.

Supply Chain Performance Enhancement:

* Contribute to supply chain performance improvements through effective use of MRP (Material Requirements Planning) tools and techniques.
* Initiate and support process improvement efforts aimed at increasing efficiency and achieving key performance indicators.
* Stay current on industry best practices and emerging trends to continuously strengthen supply chain planning capabilities.

### Required qualifications:

* Proven experience in materials planning, procurement, or supply chain management, with leadership experience preferred.
* Expertise in MRP systems and a strong understanding of materials planning and supply chain principles.
* Excellent analytical, problem-solving, and decision-making skills.
* Strong communication and interpersonal skills to work effectively across teams and departments.
* Experience in implementing system enhancements and leveraging tools to drive efficiency.
* Demonstrated ability to lead cross functional alignment in a fast-paced environment.

### Preferred qualifications:

* Bachelor’s degree in Supply Chain Management, Operations, Engineering, Business, or a related field (or equivalent practical experience).
* 3–5+ years of experience in materials planning, production planning, or supply chain operations within a manufacturing or engineer-to-order environment.
* Hands-on experience with MRP/ERP systems and strong proficiency in Excel or other data analysis tools.
* Demonstrated ability to analyze demand, inventory, and consumption data to drive accurate planning decisions.
* Strong cross-functional communication skills with the ability to work effectively with engineering, procurement, and operations teams.
