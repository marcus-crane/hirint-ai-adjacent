### Job Description:

The V-BAT Internal Training Instructor is responsible for executing and maintaining standardized training for Shield AI Aircraft Operators and Maintenance Technicians. This role serves as a training standardization authority, ensuring all personnel are qualified, current, and prepared to support operational requirements.

### What you'll do:

* Deliver initial qualification training for Aircraft Operators and Maintenance Technicians
* Conduct instructor upgrades, evaluations, and endorsements required for operational events
* Ensure adherence to standardized procedures, checklists, and training requirements
* Maintain oversight of individual qualifications, currency, and additional requirements
* Evaluate trainee performance and certify readiness for operational tasking
* Support development and refinement of training materials, syllabi, and evaluation criteria
* Enforce training standards and identify gaps in performance, curriculum, or execution
* Coordinate with operations, standardization, and maintenance teams to align training with mission requirements
* Support test events, exercises, and deployments as required
* Provide mentorship and guidance to operators and technicians to maintain a high standard of performance

### Required qualifications:

* Experience as a UAS operator, maintainer, or military aviation instructor
* Background in structured training environments with formal evaluation processes
* Strong understanding of aviation procedures, safety, and standardization
* Experience conducting evaluations, certifications, or qualifications
* Ability to enforce standards and provide clear, direct feedback
* Strong communication and organizational skills

### Preferred qualifications:

* Instructor Pilot (IP), Evaluator (EP), or equivalent designation
* Experience with Group 3–5 UAS or comparable systems
* Background in maintenance training or certification programs
* Experience supporting operational or deployed environments
* At least 3000 documented unmanned flight hours or 1500 manned flight hours
* FAA Private Pilot License (PPL)
* FAA Instrument Rating and/or Commercial Pilot Rating
* FAA Part 107 Remote Pilot Certificate

### Work Environment:

* Combination of classroom, simulator, and field environments
* Travel required for training support, testing, and deployments
* Work may include austere or remote locations
