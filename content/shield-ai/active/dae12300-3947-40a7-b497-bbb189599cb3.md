### Job Description:

We’re looking for a hands-on, reliable Facilities Technician to support the ongoing success of our test sites in the Bonham and Frisco areas. In this role, you’ll be a key player in maintaining the functionality, safety, and efficiency of our facilities—ensuring our teams can operate at peak performance.

This position is perfect for someone who thrives in a dynamic, fast-paced environment and takes pride in keeping things running smoothly. You’ll work closely with cross-functional teams, respond quickly to facility needs, and be an essential part of day-to-day operations.

### What you'll do:

* Perform routine maintenance on facility equipment, including inspections and troubleshooting.
* Conduct repairs and replacements of defective or worn-out components.
* Maintain a clean and safe work environment by adhering to safety regulations and guidelines.
* Collaborate with facility staff to ensure smooth operations and address any maintenance-related concerns.
* Respond to emergency maintenance requests promptly and efficiently.
* Provide light facilities support (move chairs, set up banners, Conference room support)
* Drive to other locations for routine maintenance inspections using the company vehicle.
* Operate forklift, scissor lift, and pallet jack.
* Assist other roles as needed.

### Required qualifications:

* High school diploma or equivalent.
* Experience in warehouse maintenance or a related field.
* Physical ability to lift heavy objects (up to 49 lbs) and perform tasks that require bending, kneeling, and standing for extended periods.
* Must be able to pass a driver's license background check.

### Preferred qualifications:

* Excellent problem-solving skills and attention to detail.
* Ability to work independently and as part of a team.
* Good communication skills and the ability to follow written and verbal instructions.
