### Job Description:

The Supply Chain & Procurement Functional Lead will own Oracle Fusion Procurement, Inventory, Planning, and Cost Management processes to ensure material integrity, supplier governance, and MRP reliability in a complex manufacturing environment.

This role is critical to maintaining serialized traceability and material compliance across programs.

You must have experience leading an Oracle Fusion implementation at scale.

### What you'll do:

**Oracle Fusion Module Ownership**

* Procurement
* Supplier Portal
* Self-Service Procurement
* Inventory Management
* Cost Management
* Planning (MRP)
* Product Hub (if implemented)

**Aerospace & Manufacturing Focus**

* Serialized and lot-controlled inventory
* Subcontract purchase orders
* Receiving and inspection controls
* MRP parameter governance
* Material availability stabilization

**Process Leadership**

* Establish item master governance standards
* Improve MRP signal accuracy
* Reduce inventory discrepancies
* Align procurement workflows with compliance requirements
* Lead cross-functional planning alignment sessions
* Apply AI-enabled capabilities within Oracle Cloud SCM and Procurement to support mission-critical supply chain operations, leveraging predictive insights and intelligent automation to drive agility, resilience, and operational excellence.

### Required qualifications:

* 8+ years ERP supply chain systems experience
* Strong understanding of MRP and inventory control
* Experience in manufacturing or aerospace environments
* Oracle Fusion SCM experience is  must

### Preferred qualifications:

* Experience managing serialized inventory
* Familiarity with government material compliance requirements
* Oracle Cloud certification highly desired
* Lean or Six Sigma certification
