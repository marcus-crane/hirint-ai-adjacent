**Job Description:**

We are seeking a highly motivated Production Supervisor to oversee V-BAT manufacturing operations. The ideal candidate will be responsible for ensuring efficient production workflows, maintaining quality standards, and driving continuous process improvement in a fast-paced, high-tech environment. This role requires strong leadership, technical expertise, and a deep understanding of aerospace manufacturing and defense industry requirements.

### What you'll do:

* Supervise daily production operations, ensuring adherence to schedules, safety regulations, and quality standards.
* Lead and develop a team of technicians and assembly personnel, fostering a culture of accountability and innovation.
* Implement and maintain lean manufacturing principles to optimize efficiency and reduce waste.
* Monitor key performance indicators (KPIs) and drive continuous improvement initiatives.
* Collaborate with engineering, supply chain, and quality teams to resolve production challenges and ensure seamless execution of manufacturing plans.
* Ensure compliance with aerospace and defense industry standards, including AS9100, ITAR, and other regulatory requirements.
* Maintain and enforce safety protocols to create a secure and compliant work environment.
* Train and mentor production staff, promoting best practices and technical skill development.
* Troubleshoot production issues, implement corrective actions, and provide technical support as needed.
* Participate in new product introduction (NPI) activities, ensuring manufacturability and scalability of designs.

### Required qualifications:

* Bachelor’s degree in Engineering, Manufacturing, or a related field preferred. Equivalent experience will be considered.
* 5+ years of experience in a production supervisory role in a medium to high rate production environment for complex assemblies/products.
* Experience with lean manufacturing, Six Sigma, and continuous improvement methodologies.
* Excellent problem-solving skills and the ability to make data-driven decisions.
* Strong communication and leadership abilities to manage cross-functional teams effectively.
* Proficiency in ERP/MRP systems and production planning tools.
* Ability to work in a fast-paced, evolving startup environment while maintaining a focus on operational excellence.

### Preferred qualifications:

* Experience working with aerospace composites, avionics, propulsion systems, or final aircraft integration.
* Strong knowledge of aerospace manufacturing processes, assembly, and testing.
* Familiarity with defense-related compliance requirements (e.g., ITAR, DFARS).
* Certifications in Lean Six Sigma, PMP, or equivalent.
* Experience in a rapidly scaling production environment.
