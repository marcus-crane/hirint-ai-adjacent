**Job Description:**

We are growing the Avionics Engineering team to support development of the X-BAT aircraft. Our team is designing a flight critical, custom avionics system to support communications, compute, AI, power, telemetry, and all other needed aircraft systems.

An Avionics Specialist supports the entire Avionics team and other cross-functional teams. It is an essential role spanning design, verification, procurement, manufacturing, and test.

### What you'll do:

* Set up and execute test campaigns for custom circuits and sub-systems such as verifying logic levels, voltage rails, and basic circuit functions
* Support engineers through environmental qualification that includes temperature, vibration, altitude, humidity, etc.
* Debug and root cause system and circuit issues
* Design, verify, and deploy simple PCBAs
* Architect, design, and build complex wire harnesses and cable assemblies
* Assist with equipment procurement, set up lab space, and help set up prototype avionics manufacturing
* Capture results detailed and clear documentation

### Required qualifications:

* 2+ years' experience working in an electrical engineering environment and using test equipment such as an oscilloscope, multimeter, thermal chamber, etc.
* Proficiency reading electrical circuit schematics and applying them to root cause analysis
* Hands-on experience with harness design and build
* Familiarity with CAD software such as Altium or equivalent
* Familiarity with a PLM system such as Arena or equivalent
* Ability to work independently and lead in fast-paced, cross-functional teams
* A demonstrated record of working hard, being trustworthy, holding yourself to high standards, and being kind to others
* Ability to obtain a S//SAR level security clearance desired.
