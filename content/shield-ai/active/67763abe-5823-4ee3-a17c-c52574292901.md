### Job Description:

Help keep the next generation of autonomous aircraft mission-ready. In this hands-on role, you'll maintain and support advanced unmanned aircraft systems, ensuring reliability, readiness, and peak performance while working alongside cross-functional teams to deliver mission-critical capabilities.

### What you'll do:

* Conduct organizational and depot level maintenance on V-BAT unmanned aircraft systems and associated Ground Support Equipment (GSE)
* Comply with written training and certification requirements for maintenance personnel.
* Support development of maintenance task instructions for inclusion in future maintenance manuals.
* Develop relationships with adjacent organizations to ensure maintenance best practices are in place throughout the organization.
* Comply with the company’s Tool Control, FOD prevention, and HAZMAT programs.
* Internal customer V-BAT Organizational and Depot Level Maintenance tasks

### Required qualifications:

* Experience with aviation maintenance and operations, including coordinating work across multiple departments.
* Excellent communications skills.
* A demonstrated record of working hard, being a trustworthy teammate, holding self and others to high standards, and being kind to others.

### Preferred qualifications:

* FAA Airframe and Powerplant (A&P) Certification
* Experience in field of Unmanned Aerial Systems (UAS)
* Support international shipments for demonstrations and COCOs
