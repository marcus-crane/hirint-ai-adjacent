### Job Description:

We are seeking a highly experienced Senior Staff Network Architect to lead the design, implementation, and optimization of complex network infrastructures. This role requires deep expertise across network engineering, cybersecurity protocols, and server room / hardware infrastructure, with a strong ability to architect scalable, secure, and resilient systems.

The ideal candidate will operate at a strategic level while remaining hands-on, serving as a technical leader and key decision-maker across network and infrastructure domains.

### Required qualifications:

* Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science, Network Engineering, Electrical Engineering, or related field
* 10+ years of experience in network engineering and architecture roles
* Deep knowledge of network protocols (TCP/IP, DNS, DHCP, etc.)
* Strong expertise in cybersecurity frameworks and protocols (Zero Trust, NIST, ISO 27001, firewalls, IDS/IPS, encryption standards)
* Hands-on experience with enterprise networking hardware (routers, switches, firewalls, load balancers)
* Proven experience designing and managing data centers / server rooms, including physical infrastructure
* Experience with cloud networking (AWS, Azure, or GCP) and hybrid environments
* Strong understanding of high availability, redundancy, and disaster recoveryarchitectures

### Preferred qualifications:

* Industry certifications such as CCIE, CCNP, CISSP, or equivalent
* Experience modeling complex network systems in a modeling tool such as NX or SolidWorks.
* Experience designing architectures for classified networks.
* Experience with software-defined networking (SDN) and network automation
* Familiarity with DevOps and Infrastructure as Code (IaC) tools (e.g., Terraform, Ansible)
* Knowledge of compliance requirements (e.g., FedRAMP, DoD, or other regulated environments)
* Experience supporting AI/ML, high-performance computing, or large-scale distributed systems
