### Job Description:

We are currently seeking an Inventory Clerk with Inventory control experience. Immediate need for a detail-oriented person with great communication skills. This is a full-time warehouse position that requires daily communication coordinating with teammates in a fast paced, collaborative environment.

### What you'll do:

* Maintaining and updating inventory records in Costpoint (ERP)
* Counting material, equipment, or supplies in stock (cycle counts)
* Reporting discrepancies between physical counts and computer records
* Physically transferring parts received from Production and stocking our inventory
* Completing parts transfers in Costpoint (ERP)
* Issuing parts to Maintenance and other customers
* Issuing kits, material and equipment or supplies to production
* Verify all returned kits inventory to be accurate and ensuring all goods are in acceptable condition
* Properly stocking inventory in designated locations
* Provide great customer service when interacting with internal or external customers and ensure all parties receive a positive interaction
* Supports kit builds, packouts, and operational equipment staging
* Assists with deployments and demo preparation
* Other various tasks as assigned

### Required qualifications:

* Minimum 1 year of Inventory Clerk with ERP experience
* Able to work 9am-5pm or 10am-6pm
* Minimum 1 year work experience as an Inventory Clerk or similar role
* Excellent math and analytical skills
* Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
* Experience working in a fast-paced environment while maintaining attention to detail
* Experience with software such as Microsoft Excel and other company spreadsheet programs
* Ability to problem solve quickly and prioritize daily tasks according to their importance
* Personal accountability and integrity are paramount, as an Inventory clerk is tasked with an accurate accounting of all **inventory**
* Ability to lift up to 50 pounds, as many packages are large

### Preferred qualifications:

* 3+ year of ERP inventory experience
* Forklift Operator Certified
* Experience with RFID scanner inventory systems
* Manufacturing experience
* MS Office experience
