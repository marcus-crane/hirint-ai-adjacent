### Job Description:

Shield AI is seeking a **Staff Marketing Manager, Employer Brand** to lead the strategy, development, and execution of our global employer brand marketing efforts. This is a high-impact, individual contributor role responsible for shaping how we attract, engage, and convert top-tier talent across technical and mission-critical domains.

You will own the employer brand marketing strategy end-to-end—from audience segmentation and storytelling to channel strategy, campaign execution, and performance measurement. You’ll partner closely with Recruiting, Communications, and leadership to position Shield AI as a destination for builders, engineers, and operators driven by mission and impact.

This role requires both strategic thinking and hands-on execution—someone who can define the vision while also building and launching campaigns in a fast-moving environment.

### What you'll do:

**Employer Brand Strategy & Positioning**

* Lead and evolve Shield AI’s employer brand marketing strategy to attract high-caliber talent across technical, defense, and corporate functions
* Define target talent segments and craft differentiated messaging aligned to mission, product, and impact
* Shape and scale a compelling employer value proposition grounded in real employee stories and business priorities

**Social & Channel Strategy**

* Define and lead Shield AI’s global employer brand social media strategy across platforms (e.g., LinkedIn, X, YouTube, Instagram, emerging channels), aligned to hiring priorities and talent segments
* Own the end-to-end social ecosystem—including channel positioning, content strategy, editorial planning, publishing, and performance optimization
* Build and scale a distinct, mission-driven social voice that differentiates Shield AI and resonates with technical and mission-oriented talent
* Partner with Communications and Brand teams to ensure alignment between corporate, product, and employer narratives while tailoring for talent audiences
* Establish and operationalize a content engine for social, including employee storytelling, behind-the-scenes content, and campaign amplification
* Launch and optimize paid + organic social programs to drive awareness, engagement, and candidate conversion
* Implement testing frameworks (creative, format, audience, platform) to continuously improve performance and inform channel investment strategy

**Campaigns, Content & Channels**

* Lead the development and execution of integrated recruitment marketing campaigns across digital, social, and emerging channels
* Own content strategy across the candidate journey—from awareness through conversion—including careers site, social media, and campaign assets
* Partner with internal teams to bring authentic employee and mission-driven stories to life
* Build scalable content and campaign frameworks that enable recruiting teams to engage talent more effectively

**Analytics, Insights & Optimization**

* Define success metrics and build reporting frameworks to measure employer brand performance across channels and campaigns
* Translate data and candidate insights into actionable recommendations that improve targeting, messaging, and conversion
* Design and execute media and channel experiments to optimize performance and inform investment strategy

**Cross-Functional Leadership & Influence**

* Partner closely with Recruiting, Communications, Product Marketing, and Leadership to align employer brand efforts with hiring priorities and company narrative
* Act as a thought leader and advisor to stakeholders on talent market trends, candidate behavior, and employer brand strategy
* Enable recruiters and hiring leaders with tools, insights, and best practices to strengthen candidate engagement

**Programs & Operations**

* Manage employer brand marketing budgets and external partners where applicable
* Identify opportunities to extend employer brand impact beyond top-of-funnel attraction into deeper stages of the candidate journey
* Build scalable systems, processes, and playbooks to support rapid company growth

### Required qualifications:

* Bachelor's degree in marketing, Communications, or a related field
* 8 years of experience in marketing, with a focus on employer brand, talent marketing, or digital marketing
* Proven track record of developing and executing marketing strategies that drive measurable results
* Strong analytical skills with experience using data to inform strategy and optimize performance
* Deep understanding of digital channels, social platforms, and content strategy

### Preferred qualifications:

* Experience in high-growth technology, AI, robotics, defense, or mission-driven environments
* Experience marketing to technical talent (e.g., software engineers, AI/ML, robotics)
* Strong storytelling instincts with the ability to translate complex technology and mission into compelling narratives
* Exceptional cross-functional leadership and influencing skills in matrixed organizations
* Experience building employer brand measurement frameworks and running multi-channel campaigns
* MBA or advanced degree a plus
