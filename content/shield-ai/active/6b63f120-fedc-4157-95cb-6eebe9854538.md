**Job Description:**

As the Battery owner, you will be responsible for all aspects of battery systems for X-BAT, the Launch and Recovery Vehicle, and ground equipment. This includes the architecture, design, qualification, integration, and deployment to production.

You will help us build a team of world class engineers to support all design needs. A vertically integrated battery pack design will enable top performance, ease of integration, reliability, and rapid supply chain procurement. If you enjoy working cross-functionally and solving hard problems, please apply!

### What you'll do:

* Define and execute the vision of the battery system for X-BAT, the Launch and Recovery Vehicle, and all ground equipment
* Deploy high voltage batteries (270V and higher) to support a power system on the order of 100kW
* Design, develop, and integrate state-of-the-art high-power systems tailored for optimal performance and reliability in harsh environments (high altitude, radiation, etc.)
* Execute make/buy trades that align with a long term development roadmap
* Help hire and build the Power System team
* Build a world class power electronics lab that serves all battery needs
* Ensure system performance and reliability is met through rigorous verification
* Debug complex problems related to the electrical design, environmental, manufacturing, etc.
* Provide mentorship, design review, and program execution leadership
* Work in a fast-paced environment

### Required qualifications:

* You have a B.S. in Electrical Engineering or equivalent
* Battery design experience on large aircraft, or an equivalent power system (rocket, satellite, submarine, etc.)
* 5+ years relevant experience
* Have delivered battery power systems to market
* Proficient with electrical engineering development tools, simulation, and test equipment
* Strong technical fundamentals and analysis
