### **About Shield AI:**

Shield AI is a venture-backed defence technology company building the world’s best AI pilot and next-generation autonomous aircraft. Founded in 2015, Shield AI’s mission is to protect service members and civilians with intelligent systems. With a recent valuation of approximately $12.7B, Shield AI develops autonomy software, unmanned aircraft systems, and AI-enabled ISR capabilities that actively support defence and security operations worldwide.

Shield AI works side-by-side with military and government customers to solve complex operational challenges in some of the world’s most demanding environments. The company’s products, including V-BAT, X-BAT, Hivemind autonomy software, and advanced mission systems are designed to deliver operational advantage while reducing risk to human operators.

Shield AI believes the greatest victory requires no war.

### **Job Description:**

Shield AI is seeking a Capture Manager - Greece to drive strategic defence market expansion and capture activities. This individual will lead customer engagement efforts, shape and capture new business opportunities, and strengthen Shield AI’s position across government, military, and industrial stakeholders.

As a key member of the international growth team, the Capture Manager will represent Shield AI’s autonomy and aircraft portfolio, including V-BAT, X-BAT, ViDAR-enabled ISR capabilities, and Hivemind autonomy software across customer engagements, operational demonstrations, strategic partnerships, and competitive pursuits.

This role combines strategic capture management, defence business development, customer engagement, and district growth initiatives. The ideal candidate is a highly motivated and mission-driven professional with deep knowledge of defence acquisition environments, strong district relationships, and the ability to operate independently in a fast-paced and high-growth environment.

The Capture Manager will play a critical role in identifying and shaping opportunities, building sales pipeline, and support executing those to drive long-term growth and operational impact for Shield AI across the district.

### What you'll do:

* Lead district capture and business development activities across assigned defence and government markets.
* Develop and execute capture strategies to position Shield AI for competitive advantage and contract awards.
* Build and maintain trusted relationships with military, government, industry, and strategic partner stakeholders.
* Represent Shield AI at trade shows, defence exhibitions, operational demonstrations, and industry engagements.
* Present and communicate Shield AI’s product portfolio, operational capabilities, and mission value to customers and partners.
* Coordinate and execute customer demonstrations, evaluations, and operational engagements.
* Identify and grow new business opportunities aligned with regional growth objectives.
* Work cross-functionally to support customer needs and regional growth efforts.
* Provide market intelligence, customer feedback, and competitive insights to internal stakeholders.
* Support long-term strategic planning and regional growth initiatives.
* Support maintaining accurate opportunity tracking, forecasting, and CRM reporting.
