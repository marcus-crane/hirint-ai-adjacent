**Job Description:**

We are growing the Avionics Engineering team to support development of the X-BAT aircraft. Our team is designing a flight critical, custom avionics system to support communications, compute, AI, power, telemetry, and all other needed aircraft systems.

An Avionics Technician/Specialist supports the entire Avionics team and other cross-functional teams. It is an essential role spanning design, verification, procurement, manufacturing, and test. You will work specifically with the Electrical Engineering team as they design and verify all X-BAT electrical systems and circuit boards.

### Required qualifications:

* 3+ years working in an electrical engineering lab or equivalent
* Experience performing benchtop testing with basic EE equipment (multimeter, oscilloscope, electronic load, thermal chamber, etc.)
* Can design basic test fixtures
* May be able to design basic wire harnesses or simple circuit boards
* Able to rework circuit boards down to at least 0402 parts
* Proficiency reading and interpreting schematics
* Experience working on multidisciplinary hardware development projects, which can be from an internship, job, club, team, personal project, etc…
* Demonstrated history of showing ownership of project outcomes and delivering exceptional results
* Record of working hard, being a trustworthy teammate, holding yourself and others to high standards, and being kind to others
