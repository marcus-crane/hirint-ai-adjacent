Job Description:

As a DevOps Engineer, you’ll own our CI/CD and automation stack — driving developer velocity, pipeline health, and large-scale test execution from single-agent runs to cluster-orchestrated mission scenarios. This is a hands-on IC role that removes bottlenecks, accelerates iteration, and ensures every engineer can test and ship with speed and rigor.

What you'll do:

* Own and operate the CI/CD system across autonomy, embedded, and ground software.

* Improve developer velocity by reducing build/test cycle time and eliminating pipeline bottlenecks.

* Scale simulation test execution in CI — from containerized single-agent runs to orchestrated clusters.

* Manage infrastructure for builds and tests (Azure DevOps, Git, Artifactory, cloud build agents).

* Fully automate common tasks to create a self-service DevOps model allowing developers to be fully empowered.

* Partner with developers to resolve build/test failures quickly and set best practices for integration.

* Integrate CI pipelines with HIL and simulation environments for automated validation.

* Implement monitoring, dashboards, and alerts for CI/CD health and performance.

* Collaborate with the Developer Ops teams across Shield AI to introduce new technologies, best practices, and shared standards.

Required qualifications:

* BS in CS, CE, EE, or related field with 7+ years industry experience (or MS/PhD with 5+ years).

* Strong expertise in CI/CD tools (Azure DevOps, Jenkins, GitHub Actions).

* Proficiency with containers and orchestration (Docker, Kubernetes, cloud build clusters).

* Strong scripting skills (Python, Bash, PowerShell).

* Hands-on experience integrating automated testing into CI pipelines.

* Demonstrated success improving developer productivity at scale.

* Demonstrated track record of excellence as a primary contributor on projects - showcasing the ability to navigate through development cycles, overcome obstacles, and deliver high-quality solutions in a fast-paced environment

Preferred qualifications:

* Experience building simulation or HIL-based test pipelines.

* CMake/C++ expertise or equivalent.

* Strong understanding of dependency management and proficiency with either Conan or CPM.

* Familiarity with infrastructure-as-code (Terraform, Ansible) and monitoring (Grafana, Prometheus).

* Background in robotics, autonomy, or aerospace systems.
