### Job Description:

The Associate Systems Administrator provides day-to-day enterprise end-user IT support, systems administration, infrastructure maintenance, and asset lifecycle support. This role is responsible for helping maintain a secure, reliable, and efficient IT environment by delivering white-glove technical support to employees, with particular focus on executive users, executive assistants, board-facing support needs, and visiting leaders. The ideal candidate combines solid technical troubleshooting skills, sound judgment within established processes, and a customer-focused approach, and operates effectively in a fast-paced enterprise environment.

### What you'll do:

* Deliver excellent customer service while supporting employees across multiple locations.
* Provide day-to-day technical support for endpoint and user systems across Windows, macOS, and Linux environments.
* Install, configure, maintain, and troubleshoot hardware, software, mobile devices, and peripheral equipment for end users.
* Deliver white-glove support for executive users, executive assistants, board-related activities, and visiting leaders with a high level of professionalism, responsiveness, and discretion.
* Support conference room, collaboration, and meeting technology for executive meetings, leadership reviews, and other high-visibility events.
* Administer user accounts, permissions, and access controls through Active Directory and Azure AD in accordance with established security and compliance requirements.
* Support endpoint management and security controls to maintain a secure and compliant computing environment.
* Perform local IT asset lifecycle activities, including device provisioning, deployment, inventory tracking, maintenance, refresh coordination, and secure decommissioning.
* Maintain accurate asset records and software licensing information using IT service management and asset management tools.
* Respond to and resolve service requests and technical incidents in a timely manner, escalating more complex issues through established support paths.
* Create and maintain technical documentation, standard operating procedures, and knowledge base content.
* Collaborate with infrastructure, network, and security teams to support enterprise IT operations and coordinated issue resolution.
* Participate in planned maintenance activities and support occasional after-hours or on-call responsibilities as required.

### Required qualifications:

* 5+ years of experience in systems administration, desktop support, or enterprise IT support roles.
* 3+ years of experience supporting executives, executive assistants, or other VIP users in a corporate environment.
* Experience supporting Windows, macOS, and Linux operating systems in a business environment.
* Working knowledge of Active Directory, Azure AD, and identity and access management concepts.
* Experience troubleshooting endpoint hardware, software, operating system, and basic collaboration technology issues.
* Familiarity with networking fundamentals, including DNS, DHCP, TCP/IP, switching, and wireless networking.
* Experience using IT service management and asset management platforms such as Jira Service Management, Snipe-IT, ServiceNow, or similar tools.
* Strong troubleshooting, documentation, communication, and customer service skills.
* Ability to communicate effectively with both technical and non-technical stakeholders, including senior leaders.
* Ability to handle sensitive situations with discretion, urgency, and strong follow-through.
* Ability to participate in an on-call rotation and support occasional after-hours maintenance activities.

### Preferred qualifications:

* 6+ years of enterprise IT experience.
* Experience providing executive or white-glove technical support in a fast-paced corporate environment.
* Experience with Microsoft Endpoint Manager (Intune), JAMF, or other endpoint management platforms.
* Basic scripting or automation experience using PowerShell, Bash, or similar tools.
* Familiarity with AV, conferencing, collaboration, and room-support technologies used in executive settings.
* Familiarity with endpoint security tools and enterprise security best practices.
* Experience using AI-powered tools to improve operational efficiency and workflow automation.
