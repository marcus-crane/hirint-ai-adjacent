### **Job Description:**

Shield AI is seeking an experienced Policy, Procedure & Process Compliance Analyst to support the development, implementation, and oversight of compliant business systems and internal controls across our government contracting operations.  
This role plays a key part in ensuring alignment with FAR, DFARS, and business system requirements (e.g., DFARS 252.242-7005 and related clauses). You will work cross-functionally to design, document, and improve policies and processes that withstand audit scrutiny and enable operational excellence.

### What You'll Do:

* Develop, document, and maintain policies, procedures, and process narratives supporting government contracting compliance
* Ensure alignment with DFARS business system requirements (e.g., Accounting, Purchasing, Estimating, MMAS)
* Evaluate internal processes and controls for compliance with FAR, DFARS, and other regulatory standards
* Support DCAA/DCMA audits and business system reviews, including documentation, walkthroughs, and corrective actions
* Conduct internal compliance assessments and gap analyses; track remediation efforts
* Partner with stakeholders across Finance, Contracts, Supply Chain, and Program Management to ensure consistent process execution
* Lead or support process improvement initiatives to enhance efficiency while maintaining compliance
* Maintain documentation repositories and ensure version control and audit readiness

### Required Qualifications:

* Bachelor’s degree in Business, Accounting, Finance, or equivalent professional experience
* 6+ years of experience in government contracting compliance, business systems, or internal controls
* Strong knowledge of FAR, DFARS, and DFARS Business System rules
* Experience supporting DCAA/DCMA audits or Contractor Purchasing System Reviews (CPSR)
* Demonstrated experience developing and documenting policies, procedures, and process flows
* Strong understanding of internal control frameworks (e.g., COSO)
* Excellent analytical, documentation, and communication skills

### Preferred Qualifications:

* Experience in Aerospace & Defense or similarly regulated industry
* Familiarity with ERP systems (e.g., Deltek Costpoint, SAP, Oracle)
* Experience with business system implementations or remediation efforts
* Professional certification (e.g., CPA, CISA, PMP, or Lean Six Sigma)
* Experience with risk management and compliance tools
