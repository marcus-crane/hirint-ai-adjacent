**Job Description:**

As a Maintenance Technician, you will play a key role in ensuring the reliability, safety, and mission readiness of Shield AI’s V-BAT unmanned aircraft systems (UAS) and associated Ground Support Equipment (GSE).

### What you'll do:

* Conduct organizational-level maintenance on V-BAT unmanned aircraft systems and associated Ground Support Equipment (GSE).
* Construct wiring harnesses and solder necessary components to maintain V-BAT unmanned aircraft systems and their associated GSE.
* Comply with written training and certification requirements for maintenance personnel.
* Support the development of maintenance task instructions for future maintenance manuals.
* Develop relationships with adjacent organizations to ensure maintenance best practices are in place.
* Comply with the company's Tool Control, FOD prevention, and HAZMAT programs.

### Required qualifications:

* Ability to travel as needed.
* Ability to obtain a US security clearance.
* Experience in aviation maintenance and operations, including coordination across multiple departments.
* Significant experience and proficiency with Microsoft Office Suite.
* Excellent verbal and written communication skills.
* Proven record of working hard, being a trustworthy teammate, holding oneself and others to high standards, and showing kindness to others.
* USA-issued Passport.

### Preferred qualifications:

* FAA A&P license (preferred) or possesses equivalent internal or external certification demonstrating technical competency.
* Experience in the field of Unmanned Aerial Systems (UAS).
* Experience in avionics.
* IPC Certification.
