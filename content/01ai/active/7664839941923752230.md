## 职位描述

我们正在招聘一名认真细致的初级会计（Junior Accountant），负责公司的日常财务运营工作，包括资金管理、账务处理及税务申报等。本岗位适合具备良好组织能力、能够按时完成工作的候选人，要求能够熟练使用中文和俄语进行工作沟通。

We are seeking a meticulous Junior Accountant to handle daily financial operations, including cash management, bookkeeping, and tax filing. This is an entry-level role ideal for candidates who are organized, deadline-driven, and bilingual in Chinese and Russian.

\* 监控银行账户余额，办理转账及付款业务，并完成银行对账工作。

Monitor bank balances, process transfers/payments, and reconcile bank statements.

\* 在财务系统中准确录入各项业务；维护总账及明细账；协助完成月度结账工作。

Record all transactions in the accounting system; maintain general ledger and sub-ledgers; assist with month-end closing.

\* 负责增值税、企业所得税、代扣代缴税等当地税务申报；确保税款按时缴纳；与税务机关保持日常沟通，确保公司税务合规。

Prepare and file VAT, corporate income tax, withholding tax, and other local tax returns; ensure timely tax payments and liaise with tax officers.

\* 编制现金流汇总、费用报表及其他日常财务报表，为内部管理提供支持。

Generate cash flow summaries, expense reports, and other routine financial statements for internal review.

\* 协助财务经理或CFO完成预算编制及临时性财务分析工作。

Assist the Finance Manager/CFO with budget preparation and ad-hoc financial analysis.

\* 整理发票、收据及合同等财务资料，建立并维护规范的财务档案管理体系。

Organize invoices, receipts, and contracts; maintain proper filing for all financial records.

## 任职要求

\* 会计、财务或相关专业本科及以上学历。

Bachelor’s degree in Accounting, Finance, or a related field.

\* 具有1–3年财务或会计相关工作经验。

1–3 years of relevant accounting or finance experience.

\* 熟悉当地会计准则及税务法规；熟练使用Excel及财务软件。

Knowledge of local accounting standards and tax regulations; proficient in Excel and accounting software.

\* 中文和俄语听说读写流利（必需）；具备英语能力者优先。

Fluent in both written and spoken Chinese and Russian. English is a plus.

\* 工作认真细致，责任心强；具备良好的组织协调能力；能够按时完成工作任务；具有团队合作精神，并能妥善处理公司机密财务信息。

High accuracy, strong organizational skills, ability to meet tight deadlines, team-oriented, and trustworthy with confidential data.
