SambaNova Suite™ is the first full-stack, generative AI platform, from chip to model, optimized for enterprise and government organizations. ### Job Overview

SambaNova Systems is looking for a talented and motivated **Network Architect** to help us solve the most challenging and impactful problems in machine learning and artificial intelligence.

### Roles and Responsibilities

As a Network Architect at SambaNova, you'll be responsible for defining the next generation of silicon and networking fabric solutions to enable state-of-the-art scale-up competitiveness for supercomputer scale AI/ML systems.

* Conduct research and development in state of the art networking solutions
* Define and own networking architecture specifications of performant, robust and efficient  communications for the next generation super-pod scale ML HW
* Define and own ML networking roadmap, leading a cross functional effort of software, systems and silicon
* Collaborate with executive leadership to align network initiatives against product competitiveness requirements
* Stay on top of and contribute to industry standards representing SambaNova

### Minimum Qualifications

* Experience

+ BS, MS, or PhD degree in Computer Science, Computer Engineering or Electrical Engineering
+ 10+ years of industry experience in ML / supercomputer / public cloud / networking
+ Proven track record of successful full-cycle development and delivery from planning to product in networking / ML scale up processors and systems

* Knowledge and Skills

+ Track record of ownership and definition of various networking and routing protocols
+ Deep understanding and hands-on experience defining various networking topologies for large machine clusters.
+ Deep understanding of congestion management, QoS, security isolation
+ Deep understanding of reliability, availability and serviceability from networking perspective
+ Capable of abstracting and analyzing network performance from real world application
+ Capable of breaking down networking requirements into silicon, packaging, system SW, RunTime SW and Compiler SW requirements
+ Strong hands-on analytical and debug skills to make progress on challenging problems in an ambiguous and highly dynamic environment
+ Strong coding skills with C++/Python/etc

* Leadership and teamwork

+ Highly motivated, self-driven, independent, organized and strong collaboration and communication skills
+ Cross-functional team leadership experience

### Preferred Qualifications

* 15+ years of industry experience in ML / supercomputer / public cloud / networking industries
* Good understanding of ASIC development cycles
* Knowledge of machine learning architecture and/or computer architecture
* Experience in building or proficiently using network simulators
* Experience in leading silicon, packaging, system HW, system SW cross-functional teams
* Experience in conducting perf and perf/tco trade-off analysis on different interconnect choices
* Familiarity with off the shelf optical IP and optical switches
* Understanding / hands-on experience in end to end optical systems
* Hands-on experience in working with IP/Switch hardware vendor to enhance / customize solutions
