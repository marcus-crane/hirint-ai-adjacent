**About the Role**

We are seeking a hands-on Manufacturing Test Engineer to design, develop, and optimize manufacturing test systems for complex electronic and system-level products. This role is critical in ensuring product quality, test coverage, and scalability across production environments. You will work cross-functionally with engineering, manufacturing, and suppliers to build robust automated test solutions, reduce test escapes, and support new product introduction (NPI).

**Key Responsibilities**

**Test Development & Automation**

* Develop and validate automated test programs and fixtures for manufacturing environments
* Select and integrate appropriate test equipment for production testing
* Design and implement scalable test architectures for system and PCBA-level testing
* Create detailed and robust manufacturing test procedures and documentation

**Failure Analysis & Continuous Improvement**

* Partner with manufacturing and Failure Review Board (FRB) teams to analyze test failures
* Drive root cause analysis and corrective actions to reduce test escapes
* Continuously improve test coverage, reliability, and throughput

**Manufacturing Support**

* Provide hands-on support to manufacturing test technicians for troubleshooting and repair
* Train and guide manufacturing teams on proper use of test equipment and fixtures
* Ensure stability and uptime of test systems on the production floor

**Test Systems & Platform Development**

* Lead end-to-end test system development including concept, architecture, design, prototyping, validation, and deployment
* Collaborate with suppliers and third-party vendors for test solutions
* Develop scalable test platforms using internal and external technologies

**Software & Test Validation**

* Define and implement test protocols for software validation
* Ensure test plans meet quality standards and production requirements

**New Product Introduction (NPI)**

* Support NPI efforts by developing and deploying manufacturing test solutions
* Collaborate with local and remote design teams to ensure test readiness for production
* Validate test coverage and readiness before product ramp

**Cross-Functional Leadership**

* Provide technical oversight for contract manufacturers (CMs) and internal manufacturing processes
* Mentor and train junior manufacturing and test engineers
* Drive best practices across manufacturing test engineering

**Required Qualifications**

* Bachelor’s degree in Electrical Engineering (BSEE) or related field
* 5–7+ years of experience in PCBA and/or system-level manufacturing environments
* Strong hands-on experience with manufacturing test systems and troubleshooting
* Experience with automated test development and test equipment integration
* Strong problem-solving skills and ability to work cross-functionally

**Preferred Qualifications**

* Experience with hardware/software integration testing
* Familiarity with test frameworks, scripting, and automation tools
* Experience working with contract manufacturers and global production environments
* Knowledge of quality and reliability methodologies (e.g., root cause analysis, DFx)
* Experience supporting high-volume manufacturing and product ramp

**Why This Role Matters**

This role is critical to ensuring product quality and manufacturing scalability. You will directly impact yield, reliability, and customer satisfaction by building robust and efficient test systems.
