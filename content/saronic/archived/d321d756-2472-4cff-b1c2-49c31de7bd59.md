Saronic Technologies is a leader in revolutionizing autonomy at sea, dedicated to developing state-of-the-art solutions that enhance maritime operations through autonomous and intelligent platforms.

**Job Overview**

As a **Mechanical Test Engineer**, you will be responsible for designing, executing, and analyzing mechanical and structural tests on components and full systems for our autonomous surface vessels. You’ll play a critical role in validating designs, improving reliability, and ensuring our systems meet performance and environmental requirements for military operations.

**Responsibilities**

* Plan and execute mechanical, structural, and environmental tests on ASV components and full systems (including vibration, shock, thermal, and fatigue tests)
* Develop test procedures, fixtures, instrumentation setups, and data acquisition systems
* Analyze test results to identify design weaknesses, material limitations, or integration issues
* Collaborate with design, electrical, and software teams to close feedback loops and iterate quickly
* Assist in field testing campaigns, including at-sea trials and system deployments
* Document test plans, reports, and technical findings for internal use and DoD compliance
* Support root-cause analysis and troubleshooting during development and production

**Qualifications**

* BS or MS in Mechanical Engineering, Aerospace Engineering, or a related field
* 3+ years of experience in mechanical testing, validation, or systems integration (preferably in defense, marine, aerospace, or robotics industries)
* Strong understanding of structural mechanics, materials, failure modes, and environmental testing standards (e.g., MIL-STD-810)
* Experience with mechanical instrumentation, strain gauges, accelerometers, and data acquisition systems
* Proficiency in CAD (e.g., SolidWorks) and basic FEA tools for stress and modal analysis
* Hands-on skills with test setup, mechanical assembly, and fabrication tools
* Comfortable working in lab, dockside, or field environments
* Strong written and verbal communication skills
* US Citizenship required; eligibility for DoD security clearance preferred
* Familiarity with marine or defense systems, including autonomous vehicles or maritime platforms
* Experience with data processing tools such as Python or MATLAB
* Knowledge of reliability testing methods (HALT/HASS), root cause analysis techniques, and design-for-testability
* Previous involvement in DoD test campaigns, test readiness reviews, or technical reporting
