Saronic Technologies is a leader in revolutionizing autonomy at sea, dedicated to developing state-of-the-art solutions that enhance maritime operations through autonomous and intelligent platforms.

**Position Overview**

We are seeking a Quality Engineer – Quality Improvement responsible for driving

quality performance across the full product and process lifecycle, from initial

concept through production and post-delivery support. This role leads cross-

functional efforts to identify, prioritize, and resolve quality issues while

implementing sustainable improvements that enhance reliability, efficiency, and

customer satisfaction. The ideal candidate thrives in environments requiring

ownership, coordination, and disciplined execution of improvement initiatives.

**Key Responsibilities**

* Lead and support Lean manufacturing initiatives to improve quality, efficiency, and throughput
* Apply Lean tools (5S, Kaizen, Value Stream Mapping, Root Cause Analysis, etc.) to identify and eliminate waste
* Utilize A3 problem-solving methodology to investigate issues and implement sustainable corrective actions
* Support and align improvement activities with Policy Deployment (Hoshin Kanri) objectives and strategic goals
* Analyze quality data and process metrics to identify trends and areas for improvement
* Collaborate with cross-functional teams to drive process standardization and best practices
* Support internal audits, corrective/preventive actions (CAPA), and continuous improvement projects
* Train team members on Lean principles, A3 thinking, and quality tools

**Qualifications**

* Bachelor’s degree in Engineering or a related technical field
* 3+ years of experience in quality engineering, manufacturing, or continuous improvement roles
* Strong working knowledge of quality tools and methodologies (Six Sigma, Lean, SPC, FMEA, Control Plans)
* Experience leading cross-functional initiatives and influencing stakeholders without direct authority
* Experience with A3 problem-solving methodology and understanding of Policy Deployment (Hoshin Kanri)
* Strong analytical and communication skills

**Preferred Qualifications**

* Six Sigma Green Belt or Black Belt certification
* Experience in high-mix, low-volume manufacturing environments
* Familiarity with APQP, PPAP, and advanced quality planning processes

**Physical Demands:**

* Prolonged periods of sitting at a desk and working on a computer
* Occasional standing and walking within the office
* Manual dexterity to operate a computer keyboard, mouse, and other office equipment
* Visual acuity to read screens, documents, and reports
* Occasional reaching, bending, or stooping to access file drawers, cabinets, or office supplies
* Lifting and carrying items up to 20 pounds occasionally (e.g., office supplies, packages)
