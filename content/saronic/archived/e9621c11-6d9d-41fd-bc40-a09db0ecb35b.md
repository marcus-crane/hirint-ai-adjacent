Saronic Technologies is a leader in revolutionizing autonomy at sea, dedicated to developing state-of-the-art solutions that enhance maritime operations through autonomous and intelligent platforms.

# **Job Overview**

Saronic Technologies is seeking a detail-oriented and analytical **Inventory Control Specialist** responsible for overseeing and optimizing inventory management processes to ensure the availability of essential components and materials required for the production of autonomous surface vessels.

This role plays a critical part in maintaining accurate inventory records, supporting production continuity, and safeguarding sensitive materials in a regulated defense environment. The ideal candidate will have strong analytical skills, a commitment to accuracy, and experience operating within a fast-paced manufacturing setting.

# **Responsibilities**

* **Inventory Management**

  + Maintain accurate and up-to-date records of all inventory items within ERP/inventory systems.
  + Monitor inventory levels, safety stock, and reorder points to prevent stockouts and overstock situations.
  + Conduct regular cycle counts and physical inventories; investigate and reconcile discrepancies.
  + Track material movement across warehouse and production locations to ensure system accuracy.

  **Supplier & Internal Collaboration**

  + Collaborate with suppliers to ensure timely and accurate deliveries of components and materials.
  + Support procurement and logistics teams in resolving receiving discrepancies or inventory-related issues.
  + Communicate inventory risks, shortages, and excess materials to management and cross-functional stakeholders.

  **Quality & Compliance**

  + Work closely with quality control teams to ensure incoming materials meet established standards.
  + Identify and escalate quality or documentation issues impacting inventory integrity.
  + Ensure compliance with relevant defense regulations, internal security protocols, and material handling requirements.
  + Safeguard inventory against theft, damage, misplacement, and unauthorized access.

  **Documentation & Reporting**

  + Generate inventory reports including stock levels, usage trends, turnover rates, and accuracy metrics.
  + Maintain detailed documentation of inventory transactions and adjustments.
  + Support audits by preparing required documentation and reconciling inventory records.

  **Process Improvement**

  + Continuously evaluate and improve inventory control procedures and systems.
  + Identify opportunities to enhance efficiency, reduce carrying costs, and improve material visibility.
  + Support system improvements within ERP/MRP platforms.

# **Qualifications**

* Associate’s degree in Supply Chain Management, Logistics, or related field preferred.
* Proven experience in inventory management and control, preferably in a manufacturing or defense environment.
* Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
* Proficiency in inventory management software, ERP/MRP systems, and Microsoft Office Suite.
* Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.
* Detail-oriented with a strong commitment to data accuracy.
* Ability to work effectively in a fast-paced, dynamic environment.
* Knowledge of defense industry regulations and compliance standards (e.g., ITAR, DFARS) is a plus.
