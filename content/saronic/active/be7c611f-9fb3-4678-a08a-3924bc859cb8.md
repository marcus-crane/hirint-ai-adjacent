Saronic Technologies is a leader in revolutionizing autonomy at sea, dedicated to developing state-of-the-art solutions that enhance maritime operations through autonomous and intelligent platforms.

### Position Overview

The Training Site Coordinator serves as the operational and administrative backbone of a Saronic training site, ensuring that training programs are executed efficiently, on schedule, and in alignment with organizational standards.

This role is responsible for coordinating logistics, scheduling, resources, and communication across training events, supporting both internal and customer-facing programs. The Training Site Coordinator works closely with the Mission Training Manager, Training Operations Manager, instructors, technicians, and training improvement personnel to enable seamless training delivery.

This is not a technical training, instructional design, or engineering role. Instead, it operates at the intersection of planning, logistics, and execution—ensuring that all elements required for successful training are in place and aligned.

### Core Responsibilities:

### 1. Training Event Planning & Scheduling

* Coordinate scheduling of training courses across internal and customer programs
* Maintain training calendars, resource allocation plans, and instructor schedules
* Ensure alignment between site-level execution and headquarters training plans
* Track course capacity, enrollment, and attendance across training events
* Support long-range planning in coordination with the Mission Training Manager and Training Operations Manager

### 2. Training Logistics & Site Operations

* Coordinate all logistics required for training execution, including classrooms, vessels, equipment access, and materials
* Ensure training spaces are prepared, organized, and ready for delivery
* Support setup and teardown activities in coordination with Training Technicians
* Manage on-site flow of training events, including check-in, coordination, and issue escalation
* Maintain site readiness for concurrent or high-tempo training operations

### 3. Cross-Functional Coordination

* Serve as the central coordination point between:

  + Mission Training Manager (site leadership)
  + Training Operations Manager (planning and oversight)
  + Lead Instructors (delivery execution)
  + Training Site Technicians (technical readiness)
  + Training Improvement Specialists (quality and standardization)
* Communicate schedule changes, requirements, and updates across stakeholders
* Ensure all parties are aligned on training plans, timing, and resource needs

### 4. Administrative Support & Reporting

* Maintain training records, attendance logs, and completion tracking
* Support data entry and reporting within Learning Management Systems (LMS) and internal tools
* Generate reports on training throughput, utilization, and scheduling performance
* Assist with documentation of training events, including rosters, schedules, and coordination artifacts
* Support audit readiness and compliance documentation as required

### 5. Customer & Participant Experience

* Serve as a primary point of contact for trainees during training events
* Coordinate onboarding, check-in, and day-to-day administrative support for participants
* Ensure a professional, organized, and high-quality training experience
* Escalate issues impacting training delivery or participant experience

### 6. Continuous Improvement & Process Support

* Capture lessons learned from training execution and site operations
* Identify inefficiencies in scheduling, logistics, or coordination processes
* Support implementation of standardized processes and SOPs across training sites
* Partner with Training Improvement Specialists to reinforce consistent execution practices

### Required Qualifications:

* 1–4 years of experience in operations coordination, logistics, training support, or administrative roles
* Strong organizational and time management skills with ability to manage multiple concurrent activities
* Excellent communication skills across technical and non-technical stakeholders
* Experience coordinating schedules, resources, or events in a dynamic environment
* High attention to detail and ability to maintain accurate records and documentation
* Proficiency with Microsoft Office tools (Excel, Outlook, Word, PowerPoint)

### Preferred Qualifications:

* Experience supporting training programs, field operations, or customer-facing events
* Familiarity with Learning Management Systems (LMS)
* Experience in defense, maritime, or regulated environments
* Experience working in fast-paced, operational or startup environments

### Role Context at Saronic

Training at Saronic includes multiple specialized roles:

* **Mission Training Manager** – leads site operations, strategy, and execution of training programs
* **Training Improvement Specialist** – ensures instructional quality, standardization, and alignment with approved content
* **Training Site Technician** – ensures technical readiness of systems, equipment, and training environments

The **Training Site Coordinator** complements these roles by owning planning, logistics, and administrative execution—ensuring that training programs run smoothly without owning technical systems, curriculum development, or instructional delivery.
