Saronic Technologies is a leader in revolutionizing autonomy at sea, dedicated to developing state-of-the-art solutions that enhance maritime operations through autonomous and intelligent platforms.

# **Technical Knowledge/Job Summary**

* Understand weld symbols
* Read, interpret, and follow blueprints and sketches to understand the specifications of a project
* Efficiently and safely perform welding tasks using various welding processes and equipment
* Properly use weld measurement tools and gauges
* Must have a thorough knowledge of various metals characteristics and qualities including the correct welding processes required and the effects of welding on the material
* Ability to inspect completed welds for conformance to specifications and know how to correct defects when necessary
* Will be required to work aloft (on scaffold or on manlifts) and in confined spaces.
* On site training is provided.

# **Job Proficiency**

* Complete carbon steel, aluminum, and stainless steel fillet and butt weld certifications using the SMAW (Stick), GMAW (MIG), FCAW (Flux Core), and GMAW-P process (multi-pass) in 3G and 4G positions
* Complete stainless steel, Cupro-Nickel, and carbon steel pipe welds (6G)
* Self-assess quality of work to ensure compliance with industry standards and regulations
* Perform routine maintenance on welding equipment and tools
* Perform structural welding on ship components such as hulls, decks, bulkheads, frames, and foundations.
* Prepare work surfaces by grinding, cutting, and cleaning to ensure quality welds.
* Perform grinding, cutting, torching, and pick up work as needed.
* Conduct weld fit-up and tacking according to engineering specifications and welding best practices
* Work in various positions, including overhead, vertical, and flat welding, often in tight or elevated spaces
* Inspect all equipment daily (torch, torch hoses, grinder, levels, tapes, etc.)

  "Pre-employment skills testing may be required during the interview process."

# **Culture and Collaboration**

* Follow all shipyard safety policies, including proper use of PPE, fire prevention, and confined space protocols.
* Assist in the training and development of junior welders
* Collaborate and communicate with welders, metal fitters, pipefitters, electricians, and other trades as required to complete vessel construction or repair and to maintain efficient work flows
* Work independently and as part of a team in a fast-paced ship production environment
* Maintain a clean and safe work area
* Maintain a positive attitude toward customers and represent Saronic professionally
* Make a personal commitment and be accountable for HSE results, driving toward the achievement of defined goals
* Welding performance is subject to successfully passing all NDT requirements as set forth by classification society.
