Saronic Technologies is a leader in revolutionizing autonomy at sea, dedicated to developing state-of-the-art solutions that enhance maritime operations through autonomous and intelligent platforms.

We are seeking a Senior FP&A Analyst – Program to join our Finance team as the dedicated partner to Program Management. In this role, you will serve as the primary finance point of contact for revenue-generating contracts, owning financial visibility into contract performance and supporting execution against cost, schedule, and margin objectives.

You will play a key role in connecting program execution with financial outcomes–translating contract structures and operational progress into clear forecasts, tracking performance against financial targets, and enabling informed decision-making across program leadership.

This role is ideal for someone who enjoys working cross-functionally, bringing structure to complex environments, and driving accountability and clarity at the contract level in a fast-paced, evolving organization.

# **Key Responsibilities:**

* Own financial tracking and analysis for revenue-generating contracts, including FFP, T&M, and % complete structures
* Develop and maintain contract-level financial models, including revenue, cost, and gross margin forecasts
* Build and manage EACs (Estimate at Completion) and ETCs (Estimate to Complete) across programs
* Partner closely with Program Managers to understand execution status, risks, and key milestones
* Analyze gross margin performance, identifying key drivers such as labor utilization, material costs, scope changes, and rate variances
* Translate contract performance into bottoms-up revenue and margin forecasts aligned with company FP&A processes
* Collaborate with Accounting on revenue recognition (including % complete), accruals, and month-end close alignment
* Partner with Contracts and Growth teams on contract structure, modifications, and financial implications of scope changes
* Prepare and deliver reporting and variance analysis that synthesizes financial and operational data into actionable insights
* Identify risks and opportunities across programs and proactively recommend actions to improve financial performance
* Build scalable processes, dashboards, and tools to improve visibility into contract performance and margin

# **Required Qualifications:**

* 5+ years of experience in FP&A, program finance, project finance, or related analytical roles
* Strong financial modeling and analytical skills with high attention to detail
* Experience working with or supporting contract-based revenue (e.g., FFP, T&M, % complete)
* Proven ability to partner with cross-functional stakeholders and influence decision-making with data
* Strong communication skills with the ability to translate complex financial concepts into clear insights
* Comfortable operating in fast-paced environments with evolving priorities and multiple workstreams
* Bachelor’s degree in Finance, Accounting, Economics, or a related field

# **Preferred Qualifications:**

* Experience with government contracts, defense, or aerospace programs
* Familiarity with revenue recognition principles (ASC 606), especially % complete / cost-to-cost methods
* Experience with COGS and gross margin modeling at a program or contract level
* Experience at a manufacturing company
* Exposure to ERP and financial planning systems (e.g., NetSuite, Foundry, or similar)
* Experience building financial processes or reporting frameworks from the ground up
