Saronic Technologies is a leader in revolutionizing autonomy at sea, dedicated to developing state-of-the-art solutions that enhance maritime operations through autonomous and intelligent platforms.

# **Job Overview**

We are seeking an experienced Marine Design Manager to lead our team of marine designers and drafters in support of autonomous surface vessel development. This role is central to delivering high-quality vessel design across disciplines, setting technical standards, and driving impact in defense innovation.

**Responsibilities**

* Lead and manage a team of marine designers and drafters across 2D and 3D modeling, technical drawings, and production support
* Own the development and enforcement of drawing standards and best practices across the design organization
* Partner with naval architects, marine engineers, structural engineers, and electrical engineers to drive new vessel design implementation
* Oversee new vessel construction documentation, including ship construction drawings and as-built records
* Mentor, develop, and grow team members in CAD modeling and ship design best practices
* Ensure marine regulatory compliance across all design documentation
* Coordinate with production and build engineering teams to manage design-to-build handoffs and resolve technical issues
* Drive continuous improvement in design processes, tooling, and team workflows

**Qualifications**

* Bachelor's degree or technical certification in Naval Architecture, Drafting, Engineering Technology, or a related field (Associate degree considered with significant experience)
* 10+ years of CAD drafting and design experience in the marine industry
* 3+ years of experience managing or leading a team of marine designers
* Strong expertise across one or more areas of vessel design: general, mechanical, electrical, structural, and piping design
* Deep knowledge of maritime regulatory requirements, construction tolerances, and vessel construction processes
* Demonstrated experience managing drawing submittals to classification societies such as ABS, DNV, and LR
* Proficiency with 2D and 3D CAD software including NX, ShipConstructor, AutoCAD, and Rhino
* Strong communication and cross-functional collaboration skills
