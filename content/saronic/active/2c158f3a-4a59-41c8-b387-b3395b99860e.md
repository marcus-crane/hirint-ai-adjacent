Saronic Technologies is a leader in revolutionizing autonomy at sea, dedicated to developing state-of-the-art solutions that enhance maritime operations through autonomous and intelligent platforms.

**Job Summary/Technical Knowledge**

* Identify hazards and maintain clean and organized working areas
* Understand and execute procedures for routine preventive maintenance and basic repairs of a variety of Saronic equipment.
* Be a technical resource for our production and procurement teams
* Knowledge of the installation, repair, and maintenance of the mechanical and electrical systems; and the related components of industrial machinery and equipment
* Will be required to work aloft (on scaffold or on manlifts) and in confined spaces.
* On site training is provided.

**Job Proficiency**

* Perform routine maintenance and inspections on shipyard equipment, including but not limited to: telehandlers, forklifts, cherry pickers, manlifts, welding machines, power tools, industrial manufacturing equipment, compressors, etc.
* Troubleshoot and repair mechanical, electrical and pneumatic issues to ensure optimal shipyard operations
* Conduct preventative maintenance to minimize equipment failures and downtime
* Maintain accurate records of maintenance activities including repairs made and parts used
* Assist with installation and commissioning of new equipment as necessary
* Performing routine maintenance and emergency repairs

**Culture and Collaboration**

* Follow all shipyard safety policies, including proper use of PPE, fire prevention, and confined space protocols.
* Assist in the training and development of junior maintenance technicians
* Collaborate and communicate with welders, metal fitters, pipefitters, electricians, and other trades as required to complete vessel construction or repair and to maintain efficient work flows
* Work independently and as part of a team in a fast-paced ship production environment
* Maintain a clean and safe work area
* Maintain a positive attitude toward customers and represent Saronic professionally
* Make a personal commitment and be accountable for HSE results, driving toward the achievement of defined goals
* Collaborate with Maintenance Supervisor(s), Production Manager(s), and other team members to prioritize and schedule maintenance tasks
