Saronic Technologies is a leader in revolutionizing autonomy at sea, dedicated to developing state-of-the-art solutions that enhance maritime operations through autonomous and intelligent platforms.

We are seeking an experienced and results-driven Senior Network Engineer to join our IT team and lead the design, deployment, and management of mission-critical network infrastructure. The ideal candidate will bring deep expertise in enterprise networking and hands-on experience with leading solutions/technologies provided by Cisco, Palo Alto, and Aruba. This position requires a proactive professional who can ensure high availability, performance, and security across our global network infrastructure

# **Key Responsibilities:**

* Design, implement, and support enterprise-grade network solutions, with a focus on scalability, security, and performance.
* Administer and maintain Cisco Catalyst switches and Aruba wireless infrastructure to support wired and wireless connectivity across multiple locations.
* Manage and secure network perimeters using Palo Alto firewalls and configure site-to-site and client-based VPN connections.
* Monitor network health and performance using SolarWinds or other monitoring tools; respond to alerts and resolve issues proactively.
* ·Lead infrastructure upgrade projects, network segmentation, and expansion efforts, ensuring minimal disruption to operations.
* Collaborate with cross-functional teams to integrate network services with cloud and hybrid environments.
* Conduct in-depth troubleshooting and root cause analysis for complex network issues, including hardware, software, and configuration anomalies.
* Develop and maintain comprehensive network documentation, including topology diagrams, IP schemas, and operational procedures.
* Ensure compliance with security standards and assist with internal/external audits as required.

# **Required Qualifications:**

* Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or a related field
* 7+ years of experience in enterprise network engineering roles.
* Strong expertise with Cisco Catalyst switches and Aruba wireless technologies.
* Proven experience configuring and managing Palo Alto Networks firewalls and VPN solutions.
* Proficient in network monitoring and alerting using SolarWinds or similar platforms.
* Solid understanding of Layer 2/3 protocols, including VLANs, STP, BGP, OSPF, and EIGRP.
* Familiarity with security frameworks, access control policies, and segmentation strategies.
* Experience supporting networks in hybrid or cloud environments (AWS/AWS GovCloud, Azure, or GCP) is a plus.
* Excellent communication, organizational, and documentation skills.
* Ability to manage multiple projects and priorities in a dynamic environment.

# **Preferred Qualifications:**

* advanced degrees or certifications such as CCNP, CCIE, or PCNSE
* Experience with Zero Trust Network Architecture (ZTNA) design and SASE/SSE implementation.
* Working knowledge of network automation using scripting tools (e.g., Python, Ansible).
* Familiarity with NAC (Network Access Control) solutions and [802.1X](http://802.1X) implementations.
* Prior experience supporting IT operations in regulated industries (e.g., defense, healthcare, finance).
* Exposure to SD-WAN, IPAM, and advanced QoS configurations.
* Experience with disaster recovery and business continuity planning for network services.
