Saronic Technologies is a leader in revolutionizing autonomy at sea, dedicated to developing state-of-the-art solutions that enhance maritime operations through autonomous and intelligent platforms.

# **Responsibilities**

• Triage documentation requests — scope with stakeholders and assign work to writers.

* Own sprint planning and manages backlog in an agile environment.
* Set and track deadlines that align with product release schedules and engineering milestones.
* Maintain visibility into writer capacity, level workloads across the team, and flag resourcing risks early.
* Apply a light-touch coordination framework to early-stage R&D products, with a clear path for graduating them to full sprint management.
* Build and maintain reporting for ticket age, burndown, completion rate, sprint velocity, doc coverage, and overall backlog health.
* Manage publication and administrative workflows from review through delivery.
* Manage the documentation review flow in GitHub. Review and merge pull requests, follow up with stakeholders to review, close out sprint milestones. keep work moving toward delivery.
* Maintain the develop branch and prepare main for publication.
* Create and manage publication tags.
* Manage docs deployment (CI/CD pipeline).
* Create and publishes the changelog, keep version tracking and document records current across products.

# **Qualifications**

• Scrum master experience (or equivalent agile delivery ownership), in addition to 5–7 years in technical writing, content operations, content management, or a closely related field.

• Demonstrated ability to plan and manage content sprints in an agile or scrum environment.

• Strong organizational skills with experience managing backlogs, intake processes, and cross-functional timelines.

• Proficiency with a docs-as-code workflow in GitHub.

• Proficiency with issue tracking and reporting in Linear (or an equivalent tool), including building burndown, velocity, and backlog-health metrics.

• Experience collaborating with engineering, product, and program teams.

• Clear communicator — able to translate ambiguity into structured, actionable plans for writers.

• Experience writing operations, maintenance, and service documentation.

• Comfortable working as a self-starter in a fast-paced environment.

# **Physical Demands**
