Saronic Technologies is a leader in revolutionizing autonomy at sea, dedicated to developing state-of-the-art solutions that enhance maritime operations through autonomous and intelligent platforms.

# **Job Overview**

We are seeking talented and motivated marine designers and drafters to support the development of autonomous surface vessels. This role will provide hands-on experience in traditional vessel design and advanced autonomous technologies; driving impact in defense innovation.

# **Responsibilities**

* Support new vessel construction through detailed 2D and 3D modeling and technical drawing production
* Create and modify ship construction drawings
* Work directly with naval architects, marine engineers, and electrical engineers on new vessel design implementation
* Maintain strict drawing standards
* Create documentation for marine regulatory compliance

# **Qualifications**

* Associate degree or technical certification in Drafting, Engineering Technology or similar field of study
* Minimum 3 – 5 years of CAD drafting experience
* Strong fundamentals in one or more areas of vessel design: general design, mechanical design, electrical design, structural design, and piping design
* Knowledge of maritime regulatory requirements, construction tolerances, and vessel construction processes and sequences
* Proficiency with 2D and 3D CAD software like NX, ShipConstructor, AutoCAD, and Rhino
