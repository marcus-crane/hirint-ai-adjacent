Saronic Technologies is a leader in revolutionizing autonomy at sea, dedicated to developing state-of-the-art solutions that enhance maritime operations through autonomous and intelligent platforms.

**Job Summary:**

We are seeking a Product Designer who will play a pivotal role in designing user-centered solutions for our autonomous surface vessels and related systems. You will collaborate closely with cross-functional teams, including engineering, operations, and DoD stakeholders, to create intuitive, functional, and aesthetically compelling designs.

From early-stage concepts to final product iterations, you will ensure that the design vision aligns with both user needs and technical requirements. This role is ideal for a versatile designer who thrives in a fast-paced environment and is passionate about working on impactful, high-tech projects.

**Responsibilities:**

* Contribute to the overall direction and decision-making for product and design strategy
* Own all aspects of design execution from discovery, ideation, prototyping, testing, and implementation
* Use storytelling to align and bring stakeholders along in your design process
* Play an influential role in shaping the end-to-end experiences for critical operator workflows
* Maintain a high bar for craft-driving novel experiences at the edge, and bring back the results to evolve the underlying platform experience
* Balance short-term tactical bets while making meaningful progress toward long-term strategic pursuits
* Help to shape our ways of working as a centralized product design function operating in a fast-paced environment

**Qualifications:**

* 6+ years of industry experience
* High attention to detail and mastery of visual & interaction design craft
* Online portfolio demonstrating multimodal experiences
* Ability to clearly articulate the why, process, and impact of your work
* No prior defense experience necessary
