Saronic Technologies is a leader in revolutionizing autonomy at sea, dedicated to developing state-of-the-art solutions that enhance maritime operations through autonomous and intelligent platforms.

# **Job Overview**

We’re looking for people who are energized by hard problems, motivated by mission impact, and thrive in fast-moving, collaborative environments. Ideally, you are passionate about maritime autonomy, defense technology and love building comprehensible data models. You prioritize speed and efficiency and are interested in generating powerful analytical models and applications within our organization.

# **What You’ll Do**

* Maintain and build the Foundry platform alongside Palantir specialists.
* Collaborate with internal stakeholders
* Collect requirements and write code that results in secure, timely, accurate, and reliable data models in Palantir Foundry

# **Requirements**

* 5+ years of experience in Information Technology
* 2-5 years of experience with hands-on client-facing development and data integration and analytics platforms using Palantir Foundry
* Experience with Python and SQL
* Experience with Pipeline Builder and Foundry's application development ecosystem
* Experience with data modeling, ontology mapping, or data visualization within Foundry
* Experience with Git-based code repositories and CI/CD workflows
* Experience with Product Lifecycle Management, Configuration Management, MRP, ERP.
* Experience in engineering, production and manufacturing environments and identifying processes that incorporate the three.
* Ability to lead fast-paced, Agile environments
* Bachelor's degree

# **Physical Demands**

* Prolonged periods of sitting at a desk and working on a computer.
* Occasional standing and walking within the office.
* Manual dexterity to operate a computer keyboard, mouse, and other office equipment.
* Visual acuity to read screens, documents, and reports.
* Occasional reaching, bending, or stooping to access file drawers, cabinets, or office supplies.
* Lifting and carrying items up to 20 pounds occasionally (e.g., office supplies, packages).
