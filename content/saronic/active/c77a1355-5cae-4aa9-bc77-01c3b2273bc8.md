Saronic Technologies is a leader in revolutionizing autonomy at sea, dedicated to developing state-of-the-art solutions that enhance maritime operations through autonomous and intelligent platforms.

# **Job Overview**

We are seeking a highly skilled and motivated Senior Systems Engineer to lead the design, deployment, and ongoing maintenance of our data center infrastructure. This position is critical to supporting our mission-critical systems and ensuring high availability, scalability, and performance across both on-premise and cloud environments.

The ideal candidate will have deep technical expertise in both Windows and Linux environments, extensive experience with monitoring and disaster recovery solutions, and a strong foundation in virtualization, cloud services, storage, and preferably high-performance computing (HPC). This role requires a hands-on, detail-oriented engineer who thrives in a fast-paced, high-growth environment.

# **Responsibilities**

* Lead architecture, deployment, and lifecycle management of data center infrastructure components.
* Design and implement scalable, secure, and resilient systems in hybrid environments (on-prem and cloud).
* Administer and maintain enterprise Windows and Linux systems.
* Develop and enforce backup, disaster recovery, and business continuity strategies.
* Optimize storage and compute infrastructure for high performance and cost-effectiveness.
* Collaborate with cross-functional teams to support application needs and integration projects.
* Document systems designs, procedures, configurations, and provide knowledge transfer.
* Proactively monitor systems performance and reliability, and troubleshoot issues as they arise.
* Ensure compliance with industry standards and internal security policies.
* Participate in an on-call rotation and respond to incidents with urgency and professionalism.

# **Qualifications**

* Bachelor’s degree in computer science, Information Systems, Engineering, or related field (or equivalent experience).
* 7+ years of experience in systems engineering or infrastructure roles.
* Proficiency in both Windows Server and Linux (RHEL/CentOS/Ubuntu) environments.
* Proven experience in data center design, build-out, and operations.
* Strong knowledge of backup and disaster recovery solutions (e.g., Veeam, Commvault, Rubrik).
* Experience with cloud platforms such as AWS/AWS GovCloud, Azure, or GCP.
* Familiarity with enterprise storage solutions (e.g., NetApp, Dell EMC, Ceph).
* Experience with virtualization technologies (e.g., VMware, Hyper-V).
* Strong scripting and automation skills (e.g., PowerShell, Bash, Python).
* Understanding of networking fundamentals and integration with infrastructure systems.
* Excellent problem-solving skills, with the ability to multitask and work under pressure.

# **Physical Demands:**

* Prolonged periods of sitting at a desk and working on a computer
* Occasional standing and walking within the office
* Manual dexterity to operate a computer keyboard, mouse, and other office equipment
* Visual acuity to read screens, documents, and reports
* Occasional reaching, bending, or stooping to access file drawers, cabinets, or office supplies
* Lifting and carrying items up to 20 pounds occasionally (e.g., office supplies, packages)
