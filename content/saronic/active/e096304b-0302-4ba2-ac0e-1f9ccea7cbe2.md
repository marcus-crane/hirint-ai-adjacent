Saronic Technologies is a leader in revolutionizing autonomy at sea, dedicated to developing state-of-the-art solutions that enhance maritime operations through autonomous and intelligent platforms.

### Job Overview

We are seeking a Salesforce Developer with strong experience in Field Service Lightning to build and optimize field service solutions. This role will focus on scheduling, dispatch, and mobile workforce enablement within Salesforce.

**Responsibilities**

* Design and develop solutions within Salesforce Field Service Lightning
* Customize FSL components including work orders, service appointments, and scheduling
* Develop Apex, Lightning components, and automation for FSL use cases
* Integrate FSL with other enterprise systems such as ERP and middleware
* Collaborate with stakeholders to improve field service processes
* Support mobile solutions and offline capabilities
* Ensure performance, scalability, and usability of FSL implementations
* Troubleshoot and resolve platform issues
* Maintain technical documentation and best practices

**Qualifications**

* 5 to 8 plus years of Salesforce development experience
* Hands-on experience with Salesforce Field Service Lightning
* Strong experience with Apex and Lightning components
* Understanding of scheduling and field operations workflows
* Experience with integrations and APIs
* Salesforce FSL certification preferred
* Strong problem-solving and collaboration skills
* Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or related field

**Physical Demands**

* Prolonged periods of sitting at a desk and working on a computer.
* Occasional standing and walking within the office.
* Manual dexterity to operate a computer keyboard, mouse, and other office equipment.
* Visual acuity to read screens, documents, and reports.
* Occasional reaching, bending, or stooping to access file drawers, cabinets, or office supplies.
* Lifting and carrying items up to 20 pounds occasionally (e.g., office supplies, packages).
