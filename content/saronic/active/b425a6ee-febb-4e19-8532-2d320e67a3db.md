Saronic Technologies is a leader in revolutionizing autonomy at sea, dedicated to developing state-of-the-art solutions that enhance maritime operations through autonomous and intelligent platforms.

# **Job Overview**

We’re looking for a Product Marketing Manager who can translate complex technical capabilities into clear, compelling narratives that resonate with defense and government audiences. You’ll own the product marketing function — shaping how our platforms and autonomy stack are positioned, messaged, and presented to the customers and stakeholders who matter most.

This role sits at the intersection of product, growth, and creative, and is ideal for someone who thrives in fast-moving environments where the technology is as ambitious as the mission.

# **Key Responsibilities**

* Define and drive product positioning, messaging frameworks, and win themes across Saronic’s portfolio of autonomous surface vessels and software capabilities
* Own and implement marketing campaigns designed to further audience understanding of Saronic’s core capabilities
* Work closely with growth to help maintain a library of product marketing collateral including presentation decks, slick sheets, capability briefs, and other materials
* Partner closely with the product team to deeply understand platform capabilities, roadmap priorities, and technical differentiators — and translate them into customer-facing value propositions
* Collaborate with the growth and business development teams to ensure marketing materials directly support capture efforts, proposals, and customer engagements
* Work with the creative team to brief and produce high-quality visual assets, graphics, and multimedia content that support product marketing initiatives
* Own and contribute to product-related web copy, ensuring messaging is consistent, current, and optimized for target audiences
* Support trade show and event presence by helping define key content themes and overall participation strategy
* Track and analyze the effectiveness of product marketing efforts, iterating based on customer feedback and market response

# **Basic Qualifications**

* 5+ years of product marketing experience, preferably in defense, aerospace, or other highly technical industries
* Demonstrated ability to distill complex, technical subject matter into clear, persuasive messaging for non-technical and semi-technical audiences
* Exceptional writing and editing skills
* Experience producing marketing collateral for government or defense customers, including familiarity with how defense procurement and capture processes work
* Track record of close collaboration with product management, engineering, and sales/BD teams
* Self-starter comfortable operating in a high-velocity startup environment
* Bachelor’s degree in marketing, communications, engineering, or a related field
* U.S. citizenship required

# **Preferred Qualifications**

* Strong visual sensibility and experience briefing creative teams on asset production
* Familiarity with defense contract vehicles, proposal support, and the language of government buyers
* Experience with platforms such as Salesforce or similar CRM/marketing automation systems
* Experience working in high-growth startup environments
* Ability to operate autonomously and collaborate across technical and non-technical teams
* Active security clearance is helpful but not required
