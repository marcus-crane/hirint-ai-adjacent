Saronic Technologies is a leader in revolutionizing autonomy at sea, dedicated to developing state-of-the-art solutions that enhance maritime operations through autonomous and intelligent platforms.

# **Job Overview**

We are seeking a skilled Manufacturing Engineer with a specialization in composites to support the design, development, and optimization of manufacturing processes for autonomous surface vessels. The ideal candidate will bring hands-on experience in composites manufacturing and a passion for driving continuous improvement in a fast-paced marine environment.

# **Key Responsibilities**

* Develop, implement, and optimize composite (fiberglass) manufacturing processes.
* Support production teams by troubleshooting process issues, improving quality, and reducing waste.
* Collaborate with design and engineering teams to ensure manufacturability of composite parts and assemblies.
* Create and maintain detailed process documentation, work instructions, and quality standards.
* Implement lean manufacturing and process improvement initiatives to increase efficiency and throughput.
* Support tooling design, maintenance, and optimization for composite manufacturing.
* Ensure compliance with safety and environmental regulations specific to composites handling and processing.
* Provide training and technical guidance to production staff on composite manufacturing best practices.

# **Qualifications**

* Bachelor’s degree in Mechanical Engineering, Manufacturing Engineering, Materials Science, or related field (or equivalent hands-on experience).
* Professional experience in composite manufacturing, preferably in the marine, aerospace, or automotive industry.
* Understanding of composite material properties, curing cycles, and process control. Preferably in fiberglass processes.
* Ability to read engineering drawings and translate designs into effective manufacturing processes.
* Familiarity with lean manufacturing, Six Sigma, or similar process improvement methodologies.
* Strong problem-solving skills with the ability to work hands-on in a production environment.
* Excellent communication and collaboration skills across cross-functional teams.

# **Preferred Skills:**

* Experience with CAD/CAM software for tooling and process planning.
* Knowledge of quality control techniques specific to composites (NDT methods, visual inspection, destructive testing).
* Background in boatbuilding or marine manufacturing strongly desired.
* Broad understanding of product lifecycle, bill-of-materials management, and material traceability.
* Project management experience, including process scale-up and introduction of new materials or technologies.
