Saronic Technologies is a leader in revolutionizing autonomy at sea, dedicated to developing state-of-the-art solutions that enhance maritime operations through autonomous and intelligent platforms.

# **Job Overview:**

We're seeking a Security Liaison responsible for ensuring the safety, security, and compliance of all visitors, vendors, contractors, and other non-authorized personnel while on company property. This position serves as an authorized escort, providing continuous oversight and guidance to ensure adherence to company security policies, safety requirements, and operational procedures.

# **Responsibilities:**

* Escort vendors, contractors, visitors, and other non-cleared personnel throughout company facilities as required.
* Monitor escorted personnel to ensure compliance with all company security, safety, and operational policies and procedures.
* Prevent unauthorized access to restricted, controlled, or sensitive areas.
* Verify visitor credentials, badges, and access authorizations before granting entry into designated spaces.
* Maintain continuous visual contact with escorted individuals while they are in authorized areas.
* Report security violations, suspicious activity, safety concerns, or policy non-compliance to Security Management immediately.
* Ensure visitors and contractors follow all applicable safety requirements, including the use of required personal protective equipment (PPE).
* Assist with visitor check-in and check-out procedures, badge issuance, and access control processes.
* Document escort activities, incidents, and visitor movements in accordance with company requirements.
* Provide guidance on facility rules, emergency procedures, and workplace expectations to escorted personnel.
* Support security operations during audits, inspections, and special projects as needed.
* Maintain professionalism and customer service while enforcing security requirements.

# **Qualifications:**

* High school diploma or equivalent.
* Ability to understand and enforce security policies, procedures, and access control requirements.
* Strong attention to detail and situational awareness.
* Excellent verbal communication and interpersonal skills.
* Ability to remain alert and attentive for extended periods.
* Ability to work independently and exercise sound judgment.
* Basic computer skills for logging activities and completing reports.

# **Physical Demands:**

* Ability to walk and stand for extended periods throughout the workday.
* Ability to escort personnel throughout large facilities, including office, manufacturing, warehouse, and outdoor environments.
* Ability to occasionally lift up to 25 pounds.
* Ability to respond quickly to security or safety incidents when necessary.

# **Work Environment:**

This position may operate in office, manufacturing, warehouse, or outdoor environments. The Security Escort must maintain a high level of vigilance while interacting with a variety of visitors and contractors in a professional and courteous manner.
