Saronic Technologies is a leader in revolutionizing autonomy at sea, dedicated to developing state-of-the-art solutions that enhance maritime operations through autonomous and intelligent platforms.

# **Job Overview**

We are seeking an **Electrical Engineer** to join the R&D Test Team. The engineer’s responsibilities include supporting the improvement of existing multi-disciplinary systems related to developing new autonomous vehicle concepts and prototypes. Technical activities include assisting in engineering, designing, building, and testing of mechanical, electrical, electronic, and software driven systems in the process of developing various autonomous vehicle systems.

The candidate must be able to apply engineering fundamentals, work within demanding timeframes, and develop practical solutions to technical and logistical problems while learning on the job.

# **Responsibilities**

* Assist in understanding the technical requirements, problems, or client briefs and support development of solutions that satisfy the requirements.
* Support engineering, design, and prototype development of electro-mechanical systems and software for autonomous vehicles and related devices and subsystems.
* Apply fundamental engineering principles, best practices, and analysis in areas including mechanical, electrical, electronics, and software under guidance of senior engineers.
* Assist in conducting applied research, documenting findings, and presenting results.
* Support development of design documents for parts, sub-systems, and final integrated systems.
* Assist in selecting the required tools and materials for manufacturing, assembly, and maintenance processes.
* Support integration of electro-mechanical systems and software into fully integrated systems.
* Execute proof of concept, functionality, and reliability testing of components and sub-systems.
* Support testing efforts in the laboratory and field environments, including marine environments.
* Monitor systems during testing and collect and organize data for analysis.
* Assist in troubleshooting electrical and system-level issues under direction.
* Identify basic trends in runtime and failures of ASV components and subsystems through data analysis.
* Support identification of areas of weakness in systems and processes and contribute ideas for improvement.
* Responsibilities support the development of ASV platforms and the software to interface with them.

# **Qualifications**

* BS in electrical engineering, robotics engineering, mechatronics engineering, or similar.
* 0-4 years of relevant experience (internships or co-ops applicable).
* Foundational understanding of engineering fundamentals, best practices, and analysis methods.
* Basic experience operating, maintaining, diagnosing, testing, or repairing electrical or electro-mechanical systems.
* Exposure to power calculations, measurement, and low voltage systems is a plus.
* Familiarity with electrical schematic development and usage.
* Exposure to SPICE-based circuit simulations or equivalent is a plus.
* Exposure to PCB design and development is a plus.
* Familiarity with networking concepts: ethernet, CAN, serial is a plus.
* Exposure to sensors and data acquisition systems.
* Basic software skills (Python, C/C++, Linux, or similar) preferred.
* Ability to work in collaborative team environments and follow technical direction.
* Strong willingness to learn and grow in a hands-on R&D environment.

# **Preferred Qualifications**

* Interest or experience in autonomous vehicle systems
* Exposure to robotics, controls, or embedded systems
* Experience working in laboratory or prototyping environments
* Exposure to generating schematics, bill of materials, or work instructions
* CAD modeling experience
* Familiarity with electrical engineering simulation tools
