Saronic Technologies is a leader in revolutionizing autonomy at sea, dedicated to developing state-of-the-art solutions that enhance maritime operations through autonomous and intelligent platforms.

# **Job Overview**

We are seeking talented and motivated marine designers and drafters to support the development of autonomous surface vessels. This role will provide hands-on experience in traditional vessel design and advanced autonomous technologies; driving impact in defense innovation.

# **Responsibilities**

* Support new vessel construction through detailed 2D and 3D modeling and technical drawings and production support
* Create and modify ship construction drawings
* Work directly with naval architects, marine engineers, structural engineers, and electrical engineers on new vessel design implementation
* Develop and maintain strict drawing standards and best practices
* Mentor and guide other designers in both CAD modeling and ship design best practices
* Create documentation for marine regulatory compliance
* Work with production and build engineering team to document as built details

# **Qualifications**

* Associate degree or technical certification in Drafting, Engineering Technology or similar field of study
* 6+ years of CAD drafting experience in the marine industry
* Experience leading a team of marine designers
* Strong expertise in one or more areas of vessel design: general design, mechanical design, electrical design, structural design, and piping design
* Knowledge of maritime regulatory requirements, construction tolerances, and vessel construction processes and sequences
* Experience creating and submitting drawing for submittal to classification societies such as ABS, DNV, and LR.
* Proficiency with 2D and 3D CAD software like NX, ShipConstructor, AutoCAD, and Rhino
