Saronic Technologies is a leader in revolutionizing autonomy at sea, dedicated to developing state-of-the-art solutions that enhance maritime operations through autonomous and intelligent platforms.

# Job Overview

Saronic is seeking a Senior EWIS Engineer to own and drive EWIS architecture execution for a platform or major subsystem, translating schematic intent into manufacturable harness documentation and reliable vessel integration. This role leads technical decisions, supports production scale-up, interfaces directly with manufacturing and suppliers, and mentors junior EWIS engineers while ensuring compliance with applicable standards and program requirements.

# **Responsibilities & Requirements**

* Develop and maintain EWIS architectures, schematics, and harness assembly drawings in Zuken E3 with a strong emphasis on design intent, configuration control, and manufacturability.
* Lead complex 3D harness routing and packaging trade studies using NX (or equivalent), balancing integration constraints, serviceability, and production repeatability.
* Independently size wiring, shields, and protection devices considering continuous/transient loads, voltage drop, derating, thermal environment, and fault conditions.
* Identify and mitigate EMI/EMC risk through grounding/bonding strategy, routing/separation, shielding, and termination choices aligned to system architecture.
* Review and approve EWIS designs produced by Level 1 engineers; mentor and raise documentation and design quality across the team.
* Serve as a primary EWIS interface to manufacturing and external suppliers; drive build readiness, incorporate DFM feedback, and mitigate schedule/technical risk.
* Plan and review EWIS verification & validation activities; lead root-cause analysis and corrective actions for complex wiring, EMI, and integration issues.
* Interpret and enforce IPC/WHMA, ABYC, ABS, and applicable MIL standards within projects, ensuring consistent application across designs and releases.

# **Qualifications & Skills**

* Bachelor’s degree in Electrical Engineering, Mechatronics, or related field.
* 5–12+ years of EWIS, harness engineering, or electrical integration experience with demonstrated ownership.
* Proficiency with Zuken E3 (or equivalent) and 3D harness routing/integration tools (e.g., NX or equivalent).
* Deep expertise in EWIS integration and production support, including independent wire/shield/protection sizing and robust grounding/bonding/shielding strategies.
* Strong ability to interpret and produce complex technical documentation (schematics, BOMs, harness/installation drawings) and ensure design intent carries through to installation.
* Proven ability to lead cross-functional technical execution, improve workflows/documentation quality, and mentor engineers.
