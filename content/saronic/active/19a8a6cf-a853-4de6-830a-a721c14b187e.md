Saronic Technologies is a leader in revolutionizing autonomy at sea, dedicated to developing state-of-the-art solutions that enhance maritime operations through autonomous and intelligent platforms.

### Job Overview

We are seeking a skilled Salesforce Developer to design, develop, and maintain scalable solutions within the Salesforce platform. This role will support business processes across sales, service, and operations while ensuring alignment with enterprise architecture and integration strategies.

**Responsibilities**

* Design, develop, and implement Salesforce solutions using Apex, Lightning, and related technologies
* Customize Salesforce objects, workflows, and automation
* Develop and maintain integrations with external systems such as NetSuite and MuleSoft
* Collaborate with business analysts and architects to translate requirements
* Ensure code quality through best practices and testing
* Support deployment, release management, and CI/CD processes
* Troubleshoot and resolve system issues
* Maintain documentation for solutions and enhancements
* Ensure platform performance, scalability, and security

**Qualifications**

* 5 to 8 plus years of Salesforce development experience
* Strong experience with Apex, Lightning Web Components, and Visualforce
* Experience with Salesforce Sales Cloud and Service Cloud
* Experience with integrations using APIs and middleware
* Familiarity with DevOps tools and deployment pipelines
* Strong understanding of data modeling and platform limits
* Salesforce certifications preferred
* Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or related field

**Physical Demands**

* Prolonged periods of sitting at a desk and working on a computer.
* Occasional standing and walking within the office.
* Manual dexterity to operate a computer keyboard, mouse, and other office equipment.
* Visual acuity to read screens, documents, and reports.
* Occasional reaching, bending, or stooping to access file drawers, cabinets, or office supplies.
* Lifting and carrying items up to 20 pounds occasionally (e.g., office supplies, packages).
