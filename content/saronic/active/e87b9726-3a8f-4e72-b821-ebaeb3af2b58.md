Saronic Technologies is a leader in revolutionizing autonomy at sea, dedicated to developing state-of-the-art solutions that enhance maritime operations through autonomous and intelligent platforms.

# **Job Overview**

As a Senior Technical Sourcer, you will identify and engage world-class engineering talent across autonomous systems, robotics, AI/ML, hardware, and electrical. You will focus on uncovering highly specialized, often passive candidates and partnering closely with recruiters and engineering leaders to build strong, forward-looking talent pipelines in a fast-paced defense environment.

# **Responsibilities**

* Proactively source hard-to-find talent across Computer Vision, Machine Learning, Robotics, Electrical, Embedded, Autonomy, and Systems Engineering
* Develop creative sourcing strategies using LinkedIn Recruiter, GitHub, Stack Overflow, referrals, events, and targeted research
* Build and maintain long-term pipelines for critical and future hiring needs
* Partner with recruiters and hiring managers to define search strategies and align on priorities
* Conduct initial screens to assess technical depth, domain expertise, and program eligibility (including defense requirements)
* Provide market insights and track sourcing metrics to continuously improve effectiveness
* Maintain accurate and organized records of sourcing activity within the ATS
* Track and analyze sourcing metrics including pipeline health, conversion rates, and time-to-engagement
* Ensure a high-quality candidate experience through thoughtful communication and timely follow-up

# **Qualifications**

* 5+ years of technical sourcing or recruiting experience, including 3+ years supporting deeply technical engineering roles
* Proven success sourcing in Computer Vision, Machine Learning, Electrical, Embedded, Robotics, or related fields
* Strong proficiency with advanced sourcing tools and Boolean techniques
* Experience in defense, aerospace, robotics, manufacturing, or other regulated environments
* Excellent communication skills and ability to influence senior technical stakeholders
* Ability to manage multiple high-priority searches independently in a startup environment

Preferred:

* Experience supporting roles within defense, aerospace, robotics, manufacturing, or other highly regulated technical environments
* Background in high-growth or scaling organizations
* Working knowledge of autonomous systems and hardware/software integration landscapes

# **Physical Demands**

* Prolonged periods of sitting at a desk and working on a computer.
* Lifting and carrying items up to 20 pounds occasionally (e.g., office supplies, packages).
