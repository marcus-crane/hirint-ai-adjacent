Saronic Technologies is a leader in revolutionizing autonomy at sea, dedicated to developing state-of-the-art solutions that enhance maritime operations through autonomous and intelligent platforms.

# **Job Overview**

We are seeking a highly motivated and skilled Wire Harness Technician with mechanical experience to join our team at our Austin-based startup dedicated to developing cutting-edge autonomous surface vessels for the Department of Defense. As a Wire Harness Technician, you will play a crucial role in the assembly and integration of electrical systems within our vessels, ensuring they meet the highest standards of quality and reliability.

# **Responsibilities**

* Wire Harness Assembly: Construct, route, and install wire harnesses according to engineering schematics and specifications.
* Component Integration: Collaborate with mechanical engineers and other team members to integrate wire harnesses into various components of the vessel, such as propulsion systems, sensors, and communication devices.
* Quality Control: Conduct thorough inspections of completed wire harness assemblies to verify accuracy, functionality, and compliance with industry standards and project requirements.
* Troubleshooting: Identify and troubleshoot electrical and mechanical issues related to wire harnesses during assembly and testing phases, implementing solutions to ensure optimal performance.
* Documentation: Maintain detailed records of wire harness assembly procedures, modifications, and testing results to facilitate future reference and continuous improvement efforts.
* Safety Compliance: Adhere to all safety protocols and regulations to create a safe working environment for yourself and your colleagues.
* Collaboration: Work closely with cross-functional teams, including electrical engineers, mechanical technicians, and project managers, to achieve project milestones and deliverables within established timelines.

# **Requirements**

* Technical Expertise: Proficiency in wire harness assembly techniques, including soldering, crimping, and connector termination, with a strong understanding of electrical and mechanical principles.
* Experience: Previous experience in a similar role, preferably in a manufacturing or engineering environment, with a demonstrated track record of success in assembling and integrating complex electrical systems.
* Attention to Detail: Meticulous attention to detail and precision, with the ability to accurately interpret engineering drawings, wiring diagrams, and technical specifications.
* Problem-Solving Skills: Strong analytical and troubleshooting abilities, with the capacity to identify and resolve electrical and mechanical issues efficiently and effectively.
* Team Player: Excellent communication and interpersonal skills, with a collaborative mindset and the ability to work effectively as part of a multidisciplinary team.
* Adaptability: Flexibility to adapt to evolving project requirements and priorities in a fast-paced startup environment, with a proactive and can-do attitude.
* Education: High school diploma or equivalent required; additional technical certifications or coursework in electrical engineering, mechanical engineering, or a related field preferred
