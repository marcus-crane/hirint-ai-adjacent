Saronic Technologies is a leader in revolutionizing autonomy at sea, dedicated to developing state-of-the-art solutions that enhance maritime operations through autonomous and intelligent platforms.

# **Job Overview:**

We are seeking a reliable, experienced, and safety-focused Shuttle Driver responsible for transporting passengers safely and efficiently to and from designated locations. The ideal candidate will have a strong driving record with no moving violations (including speeding tickets or other infractions that negatively impact a driving record), demonstrate professionalism on the road, and prioritize passenger safety at all times.

# **Responsibilities:**

• Safely operate a shuttle vehicle in accordance with all traffic laws, company policies, and safety regulations  
• Transport passengers along assigned routes and schedules with a focus on smooth, safe driving practices  
• Pick up and drop off passengers at designated locations in a timely manner  
• Assist passengers with boarding, luggage, and accessibility needs as required  
• Maintain constant awareness of road conditions, traffic patterns, and passenger safety  
• Conduct thorough pre-trip and post-trip inspections of the vehicle  
• Maintain cleanliness and basic upkeep of the shuttle vehicle  
• Communicate promptly with dispatch or supervisors regarding delays, incidents, or route issues  
• Provide a professional, courteous, and customer-focused experience for all passengers

# **Qualifications:**

• Valid driver’s license (CDL may be required depending on vehicle type)  
• Clean driving record with no moving violations, including speeding tickets or other infractions  
• Proven safe driving history and strong understanding of defensive driving practices  
• Previous professional driving or transportation experience preferred  
• Ability to work flexible hours, including evenings, weekends, or holidays  
• Strong communication and interpersonal skills  
• Ability to remain calm, alert, and professional in all driving conditions

# **Physical** Requirements:

• Ability to sit for extended periods while operating a vehicle  
• Ability to assist passengers with boarding   
• Good vision and hearing for safe vehicle operation

# Preferred Qualifications:

• Commercial Driver’s License (CDL) with passenger endorsement  
• Knowledge of local routes and traffic patterns  
• First Aid/CPR certification
