Saronic Technologies is a leader in revolutionizing autonomy at sea, dedicated to developing state-of-the-art solutions that enhance maritime operations through autonomous and intelligent platforms.

# **Job Overview:**

As an HR Generalist at Saronic, you will play a crucial role in shaping our company culture and supporting our rapid growth. The HR Generalist will support a broad range of HR functions, including HRIS management, onboarding, employee relations, performance management, and compliance. Your contributions will help us retain top talent while fostering an inclusive and innovative workplace.

# **Responsibilities:**

* **HR Information Systems (HRIS) Management:**Administer and maintain HRIS, ensuring data integrity and accuracy**.**Generate reports and analytics to support decision-making and HR strategy. Troubleshoot HRIS issues and liaise with vendors as necessary.
* **Onboarding and Training:**Manage the onboarding process for new hires, ensuring a smooth transition into the company and develop training materials and programs that align with our mission and industry standards.
* **Compliance and Record Keeping:**Ensure compliance with federal and state employment laws and regulations, particularly regarding defense industry standards**.**Maintain employee records and HR databases, ensuring confidentiality and accuracy
* **HR Policies and Procedures:**Assist in the development and implementation of HR policies and procedures**.**Stay updated on industry trends and best practices to enhance our HR processes

# **Qualifications:**

* Bachelor’s degree in Human Resources, Business Administration, or a related field
* 5+ years of experience in HR, preferably in a fast-paced startup environment
* Knowledge of HR practices, labor laws, and regulations
* Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
* Strong organizational skills and attention to detail
* Ability to maintain confidentiality and handle sensitive information
* Proficiency in HR software and Microsoft Office Suite
