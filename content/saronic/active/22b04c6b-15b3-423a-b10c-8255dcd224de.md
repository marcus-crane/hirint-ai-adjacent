Saronic Technologies is a leader in revolutionizing autonomy at sea, dedicated to developing state-of-the-art solutions that enhance maritime operations through autonomous and intelligent platforms.

# **Job Overview**

We are seeking a talented Manufacturing Tooling Engineer to join our mission. Saronic's engineering team prides itself on offering innovative solutions to everyday mechanical problems. While acting as mentors to our engineering and production teams, our marine mechanical engineers engage in rigorous design and analysis processes using their strong engineering fundamentals paired with a first principles approach to engineering. Our ideal candidate will have experience with the entire engineering design lifecycle, carrying mechanical solutions from concept to full rate production.

# **Requirements**

* Design, build and validate both manufacturing and assembly tooling to support the Corsair & Mirage product lines
* Interface with design and manufacturing engineering to ensure tooling design meets the hardware design requirements and supports full rate production
* Evaluate mechanical designs for potential flaws and offer modifications to eliminate potential problems
* Prepare assembly or product layout and detailed drawings applying GD&T, and participate in the manufacture or building of tooling
* Analyze tooling designs using hand calculations and finite element models
* Develop validation plans for tooling to ensure requirements are satisfied
* Ensure that design implementation, test, and maintenance methods/processes are developed and executed in a manner that supports company quality standards

# **Qualifications**

* Bachelor's degree or greater in Mechanical Engineering or a related field
* 2+ years designing complex manufacturing or assembly tooling
* Strong fundamentals in core engineering concepts, including statics, solid mechanics, and machine design
* Proven experience with tooling development and the entirety of the product life cycle
* Proficiency with CAD and finite element analysis software, such as Siemens NX and FEMAP
* Proven experience developing innovative solutions on a range of mechanical problems
