Figure is an AI robotics company developing autonomous general-purpose humanoid robots. The goal of the company is to ship humanoid robots with human level intelligence. Its robots are engineered to perform a variety of tasks in the home and commercial markets. Figure is headquartered in San Jose, CA.

We're seeking a Humanoid Environment Specialist to support our offsite data collection operations. This hands-on role involves traveling to collection homes across the country to set up realistic environments — arranging furniture, props, and scene configurations—that enable high-quality data capture for training our humanoid robots.

Your work directly impacts Figure's path to generalized humanoids by ensuring our robots learn from diverse, well-constructed real-world scenarios.

**Responsibilities**

* Travel to offsite data collection locations (75%+ travel required)
* Set up and configure collection home environments, including furniture arrangement, prop placement, and scene staging
* Ensure environments meet specifications for optimal data collection quality
* Coordinate with the data collection team to understand scene requirements and timelines
* Load, unload, and transport equipment, furniture, and props to collection sites
* Maintain inventory of props and equipment; report damage or replacement needs
* Troubleshoot on-site issues and adapt setups as needed
* Document site configurations and capture photos for reference
* Ensure collection spaces are safe and compliant with operational standards

**Requirements**

* 1+ years of experience in set design, staging, event setup, warehouse operations, or similar hands-on role
* Ability to travel 75% or more, including extended stays at remote locations
* Comfortable with physical work: lifting 30+ lbs regularly, moving furniture, standing for extended periods
* Valid driver's license and clean driving record - Comfortable driving a van
* Strong attention to detail and ability to follow setup specifications precisely
* Self-starter who can work independently with minimal supervision
* Good communication skills and ability to coordinate with remote teams
* Flexibility to adapt to changing schedules and site conditions
* Must be able to pass a background check

**Bonus Qualifications:**

* Familiarity with smart home devices or consumer electronics setup
* Experience with inventory management or logistics
* Basic photography skills for documenting site configurations
* Forklift certification or experience with equipment handling
* Experience in film/TV production, photography sets, or real estate staging

This is a contract position with potential to convert to full time, based on performance and business needs. The US base hourly range for this contract position is $28 - $38/hr.
