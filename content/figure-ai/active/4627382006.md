Figure is an AI robotics company developing autonomous general-purpose humanoid robots. The goal of the company is to ship humanoid robots with human level intelligence. Its robots are engineered to perform a variety of tasks in the home and commercial markets. Figure is headquartered in San Jose, CA.

We are seeking a Mechanical Engineer focused on Thermal Prototyping to rapidly design, prototype, and test thermal solutions that reduce noise and improve system reliability across our robot platform. This role is extremely hands-on: continuously building models, running benchtop experiments, instrumenting components, and helping push the boundaries of silent thermals for humanoid robotics.

**Responsibilities:**

* Design, build, and iterate 3D CAD models of thermal components: heat sinks, cold plates, airflow channels, fans, ducts, insulation, and passive/active cooling structures.
* Prototype concepts using FDM/SLA prints, CNC, sheet metal, foam, gasketing, and rapid thermal mockups.
* Run experiments that characterize noise sources and thermal loads across heat generating components including motors, gearboxes, electronics, and actuators.
* Evaluate fan characteristics and system impedance to optimize for minimal acoustic signature
* Instrument prototypes with thermocouples, microphones, accelerometers, airflow sensors, and DAQ hardware.
* Perform thermal bench testing, steady-state and transient measurements, and acoustic testing (near-field, far-field).
* Perform smoke tests, dye flow visualization, and IR thermography to understand heat flow and hotspot mechanisms.

**Requirements:**

* 2+ years of experience in mechanical, thermal, or electromechanical hardware development.
* Bachelor's or Master's degree in Mechanical Engineering, Thermal Engineering, Mechatronics, or similar.
* Strong 3D CAD proficiency
* Experience building prototypes and running hands-on thermal/acoustic tests.
* Familiarity with simulation tools (CFD, FEA, or equivalent).
* Comfort working with fans, heat sinks, interface materials, and general thermal componentry.
* Ability to design, execute, and document structured experiments.

**Bonus Qualifications:**

* Background in acoustic modeling, vibration damping, or noise isolation techniques.
* Experience with DAQs, IR cameras, sound chambers, hot-wire anemometers, or other instrumentation.
* CATIA V6 experience.

The US base salary range for this full-time position is between $120,000 - $180,000 annually.
