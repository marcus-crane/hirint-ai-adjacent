Figure is an AI Robotics company developing a general purpose humanoid. Our humanoid robot, Figure 03, is designed for commercial tasks and the home. We are based in San Jose, CA and require 5 days/week in-office collaboration. It’s time to build.

We are looking for an experienced Power Electronics Engineer to help us design and test critical hardware to power our Humanoid.

**Responsibilities:**

* Develop conceptual power architectures and conduct trade studies to support key design decisions
* Design and validate PCBAs for critical components of the humanoid like power converters and motor controllers
* Support design reviews (schematics, layouts, etc)
* Support system integration and test activities by contributing to test execution and debugging

**Requirements:**

* Bachelor or graduate degree in Electrical Engineering or related field
* 3+ years of industry experience
* Knowledge of power converter topologies and control theory
* Experience working with low voltage (<60V) DC/DC power converters or motor controllers
* Experience sizing and designing discrete power electronics (controllers, gate drivers, MOSFETs, inductors, XFRMs, caps, sensing, etc)

**Bonus Qualifications:**

* Experience with Altium
* Experience with PCB layout, designing for power converters and EMC mitigations
* Experience simulating power electronic circuits
* Experience with high voltage applications and protection circuitry
* Experience working with embedded control systems and/or digital power control
* Strong knowledge of C or Python

The US base salary range for this full-time position is between $150,000 - $250,000 annually.
