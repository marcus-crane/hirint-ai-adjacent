Figure is an AI Robotics company autonomous general-purpose humanoid robots. The goal of the company is to ship humanoid robots with human level intelligence. Its robots are engineered to perform a variety of tasks in the home and commercial markets. We are based in North San Jose, CA and require 5 days/week in-office collaboration. It’s time to build.

We are looking for a Finance Manager. In summary, the role will have responsibilities spread between FP&A and Strategic Finance.

**Responsibilities**:

* Financial Planning & Analysis (FP&A): Conduct budgeting, forecasting, and variance analysis to support business objectives.
* Strategic Finance: Provide data-driven financial insights and recommendations to senior leadership for strategic decision-making.
* Financial Modeling: Develop and maintain complex financial models incorporating extensive data analysis.
* Cross-Functional Collaboration: Partner with various departments to understand their data needs and solve capital allocation problems using analytical methods.
* Reporting: Prepare and present comprehensive financial and data analytics reports to stakeholders.
* Process Improvement: Identify and implement enhancements to financial processes through data analytics and automation.

**Requirements**:

* Education: Agnostic. A track record as a proactive self-learner is more important.
* Experience: Minimum of 5 years of experience in finance roles, with a balanced background in FP&A, strategic finance within the manufacturing industry.
* Analytical Skills: Strong analytical and problem-solving abilities with attention to detail.
* Communication: Excellent verbal and written communication skills; ability to convey complex financial and data concepts clearly.

**Bonus Qualifications**:

* Experience working in Deeptech e.g. AI.

The US base salary range for this full-time position is between $200,000 - $260,000 annually.
