Figure is an AI robotics company developing autonomous general-purpose humanoid robots. The goal of the company is to ship humanoid robots with human level intelligence. Its robots are engineered to perform a variety of tasks in the home and commercial markets. Figure is based in San Jose, CA and require 5 days/week in-office collaboration.

We are seeking a **Systems Integration Engineer – Head Subsystem** to lead the bring-up, integration, and validation of the robot’s head electronics. You will own electrical integration from early prototypes through full-robot and production builds, ensuring robust power delivery, signal integrity, thermal performance, and system reliability.

**Key Responsibilities:**

* Lead electrical bring-up and system integration of the head subsystem across embedded compute, power regulation, sensing, display, audio, and communication interfaces.
* Plan and execute subsystem and system-level validation of power delivery, high-speed interfaces (USB, DisplayPort, MIPI, PCIe, Ethernet, SGMII), RF, and thermal performance.
* Develop and execute electrical validation plans covering power integrity, signal integrity, EMI susceptibility, and thermal behavior.
* Debug complex cross-disciplinary issues across hardware, firmware, and software using telemetry, oscilloscopes, DAQs, logic analyzers, and other lab instrumentation.
* Drive integration of power architectures, grounding strategies, and interface definitions to ensure reliable operation in a compact, high-density electromechanical assembly.
* Analyze system data and define KPIs to track electrical performance, reliability, and fleet health over time.
* Document bring-up procedures, validation workflows, test results, and integration learnings to support scaling to production and manufacturing test.

**Requirements:**

* Bachelor’s in Electrical or Systems Engineering (or equivalent experience).
* 4–8+ years of experience in electrical systems integration, validation, or test for embedded or robotic systems.
* Strong understanding of power systems, power regulation, grounding, and signal integrity in embedded compute platforms.
* Experience with high-speed digital and communication interfaces (USB, PCIe, Ethernet, MIPI, DisplayPort).
* Hands-on experience with electrical and thermal validation using oscilloscopes, DAQs, power analyzers, and thermal instrumentation.
* Proficiency in Python for automation, scripting, and data analysis; familiarity with C++ a plus.
* Strong debugging, documentation, and cross-functional collaboration skills.

**Preferred Experience:**

* Experience integrating embedded compute, displays, RF, or audio systems in robotics, automotive, aerospace, or consumer electronics.
* Background in EMI/EMC testing, mitigation, or compliance support.
* Experience with thermal management and cooling strategies in dense electronic assemblies.
* Familiarity with manufacturing bring-up, factory test development, or sustaining engineering.
* Willingness to travel occasionally for on-site integration or manufacturing support.

The US base salary range for this full-time position is between $150,000 and $250,000 annually.
