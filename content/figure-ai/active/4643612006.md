Figure is an AI robotics company developing autonomous general-purpose humanoid robots. The goal of the company is to ship humanoid robots with human level intelligence. Its robots are engineered to perform a variety of tasks in the home and commercial markets. Figure is based in San Jose, CA and requires 5 days/week in-office collaboration.

We are looking for a Mechanical Design Engineer with experience owning complex mechanical modules from concept through production. The ideal candidate has operated in a high-tech, fast-paced environment, is comfortable driving across multiple stakeholders, and has a strong background in design, testing, validation, and scaling hardware to production. Robotics experience is not required.

**Responsibilities:**

* Design mechanical parts and assemblies in 3D CAD that meet structural, durability, manufacturability, and industrial design requirements
* Generate accurate 2D drawings using GD&T in accordance with ASME Y14.5
* Perform tolerance stack-ups and review quality and inspection reports to ensure design intent is met
* Prototype, build, and test components / systems to validate performance, reliability, and lifetime requirements
* Lead and support testing / validation efforts at the component, module, and system level
* Collaborate closely with electrical engineers and systems/integration teams on electromechanical designs
* Partner with supply chain, contract manufacturers, manufacturing engineers, and quality teams to drive designs through NPI and into production
* Own and drive Failure Analysis & Corrective Actions (FA/CA) for complex issues discovered during manufacturing, testing, or field use
* Inspect first articles, support build readiness, and continuously support hardware on development platforms

**Requirements:**

* 3+ years of experience in mechanical engineering
* Bachelor’s or Master’s Degree in Mechanical, Biomechanical, Robotics, Mechatronics, or similar field
* Expert in using 3D CAD software to design parts with complex geometry
* Experience owning and designing complex mechanical systems/modules end-to-end, including requirements definition, design tradeoffs, build, and validation
* Experience working across multiple disciplines with competing and strict technical requirements
* Experience with engineering analysis and simulation tools (e.g., FEA, MATLAB, or equivalent)
* Strong understanding of statistical analysis methods (e.g., Cp/Cpk, Design of Experiments (DOE), variance and sensitivity studies) for component- and system-level characterization, validation, and manufacturing readiness
* Experience shipping hardware at scale, working with contract manufacturers, supporting NPI builds, and driving complex Failure Analysis & Corrective Actions
* Familiarity with electromechanical integration, including sensors, motors, motor drivers, wire harnesses, and communication interfaces

**Bonus Qualifications:**

* 5+ years of experience
* Experience in controls, integration, and validation
* CATIA V6 Experience

The US base salary range for this *full*-time position is between $120,000 - $250,000 annually.
