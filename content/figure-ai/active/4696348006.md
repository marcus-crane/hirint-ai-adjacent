Figure is an AI Robotics company developing autonomous general-purpose humanoid robots. The goal of the company is to ship humanoid robots with human level intelligence. Its robots are engineered to perform a variety of tasks in the home and commercial markets. We are based in North San Jose, CA and require 5 days/week in-office collaboration. It’s time to build.

We are building the data foundation that powers our humanoid robots. The Data Operations team creates and curates the training data our AI systems learn from, and the Data Creators organization owns how that data gets captured, at what volume, and to what standard, across our sites.

As a Project Coordinator, you'll own a data collection project end to end, from setup through delivery. You'll be the primary point of contact between project execution and Helix engineering, translating data requirements into a capture plan the team can run, keeping delivery on schedule, and clearing the infrastructure and hardware issues that get in the way. You'll work closely with your Data Quality counterpart to catch and resolve issues at data creation time.

**Responsibilities**:

* Own a data collection project end to end, from setup through delivery, against timeline and quality targets.
* Serve as the primary point of contact with a Helix engineer for your project, and translate data requirements into an executable capture plan.
* Ensure on-time delivery of your project's data; track progress and manage dependencies.
* Highlight and resolve infrastructure, hardware, and facilities issues that block capture.
* Partner with the Data Quality team to catch and resolve issues at data creation time.
* Write and maintain project-level SOPs, and update them through post-project retros.
* Contribute to task design and capture setup for your project type.
* Manage direct reports where applicable, including structured performance reviews.

**Requirements**:

* 2+ years in operations, data collection, production coordination, or a related field, with demonstrated ownership of a project or workstream end to end.
* Experience keeping a project on schedule and managing dependencies across people, hardware, and facilities.
* Experience writing or maintaining SOPs that other people executed against.
* Comfort working directly with engineering or technical stakeholders to translate requirements into operational guidance.
* Clear communication and the ability to be the single point of contact for a project.
* People management experience, or readiness to take on direct reports.

**Bonus Qualifications**:

* Background in robotics, autonomous systems, motion capture, or physical AI data.
* Familiarity with motion capture or wearable sensor hardware
* Experience in high-growth startup operations.

The US base salary range for this full-time position is $40/hr.
