## **Who we are**

Helsing is a defence AI company. Our mission is to protect our democracies. We aim to achieve technological leadership, so that open societies can continue to make sovereign decisions and control their ethical standards. 

As democracies, we believe we have a special responsibility to be thoughtful about the development and deployment of powerful technologies like AI. We take this responsibility seriously. 

We are an ambitious and committed team of engineers, AI specialists and customer-facing programme managers. We are looking for mission-driven people to join our European teams – and apply their skills to solve the most complex and impactful problems. We embrace an open and transparent culture that welcomes healthy debates on the use of technology in defence, its benefits, and its ethical implications.

## **The role**

Being an integrated element of Production Quality, as Incoming Inspector in Helsings Production environment you are responsible for the Quality Assurance Gate on supplied goods into our warehouse by verification of conformity, as well outbound quality aspects on customer deliverables or supplier returns in close relation with our Logistic,  Supply Chain - and further Production-Quality functions.

## The day-to-day

* Execute incoming goods inspection based on applicable specifications and secure required documentation
* Maintain and process reporting of non-conform material including conform handling and marking
* Contribute to and drive root cause corrective action and improvement activities
* Oversight and control of blocked / quarantined material in your area including scrap control
* Ensure process compliance to internal QMS, EN9100 and others applicable standards

## **You should apply if you**

* Can show up with a professional education in electronics, mechanics or mechatronics with min. 3 years of quality assurance experience in electronics (machinery or plant engineering, automotive), experience in warehouse inbound QA is of advantage
* Demonstrate applied and proven experience on inspection methods by visual means or via the use of standard test / measurement equipment and able to read, understand, and apply technical documents ( Engineering drawings / Inspection procedures )
* Have the experience in maintaining IT Software applications like MS Office, QA SW if applicable. SAP experience is of advantage.
* Can work independently as well in a team and subordinate him/herself with the ability to assert oneself and respect other positions
* Maintain a process oriented attitude and mindset with flexibility and creativity to achieve given goals
* Carry good communication skills in English, German of advantage
* Are flexible to work in shift

**Note: We operate in an industry where women, as well as other minority groups, are systematically* *under-represented. We encourage you to apply even if you don’t meet all the listed qualifications; ability and impact cannot be summarised in a few bullet points.**
