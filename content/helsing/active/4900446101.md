## **Who we are**

Helsing develops artificial intelligence-enabled capabilities to protect and defend democracies. We build Altra, an AI-powered drone software platform, and HX-2, our autonomous drone. We are growing our US operations, cultivating an ambitious and committed team of mission-driven professionals to apply their skills to solve challenging problems. 

## **The role**

You will work on Altra, Helsing's drone software platform, primarily written in Rust. You will design and build systems-level software that powers autonomous capabilities in challenging operational environments. You don't need to be a drone expert — but you should be a strong systems programmer who thrives in low-latency, high-reliability environments and is excited to apply those skills to a mission-critical product. 

## **The day-to-day**

* Designing, building, and maintaining Rust services across the Altra platform

* Collaborating with ML engineers to integrate AI/ML pipelines into the broader system

* Working across the stack, from networking and protocol implementation to application-level services

* Contributing to increasingly advanced autonomy features

* Developing and testing software using simulation environments

* Writing software that must perform reliably on resource-constrained platforms under real-world constraints

## **You should apply if you**

* Have 5+ years of experience in systems-level software engineering

* Have a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field

* Are proficient in Rust, or have strong experience in C++ or another systems-level compiled language with a willingness to work in Rust daily

* Have experience building distributed systems, particularly in unreliable or constrained network environments

* Have worked with real-time or low-latency systems where performance and reliability are critical

* Have experience with at least one or two areas relevant to our stack: video streaming, pub/sub messaging, sensor data processing, embedded systems, protocol design, or robotics

* Have designed or built systems that interface with AI/ML pipelines — streaming data to models, handling inference outputs, managing pipeline orchestration

* Are familiar with Python, particularly for interfacing with ML/AI components

* Are a U.S. citizen eligible to obtain a security clearance

## **Nice to have**

* Direct experience with Rust in production systems

* Background in robotics, autonomous vehicles, or drone systems

* Experience with sensor fusion or multi-sensor data processing

* Familiarity with swarming, multi-agent systems, or decentralized coordination algorithms

* Experience with constrained or embedded platforms

* Familiarity with Nix or NixOS for reproducible builds and development environments

* Experience with SITL/HITL (Software/Hardware-In-The-Loop) simulation and testing frameworks for autonomous systems

* Experience with RF communications, telemetry, or datalink systems

* Background in defense or national security
