## **Who we are**

Helsing is a defence AI company. Our mission is to protect our democracies. We aim to achieve technological leadership, so that open societies can continue to make sovereign decisions and control their ethical standards. 

As democracies, we believe we have a special responsibility to be thoughtful about the development and deployment of powerful technologies like AI. We take this responsibility seriously. 

We are an ambitious and committed team of engineers, AI specialists and customer-facing programme managers. We are looking for mission-driven people to join our European teams – and apply their skills to solve the most complex and impactful problems. We embrace an open and transparent culture that welcomes healthy debates on the use of technology in defence, its benefits, and its ethical implications.

## **The role**

Finance Transformation at Helsing is not a back-office function: it is one of the forces shaping the company's strategic direction. As Transformation PMO Lead, you will govern the portfolio of all major company-wide programmes, ensuring that Finance serves as the connective tissue holding Helsing's strategic agenda together. You will build and maintain the single source of strategic truth for the CEO and leadership team, design the governance cadence that keeps decisions moving, and surface risks before they become problems. This role requires the ability to operate with equal effectiveness at Board level and within a cross-functional sprint. A strong PMO succeeds through relationships and clarity, not authority. This role can be based anywhere in Europe.

## **The day-to-day**

* Own the Finance Transformation programme plan and the cross-company project portfolio, including milestones, dependencies, resource allocation, critical path, and budget governance
* Design and facilitate Helsing's strategic governance cadence, covering weekly status rhythms, monthly steering committees, and quarterly Board updates, ensuring each session produces a clear decision rather than a status report
* Track the company's top strategic programmes, including HX-2 production, AI software initiatives, M&A activity, and Finance Transformation workstreams, against milestones and budgets, surfacing resource conflicts before they escalate
* Maintain the programme risk register and bring blockers to the appropriate decision-makers with clear, well-structured options rather than open-ended problems, and own the Finance Transformation budget with quarterly return on investment reporting
* Use AI tools such as Notion AI, Microsoft Copilot, or custom LLM agents to auto-synthesise status inputs from cross-functional leads and produce CFO-ready dashboards, and deploy automated dependency-tracking logic that flags critical-path risks before they reach a steering committee

## **You should apply if you**

* Have led complex, multi-workstream transformation programmes in tech, deep-tech, defence, or a fast-scaling environment, with a track record of managing real organisational dependencies across functions
* Hold a programme management qualification such as PMP or PRINCE2, and have applied these frameworks in mixed engineering and finance contexts
* Have built Board-ready governance dashboards and presented strategic tracking directly to C-suite and Board-level audiences
* Are fluent in financial modelling and budget governance, with the literacy to challenge a programme business case, track a transformation budget, and ask the right questions in a project review
* Build trust and influence across Engineering, Legal, HR, and Finance without relying on hierarchy, and communicate fluently in English; German is a strong advantage
