## **Who we are**

Helsing is a defence AI company. Our mission is to protect our democracies. We aim to achieve technological leadership, so that open societies can continue to make sovereign decisions and control their ethical standards. 

As democracies, we believe we have a special responsibility to be thoughtful about the development and deployment of powerful technologies like AI. We take this responsibility seriously. 

We are an ambitious and committed team of engineers, AI specialists and customer-facing programme managers. We are looking for mission-driven people to join our European teams – and apply their skills to solve the most complex and impactful problems. We embrace an open and transparent culture that welcomes healthy debates on the use of technology in defence, its benefits, and its ethical implications.

## **The role**

We are in search of a Graphic Designer to uphold the Helsing brand to the highest standards. In this role, you will implement the visual design of the brand, ensuring outstanding delivery across all marketing channels. We seek a creatively driven individual with impeccable aesthetic judgment, capable of communicating innovative products, and able to craft brand interactions that authentically reflect our values and mission.

## **The day-to-day**

* Own projects, from creative brief to production
* Maintain a high-quality, consistent visual aesthetic across all touchpoints
* Create presentation materials to support business and customer communications
* Design compelling visuals for digital, print and experiential environments
* Collaborate with product, program and communications teams to devise engaging ways of communicating our products

## **You should apply if you**

* Possess a diverse portfolio demonstrating high-quality design across various styles and disciplines. **Please include a PDF or link to your portfolio, along with links to relevant websites and videos** **showcasing your best work**
* Exhibit a strong understanding of typography, colour theory, layout, and composition
* Are experienced in articulating complex or abstract products through design
* Are savvy at building presentations and visualising proposals
* Are known for a meticulous, pixel perfect approach
* Are capable of independently managing multiple projects, anticipating challenges, and formulating intelligent solutions
* Possess a quick learning curve and intellectual curiosity to learn new tools such as AI image generation, and explore new areas such as radar systems, military doctrine, cognitive load, and ethics
* Are proficient in Figma and Adobe Creative Suite, especially Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign

*Note: We operate in an industry where women, as well as other minority groups, are systematically* *under-represented. We encourage you to apply even if you don’t meet all the listed qualifications; ability and impact cannot be summarised in a few bullet points.*

## **Nice to Have**

* Another creative skill such as copywriting, photography or videography
* Knowledge of After Effects or Premiere
* A second language, ideally French or German

The defence industry is entering the most exciting phase of the technological development curve. Advances in our field of world are not incremental: Helsing is part of, and often leading, historic leaps forward

At Helsing you will have a say in how we (and you!) work, the opportunity to engage on what does and doesn’t work, and to take ownership of aspects of our culture that you care deeply about
