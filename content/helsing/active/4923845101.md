## **Who we are**

Helsing is a defence AI company. Our mission is to protect our democracies. We aim to achieve technological leadership, so that open societies can continue to make sovereign decisions and control their ethical standards. 

As democracies, we believe we have a special responsibility to be thoughtful about the development and deployment of powerful technologies like AI. We take this responsibility seriously. 

We are an ambitious and committed team of engineers, AI specialists and customer-facing programme managers. We are looking for mission-driven people to join our European teams – and apply their skills to solve the most complex and impactful problems. We embrace an open and transparent culture that welcomes healthy debates on the use of technology in defence, its benefits, and its ethical implications.

## **The role**

As a Wireless Communications Systems Engineer, you will develop modern wireless communication and edge-computing systems that operate reliably in demanding environments. You will work across the full lifecycle, from system architecture and link-budget analysis to prototyping on embedded hardware.   
You will design SDR-based solutions, implement signal-processing software in Python and C/C++, and integrate them on platforms such as NVIDIA Jetson and Raspberry Pi.   
Working within an interdisciplinary engineering team, you will move between RF, embedded systems and software to turn technical concepts into field-ready capabilities. Your work will directly strengthen the communication systems that underpin Helsing's mission to protect democracies.

## The day-to-day

* Design, integrate and optimise wireless communication and SDR-based systems, from link-budget analysis through to deployed signal-processing solutions

* Build, test and debug prototypes on embedded and edge-computing platforms such as Raspberry Pi, NVIDIA Jetson and microcontrollers

* Develop software in Python and C/C++, and configure Linux-based systems for real-world operation

* Define technical concepts and system architectures that bridge the RF, embedded and software domains
* Tune end-to-end systems to meet measurable performance, reliability and field-readiness targets

## **You should apply if you**

* Hold a degree in communications, electrical or telecommunications engineering, computer science with an embedded or wireless focus, or have equivalent practical expertise

* Have a deep command of digital wireless communications and SDR tools such as GNU Radio, including link-budget analysis and digital signal processing

* Have built and integrated embedded Linux systems, with hands-on Python development

* Translate technical concepts into working implementations across architecture, analysis and practical engineering

* Work in a structured, methodical way and collaborate effectively within interdisciplinary teams

* Are a clear communicator: You can translate complex requirements into actionable advice for technical and non-technical stakeholders alike with professional fluency in German and English

*Note: We operate in an industry where women, as well as other minority groups, are systematically under-represented. We encourage you to apply even if you don't meet all the listed qualifications; ability and impact cannot be summarised in a few bullet points.* 

## **Nice to Have**
