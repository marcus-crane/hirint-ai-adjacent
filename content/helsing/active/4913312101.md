As democracies, we believe we have a special responsibility to be thoughtful about the development and deployment of powerful technologies like AI. We take this responsibility seriously.

---

**The role**

As (Senior) Programme Manager for Integrated Logistics Support, you will own end-to-end ILS delivery for CA-1, from concept definition through to Final Operational Capability and sustained in-service support. Working across maintenance planning, supply chain, technical documentation, training and support equipment, you will ensure the programme meets its through-life support commitments. You will structure and cost ILS proposals for defence customers, manage complex master schedules, and hold cost and risk baselines to account. Your work will be central to Helsing's ability to deliver operationally ready, sustainably supported capabilities to its customers.

---

**The day-to-day**

* Own the full ILS lifecycle for CA-1, coordinating across logistics elements including maintenance planning, spare parts management, technical data, training and support equipment to meet programme milestones
* Build and manage ILS Master Schedules, risk registers, and cost baselines, providing stakeholders with clear, evidence-based status reporting and corrective action plans
* Lead ILS bid and proposal activity, structuring and costing integrated support offers that are technically credible and commercially competitive for defence customers
* Define and implement in-service support frameworks, including performance-based logistics and availability contracting arrangements, to secure long-term operational readiness
* Collaborate with engineering, procurement, and customer-facing teams to align ILS outputs with programme requirements from Initial Operational Capability through to sustained in-service phases

---

**You should apply if you**

* Have delivered Integrated Logistics Support or Integrated Product Support programmes within a defence or aerospace environment, taking ownership from concept through to in-service sustainment
* Bring hands-on experience across multiple ILS elements, including maintenance planning, supply support, technical documentation, training, and support equipment
* Have structured, costed, and led ILS proposals and bids for defence customers, with a clear understanding of how support solutions are evaluated commercially and technically
* Have managed complex programme controls, including master schedules, milestone tracking, risk registers, and cost baselines, across multi-stakeholder environments
* Understand through-life support concepts including performance-based logistics, availability contracting, and in-service sustainment planning

**Note:** We operate in an industry where women, as well as other minority groups, are systematically under-represented. We encourage you to apply even if you don't meet all the listed qualifications; ability and impact cannot be summarised in a few bullet points.
