## **Who we are**

Helsing is a defence AI company. Our mission is to protect our democracies. We aim to achieve technological leadership, so that open societies can continue to make sovereign decisions and control their ethical standards. 

As democracies, we believe we have a special responsibility to be thoughtful about the development and deployment of powerful technologies like AI. We take this responsibility seriously. 

We are an ambitious and committed team of engineers, AI specialists and customer-facing programme managers. We are looking for mission-driven people to join our European teams – and apply their skills to solve the most complex and impactful problems. We embrace an open and transparent culture that welcomes healthy debates on the use of technology in defence, its benefits, and its ethical implications.

## **The role**

We are seeking an experienced and highly motivated Associate Director / Senior Programme Manager to lead the development and delivery of our space-based tactical surveillance, reconnaissance, and target acquisition system. This is a critical leadership position responsible for orchestrating a complex, multi-year programme as part of our [recently announced consortium](https://helsing.ai/de/newsroom/helsing-und-ohb-gruenden-joint-venture-kirk-fuer-taktische-aufklaerungssysteme-aus-dem-weltraum). As part of the joint venture you will be responsible for ensuring the overall programme’s success whilst also focusing on the specific AI and software elements being developed by Helsing.

## **The day-to-day**

* **Leadership**: Provide overall direction and management for the ISR constellation, ensuring alignment with the company’s strategic objectives and stakeholder requirements.
* **Cross-functional Management:** Coordinate and lead multidisciplinary teams from different partner companies (payload, bus, software, as well as launch, ground and operations segments) to achieve programme milestones.
* **Technical Oversight:** Ensure the successful development of artificial intelligence and software and how this is integrated and testing into the overall constellation.
* **Schedule and Budget Management:** Develop, maintain, and control integrated master schedules, cost baselines, and resource allocation to ensure the programme is delivered on time and within budget.
* **Risk Management:** Proactively identify, assess, and mitigate programme risks and opportunities, developing contingency plans as needed.
* **Stakeholder Engagement:** Serve as the primary interface with internal leadership, external partners, customers, and government stakeholders, managing expectations and fostering strong relationships.
* **Reporting and Communication**: Provide regular, clear, and concise status updates, reports, and presentations to senior management, customers, and stakeholders on programme progress, issues, and accomplishments.
* **Contract Management:** Oversee contract execution, manage supplier relationships, and ensure compliance with all relevant regulations and standards.
