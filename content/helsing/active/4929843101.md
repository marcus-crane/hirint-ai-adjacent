## **Who we are**

Helsing develops artificial intelligence-enabled capabilities to protect and defend democracies. We are growing our US operations, cultivating an ambitious and committed team of mission-driven professionals to apply their skills to solve challenging problems.

## **The role**

You will serve as the strategic voice during a pivotal growth phase — establishing, translating, and scaling our brand identity across the United States defense market. This is more than managing social media accounts or drafting press releases; it is an opportunity to lead the narrative on how AI-enabled capabilities protect democratic values. You will act as the vital bridge between our US business and our European parent company, applying global brand guidelines while crafting highly targeted, localized communications that resonate with the US Department of War, national security partners, and the broader aerospace and defense ecosystem. 

If you thrive in fast-paced, high-stakes environments where your storytelling, relationship building, and strategic communications directly enable breakthrough technologies for the warfighter, we want to hear from you.

## **The day-to-day**

* Coordinate closely with our parent company’s marketing and communications teams to adapt and deploy global brand guidelines, ensuring a unified global presence while maintaining hyper-relevance to US government and commercial audiences.

* Identify, build, and nurture strategic communications and marketing partnerships with US defense trade presses, journalists, contractors, technology integrators, research organizations, and academic institutions to amplify our market presence.

* Own the end-to-end planning, execution, and strategy for our participation in major US defense, AI, and national security conferences (e.g., AUSA, Sea-Air-Space, AFA).

* Write and edit high-impact collateral, white papers, case studies, website content, and executive communications tailored to the nuances of the US defense procurement landscape.

* Manage local agency partners to drive strategic media placements, monitor industry coverage, and coordinate PR campaigns that build trust and credibility.

* Ensure internal communications align with external narratives, keeping our rapidly expanding US team inspired, informed, and cohesive.

## **You should apply if you**

* You are energized by fast-paced environments, work beautifully under pressure, and are deeply motivated by mission-driven work.

* You possess exceptional written and verbal communication skills with a proven ability to translate highly complex, deep-tech AI capabilities into clear, compelling narratives for diverse audiences.

* You have 7+ years of experience in Marketing, Communications, or Public Relations, with a strong preference for candidates who have worked within the US defense tech, aerospace, or deep-tech startup ecosystems.

* You have experience operating across international matrix structures, partnering seamlessly with global stakeholders, localized leadership, and external agency partners.
* You have a track record of executing high-profile industry events, managing budgets, and organizing complex logistics under tight deadlines.

Note: We encourage you to apply even if you don’t meet all the listed qualifications; ability and impact cannot be summarized in a few bullet points.
