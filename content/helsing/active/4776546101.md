## **The role**

As Systems Safety Engineer, you will own the application of a comprehensive safety approach within a specific programme, ensuring that safety practices are integrated rigorously across design, development and delivery lifecycles. Working closely with engineering and delivery teams, you will conduct safety assessments against ARP4761A and MIL-STD-882, govern safety requirements traceability and present structured safety cases to programme stakeholders. Your work directly determines whether Helsing's systems meet their safety objectives and comply with applicable standards, contributing to both programme-level compliance and the continuous maturation of safety processes across the organisation.

## **The day-to-day**

* Conduct system safety assessments including AFHA/SFHA, PASA/PSSA, ASA/SSA and Particular Risk Analysis in accordance with ARP4761A, and perform hazard analyses and risk assessments at system, subsystem and component level in compliance with MIL-STD-882

* Develop, manage and maintain system safety requirements with full traceability, supporting engineering teams in integrating safety considerations into their design and development processes from the outset rather than retrospectively

* Contribute to the creation and maintenance of a System Safety Programme Plan that aligns with MIL-STD-882 and ARP4754B, ensuring documentation meets the rigour required for regulatory compliance and audit readiness

* Present safety cases clearly to programme stakeholders, translating complex safety data and risk assessments into structured, decision-ready arguments that directly influence design and programme milestone decisions

* Support and participate in safety audits to confirm that systems meet defined safety objectives, identifying gaps, driving corrective actions and feeding lessons learned back into Helsing's cross-programme safety processes

## **You should apply if you**

* Have conducted system safety assessments, including FHA, PSSA, SSA and PRA, applying ARP4761A and MIL-STD-882 within aerospace, defence or similarly regulated engineering programmes

* Have developed and managed safety requirements with full traceability using tools such as Jama Software, IBM DOORS or equivalent, with a working knowledge of FMEA, FTA and related safety analysis techniques

* Have presented safety cases to technical and non-technical stakeholders, translating complex safety arguments into clear, structured documentation that supports audit and regulatory compliance

* Apply systems engineering best practices including requirements development, verification and validation to integrate safety rigorously across multidisciplinary programme teams

* Act decisively with minimal guidance, own outcomes end-to-end and initiate solutions without waiting for direction
