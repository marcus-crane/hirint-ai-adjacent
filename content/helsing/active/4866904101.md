## **Who we are**

Helsing develops artificial intelligence-enabled capabilities to protect and defend democracies. We are growing our US operations, cultivating an ambitious and committed team of mission-driven professionals to apply their skills to solve challenging problems.

## **The role**

We are looking for a Head of Logistics to design, build and lead the logistics function for Helsing's US business. You will be responsible for the end-to-end movement of hardware, components and finished products across our US operations — from inbound supply chain through to customer delivery, field deployment and sustainment. You will set the strategy, build the team, establish the systems, and create the partnerships required to scale logistics from early-stage shipments to high-volume production support. You will work closely with manufacturing, supply chain, program management, and customer-facing teams to ensure Helsing can deliver on its commitments to the US Department of Defense and allied customers.

## **The day-to-day**

* Define and execute the logistics strategy for Helsing's US business across inbound, outbound, warehousing, transportation and reverse logistics

* Build and lead a high-performing logistics team, scaling from a small founding group to a distributed organization

* Establish warehousing, fulfilment and distribution infrastructure across the US, including secure facilities where required

* Manage international and domestic freight operations, including ITAR/EAR-controlled shipments and customs compliance

* Develop and manage relationships with 3PLs, carriers, freight forwarders and customs brokers

* Implement the systems, tools and processes (WMS, TMS, ERP integration) required to give Helsing real-time visibility and control over its physical operations

* Partner with manufacturing and supply chain leaders to ensure smooth flow of materials into production and finished goods to customers

* Support field deployments, exercises and customer demonstrations with rapid, reliable logistics execution — including international movements to allied theatres

* Establish KPIs, reporting and continuous improvement practices to drive cost, speed and reliability

* Ensure full compliance with US export controls, DoD requirements, hazmat regulations and relevant transportation law

## **You should apply if you**

* 10+ years of logistics leadership experience, ideally spanning defense, aerospace, or complex hardware manufacturing

* Proven track record of building and scaling logistics operations from the ground up

* Deep understanding of US defense logistics, including working with the DoD, prime contractors, and government furnished equipment processes

* Strong knowledge of ITAR, EAR, and US customs regulations governing the movement of defense-related goods

* Experience managing 3PL and carrier relationships at scale, with strong commercial and operational instincts

* Familiarity with WMS, TMS and ERP systems, and the ability to make sound build-vs-buy decisions

* Experience supporting field operations, including international deployments and time-critical movements

* Excellent leadership and communication skills, with a track record of building diverse, high-performing teams

* US citizenship required; ability to obtain a US security clearance

* Willingness to travel domestically and internationally as required

 

Note: We encourage you to apply even if you don’t meet all the listed qualifications; ability and impact cannot be summarized in a few bullet points. 

## **Nice to Have**

* Existing US security clearance (Secret or above)
