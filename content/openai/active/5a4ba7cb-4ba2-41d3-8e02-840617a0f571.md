# **About the Role**

We’re looking for an experienced Engineering Manager to **found and lead** the ChatGPT Infrastructure team in London.

You’ll lead a **small, talent-dense, high-impact team** building the core runtime primitives and infrastructure that underpin how new ChatGPT experiences are built and scaled.

This is a **high-ownership, 0→1 leadership role** blending hands-on technical leadership, startup-style problem solving, and founding-team building.

You’ll operate with a high degree of autonomy, working closely with product and research teams to design and **build critical, load-bearing infrastructure** components, while **establishing the engineering culture** and high-trust environment that will come to define our London presence.

# **You will**

* Build and lead a **small, talent-dense team** responsible for foundational runtime systems powering ChatGPT.
* Drive the development of **core infrastructure primitives** that product engineers rely on to build new ChatGPT experiences (e.g., runtime frameworks, service integration patterns, and extensibility mechanisms within the ChatGPT runtime).
* Define and evolve the **technical architecture of the ChatGPT runtime**, unlocking new feature capabilities as part of our next phase of rapid growth.
* Work closely with product and research teams to ensure infrastructure enables **rapid product development while maintaining strong reliability and operational discipline**.
* **Own the reliability of the systems your team builds**—participating in incident response, supporting deployments, and helping evolve our operational model toward a follow-the-sun global infrastructure team.
* Hire and develop the early London team, helping establish the **technical bar, culture, and operating model** that will define our London engineering presence.
* Serve as a **technical leader and connector** between the London office and the broader global ChatGPT infrastructure organization.

# **Qualifications**

* 7+ years of hands-on software engineering experience, ideally **building large-scale distributed systems, developer platforms, or infrastructure products**.
* Experience **building and leading high-caliber engineering teams**, particularly in startup or early-stage initiatives within larger companies.
* Proven ability to create a **high-trust, high-agency culture** where strong engineers can move quickly, take ownership, and operate effectively in ambiguous environments.
* Strong judgment in **hiring and talent development**, with a track record of building small, high-performing teams and maintaining a high technical bar.
* Track record of remaining **deeply technical as a leader**, contributing to architecture and design decisions while enabling others to do their best work.
* Experience operating in **0→1 or high-ambiguity environments**, where teams are defining problems and building systems from scratch.
* Experience running production systems in cloud environments (e.g., AWS, GCP, Azure).
* Based in or willing to relocate to London.
