**About the Team**

OpenAI's Industrial Compute organization is building the world's most advanced AI infrastructure ecosystem. Through a combination of strategic partnerships and self-built campuses, we are delivering hyperscale AI infrastructure capable of supporting frontier model training and inference at unprecedented scale.

Our Design & Engineering team develops the technical standards that enable Industrial Compute to build faster, smarter, and more efficiently than traditional data center programs. We partner closely with Architecture, Mechanical, Electrical, Structural, Construction, Infrastructure Delivery, Capacity Planning, and external engineering firms to continuously improve how AI campuses are designed and delivered.

As our self-build portfolio continues to expand, Civil Engineering is responsible for developing scalable site designs that improve constructability, reduce deployment timelines, and establish repeatable standards for future AI campuses.

**About the Role**

We are seeking a **Civil Engineer** to lead civil engineering strategy across Industrial Compute's self-build data center portfolio. This role will own the technical direction for site development and structural engineering while partnering closely with external engineering consultants, construction partners, and internal infrastructure teams to continuously improve how our campuses are designed and built.

Rather than simply reviewing consultant deliverables, you will drive engineering optimization across site grading, foundations, structural systems, utility coordination, and constructability. You will challenge traditional design assumptions, identify opportunities to reduce schedule and cost, and help establish standardized, modular engineering approaches that accelerate future deployments.

Working closely with Mechanical, Electrical, Power, Cooling, and Infrastructure Delivery teams, you will ensure civil infrastructure supports the demanding requirements of next-generation AI compute while enabling Industrial Compute to scale globally.

**Key Responsibilities**

* Lead civil engineering strategy across Industrial Compute's self-build infrastructure portfolio.
* Partner with engineering consultants and EPC firms to develop civil and structural designs for hyperscale AI campuses.
* Drive continuous improvement of site design standards to reduce construction schedule, cost, and execution risk.
* Optimize grading, earthwork, drainage, foundations, utility routing, and constructability across multiple campuses.
* Develop structural standards supporting high floor loading, vibration control, durability, and long-term serviceability for AI infrastructure.
* Establish modular and prefabricated civil engineering approaches that improve repeatability and deployment speed.
* Coordinate closely with Mechanical, Electrical, Power Generation, Cooling, and Infrastructure Delivery teams throughout design and construction.
* Review engineering calculations, drawings, specifications, and consultant deliverables for technical quality and consistency.
* Support geotechnical investigations, constructability reviews, value engineering, and design optimization initiatives.
* Evaluate emerging construction methodologies and engineering technologies that improve speed, scalability, and reliability.
* Support engineering throughout construction, commissioning, and operational handoff.
* Help establish global civil engineering standards for future Industrial Compute campuses.
