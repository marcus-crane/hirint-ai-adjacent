**About OpenAI**

OpenAI is an AI research and deployment company working to ensure that artificial general intelligence benefits everyone. We build and deploy powerful AI systems with a focus on real-world impact, safety, and responsible adoption. Our work sits at the intersection of technology, business, and society, and comes with both opportunity and scrutiny.

Our communications team supports this mission by clearly and authentically explaining our technology, products, and approach, helping business leaders, partners, developers, and policymakers understand how AI can be adopted responsibly and at scale to deliver tangible benefits.

### **The role**

We’re looking for a senior consumer and product communications lead for APAC, based in Singapore, to shape how OpenAI and its products are understood by people across the region.

Reporting to the Head of Communications for APAC, this role owns regional consumer and product communications strategy and execution, with a focus on helping audiences understand the everyday value of products like ChatGPT and Codex, building trust, and supporting long-term adoption across APAC markets. You’ll shape how OpenAI is understood by consumers, creators, developers, local communities, and the media.

This is a builder role for someone equally comfortable setting regional strategy, developing strong narratives, and helping local teams turn global priorities into work that resonates in their markets. You’ll partner closely with global teams, local communications leaders, and cross-functional partners to deliver impact on the ground.

### **What you’ll do**

* Set and execute the APAC consumer and product communications strategy, aligned with regional priorities and global narratives
* Develop clear, accessible narratives that explain the everyday benefits and usefulness of OpenAI’s products, including ChatGPT and Codex, for audiences across APAC
* Work with local comms leads to turn global launches, product updates, and research into locally relevant stories, campaigns, and media moments
* Lead earned strategy for consumer and product audiences across the region, including consumer, technology, lifestyle, culture, and creator media
* Identify and build stories that show how people across APAC are using OpenAI’s products in meaningful, practical, and locally relevant ways
* Help define the regional approach to consumer education, product storytelling, and creator or influencer engagement, working closely with local teams and relevant global partners
* Support local teams in building strong, repeatable consumer communications programmes across priority APAC markets, not just one-off launch moments

### **What you bring**

* 12+ years’ experience in consumer, product, or technology communications
* Deep experience developing consumer-facing narratives for technology products and translating complex capabilities into clear, relatable benefits
* Strong earned media experience, with relationships across consumer, technology, lifestyle, and culture media
* A good understanding of how products become part of everyday life, and how communications can help drive understanding, trust, and adoption
* Proven experience partnering closely with product, marketing, legal, policy, and research teams
* Experience operating at exec level and advising leaders in high-profile, ambiguous environments
* Ability to flex confidently across product, consumer, corporate, and policy topics with sound judgement
* Experience working in international or regional roles within global companies and complex operating environments

### **You’ll thrive in this role if**

* You like building from scratch and shaping how a company shows up with everyday users
* You have a creative, audience-first mindset and go beyond traditional product PR and media relations
* You are interested in what makes technology feel useful, relatable, and worth adopting in different markets
* You care as much about trust and understanding as you do about reach
* You are comfortable moving between big regional strategy and the practical work needed to help local teams succeed
* You’re comfortable navigating complexity, scrutiny, and fast change
* You want to work in a small, senior team where autonomy and judgement really matter
