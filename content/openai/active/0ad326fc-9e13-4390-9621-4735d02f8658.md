**About the Team**

OpenAI Finance ensures the organization is positioned for long-term success as we pursue our mission. The Revenue team plays a critical role in enabling OpenAI to scale its commercial offerings — overseeing billing operations, deal desk, revenue systems, and revenue accounting. We work cross-functionally with Product, Engineering, and Go-To-Market teams to support new monetization strategies, improve operational efficiency, and maintain financial integrity as the business grows.

**About the Role**

As OpenAI scales monetization across self-serve, enterprise, partnerships, ads, and emerging business models, we need a strong operator to bridge Revenue business needs with technical execution. This role will sit at the center of Finance, Systems, and Engineering to ensure revenue capabilities are designed, prioritized, tested, and launched in a scalable and controlled way.

The Revenue Enablement Senior Manager will own the business-side systems roadmap for Revenue, translating finance and operational requirements into executable workstreams across Financial Engineering and Business Platform teams. This person will drive launch readiness, controls, process automation, and continuous improvement across deal-to-cash and revenue operations workflows.

This is ideal for someone who combines finance systems fluency, program management rigor, and strong cross-functional influence.

**In this role, you will:**

* Own the Revenue business systems roadmap across quoting, billing, invoicing, collections, rev rec support, reporting, and close enablement.
* Translate Revenue Accounting, Billing Ops, Deal Desk, and Finance requirements into clear BRDs, requirements docs, process flows, and prioritized engineering asks.
* Serve as Revenue DRI for system initiatives delivered by Financial Engineering and Enterprise Business Platform teams.
* Lead end-to-end UAT, launch readiness, and hypercare for monetization and systems launches.
* Design scalable operational processes and controls that reduce manual work and improve close accuracy.
* Partner with FinEng, Enterprise Business Platform, GTM Systems, Accounting, Tax, FP&A, and Product teams to enable new business models.
* Drive continuous improvement across existing tooling, workflows, and data quality.

**You might thrive in this role if:**

* 8+ years in Revenue Operations, Finance Systems, Billing Operations, Revenue Accounting, or BizOps.
* Experience owning business requirements for systems builds with engineering teams.
* Strong program management skills across cross-functional initiatives.
* Deep familiarity with quote-to-cash workflows (CRM, CPQ, billing, ERP, reporting).
* Strong analytical skills with SQL / data reconciliation mindset.
* Knowledge of ASC 606 and revenue controls.
* Strong judgment, clear communication, and the ability to operate independently in ambiguous, fast-moving environments.
* Passion for technology, AI, and building systems and processes that scale responsibly.

**Workplace & Location**
