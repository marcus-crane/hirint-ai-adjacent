**About the Role**

As the Compute Capital Markets Leader focused on infrastructure, you will help shape and drive the strategic investment decisions behind OpenAI’s compute infrastructure. This is a highly cross-functional role, partnering closely with engineering, research, finance, and external partners to secure the infrastructure that powers our mission.

Compute is the engine of AI progress. Access to world-class infrastructure enables the best researchers to build the best models, which in turn deliver increasingly powerful intelligence to people and businesses around the world.

**Capital Strategy & Financing**

* Translate compute demand, GPU procurement, data center development, and power infrastructure into clear, investable frameworks for lenders, investors, and strategic partners
* Build durable financing architecture for hyperscale AI infrastructure—not just individual transactions, but repeatable long-term strategic advantage
* Partner across Finance, Treasury, and Capital Markets to optimize capital structure, expand financing capacity, and reduce cost of capital
* Develop innovative financing structures across debt, structured capital, partnerships, and alternative financing vehicles

## **Investment Planning & Decision Support**

* Build and maintain rigorous financial and investment models for highly structured infrastructure projects
* Support compute strategy, capacity expansion, supply agreements, and capital allocation decisions across infrastructure investments
* Develop frameworks for return on capital, deployment speed, risk, scalability, and long-term platform economics
* Evaluate partnership opportunities that improve capital efficiency, accelerate deployment timelines, or create strategic infrastructure advantage

## **Capital Markets & External Engagement**

* Engage banks, institutional investors, infrastructure funds, and strategic capital providers to support financing execution
* Develop investor materials, narratives, and data frameworks that translate technical infrastructure needs into capital markets language
* Support negotiations with capital providers and infrastructure partners to secure optimal long-term outcomes
* Synthesize complex financing decisions into clear recommendations for leadership and executive stakeholders

## **Cross-Functional Leadership & AI-Native Execution**

* Serve as the bridge between Compute, Finance, Treasury, Legal, IR, and leadership teams to align infrastructure strategy with capital strategy
* Translate complex technical infrastructure requirements into actionable financial strategy and executive decision support
* Bring an AI-native builder mindset by using OpenAI’s own products to improve workflows, forecasting, treasury operations, and financing execution
* Build scalable internal tools and operating models—not just traditional finance processes, but AI-native ways of working

**Experience**

* 12+ years of experience across infrastructure investing, private / growth equity, private credit, investment banking, or restructuring
* Bachelor’s degree or equivalent practical experience. MBA/CFA helpful but not required
* Experience with project finance, capital project and infrastructure financial analysis, large-scale investment projects
* Demonstrated exceptionally strong analytical, financial modeling, and written and oral communication skills
* Working knowledge of infrastructure investing is strongly preferred
