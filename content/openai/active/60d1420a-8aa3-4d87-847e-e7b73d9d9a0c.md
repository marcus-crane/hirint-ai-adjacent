## About the role

OpenAI is hiring a Deployed PM to help our customers get maximally deployed on our products. This person is the product counterpart to Deployment Engineers: someone who can spot product expansion opportunities inside an account, build trust with technical and executive stakeholders, drive cross-functional execution, and turn blockers into momentum. The role sits at the intersection of product, technical deployment, and customer strategy.

## In this role, you will

* Act as the product lead inside strategic customer accounts, helping customers adopt and expand their use of OpenAI products.
* Identify opportunities for deeper deployment, new integrations, and broader product usage across teams and workflows.
* Build trusted relationships with technical stakeholders, engineering leaders, and executives, including representing product vision to C-level audiences.
* Partner closely with Deployment Engineers and GTM teams to drive successful implementations and long-term account growth.
* Translate customer needs and technical blockers into clear product feedback and execution paths for internal teams.
* Work hands-on with integrations, MCPs, tooling, and lightweight technical workflows when needed to unblock an account.
* Own lightweight PM work needed to support enterprise deployment, then hand off the right insights and opportunities to core product teams.

## You might thrive in this role if

* You've operated in highly ambiguous environments and are comfortable being the connective tissue between customers, engineering, GTM, and product.
* You are highly credible with technical audiences and can work effectively with CTOs, senior engineers, and developer experience teams.
* You are also excellent with stakeholders: polished in presentations, strong in customer conversations, and trusted in executive settings.
* You can understand a customer's goals, translate them into a deployment strategy, and drive follow-through across multiple teams.
* You are builder-minded and willing to get hands-on when needed.
* You likely have a background as a technical founder, product founder, technical PM, solutions or product lead, or in another role that combines technical depth with product judgment and customer-facing execution.

## What we're looking for

* Strong technical fluency and comfort working directly with engineers.
* Strong product instincts, especially around identifying customer needs and translating them into product opportunities.
* Excellent stakeholder management and communication skills.
* Ability to present product strategy and deployment vision clearly to senior customer audiences.
* Comfort operating across strategy, execution, and hands-on problem solving.
* Bias toward action, ownership, and unblocking.
