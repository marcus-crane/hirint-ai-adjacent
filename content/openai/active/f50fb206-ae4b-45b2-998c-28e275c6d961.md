**About The Team:**OpenAI is an AI research and deployment company committed to ensuring that artificial general intelligence (AGI) benefits all of humanity. We push the boundaries of AI capabilities while prioritizing safety, ethical use, and human needs. Our mission requires a team that represents a diversity of perspectives, voices, and experiences.

**About the Role:**We are seeking a results-driven Partner Marketing Manager to lead our US based partner marketing efforts. You will work closely with product growth partnerships, cross-functional teams, and external partners to drive co-marketing initiatives, campaigns, and programs that accelerate awareness, adoption, and revenue through our partner ecosystem.

**In this role you will:**

* Own and scale partner marketing strategy for US based partners, designing and leading effective and scalable co-marketing programs that drive measurable growth through partners
* Serve as the lead marketing counterpart to strategic US based partners and act as the trusted functional advisor and primary liaison across internal stakeholders including, product growth partnerships, creative teams and other cross-functional teams to ensure alignment
* Support partnerships pre-deal, influencing marketing commitments, and joint value propositions; lead end-to-end go-to-market strategy and execution for key partnerships
* Define and operationalize performance frameworks, setting clear KPIs, delivering executive-level insights, and continuously optimizing programs to maximize impact, efficiency, and ROI
* Lead global-to-local execution, ensuring campaigns are effectively adapted across regions and markets while maintaining strategic consistency and brand integrity for US based partners.

**You Might Thrive in this Role if you:**

* Have 10+ years of experience in partner marketing, alliances marketing, co-marketing, or related roles
* Have a proven track record building and scaling partner marketing programs globally
* Have strong verbal and written communication skills; able to influence senior stakeholders internally and externally
* Are experienced in working cross-functionally (product, growth, sales, marketing) in fast moving environments
* Have a data-driven mindset; comfortable defining and tracking KPIs, analyzing performance, making recommendations
* Are comfortable managing multiple geographies and market nuances
* Have the ability to be hands-on: conceptualize campaigns, build assets, drive to execution
* Demonstrate strong project management and organizational skills
