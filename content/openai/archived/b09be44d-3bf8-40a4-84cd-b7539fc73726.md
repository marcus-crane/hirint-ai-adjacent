## **About the team**

OpenAI’s mission is to build safe artificial general intelligence (AGI) which benefits all of humanity. This long-term undertaking brings the world’s best scientists, engineers, and business professionals together to accomplish this.

In pursuit of this mission, our Enterprise Platform Technology team is responsible for enabling scalable go-to-market operations that help customers successfully adopt OpenAI’s products. The team partners across Sales, Marketing, Revenue Operations, Finance, Support, Data, and Engineering to design intelligent systems and workflows that support customer acquisition, lifecycle activation, and operational scale across the GTM ecosystem.

## **About the role**

OpenAI’s top-of-funnel GTM landscape is evolving into a signal-driven, data-orchestrated acquisition engine powered by AI-native workflows, enriched customer intelligence, and increasingly interconnected GTM systems. We are looking for a Technical Product Manager to help drive the strategy, alignment, prioritization, and execution of these systems across Salesforce and adjacent GTM platforms.

This role will partner closely with GTM Systems Engineering, Revenue Operations, Sales, Marketing, Data, and Enterprise Platform teams to operationalize scalable workflows supporting lead capture, enrichment, routing, activation, prospecting, and pipeline generation. The ideal candidate combines strong technical fluency, operational systems thinking, and stakeholder leadership with the ability to drive execution across highly cross-functional environments.

## **In this role, you'll:**

* Own and drive top-of-funnel GTM systems initiatives across Lead-to-Opportunity (LtO), enrichment orchestration, prospecting workflows, lifecycle activation, routing, and sales engagement operations
* Partner with stakeholders and engineering teams to identify operational pain points, define scalable solutions, evaluate technical tradeoffs, and execute across Salesforce and adjacent GTM platforms
* Align cross-functional stakeholders across GTM, Revenue Operations, Marketing, Finance, Security, Legal, Data, and Enterprise Platform teams while driving execution cadence, prioritization, and delivery visibility
* Help operationalize AI-native GTM workflows by defining scalable automation, orchestration, agentic workflows, and human-in-the-loop processes across the customer acquisition lifecycle

## **You might thrive in this role if you have:**

* Strong understanding of Lead-to-Opportunity (LtO) workflows, GTM systems, and data enrichment ecosystems supporting modern sales organizations
* Experience working across Salesforce, Marketing Automation platforms, enrichment providers, Sales Engagement tooling, routing engines, and adjacent GTM systems
* Familiarity with attribution flows, MQL/SQL lifecycle management, deduplication, territory assignment, lead routing, sequencing orchestration, and data normalization at scale
* Proven ability to drive complex cross-functional initiatives from discovery and stakeholder alignment through execution and operationalization
* Strong technical fluency across APIs, integrations, event-driven architectures, enterprise SaaS ecosystems, and Salesforce platform capabilities
* Experience partnering closely with Engineering, Revenue Operations, Sales, Marketing, Finance, Security, and Enterprise Platform teams
* Experience operating within SOX/SOC2-controlled environments with strong understanding of SDLC, QA, release management, and operational governance
* Strong written and verbal communication skills with the ability to clearly communicate priorities, tradeoffs, risks, and execution plans

## **Nice to Have**

* Ability to independently reason through technical system design, integration dependencies, and operational tradeoffs alongside engineering teams
* Familiarity with Salesforce platform architecture, automation patterns, APIs, and event-driven workflows
* Experience working within AI-native, high-growth, or highly ambiguous operating environments
* Exposure to global go-to-market operations, territory models, and regional business processes
* Ability to operate scrappily and iteratively while maintaining a strong bias toward scalability, governance, and long-term maintainability
