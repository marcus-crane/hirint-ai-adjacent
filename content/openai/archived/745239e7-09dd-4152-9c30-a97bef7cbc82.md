## **About the Team**

The **AI Deployment Management (ADM)** team enables organizations to turn OpenAI products into real, sustained impact through world-class enablement and training execution.

Our mission is to help customers successfully adopt and operationalize AI across their organizations. We partner with enterprises to translate the potential of OpenAI’s technology into durable capability—through structured training, technical enablement, and scalable deployment programs.

By helping customers move from experimentation to production, the ADM team accelerates time-to-value, deepens product adoption, and helps make OpenAI indispensable to how organizations work.

## **About the Role**

We are looking for a **Lead, ADM** to lead a team responsible for enabling enterprise customers to successfully deploy and adopt OpenAI products in the Enterprise.

This role combines **technical leadership, customer enablement, and people management**. You will build and develop a team of AI Deployment Managers while shaping scalable enablement programs that help organizations operationalize frontier AI technologies across our existing and emerging product suite.

You will work closely with **Sales, Technical Success, and Product teams** to ensure customers are equipped with the knowledge, tools, and best practices needed to successfully deploy OpenAI products across their organizations.

This role is based in Singapore. We use a hybrid work model of 3 days in the office per week and offer relocation assistance to new employees.

## **In This Role You'll:**

### **Lead Customer Enablement**

* Own the technical enablement strategy for OpenAI’s products, including **ChatGPT Enterprise, Codex, Agents, and APIs**
* Lead customer training and services across the full lifecycle—from onboarding to expansion and long-term adoption
* Design and deliver high-impact engagements including enterprise onboarding, developer workshops, executive briefings, and technical bootcamps
* Drive customer activation, sustained usage, and measurable business value through structured enablement programs
* Develop scalable enablement assets, playbooks, and best practices based on patterns observed across customers

### **Build and Lead a High-Performing Team**

* Hire, onboard, and develop a team of **Technical AI Deployment Managers**
* Provide mentorship and coaching to team members delivering enterprise enablement programs
* Establish best practices for technical enablement delivery across regions and customer segments
* Set clear goals and performance metrics aligned with customer adoption and product success

### **Drive Operational and Strategic Impact**

* Own deployment strategy for ADM engagements across enterprise customers
* Identify patterns across customer deployments and translate insights into scalable enablement frameworks
* Partner with product and go-to-market teams to incorporate customer insights into roadmap and strategy
* Improve enablement tooling, operational processes, and program scalability
* Managers in this role may also lead **strategic enterprise enablement engagements**, including executive briefings and advanced technical workshops.

## **You Might Thrive in This Role If You:**

* Have **10+ years of experience in technical enablement**
* Have **2+ years of experience leading technical, customer-facing teams** such as solutions engineering, technical success, developer relations, or technical enablement
* Are comfortable explaining complex technical systems to both **executive and developer audiences**
* Have strong technical depth across **APIs, coding, agents, and AI systems**
* Understand how AI systems are deployed in production, including concepts like **RAG, model evaluation, fine-tuning, and system tradeoffs**
* Have led **technical trainings, workshops, or enablement programs** for enterprise customers
* Can connect technical capabilities to real business outcomes such as productivity, efficiency, and revenue impact
* Thrive in fast-moving environments and enjoy learning new technologies quickly

## **Why This Role**

* **Lead a frontier AI enablement team**You will help define how OpenAI enables enterprise organizations to adopt AI at scale.
* **Scale impact through people**Rather than supporting a handful of customers yourself, you will develop technical leaders who enable hundreds of organizations adopting AI.
* **Shape OpenAI’s enterprise playbook**Your team will influence how enterprises operationalize frontier AI technologies, shaping product feedback loops and adoption strategies.
* **Turn cutting-edge AI into real-world impact**This role sits where frontier AI meets real-world deployment—helping organizations move from experimentation to durable production use.
