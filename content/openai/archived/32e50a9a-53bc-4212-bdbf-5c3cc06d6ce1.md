**About the Team**

OpenAI’s Workplace Events team brings our people, partners, and customers together through experiences that spark connection, collaboration, and inspiration. We design everything from employee celebrations and community gatherings to high-touch customer engagements that showcase the best of OpenAI. If you love creating memorable experiences and making operations feel effortless, you’ll feel right at home here.

**About the Role**

We’re looking for a highly organized, hospitality-minded **Workplace Events Coordinator** to help bring OpenAI’s events to life at our San Francisco headquarters. You’ll support a wide mix of programs—from weekly internal gatherings and team moments to curated customer visits and cross-functional events.

You’ll collaborate with teams across the company, own day-of logistics, and help ensure each event feels polished, welcoming, and thoughtfully executed. This is a great opportunity for someone who thrives in fast-paced environments, pays attention to the details, is a superb communicator and loves creating seamless, high-impact experiences for others.

This role is fully onsite 3–5 days per week in our San Francisco offices.

**What You’ll Do**

* Support the planning and execution of internal events, recurring programs, team moments, and customer-facing engagements.
* Own day-of logistics—room bookings and setups, catering coordination, sign-in and guest flow, décor, entertainment, supply management, and AV touchpoints.
* Partner with Workplace, Culinary, AV, Security, Executive Business Partners and other cross-functional stakeholders to keep programs aligned and well-coordinated.
* Maintain event calendars, run-of-show documents, checklists, and tooling that keep operations running smoothly.
* Provide warm, high-touch onsite hosting and troubleshoot issues in real time to ensure a smooth experience.
* Support teammates during large-scale events and high-volume days—our team succeeds because we collaborate closely.

**About You**

* 3+ years of experience in events, hospitality, workplace operations, or a related field.
* Warm, polished, and comfortable interacting with guests, cross-functional partners, and employees at all levels.
* Exceptionally organized with strong attention to detail—you enjoy bringing structure and clarity to moving pieces.
* Comfortable managing multiple programs at once and tracking details across various planning stages.
* A proactive problem-solver who stays calm under pressure and enjoys rolling up your sleeves.
* Experience with tools like Google Workspace, Jira, Prismm, Swoogo, or event/ops systems is a plus.
* Willingness to support occasional early-morning or evening events as needed.
