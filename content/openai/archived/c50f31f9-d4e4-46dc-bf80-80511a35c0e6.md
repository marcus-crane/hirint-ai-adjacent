### **About the Role**

We are seeking an experienced and versatile APAC B2B Marketing Lead to drive OpenAI’s enterprise and B2B marketing strategy across the Asia-Pacific region. This is a critical leadership role for a full-stack B2B marketer with strong regional judgment—someone who can operate across diverse markets, build foundational capabilities, and deliver high-impact programs that support revenue growth and long-term trust.

This role will be responsible for shaping and executing integrated B2B marketing initiatives that support enterprise adoption, partner-led growth, and developer-adjacent business audiences across APAC. The ideal candidate brings deep experience working with sales-led GTM teams, navigating complex stakeholder environments, and translating global strategy into locally effective execution across multiple APAC markets.

### **Key Responsibilities**

* Lead the development and execution of APAC B2B marketing strategy, aligned with global priorities and regional GTM objectives.
* Drive full-funnel B2B marketing programs across field, content, customer marketing, events, and partner marketing.
* Partner closely with GTM, Partnerships, Product Marketing, Comms, and Global Affairs to deliver integrated programs that support enterprise adoption and pipeline growth.
* Translate global B2B narratives and launches into regionally relevant, market-specific execution across priority APAC markets (e.g., India, Korea, Japan, ANZ, Southeast Asia).
* Establish strong regional marketing operating rhythms, including planning cycles, agency management, budget oversight, performance measurement, and reporting.
* Act as the regional voice for APAC B2B marketing, bringing market insight, customer perspective, and local nuance into global strategy discussions.
* Build and scale foundational B2B marketing capabilities in-region, with an eye toward future team growth and specialization.

### **Qualifications**

* 10+ years of experience in B2B or integrated marketing, ideally within technology, platforms, or high-growth environments.
* Proven track record of building and executing B2B marketing programs that support enterprise or partner-led GTM motions across multiple markets or regions.
* Strong understanding of APAC enterprise buying dynamics, including the role of trust, regulation, partners, and local proof points.
* Demonstrated ability to operate at both strategic and executional levels in fast-moving, ambiguous environments.
* Experience working closely with Sales, GTM, Partnerships, Product Marketing, and Comms teams in a matrixed organization.
* Excellent communicator and collaborator, with strong stakeholder management and influence skills across cultures and geographies.
* Experience managing teams, agencies, vendors, and regional partners across APAC preferred.

### **Nice to Have**

* Experience marketing AI, developer, or highly technical products.
* Familiarity with regulated industries or trust-sensitive enterprise environments.
* Prior experience leading regional or global marketing scopes.
