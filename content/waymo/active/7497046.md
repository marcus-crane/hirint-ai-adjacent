Waymo is an autonomous driving technology company with the mission to be the world's most trusted driver. Since its start as the Google Self-Driving Car Project in 2009, Waymo has focused on building the Waymo Driver—The World's Most Experienced Driver™—to improve access to mobility while saving thousands of lives now lost to traffic crashes. The Waymo Driver powers Waymo’s fully autonomous ride-hail service and can also be applied to a range of vehicle platforms and product use cases. The Waymo Driver has provided over ten million rider-only trips, enabled by its experience autonomously driving over 100 million miles on public roads and tens of billions in simulation across 15+ U.S. states.

Waymo's Systems Engineering team works together to blend software and hardware systems in groundbreaking new ways. We set the high performance standards that ensure our vehicles run smoothly and keep passengers safe, then design and perform the tests that validate that performance. We're looking for talented teammates who’ll help us maintain strong teamwork and are passionate about driving results.

*This role follows a hybrid work schedule and you will report to a Systems Engineer Lead.*

**You will:**

* Own the systems engineering for novel rider facing features including but not limited to early pullovers, rider pick up, and rider drop off
* Work with cross functional teams including software engineers, product managers, safety engineers, and operations leads to develop requirements and system architecture for new features that support critical user journeys
* Take ownership of the solution, considering applicability to long term goals, and appropriately use engineering judgement and experience to inform paths forward
* Collaborate closely with software engineers on design documents and support implementation with developmental testing
* Partner with our test engineering team to develop rigorous and scalable recurring testing to prevent regressions
* Create dashboards and monitoring to validate in field performance and be the point person to debug field issues when they occur, owning comprehensive cross functional retrospectives as needed

**You have:**

* A bachelor’s degree in computer science, electrical engineering, or other technical field
* 5+ years of experience in systems engineering, software development, or related field
* A systems engineering mindset and a proactive, data-driven approach to fixing problems
* An ability to read C++ to understand feature implementation and debug issues more effectively and an ability to write code for querying data sets (SQL) and creating dashboards
* Ability to think about the rider and an understanding of what might pose a safety risk for a given feature
* Aptitude to apply existing technical depth to new areas and learn quickly

**We prefer:**

* Experience in safety critical aspects of the operation of fleets or delivery of production products at scale
* Experience working on features or products with a user experience focus
* Experience defining requirements such as precision and recall for machine learning perception models
* Experience performing or collaborating on safety analyses (e.g., Hazard and Risk Assessment, Failure Modes and Effects Analysis (FMEA), Fault Tree Analysis, System-theoretic Process Analysis (STPA), Safety of the Intended Function (SOTIF), etc.)
