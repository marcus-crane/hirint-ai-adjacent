Waymo is an autonomous driving technology company with the mission to be the world's most trusted driver. Since its start as the Google Self-Driving Car Project in 2009, Waymo has focused on building the Waymo Driver—The World's Most Experienced Driver™—to improve access to mobility while saving thousands of lives now lost to traffic crashes. The Waymo Driver powers Waymo’s fully autonomous ride-hail service and can also be applied to a range of vehicle platforms and product use cases. The Waymo Driver has provided over ten million rider-only trips, enabled by its experience autonomously driving over 100 million miles on public roads and tens of billions in simulation across 15+ U.S. states.

The Real Estate Strategy Analyst will play a pivotal role in supporting strategic real estate decision-making across a dynamic client portfolio. This role blends data analysis, stakeholder engagement, and business case development to drive operational excellence and portfolio strategy. The ideal candidate thrives in a fast-paced, matrixed environment and is passionate about transforming complex data into actionable insights.

*In this hybrid role, you will report to the Senior Director, Global Places and Services*

**You will**

* Data Stewardship: Manage the end-to-end lifecycle of portfolio data, ensuring high integrity in supply/demand metrics, KPIs, and market health reporting.
* Requirements Gathering: Formalize demand forecasts into detailed real estate requirement documents for expansion, consolidation, and sequencing.
* Predictive Modeling: Build and maintain supply/demand models to generate the data-driven forecasts used for long-term strategic planning.
* Inform Documentation: Produce standardized reports and strategic briefings that translate complex analytics into actionable insights for leadership.
* Technical Liaison: Define and document technical requirements for the analytics team to ensure dashboards align with business strategy.
* Analytical Support: Execute data-heavy ad-hoc analysis to support business case development and multi-market occupier engagements.

**You have**

* Education: Bachelor’s degree in Business, Real Estate, Finance, Information Systems, or a related quantitative field.
* Experience: 4 years of experience in data analysis, corporate real estate, or business intelligence.
* Technical Writing: Proven ability to create clear, concise documentation and process workflows.
* Analytical Rigor: Exceptional attention to detail with the ability to manage large datasets without compromising accuracy.
* Agility: Ability to pivot between deep-dive data tasks and collaborative project coordination in a fast-paced environment.

**We prefer:**

* Technical Stack: Advanced Excel, experience with SQL, Python, Looker
* Strategic Thinking & Financial Acumen
* Experienced Leadership & Development
* Client Relationship Management
* Experience with Data-Driven Decision Making
* Communication & Collaboration among teams and clients

**Travel requirements:**

* 10 to 20%
