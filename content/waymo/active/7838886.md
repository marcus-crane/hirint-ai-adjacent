Waymo is an autonomous driving technology company with the mission to be the world's most trusted driver. Since its start as the Google Self-Driving Car Project in 2009, Waymo has focused on building the Waymo Driver—The World's Most Experienced Driver™—to improve access to mobility while saving thousands of lives now lost to traffic crashes. The Waymo Driver powers Waymo’s fully autonomous ride-hail service and can also be applied to a range of vehicle platforms and product use cases. The Waymo Driver has provided over ten million rider-only trips, enabled by its experience autonomously driving over 100 million miles on public roads and tens of billions in simulation across 15+ U.S. states.

As a member of the Communications team, you’ll be on the cutting edge of bringing autonomous driving technology to the public, developing strategies that will increase understanding, acceptance, and adoption of this life-saving technology. We work with reporters, editors, bloggers, and other influencers to tell stories about Waymo and the potential of our technology to change how we move and live. We partner with the engineering, business, policy, marketing, and product teams to create messaging for new products and other initiatives, counter misinformation, and shape our reputation.

*This role follows a hybrid work schedule and reports to the Head of Public Relations.*

**You will:**

* Lead communications strategy development and execution for the UK, including product updates and rapid response. Support additional comms announcements for business and tech news, working in collaboration with your U.S. PR colleagues.
* Build and maintain key relationships with UK and European media, conference organizers, researchers, and other external industry stakeholders.
* Collaborate closely with the rest of the PR team as well as key cross functional teams including Safety, Legal, Partnerships, Public Affairs, Marketing, Business Development, Strategy, Product, Engineering, and other internal teams.
* Responsible for some executive comms, including developing talking points, FAQs, and presentations.

**You have:**

* 10+ years of experience in communications or public relations, ideally at a high-growth or technology company.
* Experience developing and executing communications strategies across product, business, and rapid-response scenarios.
* Proven track record in media relations, working directly with UK-based reporters.
* Exceptional writing and storytelling skills with a sharp eye for detail and tone.
* Ability to thrive in a fast-paced environment, managing multiple complex projects independently while maintaining a high bar for excellence.
* A strategic and resourceful mindset, with the proven ability to transition between high-level planning and tactical execution.

**We prefer:**

* Experience within the technology, automotive, or autonomous vehicle industries (in house or agency)
* Direct experience launching new products or entering new markets with a sophisticated communications playbook.
* Undergraduate or first degree in Communications, Journalism, Marketing, or a related field. Speciality preferred but not required.
* A creative approach to audience engagement, with a history of finding innovative ways to reach both internal and external stakeholders.
