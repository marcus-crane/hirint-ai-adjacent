Waymo is an autonomous driving technology company with the mission to be the world's most trusted driver. Since its start as the Google Self-Driving Car Project in 2009, Waymo has focused on building the Waymo Driver—The World's Most Experienced Driver™—to improve access to mobility while saving thousands of lives now lost to traffic crashes. The Waymo Driver powers Waymo’s fully autonomous ride-hail service and can also be applied to a range of vehicle platforms and product use cases. The Waymo Driver has provided over ten million rider-only trips, enabled by its experience autonomously driving over 100 million miles on public roads and tens of billions in simulation across 15+ U.S. states.

Waymo’s Product Management Team is a mission-driven team responsible for writing and enacting first-of-their-kind playbooks to bring fully autonomous driving technology to market. Our team develops simple solutions for complex problems by orchestrating cross-functional efforts to safely drive Waymo’s technology and the products it enables forward. We do this by understanding the customer, the business, and the technology. We are humble about the scope of our work, collaborative in our approach to problem-solving, and ambitious about our vision for the future.

*In this hybrid role, you will report to a Senior Director of Product.*

**You will:**

* Own the roadmap for Pickup and Dropoff (PUDO) Systems - the infrastructure and APIs that operate as the ‘glue’ between the user experience, the vehicle / autonomy stack, and our servers.
* Drive the development of many of our cross-stack features, such as the boarding sequence (communication between the vehicle, riders' phones, and/or the cloud), handling off-nominal scenarios, customizing our PUDO platform for goods delivery, and more
* Translate company-level objectives to team-level outcomes and build a product roadmap to achieve those outcomes, backed by data and comprehensive industry understanding
* Collaborate with UX design, UX research, and engineering to translate complex rider needs into simple, elegant product solutions
* Lead cross-functional efforts across multiple engineering teams, marketing, business, regulatory, etc. to drive product decisions from conception to launch

**You have:**

* 5+ years Product Management experience
* A well-rounded mix of product development skills (e.g. user experience, analytics, engineering depth) combined with strong product sense and exemplary execution
* Demonstrated success in an innovative, fast-paced environment, characterized by excellent problem-solving and analytical skills, a growth mindset for product exploration, and navigating ambiguity with a strong sense of independence
* Experience partnering with UX teams to ship best-in-class consumer experiences
* Strong technical foundation. The ideal candidate will have a robust understanding of the hardware and software systems underpinning our autonomous vehicles and naturally thrive working with world-class software engineers.

**We prefer:**

* Degree in computer science, engineering, or a related field (or previous experience working as an engineer)
* Experience working on consumer-facing robotics products or other highly technical HW/SW systems
* Strong data analysis skills (SQL, etc) with the aptitude to dive deep to investigate issues and extract insights to inform product decisions
* Domain knowledge across the transportation, mobility, and/or autonomous vehicle industries

**Travel requirements**:

* Periodic travel (1-2 weeks per quarter) to Waymo markets and/or engineering offices (including Warsaw, PL)
