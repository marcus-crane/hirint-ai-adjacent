Waymo is an autonomous driving technology company with the mission to be the world's most trusted driver. Since its start as the Google Self-Driving Car Project in 2009, Waymo has focused on building the Waymo Driver—The World's Most Experienced Driver™—to improve access to mobility while saving thousands of lives now lost to traffic crashes. The Waymo Driver powers Waymo’s fully autonomous ride-hail service and can also be applied to a range of vehicle platforms and product use cases. The Waymo Driver has provided over ten million rider-only trips, enabled by its experience autonomously driving over 100 million miles on public roads and tens of billions in simulation across 15+ U.S. states.

*Waymo's Supply Chain and Manufacturing teams are making the technology of the future a reality. We handle all production processes and supplier development strategies: from contracts and purchasing, to designs for improved manufacturing, through quality control and delivery. We collaborate actively with Waymo engineering teams as well as external suppliers and partners to make sure everything is built to spec and delivered on time to meet the rising demands of our business.*

*This role follows an onsite work schedule and you will report to a MPM Manager.*

**You will:**

* Material Risk & CTB Management: Manage the Clear To Build (CTB) and Materials Risk Release processes for both new and sustaining complex modules
* Strategy Development: Design and execute the material management strategy to support all assigned programs, including sustaining and new products
* Inventory & MRP Accuracy: Ensure total accuracy of the MRP, materials planning, and inventory tracking at contract manufacturing facilities
* Forecast Alignment: Partner with Supply and Demand Planners to project long-term forecasts and secure long lead-time materials
* Strategic Procurement: Plan, procure, and deliver strategic consigned components to production sites
* Executive Reporting & Data Analysis: Represent material status at executive reviews and build complex models to forecast and allocate materials across factories and programs

**You have:**

* BA/BS degree required
* 10+ years of work experience as a Materials Planner / Materials Program Manager in Supply Chain in high-tech or automotive companies
* Strong analytical skills and advanced experience in systems and tools with the ability to create complex components supply and demand models
* Well-organized and high level of attention to detail
* Excellent interpersonal, verbal and written communication, including great presentations

**We prefer:**

* Masters or MBA are considered a plus
* Vast knowledge in SAP system

**Travel Requirements:**

* Weekly visits to the Contract manufacturers in Taiwan
